Frequently Asked Questions

How many cruise ships are in the database?

290+ ship profiles across 15 cruise lines, from Royal Caribbean's 51-ship fleet to Cunard and Virgin Voyages with 4 ships each. Counts include active ships, future builds, and retired vessels with dedicated profile pages.

Which cruise lines are covered?

Royal Caribbean, Carnival, Holland America, Celebrity, MSC, Norwegian, Princess, Silversea, Costa, Oceania, Regent Seven Seas, Seabourn, Explora Journeys, Virgin Voyages, and Cunard.

What information is on each ship profile?

Each ship profile includes gross tonnage, passenger capacity, debut year, ship class, deck plans, dining venues, live tracker, photo gallery, and planning resources.
