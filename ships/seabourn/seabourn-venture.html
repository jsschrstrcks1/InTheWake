Seabourn Venture — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

Seabourn Venture is an Expedition Class cruise ship operated by Seabourn Cruise Line. She entered service in 2022, measures 23,000 gross tons, and carries approximately 264 guests at double occupancy with a crew of approximately 250.

Key Facts

  • Class: Expedition Class
  • Year: 2022
  • Tonnage: 23,000 GT
  • Guests: 264
  • Crew: ~250
  • IMO: 9862023

Quick Answer: Seabourn Venture is a luxury expedition ship — 23,000 GT, 264 guests, launched in 2022. She carries two custom submarines, Zodiac landing craft, and a 26-person expedition team for polar and remote-destination voyages. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Who She's For: Adventurous luxury travelers who want expedition landings and submarine dives without sacrificing Seabourn's signature service. Venture suits those heading to Antarctica, the Arctic, or remote coastlines who want fine dining alongside Zodiac excursions.

Seabourn Venture bridges the gap between luxury cruise and expedition voyage. At 264 guests she is far smaller than the ocean-going Seabourn fleet, purpose-built for ice and remote landings with a PC6 ice-class hull. The two onboard submarines (optional, extra cost) offer undersea exploration unavailable on most other luxury lines. If you want traditional Seabourn luxury cruising without the expedition element, Encore or Ovation (604 guests) offer the larger ocean experience. Her sister ship Seabourn Pursuit offers identical expedition capabilities on different itineraries.

Frequently Asked Questions About Seabourn Venture

What dining options are on Seabourn Venture?

Seabourn Venture shares the Expedition Class dining programme with sister ship Pursuit. Confirmed venues include The Colonnade (indoor/outdoor casual dining) and The Grill by Thomas Keller (signature steakhouse). All dining on Seabourn is complimentary — no surcharges on any venue. See the Dining section above.

How big is Seabourn Venture?

Seabourn Venture measures 23,000 gross tons with 132 all-suite accommodations for 264 guests at double occupancy, served by a crew of approximately 250. She has a PC6 Polar Class ice-strengthened hull and carries two custom-built submarines for deep-sea expedition dives.

What is the difference between Seabourn Venture and Pursuit?

Venture (2022) and Pursuit (2023) are identical sister ships — same hull, same 264-guest capacity, same submarines. The choice between them comes down to itinerary and sailing dates, not ship quality. Venture was the first of the class; Pursuit followed a year later.

Where does Seabourn Venture sail?

Seabourn Venture sails expedition itineraries to remote destinations including Antarctica, the Arctic, the Kimberley coast of Australia, the Amazon, and sub-Antarctic islands. Her PC6 ice-class hull and Zodiac fleet allow access to places standard cruise ships cannot reach. A 26-person expedition team accompanies every voyage. Check the live tracker above for current position.

Does Seabourn Venture have submarines?

Yes. Seabourn Venture carries two custom-built submarines that can dive to depths allowing guests to view marine life, coral formations, and underwater landscapes. Submarine excursions are offered at select ports on expedition itineraries (additional charge applies).
