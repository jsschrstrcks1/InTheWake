Carnival Glory at Norfolk

Ship Overview

Carnival Glory is the second of five Conquest-class ships, entering service in 2003. Sailing year-round from Norfolk, Virginia — home to the world's largest naval base — she proudly features the Heroes Tribute Lounge added during her 2024 dry dock, honoring military service members.

Built by Fincantieri at Monfalcone, Italy at a cost of approximately $500 million, Carnival Glory delivers classic Fun Ship entertainment with convenient East Coast departure for Mid-Atlantic travelers.

FAQ

Where does Carnival Glory sail from?

What class is Carnival Glory?

What is the Heroes Tribute Lounge?

Added during her 2024 dry dock, the Heroes Tribute Lounge honors military service members — fitting for Norfolk's naval heritage.
