City of Dreams

Royal Caribbean — Original Production

Quick Answer: A musical journey through the world's most iconic cities. From New York to Paris, Rio to Tokyo, each destination comes alive through its signature music, dance styles, and cultural flair. A globe-trotting production celebrating the diversity and energy of urban life.

Best For: Entertainment seekers, families, anyone looking for unique cruise experiences.

Key Facts

  • Type: Original Production
  • Venue: Royal Theater
  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Duration: Approximately 50 minutes
  • Ships: Jewel of the Seas, Radiance of the Seas
  • Premiered: 2018

Frequently Asked Questions

Is City of Dreams free?

Yes, City of Dreams is complimentary and included in your cruise fare. No additional charge.

Do I need reservations for City of Dreams?

No reservations required. Seating is open and first-come, first-served. Arrive early for the best seats.

Which ships have City of Dreams?

City of Dreams is currently available on: Jewel of the Seas, Radiance of the Seas. Show lineups can change, so verify with Royal Caribbean before sailing.

How long is City of Dreams?

City of Dreams runs approximately 50 minutes.

Is City of Dreams appropriate for children?

Yes, City of Dreams is suitable for all ages. Younger children may enjoy the visual spectacle even if they don't follow the storyline.

Where should I sit for the best view?

Center seats in the middle rows offer the best sightlines. The balcony level provides a good overview of choreography and staging. Arrive 15-20 minutes early.
