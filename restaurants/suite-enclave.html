Suite Enclave

Royal Caribbean — Exclusive Suite Neighborhood

Quick Answer: Suite Enclave is an exclusive neighborhood on Icon Class ships reserved for suite-class guests. Features a private sun deck with pool, dedicated bar, lounge areas, and concierge services. Access requires booking a suite-category stateroom.

Best For: Suite guests seeking exclusivity and privacy. Luxury cruisers. Those wanting a quiet escape from the larger ship crowds. Guests celebrating special occasions.

Key Facts

  • Access: Suite guests only
  • Location: Dedicated deck area, Icon Class
  • Features: Private pool, sun deck, bar
  • Service: Dedicated concierge
  • Ships: Icon of the Seas, Star of the Seas

Exclusive luxury within the ship.

Suite Enclave elevates the suite experience on Icon Class ships with a private neighborhood that feels like a resort within a resort. While other guests enjoy the ship's many public areas, suite guests can retreat to this exclusive space with its own pool, bar, loungers, and dedicated service team. Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access Suite Enclave?

Only guests booked in suite-class accommodations have access to Suite Enclave.

Is there an extra charge?

No, access is included with your suite booking. Most amenities within are also included.

What suite categories qualify?

Access varies, but generally Junior Suite and above have some level of access. Higher tiers get more benefits.

Is it worth booking a suite for this?

If privacy, exclusivity, and premium service matter to you—and you can afford the upgrade—it adds significant value.

Which ships have Suite Enclave?

Suite Enclave is on Icon Class ships. Other ships have similar concepts like Suite Sun Deck.

Can I visit if I'm not in a suite?

No, Suite Enclave is restricted to suite guests only.
