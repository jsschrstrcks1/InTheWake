Ship Overview

Carnival Horizon is the second Vista-class ship in Carnival's fleet, delivering a perfect blend of innovation and classic Fun Ship entertainment. Launched in 2018, she features groundbreaking attractions including SkyRide (suspended cycling above the ship), the first IMAX theater at sea, and the exclusive Havana section with Cuban-inspired staterooms.

Sailing year-round from Miami, Carnival Horizon offers Eastern and Western Caribbean itineraries with stops at popular ports including Cozumel, Grand Cayman, and Nassau. Her Vista-class design provides an ideal mix of thrilling activities, family-friendly spaces, and relaxing retreats.

Signature Features

SkyRide

Suspended pedal-powered cycling attraction with 800-foot track circling the ship's top deck, 150 feet above sea level. A Vista-class exclusive.

IMAX Theater

187-seat venue showing first-run Hollywood films and documentaries — the first IMAX experience at sea. 3D capabilities included.

Havana Section

Cuban-themed cabins and suites with exclusive daytime access to the Havana Bar and Havana Pool — adults-only tropical retreat.

Dr. Seuss WaterWorks

Family aqua park featuring Cat in the Hat slides, Fun Things splash zone, and 300-gallon tipping bucket.

SportsSquare

Outdoor recreation complex with SkyCourse ropes course, mini-golf, jogging track, and basketball court.

Serenity Adult Retreat

Adults-only (21+) sanctuary with padded loungers, infinity whirlpools, and dedicated bar service. Peace at sea.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Carnival Horizon sail from?

Carnival Horizon sails year-round from Miami, Florida, offering Eastern and Western Caribbean itineraries with 6-8 day voyages.

What class is Carnival Horizon?

She is a Vista-class ship — the second of three sisters (Vista 2016, Horizon 2018, Panorama 2019) built by Fincantieri.

What is SkyRide?

SkyRide is a pedal-powered aerial cycling attraction suspended 150 feet above the ocean on an 800-foot track circling the ship's top deck. Vista-class exclusive.

Does Carnival Horizon have an IMAX theater?

Yes! The 187-seat IMAX theater shows first-run Hollywood movies and documentaries — the first IMAX experience at sea.

What is the Havana section?

The Havana section offers Cuban-themed staterooms with exclusive daytime access to the Havana Bar and Havana Pool — a tropical retreat for Havana cabin guests.

Is Carnival Horizon good for families?

Excellent for families. Features Dr. Seuss WaterWorks aqua park, Camp Ocean kids program (ages 2-11), Circle C (12-14), and Club O2 (15-17).
