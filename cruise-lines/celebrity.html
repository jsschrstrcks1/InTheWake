Celebrity Cruises

Quick Answer: Celebrity Cruises delivers modern luxury at sea with innovative Edge Class ships featuring the Magic Carpet cantilevered platform, premium dining experiences, contemporary design, and destination-focused itineraries. Known for clean lines, sophisticated atmosphere, and Michelin-level cuisine.

Is Celebrity Right for You?

Celebrity Cruises invites discerning travelers who seek modern luxury without stuffiness. The revolutionary Edge Class ships feature the Magic Carpet — a cantilevered platform that moves up and down the ship's exterior, transforming into a specialty restaurant, bar, or tender embarkation point.

Perfect for couples, solo travelers (Edge Class ships offer extensive solo staterooms), foodies who appreciate premium dining, and destination-focused cruisers who value cultural immersion. Celebrity blends relaxed sophistication with innovative design, creating spaces that feel like contemporary art galleries. Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Celebrity Cruises unique?

Celebrity Cruises offers modern luxury with innovative Edge Class ships featuring the Magic Carpet cantilevered platform, premium dining experiences, contemporary design, and destination-focused itineraries with expert-led excursions.

What is the Magic Carpet on Celebrity Edge Class ships?

The Magic Carpet is Celebrity's revolutionary cantilevered platform that moves up and down the side of Edge Class ships, transforming into different venues including a specialty restaurant, bar, and tender embarkation platform.

What type of traveler does Celebrity appeal to?

Celebrity appeals to discerning travelers seeking modern luxury without stuffiness, couples and solo travelers (especially on Edge Class ships), foodies who appreciate premium dining, and destination-focused cruisers who value cultural immersion.

How does Celebrity compare to Royal Caribbean?

Celebrity (owned by Royal Caribbean Group) offers a more refined, adult-oriented experience compared to Royal Caribbean's family-friendly mega-ships. Celebrity focuses on premium dining, contemporary design, and destination experiences, while Royal Caribbean emphasizes active entertainment and water parks.
