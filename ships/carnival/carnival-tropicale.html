Carnival Tropicale

Carnival Cruise Line • Excel-Class (On Order)

Coming 2028 Excel-Class LNG Powered 5th of 5

Overview

Carnival Tropicale will be the fifth and final Excel-class cruise ship, scheduled for delivery in 2028. As sister to Mardi Gras, Celebration, Jubilee, and Festivale, she will feature LNG propulsion, the BOLT roller coaster, and approximately 182,800 GT of innovative cruise experiences.

Like her Excel-class siblings, Tropicale will be built at the Meyer Turku shipyard in Finland and is expected to feature six themed zones, celebrity chef dining partnerships, and state-of-the-art entertainment venues. Her homeport and specific itineraries have not yet been announced.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will Carnival Tropicale launch?

Carnival Tropicale is expected to be delivered in 2028. She will be the 5th and final Excel-class ship in the current order, following Mardi Gras (2021), Celebration (2022), Jubilee (2023), and Festivale (2027).

What class is Carnival Tropicale?

Carnival Tropicale will be an Excel-class ship, Carnival's largest class at approximately 182,800 GT. The Excel-class features LNG propulsion, BOLT roller coaster, six themed zones, and celebrity chef restaurants.

Where will Carnival Tropicale be built?

Carnival Tropicale will be built at the Meyer Turku shipyard in Finland, the same yard that constructed sister ships Mardi Gras, Celebration, Jubilee, and Festivale.

What features will Carnival Tropicale have?

Based on the Excel-class platform, Carnival Tropicale is expected to feature BOLT: Ultimate Sea Coaster, six themed zones, LNG propulsion, celebrity chef restaurants (Guy Fieri, Emeril Lagasse, Shaquille O'Neal), and approximately 5,400 guest capacity.

Can I book Carnival Tropicale now?

Bookings for Carnival Tropicale are not yet available. Carnival typically opens bookings 18-24 months before a new ship's debut. Check Carnival.com for updates on when bookings will open.
