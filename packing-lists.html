Packing Lists

Your cruise starts before embarkation — with a smartly packed bag. Use these trip-tested checklists to prepare for every sailing, from solo to family adventures.

Carry-On Essentials

Keep these with you on embarkation day and flights. Assume checked bags may be delayed.

  • Passport and/or birth certificate + photo ID
  • Boarding passes, travel insurance cards
  • Medications in original containers
  • Phone, charger, and power bank
  • Change of clothes and swimwear
  • Reusable water bottle, snacks, sanitizer

Everyone

  • Aloe & sunscreen
  • Toiletries, hairbrush, deodorant
  • Magnetic hooks, laundry clips, zip bags
  • Rain jacket or umbrella
  • Converter / adapter (international)

Families

  • Baby gear (diapers, wipes, bottles)
  • Compact stroller or carrier
  • Tablets loaded with shows & games
  • Travel-size first-aid kit

Packing Tips

Tap to expand each tip below:

Pack a smart carry-on

Keep IDs, meds, and cruise docs with you; assume luggage delays.

Know dress codes

Main dining is resort casual; formal night expectations vary.

Use packing cubes

Segment outfits and compress soft items for easier unpacking.

Photo your documents

Store passport & insurance images on your phone and in the cloud.

Split family luggage

Mix everyone’s clothes across bags — no one stranded if one is lost.
