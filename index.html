Welcome Aboard

In the Wake is a living, sailor-friendly logbook designed for clarity and speed. Browse ships, restaurants, ports, packing tips, and accessibility notes in one consistent layout. Every page draws its look from a single stylesheet so improvements ripple across the fleet without manual edits. Cross‑links connect ships to their restaurants and ports, and each page opens with the essentials so you can decide quickly and confidently. Prices and availability can change at any time without notice; what you see here reflects the most recent sailing notes we’ve compiled. Treat this as a respectful companion to your planning — a place to compare options, capture favorites, and spot what matters before you board. When something’s “To Verify,” it’s flagged rather than hidden, and historical items remain labeled. Fair winds — may your next choice feel like your idea, and the perfect itinerary be just a click away.
