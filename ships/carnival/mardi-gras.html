Mardi Gras

Mardi Gras is a Unknown Class cruise ship operated by Carnival Cruise Line.

Key Facts

  • Class: Excel Class
  • IMO: 9817296

Quick Answer: Mardi Gras was Carnival's very first ship — the vessel that launched the Fun Ship revolution in 1972 and established Carnival Cruise Line as a force in the cruise industry. This page covers historical ship details and fleet origins.

Best For: Cruise history enthusiasts — the ship that started it all, famously running aground on her maiden voyage before going on to define an industry.
