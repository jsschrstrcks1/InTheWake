The Lemon Post

Royal Caribbean — Bar & Lounge

Quick Answer: The Lemon Post is a casual, relaxed bar on Icon Class ships. Located in a convenient spot for grabbing drinks throughout the day, it offers cocktails, beers, wines, and signature drinks in a friendly atmosphere.

Best For: Casual drinks during the day. People-watching on the promenade. A quick drink between activities. Groups looking for a laid-back bar without the specialty bar vibe.

Key Facts

  • Location: Royal Promenade area, Icon Class
  • Cost: À la carte or Drink Package
  • Style: Casual bar
  • Atmosphere: Relaxed, social
  • Ships: Icon of the Seas, Star of the Seas

A casual spot for any time of day.

The Lemon Post offers Icon Class cruisers a relaxed place to enjoy drinks without the formality of specialty bars. Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Lemon Post covered by the drink package?

Yes, most drinks are covered by the Deluxe Beverage Package.

Do they serve food?

The Lemon Post is primarily a bar, though snacks may be available. Nearby dining options are plentiful.

What are the hours?

The Lemon Post operates throughout the day and into the evening.

Is it crowded?

It can get busy during peak hours but generally has a relaxed vibe.

Can I get non-alcoholic drinks?

Yes, mocktails, juices, and other non-alcoholic options are available.

Which ships have The Lemon Post?

The Lemon Post is found on Icon Class ships: Icon of the Seas and Star of the Seas.
