Taste

Norwegian Cruise Line — Dining

Quick Answer: One of the main dining rooms on Breakaway-era ships with modern decor and a rotating multi-course dinner menu featuring global cuisine.

Best For: Families, first-time cruisers, anyone wanting a relaxed sit-down meal included in the fare

Key Facts

  • Price: Varies by venue
  • Hours: Varies by ship and itinerary
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Reservations: Check Norwegian Cruise Line app

Menus rotate on a 7-night cycle. Classic Entrees are available every night; appetizers, featured entrees, and desserts change nightly.

Additional Notes
  • 2026 change: NY Strip Steak now alternates with Montreal Spice-Rubbed Brisket every other night.
  • Same menu as Savor and The Manhattan Room. Freestyle Dining — no fixed seating times.
  • Ordering more than one entree incurs a $5 charge per additional entree (2026 policy).

Special Accommodations

Allergen & Dietary Notes: Norwegian Cruise Line follows allergen policies. Please disclose allergens to your server before ordering. Gluten-free, dairy-free, vegetarian, and many vegan adjustments are available on request.

Where You'll Find It

Taste is available on select Norwegian Cruise Line ships. Check your ship's deck plan or the Norwegian Cruise Line app for exact location and hours.

Taste Review: Guest Experience Summary

Introduction. Taste consistently receives positive feedback from Norwegian Cruise Line guests. This composite review reflects common themes from multiple sailings and ships across the fleet.

Food & Drinks

Guests praise the quality and presentation at Taste. The menu offers good variety with options for different tastes and dietary needs. Portions are satisfying and flavors well-balanced.

Service

Service at Taste is described as attentive and professional. Staff are friendly, knowledgeable, and happy to accommodate requests. Pacing feels comfortable and unhurried.

Atmosphere

Taste offers an inviting setting with thoughtful design that complements the dining experience. Guests appreciate the ambiance and find it a pleasant place to spend time.

Conclusion

Rating: 4/5. Taste delivers a quality experience that meets guest expectations. Worth visiting during your Norwegian Cruise Line cruise.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Taste on Norwegian Cruise Line?

One of the main dining rooms on Breakaway-era ships with modern decor and a rotating multi-course dinner menu featuring global cuisine.

How much does Taste cost?

Pricing varies — check the Norwegian Cruise Line app or NCL app for current Taste pricing.

What is the dress code for Taste?

Smart Casual is recommended. Collared shirts and closed-toe shoes for gentlemen; no swimwear or tank tops at dinner.

Do I need reservations for Taste?

Reservations are recommended, especially for dinner. Book through the Norwegian Cruise Line app or NCL app before sailing for the best availability.

What are the menu highlights at Taste?

Popular items include Grilled New York Strip Steak, Breaded Flounder Fillet, Herb-Crusted Rotisserie Chicken, Shrimp & Mushroom Alfredo, and more. The menu may vary by ship and sailing.
