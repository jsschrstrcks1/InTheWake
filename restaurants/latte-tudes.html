Café Latte-tudes

Royal Caribbean — Espresso Bar & Sweet Treats

A cozy, café-style haven for crave-worthy caffeinated creations—Starbucks-style espresso drinks with complimentary pastries and snacks, right in the heart of the ship.

Hours, Pricing & Menu

Typical Hours: ~06:00–22:00 (varies by ship & itinerary).

Price: Pastries & snacks are complimentary ($0). Specialty coffees/teas are à la carte (usually $2.50–$5.75) and are covered by the Refreshment/Deluxe packages or the Café Select Card (≈15 drinks for a fixed price).

Breakfast — 06:00–11:00
  • Cinnamon rolls, croissants (plain/almond/chocolate), Danish, muffins
  • Yogurt parfaits with granola & berries
  • Fresh fruit cups; bagels with cream cheese
All-Day Snacks — 11:00–Close
  • Cookies & brownies · donuts · scones
  • Bagels & spreads · occasional light bites
Hot Beverages (Starbucks beans)
  • Drip coffee · espresso · cappuccino · latte · macchiato
  • Chai latte · hot chocolate · Teavana hot teas
Iced & Blended
  • Iced coffee · iced lattes · refreshers
  • Frappuccino-style blended drinks (mocha/caramel/strawberry)

Note: Pastry case rotates daily; beverage sizes & pricing can vary by ship. Check the Royal App for current menus.

Good to Know

  • Packages: Refreshment & Deluxe Beverage Packages cover specialty coffees here.
  • Allergens: Dairy-free milks (oat/almond) typically available; ask staff for swaps.
  • Lines: Peak waits 5–10 minutes (mornings & pre-show). Hit mid-afternoon for shortest queues.
  • Pairing Tip: Grab a latte here, then head to Windjammer for a fuller savory plate.

Café Latte-tudes Review 2025: Cozy Brews in the Centrum

Introduction

A central, café-style stop for Starbucks-style espresso drinks with free pastries—fast, friendly, and fuss-free.

Food & Drinks

Caramel lattes, mochas, and frappes headline; pastry case rotates (cinnamon rolls, croissants, muffins). Complimentary parfaits & fruit cups appear mornings.

Service

Brisk baristas; package scans and coffee-card punches speed lines during rushes.

Atmosphere

Atrium energy with cozy café corners—great people-watching; expect a brief morning queue.

Conclusion

Rating: 4.2/5. A reliable daily ritual for caffeine fans and snack seekers.
