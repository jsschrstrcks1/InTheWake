Accessibility & Inclusion Commitment

In the Wake is built on a simple conviction: everyone deserves access to reliable, unbiased cruise information—without barriers. Whether you navigate with a keyboard, screen reader, voice software, or switch device, our goal is for every part of the site to work smoothly and clearly for you.

This is more than compliance—it’s conviction. We believe information should be as open as the ocean itself, accessible to every traveler who dreams of setting sail.

ADA & WCAG 2.1 Compliance

We design, code, and review every page against the standards of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

  • Perceivable: Text alternatives for images, clear contrast, and legible type.
  • Operable: Full keyboard navigation, logical focus order, and visible focus indicators.
  • Understandable: Consistent layout, plain language, and predictable navigation.
  • Robust: Semantic HTML and ARIA roles that support modern assistive technologies.

Our commitment includes quarterly audits, user feedback channels, and continuous iteration as guidelines evolve. Accessibility isn’t a checkbox—it’s a living process of hospitality and respect.

Contact for Accessibility Support

If you encounter any barrier while using our website, please reach out so we can fix it quickly:

accessibility@cruisinginthewake.com

We aim to respond within 48 hours with acknowledgment and follow-up. Feedback from users with disabilities directly shapes our roadmap—it’s the best way for us to improve.
