Tampa

Tampa: Florida's Underrated Cruise Gem

Tampa offers Gulf Coast cruisers a fantastic alternative to the crowded East Coast ports. With Royal Caribbean, Carnival, Norwegian, and Celebrity sailing from here, you get easy access to Western Caribbean itineraries plus one of Florida's most underrated cities to explore.

The Port of Tampa cruise terminals sit on the Ybor City channel, minutes from downtown, the historic Ybor City district, and Busch Gardens. Clearwater Beach and St. Pete are just 30 minutes away.

Tampa Local Secret: Skip the chain restaurants and head to Ybor City for Cuban sandwiches at the Columbia Restaurant — Florida's oldest restaurant has been serving them since 1905.

Getting to the Port

  • Address: Port Tampa Bay, 1101 Channelside Drive
  • From Tampa Airport (TPA): 20-25 minutes
  • From Orlando: 1.5 hours via I-4 West
  • Uber/Lyft: $15-25 from TPA airport

Parking Options

  • On-site parking: Port Tampa Bay garage adjacent to terminals
  • Cost: $15-18/day — some of the best rates among major cruise ports
  • Off-site: Several third-party lots nearby with shuttles
  • Reserve ahead: Recommended for peak sailing dates

Nearby Hotels

  • Tampa Marriott Water Street: Walking distance to port
  • Hilton Tampa Downtown: Connected to convention center
  • Grand Hyatt Tampa Bay: Resort feel on the bay
  • Hotel Haya (Ybor City): Boutique hotel in historic district

Pre-Cruise Activities

  • Ybor City: Historic Cuban district, cigars, Columbia Restaurant
  • Busch Gardens: African-themed park with world-class coasters
  • Florida Aquarium: Right on the waterfront near the port
  • Tampa Riverwalk: Scenic 2.6-mile walk along the Hillsborough River
  • Clearwater Beach: 30 minutes west — consistently rated top beach
  • St. Pete: Arts district, Dalí Museum, craft breweries

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Tampa vs. Port Canaveral — which is better?
A: Tampa is easier parking and less crowded. Port Canaveral is closer to Orlando parks. Choose based on your priorities.

Q: Is Busch Gardens worth a pre-cruise day?
A: If you love roller coasters, absolutely. It's one of the best coaster parks in Florida.

Q: Where's the best Cuban food?
A: Columbia Restaurant in Ybor City for the sit-down experience; La Segunda Bakery for quick Cuban bread and pastries.

At a Glance

Quick Answer: Florida's underrated Gulf Coast gem — Ybor City Cuban sandwiches, Busch Gardens coasters, Clearwater Beach, and some of the best parking rates among major cruise ports.
