Skylight Chapel

Royal Caribbean — Wedding & Event Venue

Quick Answer: Skylight Chapel is a beautiful dedicated chapel on select Royal Caribbean ships. Natural light streams through skylights, with ocean views creating a stunning backdrop for weddings, vow renewals, and religious services. Wedding packages available through Royal Caribbean.

Best For: Couples planning weddings at sea. Vow renewal ceremonies. Religious services. Special milestone celebrations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get married on a Royal Caribbean cruise?

Yes, Royal Caribbean offers various wedding packages with ceremonies in the chapel or other locations.

How do I book a wedding?

Contact Royal Caribbean's wedding department or work with a travel agent specializing in cruise weddings.

Is the captain's ceremony legal?

It depends on where the ship is registered and where you're sailing. The wedding department can advise on legal requirements.

Can we do a vow renewal?

Yes, vow renewal packages are available and often more flexible than weddings.

How many guests can attend?

The chapel is intimate—typically accommodating smaller groups. Reception venues for larger groups are available.

Are religious services held here?

Yes, non-denominational and various religious services may be held in the chapel.
