Dominica: My Nature Island Adventure

Sailing into Roseau with mountains dropping straight into the sea feels like entering Jurassic Park. Dominica is the nature island and I go full adventure: Trafalgar Falls first (short taxi), then the Ti Tou Gorge swim through the narrow canyon to the waterfall (cold but incredible).

Lunch is fresh callaloo soup and fried plantains at a roadside stand (you might see 'mountain chicken' on old menus, but this local frog is now protected). Afternoon is the Champagne Reef snorkel – bubbles rising from volcanic vents like you're swimming in champagne. Sunset at Mero Beach with a Kubuli beer.

The Moment That Stays With Me: Floating in Ti Tou Gorge with sunlight piercing the canyon walls – felt like Avatar in real life.

Getting Around Dominica

Roseau pier – 5-min walk to town. Taxis offer affordable shared rates for full-day tours.

  • Trafalgar Falls: 20-min taxi from pier
  • Ti Tou Gorge: Near Trafalgar, combine them
  • Champagne Reef: South coast, 30-min drive

Positively Worded Word of Warning

Dominica's lush rain and steep trails are the island's way of giving you the most epic nature shower and workout you'll ever love!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Best adventure?
A: Trafalgar + Ti Tou + Champagne Reef combo.

Q: Bring water shoes?
A: Absolutely – river rocks are slippery.

Q: Crowds?
A: Almost none – pure paradise.
