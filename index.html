Welcome

Welcome aboard In the Wake — your calm, practical companion for planning and enjoying life at sea. Here you’ll find clean, family‑friendly guidance drawn from real sailings: ship overviews with quick cross‑links to dining and venues, menu snapshots you can skim at a glance, and video picks from trusted creators so you can preview staterooms and spaces before you book. We keep the layout simple, fast, and ad‑free; the tone respectful; and the advice focused on what actually helps on embarkation day and beyond. If a detail is still being verified, we mark it clearly so nothing is hidden. Use the cards below to jump straight to restaurants, ships, ports, packing, disability tips, or drinks. May these pages help you pick the right ship, set the right expectations, and make better memories — so when you look back, the wake tells a story you’re proud to keep.
