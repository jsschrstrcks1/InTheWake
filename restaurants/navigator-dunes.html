Navigator Dunes

Royal Caribbean — Mini Golf

Quick Answer: Navigator Dunes is the mini golf course on Navigator of the Seas. Located on the Sports Deck, this themed course offers 9 holes of putting fun with ocean views as your backdrop. Equipment provided, completely complimentary, and fun for all ages and skill levels.

Best For: Families looking for activities. Groups wanting friendly competition. A casual outdoor break. Anyone who enjoys mini golf.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Holes: 9-hole course
  • Location: Sports Deck
  • Ship: Navigator of the Seas
  • Ages: All ages welcome

Sink a hole-in-one at sea.

Navigator Dunes offers classic mini golf fun aboard Navigator of the Seas. The outdoor course features themed obstacles and challenging holes while ocean breezes keep you cool. Clubs and balls are provided—no need to bring anything. It's a relaxing activity that gets you outdoors and away from the pool deck crowds. Return to the Restaurants hub →

Traveler's Logbook

What We Loved: Fun course with good variety in the holes. The ocean views while putting are something you won't get at any land-based course! Kids loved it and adults enjoyed the friendly competition.

Pro Tips:

  • Best times are early morning or late afternoon
  • Wear sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Wind is part of the challenge—enjoy it!
  • Great family activity that doesn't take too long

Our Rating: 4.2/5 — Classic ship mini golf

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?

Yes, completely complimentary including equipment.

Do I need to reserve a time?

No, just show up when there's availability.

How long is a round?

Typically 20-30 minutes for 9 holes.

Can young children play?

Yes, all ages welcome. Smaller clubs available.

What if it's windy?

Course stays open in normal winds. Closes only in severe conditions.

Where is it located?

Sports Deck, upper decks of Navigator of the Seas.
