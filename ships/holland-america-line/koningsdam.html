First Look: Koningsdam

Koningsdam is Holland America Line's flagship and the first of the groundbreaking Pinnacle Class, launched in April 2016. At 99,500 gross tons and carrying 2,650 guests, she represents Holland America's most ambitious ship design, combining the line's classic elegance with modern innovation. Her name honors both the Dutch royal family and the line's heritage, with "Koning" meaning "King" in Dutch.

The ship introduced the revolutionary Music Walk entertainment promenade, featuring Lincoln Center Stage for chamber music, B.B. King's Blues Club for Southern blues, and Billboard Onboard for rock and pop. The two-story World Stage offers 270-degree LED screens for immersive shows, while the Crow's Nest observation lounge provides stunning panoramic views from the bow.

Dining aboard Koningsdam showcases Holland America's culinary excellence with the elegant Dining Room for traditional service, plus specialty restaurants including Pinnacle Grill steakhouse, Tamarind for Pan-Asian fusion, and Sel de Mer for French seafood. The Grand Dutch Cafe celebrates the line's heritage with Dutch treats including stroopwafels, bitterballen, and jenever throughout the day.

Accommodations range from comfortable Inside staterooms to the luxurious Pinnacle Suite with its wraparound veranda and whirlpool. Neptune and Signature Suites offer exclusive access to the Neptune Lounge, featuring private concierge services, complimentary laundry, and exclusive shore excursion briefings. All staterooms feature Mariner's Dream beds with plush Euro-top mattresses.

The Greenhouse Spa & Salon offers comprehensive wellness services including thermal suites, hydrotherapy pools, and treatment rooms overlooking the sea. The ship features an extensive art collection valued at over $4 million, including pieces by contemporary artists and a massive Murano glass sculpture in the atrium created by Italian artist Cristiano Bianchin.

Koningsdam sails diverse itineraries throughout the year, including popular Caribbean, Alaska, and European sailings. Her longer Alaska cruises showcase the line's heritage of exploration, while Mediterranean voyages allow guests to experience the ship's sophisticated atmosphere paired with world-class destinations.

Dining

Koningsdam offers exceptional culinary variety befitting Holland America Line's flagship. The elegant main Dining Room serves multi-course dinners with traditional service, while the Lido Market provides casual buffet dining throughout the day. For specialty dining, Pinnacle Grill delivers premium steaks and seafood in an upscale atmosphere. Tamarind presents Pan-Asian cuisine with Japanese, Thai, and Vietnamese influences. Canaletto offers authentic Italian fare in a trattoria setting, and Sel de Mer specializes in French-inspired seafood dishes. The Grand Dutch Cafe celebrates Holland America's heritage with complimentary Dutch treats including stroopwafels and jenever.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options are available on Koningsdam?

Koningsdam offers exceptional dining variety as Holland America Line's flagship. Complimentary options include the elegant main Dining Room with traditional service, the Lido Market buffet, and the Dive-In poolside grill. Specialty restaurants require reservations and nominal fees: Pinnacle Grill for prime steaks and seafood, Tamarind for Pan-Asian cuisine, Canaletto for Italian fare, and Sel de Mer for French seafood. The Grand Dutch Cafe serves Dutch treats and beverages throughout the day at no extra charge.

What entertainment is unique to Koningsdam?

Koningsdam introduced Holland America's innovative Music Walk entertainment promenade. This includes Lincoln Center Stage for classical chamber music performances, B.B. King's Blues Club for authentic Southern blues and jazz, and Billboard Onboard for rock and pop hits. The two-story World Stage features a state-of-the-art 270-degree LED screen for immersive entertainment experiences including BBC Earth in Concert nature productions.

How do I find the deck plans for Koningsdam?

Deck plans are available through the links on this page. You can also find official deck plans on the Holland America Line website or in their Navigator cruise planner app. The ship spans 12 passenger decks with key venues including the Lido pool deck with retractable roof, the Crow's Nest observation lounge, and the Explorations Central hub for shore excursion planning.

Where does Koningsdam sail?

Koningsdam operates diverse seasonal itineraries. She typically sails Caribbean voyages from Fort Lauderdale during winter months, transitions to Alaska from Seattle in summer, and offers European itineraries including Mediterranean and Northern Europe cruises. Check the Holland America Line website for current schedules and departure ports.
