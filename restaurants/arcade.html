Video games and entertainment for all ages

Quick Answer: The Arcade offers pay-per-play video games, racing simulators, and prize machines. Games cost $1-5 each, charged to your SeaPass card. Located near Adventure Ocean on most ships. Popular with families during the day and teens at night. Set spending limits on kids' cards through Guest Services.

Overview

The Arcade provides video game entertainment for all ages, from classic games to the latest simulators. Located typically near Adventure Ocean and the teen areas, it's a popular hangout spot for kids and teens, especially on sea days and after evening activities.

Game Types

  • Racing Simulators: Car and motorcycle racing with motion seats
  • Shooting Games: Light gun games and zombie shooters
  • Sports Games: Basketball, air hockey, football toss
  • Classic Arcade: Pac-Man, racing games, fighting games
  • Prize/Claw Machines: Win small prizes and toys
  • Ticket Games: Earn tickets for prize redemption

Ship Availability

Arcade available on all Royal Caribbean ships:

  • Icon/Oasis Class: Largest arcades with latest games
  • Quantum Class: SeaPlex often includes arcade games
  • Freedom/Voyager Class: Traditional arcade setup

Games & Pricing

Typical Pricing

  • Classic Arcade Games $1-2

    Pac-Man, racing classics, fighting games.

  • Racing Simulators $2-4

    Motion-seat racing experiences, motorcycle simulators.

  • Shooting Games $2-3

    Light gun games, zombie shooters, target games.

  • Prize/Claw Machines $2-3

    Win stuffed animals and small toys.

  • Air Hockey $2-3

    Timed games, usually 3-5 minutes.

* All games charge to SeaPass card. Set spending limits at Guest Services.

Tips for Parents

Managing Spending

  • Set limits: Visit Guest Services to set daily spending limits on kids' cards
  • Cash limits: Give kids a "cruise allowance" amount in mind
  • Check charges: Review your folio regularly via app or stateroom TV
  • Ticket games: Prize redemption games can add up fast

FAQ

Is the arcade free on Royal Caribbean?

No, games are pay-per-play ($1-5 each), charged to your SeaPass card.

Can I limit my child's arcade spending?

Yes, visit Guest Services to set daily or cruise-long spending limits on any SeaPass card.

What games are available?

Racing simulators, shooting games, sports games, classics, prize machines, and air hockey. Selection varies by ship.
