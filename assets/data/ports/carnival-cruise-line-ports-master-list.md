## Private / Exclusive Destinations (Carnival-operated or leased)

- **Celebration Key** (Grand Bahama, Bahamas) – Opening July 2025, on nearly all Bahamas/Caribbean itineraries
- **Half Moon Cay** (Little San Salvador, Bahamas) – Flagship private island, year-round
- **Princess Cays** (Eleuthera, Bahamas) – Private beach resort
- **Amber Cove** (Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic) – Private port with zip lines and pools
- **Mahogany Bay** (Roatan, Honduras) – Private beach club, rebranding to **Isla Tropicale** in 2026 with pool expansions

## Notes

- Carnival's **heaviest concentration** is Caribbean/Bahamas (~150 sailings annually)
- **Private islands** (Celebration Key, Half Moon Cay, Princess Cays) appear on nearly all Bahamas itineraries
- **World cruises** (2023-2024 on Carnival Legend and Spirit) added ~100 rare ports
- **Future builds**: Carnival Festivale (2027) and Carnival Tropicale (2028) will add new deployment regions
- **Geopolitical adjustments**: Red Sea avoidance has rerouted some Middle East itineraries
- **Seasonal variations**: Hurricane season, ice/weather, and demand patterns shift some ports
