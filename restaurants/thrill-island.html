Thrill Island

Royal Caribbean — Ship Neighborhood

Quick Answer: Thrill Island is Icon Class's adrenaline-pumping neighborhood featuring Category 6 water park with 6 record-breaking waterslides (including the tallest at sea), Crown's Edge sky-high walk and plunge, FlowRider surf simulator, and more. Most attractions are complimentary. This is where thrill seekers will spend most of their time.

Best For: Thrill seekers, adventure lovers, teens, competitive families, and anyone who wants their cruise to include heart-pounding excitement.

Key Facts

  • Location: Icon Class ships only
  • Slides: 6 record-breaking waterslides
  • Signature: Crown's Edge (154 feet above sea)
  • Cost: Most attractions complimentary
  • Highlights: Tallest waterslide at sea (46 ft)

The ultimate adrenaline zone at sea.

Thrill Island pushes the boundaries of cruise ship entertainment with attractions that rival shore-side theme parks. The Category 6 water park alone features more slides than any ship before, while Crown's Edge offers a unique thrill walk suspended 154 feet above the ocean. Waterslides: Free · FlowRider: Free · Crown's Edge: Free (reservation required)

Height/Weight Requirements
  • Frightening Bolt: 52" minimum height
  • Pressure Drop: 48" minimum height
  • Storm Surge: 42" minimum height
  • Crown's Edge: Weight/health restrictions apply
  • Check individual attraction signage for all requirements

Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: Most Thrill Island attractions require physical mobility and have height/weight restrictions. The neighborhood itself is wheelchair accessible for viewing. Specific attractions like Crown's Edge have additional requirements. Consult Guest Services for individual accommodation questions.

Where You'll Find It

Thrill Island occupies a large section of Icon Class ships, spanning multiple decks at the aft of the ship. Category 6 water park is the centerpiece, with Crown's Edge located at the top of the ship's distinctive structure.

Best Times to Visit

  • Early Morning: Shortest lines for waterslides
  • Port Days: Many guests ashore, lighter crowds
  • Evening: Crown's Edge sunset reservations popular
  • Avoid: Sea day afternoons (peak crowds)

Ships with Thrill Island

  • Icon of the Seas
  • Star of the Seas (coming 2025)

5 ★ out of 5 Icon of the Seas • Family Cruise

Thrill Island Review: Theme Park Meets Cruise Ship

Introduction. We booked Icon of the Seas specifically for Thrill Island, and it exceeded every expectation. Our teenage sons (14 and 16) called it "the best part of any vacation ever."

The Waterslides

Category 6 is massive. Six slides with different experiences—from the terrifying trap-door drop of Pressure Drop to the family-friendly Storm Surge raft ride. Frightening Bolt lived up to its name. My 14-year-old went down 12 times over the cruise.

Crown's Edge

This was the highlight for me personally. Walking on glass 154 feet above the water, doing the rope course elements, then the controlled swing out over the ocean—pure adrenaline. Book this immediately when reservations open.

Tips

Go at 8 AM when it opens on sea days. By 10 AM, lines can hit 30-45 minutes for popular slides. The FlowRider had shorter waits than expected. Crown's Edge times fill up within hours of opening.

Conclusion

If you're a thrill-seeker family, Icon is the ship for you. Thrill Island alone justifies the price of the cruise. We're already looking at booking again. Rating: 5/5.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Thrill Island waterslides free?

Yes, all Category 6 waterslides are complimentary. Just meet the height requirements and wait in line.

What is Crown's Edge?

Crown's Edge is a unique attraction where you walk on glass floors 154 feet above sea level, complete rope course elements, and experience a controlled swing out over the ocean.

Does Crown's Edge cost extra?

No, Crown's Edge is complimentary but requires a reservation. Book through the Royal Caribbean app as soon as reservations open.

What are the height requirements?

Requirements vary by slide. Frightening Bolt requires 52" minimum, while family slides like Storm Surge require 42". Check signage for each attraction.

Which ships have Thrill Island?

Thrill Island is exclusive to Icon Class ships: Icon of the Seas and upcoming Star of the Seas.
