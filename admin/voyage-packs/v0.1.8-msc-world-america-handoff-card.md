## What the at-home contact does if 36+ hours pass without word

1. **Don't panic** — cruise Wi-Fi is unreliable; silence usually means signal, not emergency.
2. **Call MSC Guest Services** above; they'll confirm you boarded and relay messages.
3. **Reach Denise via Facebook Messenger** — in-person on the ship, fastest eyes on the group.
4. **Reach Tina at Maulsby Travel** for booking questions or if Guest Services isn't getting through.
5. **If MSC confirms incident:** US Consulate Tegucigalpa (Honduras); US Consulate Mérida (Mexico); ship resources (Ocean Cay). Track ship position at /tools/ship-tracker.html.
