SeaPlex

Royal Caribbean — Indoor Activity Complex

Quick Answer: SeaPlex is Royal Caribbean's revolutionary indoor multiplex exclusive to Quantum Class ships. This massive venue — the largest indoor activity space at sea — transforms throughout the day to offer bumper cars, roller skating, basketball, circus school, Xbox gaming, and more. All activities are complimentary.

Best For: Active families, teens, sports enthusiasts, rainy day entertainment, anyone seeking unique cruise activities that go beyond the typical pool deck.

Key Facts

  • Price: All activities complimentary
  • Location: Deck 15, adjacent to North Star
  • Size: Largest indoor activity space at sea
  • Activities: Bumper cars, skating, basketball, circus school, gaming
  • Hours: Activities rotate throughout the day

Bumper cars on a cruise ship. Yes, really.

When Royal Caribbean introduced SeaPlex on Quantum of the Seas, it redefined what was possible on a cruise ship. The venue's flexible design allows it to transform from bumper car arena to roller rink to basketball court depending on the time of day. Add in circus school trapeze lessons, Xbox gaming stations, and DJ dance parties, and you have a venue that appeals to every age group. Return to the Restaurants & Venues hub →

Signature Experiences

Bumper Cars

Sessions: Multiple daily · Duration: ~10 minutes per session

Yes, actual bumper cars at sea. The electric cars zip around SeaPlex's floor in organized sessions. There's something delightfully absurd about crashing into your family while sailing through the ocean. Height restrictions apply for drivers.

Roller Skating

Sessions: Scheduled throughout cruise · Skates provided

The floor transforms into a roller rink with disco lighting and music. Skates are provided in all sizes. Whether you're a confident skater or wobbling around the edges, it's pure nostalgic fun. Skating aids available for beginners.

Circus School

Classes: Scheduled sessions · Instruction provided

Learn trapeze basics, juggling, and other circus skills from trained instructors. The flying trapeze setup lets you experience the thrill of aerial arts in a safe, supervised environment. Popular with kids and adventurous adults alike.

Basketball & Sports

Available: Various times · Equipment provided

Full basketball court for pickup games, free throws, or organized competitions. Also hosts volleyball, badminton, and other sports. Great way to burn off buffet calories.

Additional Activities

  • Xbox Gaming: Multiple Xbox stations with latest games
  • Table Tennis: Ping pong tables available
  • Dance Classes: Scheduled instruction sessions
  • DJ Parties: Evening dance events
  • Game Shows: Interactive competitions

Activities rotate throughout the day — bumper cars might be morning, skating afternoon, basketball evening. Check your Cruise Compass or the Royal Caribbean app for the daily schedule.

Accessibility & Requirements

Accessibility Notes: SeaPlex's main floor is wheelchair accessible for spectating. Some activities (bumper cars, skating, circus school) require physical mobility. Xbox gaming and observation areas are fully accessible. Contact Royal Caribbean's Access Department for specific needs.

Activity Requirements

  • Bumper Cars: Minimum height requirement; participants must be able to operate controls
  • Roller Skating: All ages; skating aids available for beginners
  • Circus School: Age/weight limits for trapeze; ground activities open to all
  • Basketball: Open play; organized games may have team signups

SeaPlex Review: The Venue That Defines Quantum Class

Introduction. I'd read about SeaPlex but didn't quite believe it until I was driving a bumper car while sailing through the Atlantic. It's the kind of thing that makes you laugh at the absurdity — and then immediately get back in line for another session.

Bumper Cars

The star attraction. Sessions run about 10 minutes with ~15 cars on the floor. The cars are electric and surprisingly zippy. My competitive family turned it into an all-out war — my daughter targeted me specifically for the entire session. Worth noting: there's often a line, so check the schedule and arrive a few minutes early.

Roller Skating

The afternoon skating session transported me back to childhood birthday parties. They dim the lights, crank up the disco music, and suddenly you're in a floating roller rink. Skates are provided and fit well. My wife hadn't skated in 20 years but was doing laps by the end of the session.

Circus School

My kids signed up for trapeze lessons and I watched nervously from below. The instructors were patient and safety-focused. Seeing my 12-year-old swing through the air, catching the bar on her first try, was a cruise highlight. There's also juggling and other ground-based activities for the less adventurous.

Conclusion

Rating: 4.5/5. SeaPlex justifies the Quantum Class experience. The variety of activities means there's something for everyone, and everything being complimentary makes it easy to try things spontaneously. Pro tip: check the schedule on embarkation day and plan around the activities you most want to do.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SeaPlex free?

Yes, all SeaPlex activities are complimentary and included in your cruise fare. This includes bumper cars, roller skating, basketball, circus school, and Xbox gaming.

Do I need reservations?

Most activities are first-come, first-served during scheduled sessions. Popular activities like bumper cars can have lines, so arrive a few minutes before the session starts. Some activities may offer app-based reservations.

Are there age restrictions?

Some activities have requirements: bumper cars have a minimum height, circus school trapeze has age/weight limits. Basketball, skating, and Xbox gaming are open to all ages. Staff will confirm requirements at each activity.

What should I wear to SeaPlex?

Comfortable, active clothing and closed-toe shoes work best. For skating, any shoes are fine as skates are provided. Avoid loose jewelry for circus activities. The venue is climate-controlled.

When are activities available?

SeaPlex runs different activities throughout the day — the schedule rotates so bumper cars might be morning one day, afternoon the next. Check your Cruise Compass or the Royal Caribbean app for the daily schedule.
