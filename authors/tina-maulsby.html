About Tina

Tina Maulsby is a solo cruising enthusiast and featured contributor to In the Wake. Her articles explore the joy and freedom of cruising alone, offering practical advice and personal stories for those considering their first solo voyage.

Tina discovered solo cruising after years of waiting for the "right time" to travel with friends and family. When schedules never aligned, she decided to book a cabin for one — and found something unexpected: not loneliness, but liberation. That first solo sailing changed everything.

Now a passionate advocate for solo travel, Tina writes about the practical realities of cruising alone: how to meet people (or not), how to stay safe, how to dine solo without awkwardness, and how to embrace the unique rhythm of a trip that belongs entirely to you. Her writing is warm, honest, and encouraging for anyone wondering if they can really do this alone.

Cruising Philosophy

Tina believes that solo cruising isn't about being alone — it's about freedom, self-discovery, and the simple joy of sailing at your own pace.

"When you cruise solo, you don't have to negotiate. You don't have to compromise. You wake up and the day is yours — every excursion choice, every meal, every sunset. It's not selfish; it's self-care with a sea view."

Her goal is simple: to help others take that first step aboard, knowing they're not alone in wanting to sail alone.
