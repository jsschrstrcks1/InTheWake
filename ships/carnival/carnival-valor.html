Carnival Valor

Carnival Cruise Line • Conquest-Class

Active Fleet Conquest-Class New Orleans

Overview

Carnival Valor is the third of five Conquest-class ships in the Carnival fleet, delivering the classic "Fun Ship" experience from New Orleans, Louisiana. Built by Fincantieri in 2004 for $500 million, she offers 4-7 day voyages to the Western Caribbean and Mexico year-round.

Following her 2016 refurbishment, Valor received popular additions including Guy's Burger Joint, BlueIguana Cantina, and the RedFrog Pub. The ship accommodates nearly 3,000 guests across her 14 passenger decks, making her one of Carnival's largest vessels from the Gulf Coast region.

Godmother: Katie Couric

Katie Couric, the celebrated American journalist and television personality, christened Carnival Valor in Miami on December 17, 2004. Couric was a co-host of NBC's Today show at the time and later anchored CBS Evening News.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Carnival Valor sail from?

Carnival Valor is homeported in New Orleans, Louisiana, offering 4-7 day cruises to the Western Caribbean (Cozumel, Costa Maya, Mahogany Bay) and Mexico year-round. In May 2027, she will relocate to Mobile, Alabama as her new homeport.

Who is the godmother of Carnival Valor?

Katie Couric, the renowned American journalist and television personality, served as godmother for Carnival Valor's christening ceremony in Miami on December 17, 2004. Couric was hosting NBC's Today show at the time.

What class is Carnival Valor?

Carnival Valor is the third of five Conquest-class ships, alongside sister ships Carnival Conquest (2002), Carnival Glory (2003), Carnival Liberty (2005), and Carnival Freedom (2007). These ships feature 110,000+ gross tons and accommodate nearly 3,000 guests.

What dining options are on Carnival Valor?

Carnival Valor features Guy's Burger Joint (by Guy Fieri), BlueIguana Cantina (Mexican), Bonsai Sushi, Fahrenheit 555 Steakhouse, Cucina del Capitano (Italian), RedFrog Pub, Pizzeria del Capitano (24-hour), and the Lincoln and Washington main dining rooms. The Lido Marketplace buffet offers casual dining.

When was Carnival Valor last refurbished?

Carnival Valor underwent a major refurbishment in 2016, adding new dining venues (Guy's Burger Joint, BlueIguana Cantina, RedFrog Pub), updated entertainment spaces, and enhanced stateroom amenities to refresh the guest experience.
