North Star

Royal Caribbean — Observation Experience

Quick Answer: A jewel-shaped glass observation capsule rising 300+ feet above sea level on Quantum Class ships. Complimentary basic rides; premium champagne experiences from $39. Book via app immediately on embarkation day.

Best For: Photography enthusiasts, sunset seekers, those wanting unique panoramic views, and couples celebrating special occasions.

Key Facts

  • Height: 300+ feet above sea
  • Duration: 15-20 minutes
  • Capacity: 14 guests
  • Basic Ride: Complimentary
  • Premium: $39-79+ per person

Experiences & Pricing

Basic ride: Complimentary · Premium packages: $39-249

Complimentary

  • Standard Ride: 15-20 min with full 360° rotation and maximum elevation
  • Book via Royal Caribbean app on embarkation day

Premium Packages

  • Champagne Experience: $39+ — Champagne service with views
  • Sunset Package: $59+ — Premium time slot, champagne, canapés
  • Private Charter: $249+ — Exclusive capsule for up to 14 guests

Tips

Pro Tips: Book within the first hour of boarding—sunset slots fill within minutes. Wheelchair accessible. Climate-controlled capsule. Bring camera but no tripods. Wear dark clothing to minimize glass reflections in photos.

Hours & Booking

Sea Days: 9am - 10pm (weather permitting). Port Days: Variable. Booking: Royal Caribbean app on embarkation day (complimentary) or Cruise Planner pre-cruise (premium). Available on: Quantum, Anthem, Ovation, and Spectrum of the Seas.

The Logbook — Real Guest Soundings

4.8 ★ out of 5 Couple • Anthem of the Seas • 2024

The sunset North Star experience was the highlight of our cruise. We saw the coast of England from 300 feet up while enjoying champagne. Book it on day one—literally the first thing you do after muster. The views are absolutely incredible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is North Star free?

Basic rides are complimentary. Premium packages with champagne start at $39.

How high does it go?

300+ feet (91 meters) above sea level with 360° views.

Is it safe for those afraid of heights?

The movement is smooth, but glass floor and walls provide full visibility of height. Not recommended for severe acrophobia.
