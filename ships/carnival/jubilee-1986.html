From Caribbean Fun Ship to Pacific Adventure

Historic Significance: MS Jubilee was the second Holiday-class ship and Carnival's first to be built in Sweden. After 18 years with Carnival, she pioneered cruising in Australia and Asia under new owners.

Built slightly larger than sister Holiday, Jubilee served Carnival's Caribbean routes before finding new life as Pacific Sun for P&O Australia, bringing cruise tourism to the South Pacific.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the same as Carnival Jubilee?

No. This page covers the original MS Jubilee (1986-2017), a Holiday-class ship. The modern Carnival Jubilee is an Excel-class mega-ship launched in 2023.

Why was Jubilee scrapped first?

After unsuccessful stints in China, MS Jubilee had no viable buyers. She was the first Holiday-class ship to be scrapped in 2017, four years before her sisters Holiday and Celebration met the same fate.
