Seabourn Fleet

Seabourn delivers ultra-luxury at an intimate scale — from expedition voyages with personal submarines to Mediterranean cruises with Thomas Keller dining. Seven ships across three classes serve just 264 to 604 guests each, with all-suite accommodations, open bars, and the kind of personalized service where the crew remembers your name and your drink.

Seabourn Guide Ship Quiz Packing Lists

Expedition Class

23K GT, 264 guests. Purpose-built for polar and expedition cruising. Two custom submarines, 24 Zodiacs, PC6 ice-strengthened hull.

Signature venues: The Grill by Thomas Keller, The Restaurant, Earth & Ocean, Sushi, Expedition Lounge

  • Seabourn Venture
  • Seabourn Pursuit

Ovation Class

40K GT, 604 guests. Expanded Odyssey design with The Retreat sundeck, enhanced spa. Adam D. Tihany interiors.

Signature venues: The Grill by Thomas Keller, The Restaurant, Sushi, The Colonnade, Sky Bar, The Retreat

  • Seabourn Ovation
  • Seabourn Encore

Odyssey Class

32K GT, 458 guests. All-suite, all-veranda. The Grill by Thomas Keller, Marina water sports platform.

Signature venues: The Grill by Thomas Keller, The Restaurant, Earth & Ocean, The Patio, Marina

  • Seabourn Odyssey
  • Seabourn Sojourn
  • Seabourn Quest
