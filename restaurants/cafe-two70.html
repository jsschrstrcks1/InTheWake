Café @ Two70

Café @ Two70

Royal Caribbean — Complimentary Gourmet-Casual with Panoramic Views

A free, view-forward café for hot-pressed sandwiches, customized salads, soups, pastries, and breakfast bites — tucked inside the spectacular Two70 venue on Quantum-class ships.

Think picnic-at-sea without the crowds: quick, quality bites and a front-row seat to the wake. Open for breakfast and lunch (with occasional light dinner hours). Return to the Restaurants hub →

Menu & Price

Price: Food is typically complimentary (included in fare). Specialty coffees and select beverages may carry standard pricing. Hours and selections vary by ship/sailing; check the Royal App on board.

Hours (common): Breakfast ~6:00–11:00 AM · Lunch ~11:00 AM–3:00 PM · Light dinner on select sailings.

Special Accommodations

Allergen & Dietary Notes: Staff can adjust salads and sandwiches (e.g., no nuts, vegan swaps). Gluten-free bread options vary by sailing; quality may vary. For severe allergies, notify Royal Caribbean in advance and reconfirm on embarkation.

Where You’ll Find It

Café @ Two70 is available on Quantum and Quantum Ultra class ships (e.g., Odyssey, Anthem, Ovation), inside the Two70 venue.

Sailed aboard Odyssey of the Seas.

Royal Caribbean Café @ Two70 Review 2025: Scenic Bites that Beat the Buffet Rush

Introduction

Café @ Two70 delivers fresh, flexible lunches and easy breakfasts with a side of “wow” — those sweeping 270° windows. It’s a complimentary Quantum-class gem for light eaters dodging crowds.

Food

Build-your-own salads kept things crisp and custom, while hot-pressed melts (the turkey-avocado club in particular) arrived toasty with proper cheese pull. Yogurt parfaits and pastries cover the morning crowd; soups rotate seasonally.

Service

Counter service moves quickly even at noon peaks; staff handle tweaks and allergy notes without fuss. Lines can form near closing due to shorter hours.

Atmosphere

Arguably the best casual-lunch view on the ship: wake-watching, sunlight, and plenty of seating in and around the Two70 lounge. The aft walk becomes part of the ritual.

Conclusion

With melty sandwiches, customizable greens, and panorama-level ambiance, Café @ Two70 is a must-try on Quantum-class. Rating: 4.3/5. Aim for off-peak hours and linger for the views.
