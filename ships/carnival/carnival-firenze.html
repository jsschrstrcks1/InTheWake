Originally built as Costa Firenze in 2020, she joined Carnival in February 2024 as part of the "Costa by Carnival" program, offering "Carnival Fun Italian Style" â€” a unique blend of Italian elegance and classic Carnival entertainment.

Carnival Firenze was originally Costa Firenze, built in 2020 for Costa Cruises. She transferred to Carnival Cruise Line in February 2024 as part of the "Costa by Carnival" program.

What class is Carnival Firenze?
