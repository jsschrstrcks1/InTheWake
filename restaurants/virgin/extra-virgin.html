Extra Virgin

Virgin Voyages — Specialty Dining

Quick Answer: Italian trattoria with fresh handmade pasta, charcuterie boards, and a tableside affogato cart. Half-portions of pasta available to try more dishes.

Best For: Italian food lovers, pasta enthusiasts, couples wanting fresh handmade pasta and tableside affogato

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Extra Virgin on Virgin Voyages?

Italian trattoria with fresh handmade pasta, charcuterie boards, and a tableside affogato cart. Half-portions of pasta available to try more dishes.

How much does Extra Virgin cost?

Extra Virgin is complimentary — included in your Virgin Voyages cruise fare at no extra charge. All dining on Virgin Voyages is included.

What is the dress code for Extra Virgin?

Smart Casual is recommended. Virgin Voyages has no formal nights — dress to express, not to impress. No specific dress code is enforced, but smart casual elevates the experience.

Do I need reservations for Extra Virgin?

Reservations are recommended, especially for dinner. Book through the Virgin Voyages app before sailing for the best availability. Popular venues like The Wake and Pink Agave fill quickly.

What are the menu highlights at Extra Virgin?

Popular items include Ribollita — Cannellini Beans, Escarole Salsa Verde, Country Bread (V), Romaine & Frisée Salad — White Anchovies, Croutons, Six-Minute Egg, Crispy Artichokes — Caper Aioli & Charred Lemon (V), Semolina Fried Calamari — Lemon, Creamy Chili Dip, and more. The menu may vary by ship and sailing.
