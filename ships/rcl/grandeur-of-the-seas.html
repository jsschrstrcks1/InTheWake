Disability Commentary

Vision-class layouts offer shorter walking distances than larger ships. Elevators are central, and the Solarium is typically on a higher deck with seating close to pool entries. Door thresholds and some lounge aisles may be tight at peak times. [To Verify] Latest accessible stateroom counts, door widths, and shower configurations on Grandeur post-refresh.
