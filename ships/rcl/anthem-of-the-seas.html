The Logbook — Tales From the Wake

Quantum-Class Thrills

The first time I stepped onto Anthem, I was immediately struck by how she felt like a floating innovation lab. The North Star pod—rising 300+ feet above the waves—offers views that make you feel like you're in a sci-fi movie. But it's the RipCord by iFLY that really sets her apart: indoor skydiving at sea, with neon lights and instructors who make it accessible for all ages.

SeaPlex: Activity Central

The SeaPlex is Anthem's beating heart for active guests. During the day, it's bumper cars and basketball. By evening, it transforms into a roller skating rink under disco lights. I once spent an entire sea day there—perfect for kids, perfect for adults who secretly love bumper cars.

Two70: Where Tech Meets Theater

Two70 is where Anthem's futuristic soul shines. Floor-to-ceiling Vistarama screens create virtual windows to impossible places, while robotic performers execute aerial dances that defy gravity. It's not just entertainment—it's an experience that makes you rethink what a cruise lounge can be.
