The Grove

The Grove

Royal Caribbean — Suite Neighborhood · Al Fresco Mediterranean

An exclusive, sun-splashed terrace for suite guests serving Mediterranean mezes, light plates, and grab-and-go sips—so you never miss a moment of the sun.

Perfect for pool-day refuels and golden-hour grazing, steps from the private suites’ pool and whirlpools. Return to the Restaurants hub →

v2.257.001

Access, Hours & What’s Included

Access: Suite Neighborhood guests (Sea Class and above; policies can vary by sailing).

Typical Hours: Breakfast 07:00–11:00 · Lunch/Snacks 11:00–20:00 (weather-permitting).

Price: Complimentary for eligible suite guests ($0). Specialty coffee/cocktails available à la carte or via packages.

Breakfast — Light Starts
  • Greek yogurt parfait with house granola & honey
  • Avocado toast with feta, tomato & olive oil
  • Fresh fruit plates; warm pita with hummus & cukes
Lunch & Daytime Snacks — Mezes & Small Plates
  • Falafel wrap with tahini & pickled veggies
  • Greek salad with olives, feta, oregano vinaigrette
  • Mezze platter: dolmas, tzatziki, marinated olives, grilled halloumi, warm pita
  • Chicken souvlaki skewers with lemon-yogurt dip
  • Veggie flatbread (zucchini, eggplant, goat cheese, balsamic)
  • Shrimp ceviche with avocado & chips
Desserts & Sips
  • Baklava bites; olive-oil cake with whipped feta
  • Iced tea, lemonade, drip coffee (complimentary)
  • Espresso drinks & Med-inspired cocktails (à la carte)

Note: Outdoor service is weather-dependent; offerings rotate slightly by ship and season. Confirm in the Royal App.

Suite Policies & Accommodations

  • Eligibility: Sea/Sky/Star Class; access may be limited during private events.
  • Dietary: Vegetarian/vegan & gluten-friendly swaps available; advise staff of allergens.
  • Dress: Pool-casual cover-ups and footwear required.
  • Tip: Pair The Grove’s light plates with a Coastal Kitchen dinner for a full-day suite food plan.

Where You’ll Find It

Icon-class only (Icon of the Seas • Star of the Seas), within the Suite Neighborhood sun deck—steps from the private pool.

The Grove Review 2025: Suite Sunlit Soirée

Introduction

Al fresco Med-inspired small plates for suite guests—fresh, scenic, and wonderfully easy between pool time and sunsets.

Food

Standouts include falafel wraps, Greek salads, chicken souvlaki, and a shareable mezze platter; sweets like baklava bites round it out.

Service

Counter-service pace with sunny personalities; quick customizations and helpful pairing tips.

Atmosphere

Open-air terrace with sweeping blue views; seating fills at peak sunshine and is weather-dependent.

Conclusion

Light, lively, and suite-smart. Ideal for daytime grazing. Rating: 4.4/5.
