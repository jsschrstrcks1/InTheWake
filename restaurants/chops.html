Chops Grille

Royal Caribbean — Specialty Steakhouse

Elevate your evening with hand-cut USDA Prime steaks and succulent seafood in Royal Caribbean’s hallmark steakhouse at sea.

Chops Grille is a signature premium venue found fleet-wide, pairing classic steakhouse tradition with modern craft and service that feels personal. An anchor of the line’s culinary identity, it offers a quiet, intimate contrast to the Main Dining Room. Return to the Restaurants hub →

v2.257.001

Menu & Prices

Lunch: $21–25 · Dinner: $39–65 (+18% gratuity) · Children 6–12 half price · Varies by ship/class & Lunch/Dinner.

Starters

  • Colossal Shrimp Cocktail
  • Pan-Seared Crab Cake
  • Tuna Tartare with Wasabi Caviar
  • Wild Mushroom Soup with White Truffle Oil
  • Crispy Goat Cheese Salad / The Wedge

Mains

  • USDA Prime Filet Mignon (9 oz)
  • Bone-In Ribeye (16 oz)
  • New York Strip (12 oz)
  • Herb-Crusted Rack of Lamb
  • Grilled Branzino with Sautéed Spinach
  • Broiled 1.5 lb Maine Lobster (+ $21 add-on)

Sides & Desserts

  • Creamed Spinach · Truffle Fries · Gruyère Tater Tots
  • Mac & Cheese · Mashed Potatoes · Sautéed Mushrooms
  • New York Cheesecake · Chocolate Lava Cake · Crème Brûlée

Lunch menus are a subset of the core offerings with smaller portions and a petite filet option for exceptional value.

Ship-Specific Variants
  • Icon Class — Dry-Aged Prime Porterhouse and Truffle-Butter Lobster Tail (To Verify Pricing)
  • Oasis Class — Expanded seafood section including broiled scallops and jumbo shrimp.

Special Accommodations

Allergen & Dietary Notes: Royal Caribbean follows SAFE Food Policy. Please disclose allergens to your server before ordering. Gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegetarian accommodations are available on request. For severe allergies, contact Royal Caribbean’s accessibility team at least 30 days before sailing and confirm with the venue on embarkation day.
