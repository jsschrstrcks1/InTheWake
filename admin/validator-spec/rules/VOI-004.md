## Rule
Descriptive prose must be page-specific. The voice-audit test: "Could this paragraph be moved to a different ship/port/venue page without editing?" If yes, it's interchangeable — a machine tell and a content failure. The reader came for THIS port, not "a Caribbean port."

## Fix guidance
Add a specific detail that anchors the text to THIS page: a deck number, a restaurant name, a jitney route number, a price, a time of day, a sensory observation that could only come from being in this specific place. Like-a-human says: "If detail applies to any ship, not sharpened enough."
