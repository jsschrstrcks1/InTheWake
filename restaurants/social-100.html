Social100

Royal Caribbean — Teen Club

Quick Answer: Social100 is Royal Caribbean's teen hangout space for ages 13-17. This supervised but relaxed space offers video games, music, activities, and teen-only parties. It's where teens can hang out with peers their age while parents enjoy adult activities. Completely free.

Best For: Teens ages 13-17. Parents wanting supervised teen activities. Families cruising with teenagers.

Key Facts

  • Ages: 13-17 years old
  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Supervision: Royal Caribbean staff
  • Activities: Games, music, parties
  • Hours: Varies by day

Where teens can be teens.

Social100 gives teenagers their own space to hang out, make friends, and enjoy activities designed for their age group. Video games, music, scavenger hunts, pool parties, and late-night teen-only events keep things interesting. Staff provide supervision without being overbearing, creating an environment where teens can socialize while parents get some adult time. Return to the Restaurants hub →
