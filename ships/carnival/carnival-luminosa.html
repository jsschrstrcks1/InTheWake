First Look: Carnival Luminosa

Former Name: Costa Luminosa (2009–2022). Transferred to Carnival Cruise Line in November 2022 following a major refurbishment in Palermo, Italy.

Overview

Carnival Luminosa is a Spirit-class hybrid cruise ship that joined Carnival's fleet in November 2022 after sailing 13 years as Costa Luminosa. Built by Fincantieri at their Marghera shipyard near Venice, she originally entered service on May 5, 2009, for Costa Cruises.

Her design incorporates elements from both Spirit and Vista classes, resulting in a unique layout that distinguishes her from other Spirit-class vessels. At 92,700 gross tons with capacity for 2,260 guests, she offers an intimate atmosphere with European elegance now enhanced with Carnival's signature "Fun Ship" experiences.

Luminosa serves as Carnival's flagship in the Australian market, homeported in Brisbane and offering South Pacific, Papua New Guinea, and Australian coastal itineraries—making Carnival cruising accessible to Australian guests.

Frequently Asked Questions

What was Carnival Luminosa called before?

Carnival Luminosa sailed as Costa Luminosa from May 2009 until November 2022. She underwent a major refurbishment in Palermo, Italy, to add Carnival branding, Guy's Burger Joint, BlueIguana Cantina, and other Carnival signature venues.

What class is Carnival Luminosa?

Carnival Luminosa is classified as a Spirit-class hybrid, incorporating design elements from both Spirit and Vista classes. This makes her unique among Carnival's five Spirit-class vessels (Spirit, Pride, Legend, Miracle, Luminosa).

How big is Carnival Luminosa?

Carnival Luminosa measures 92,700 gross tons and is 965 feet (294 meters) long. She has capacity for 2,260 guests at double occupancy across 1,130 staterooms on 12 decks, with a crew of 1,050.

Where does Carnival Luminosa sail from?

Carnival Luminosa is homeported in Brisbane, Australia, offering South Pacific, Papua New Guinea, and Australian coastal itineraries. She serves as Carnival's flagship in the Australian market.

Does Carnival Luminosa have unique features?

Yes! Carnival Luminosa retained several unique features from her Costa days including a 4D Cinema experience and Grand Planetarium. These combine with new Carnival additions like Guy's Burger Joint and WaterWorks.
