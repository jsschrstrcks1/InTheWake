Rhapsody of the Seas

Vision class ships are compact and view-forward, with generous glass and classic lines.

They trade headline attractions for itinerary-first cruising—relaxed lounges, a bright solarium, and sea‑day scenery.

Dining centers on the Main Dining Room and Windjammer; refits added touches while keeping a calm vibe.

Great match for travelers who value ports and human-scale decks over mega‑ship theme‑park energy.
