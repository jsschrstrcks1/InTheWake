Broadway Melodies Theatre

Royal Caribbean — Main Theater

Quick Answer: Broadway Melodies Theatre is a two-tier main theater on select Royal Caribbean ships. True to its name, it delivers Broadway-style entertainment with production shows, musical revues, and live performances. Complimentary with open seating.

Best For: Broadway and musical theater fans. Evening entertainment seekers. Families and couples. Anyone who appreciates live performance.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Design: Two-tier theater
  • Shows: Broadway-style productions
  • Seating: Open—no reservations
  • Schedule: Early and late shows

Broadway comes to you.

Broadway Melodies Theatre delivers on its promise with production shows that capture the spirit of the Great White Way. The two-tier design provides excellent sightlines from both orchestra and balcony levels.
