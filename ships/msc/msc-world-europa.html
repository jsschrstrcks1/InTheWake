MSC World Europa — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

MSC World Europa is the lead and first ship of MSC Cruises' World Class — the line's largest and most ambitious cruise ships. She was delivered on 24 October 2022 at Saint-Nazaire by Chantiers de l'Atlantique and christened on 13 November 2022 at the Grand Cruise Terminal in Doha, Qatar by Sheikha Hind bint Hamad Al Thani, Vice-Chairperson and CEO of the Qatar Foundation — notably breaking MSC Cruises' long-standing tradition of having Sophia Loren christen every new MSC ship. She measures 215,863 gross tons and 333.30 metres in length, carries 5,079 guests at double occupancy (6,762 maximum) across 2,626 cabins with a crew of 2,138, and flies the Maltese flag from Valletta. At delivery she was the world's largest LNG dual-fuel cruise ship, powered by five Wärtsilä 14V46DF engines (80 MW total) and fitted with a 150 kW Solid Oxide Fuel Cell (SOFC) demonstrator — the first contemporary cruise ship with SOFC technology. She has sailed year-round 7-night Western Mediterranean round-trips from Genoa, Naples, Messina, Valletta, Barcelona, and Marseille since April 2023.

First Look: MSC World Europa

MSC World Europa is the lead and first ship of MSC Cruises' World Class, delivered in October 2022. At 215,863 gross tons and accommodating 5,079 guests at double occupancy (6,762 maximum), she introduced design innovations that distinguished her from MSC's earlier Meraviglia generation. Her most striking feature is the Y-shaped aft with floor-to-ceiling glass walls housing the Panorama Lounge with its interactive LED floor, opening onto a seven-deck outdoor World Promenade that runs 104 metres along the stern.

Signature attractions include the 11-deck Venom Drop @ The Spiral — a stainless-steel dry slide that runs from Deck 20 aft down to Deck 8 at the World Promenade level, making it the longest dry slide at sea. The World Galleria is the LED-ceiling indoor promenade. Entertainment highlights include the World Theatre (approximately 1,153 seats), the Luna Park Arena family entertainment zone with VR experiences, and the Panorama Lounge aft. Her 13+ dining venues include four MDRs (Bubbles, La Foglia, Esagono, Hexagon) plus chef-branded specialty restaurants from Michelin-starred Ramón Freixa (HOLA! Tacos & Cantina) and Niklas Ekstedt (Chef's Garden Kitchen).

The MSC Yacht Club enclave has 152 suites with private pools, the dedicated MSC Yacht Club Restaurant, and butler service. MSC World Europa was the world's largest LNG dual-fuel cruise ship at her delivery, powered by five Wärtsilä 14V46DF engines (80 MW total), and she was the first contemporary cruise ship to feature a Solid Oxide Fuel Cell (150 kW SOFC demonstrator powered by LNG). With seven pools, the Aurora Borealis Aquapark, and extensive family areas, MSC World Europa is MSC's flagship innovation in the 2022-2027 World Class programme.

Entertainment & Activities on MSC World Europa

Entertainment on MSC World Europa is built around the World Theatre (~1,153 seats) for main production shows, with the Luna Park Arena (~300 seats) as a family entertainment and VR experience zone. The Panorama Lounge aft — housed in the class-defining Y-shaped stern with floor-to-ceiling glass walls — features an interactive LED floor and panoramic ocean views. The indoor World Galleria promenade runs through the heart of the ship under a vast LED-dome ceiling, while the outdoor World Promenade runs seven decks and 104 metres along the Y-shaped stern at Deck 8. The most dramatic activity feature is Venom Drop @ The Spiral, an 11-deck stainless-steel dry slide that runs from Deck 20 down to Deck 8 — the longest dry slide at sea. Family attractions include the Aurora Borealis Aquapark and the full Doremiland Kids Clubs (Baby Club Chicco, Mini Club LEGO, Junior Club LEGO, Young Club, Teen Club, Web Creator Room). Dining-wise, Deck 7 is the specialty-restaurants and bars hub, with the Masters of the Sea Pub hosting MSC's first onboard microbrewery, plus Fizz Champagne Bar, The Gin Project (70+ craft gins), Elixir Mixology Bar, Malt Lounge & Smoker's Bar, and chef-branded specialty restaurants. The MSC Foundation Centre is an interactive sustainability and ocean conservation exhibit. Wellness lives at the MSC Aurea Spa and MSC Gym powered by Technogym. The private MSC Yacht Club enclave has 152 suites with private pools, a private Top Sail Lounge, the MSC Yacht Club Restaurant, and butler service. Note: MSC World Europa does NOT carry Cirque du Soleil at Sea (the MSC × Cirque partnership was never applied to World Europa and has since been phased out entirely from the MSC fleet).

MSC World Europa Deck Plans

MSC World Europa's 22-deck layout (21 guest-accessible) is organised around the class-defining Y-shaped aft. Decks 5-7 carry the four main dining rooms (Bubbles, La Foglia, Esagono, Hexagon), the World Theatre, Royal Palm Casino, and the LED-dome World Galleria indoor promenade. Deck 8 is the specialty-dining and bars hub with Butcher's Cut, Kaito Teppanyaki & Sushi Bar, HOLA! Tacos & Cantina by Ramón Freixa, Chef's Garden Kitchen by Niklas Ekstedt, La Pescaderia Fish Market, the Jean-Philippe Maury Chocolaterie, Masters of the Sea Pub with the onboard microbrewery, Fizz Champagne Bar, The Gin Project, and the exit point of the Venom Drop dry slide. Deck 8 also houses the seven-deck outdoor World Promenade that runs 104 metres along the Y-shaped stern. Decks 15-19 are the main pool and aquapark zone with Horizon Pool, Botanic Pool Garden, Zen Pool, Aurora Borealis Aquapark, Luna Park Arena, and the MSC Aurea Spa. Deck 20 aft holds the top of the 11-deck Venom Drop slide, and the forward MSC Yacht Club sundeck with private pool. The Panorama Lounge at the ship's aft — in the glass-walled Y-shaped stern — features an interactive LED floor and panoramic ocean views.

Frequently Asked Questions About MSC World Europa

What dining options are on MSC World Europa?

MSC World Europa has four complimentary main dining rooms — Bubbles, La Foglia, Esagono, and Hexagon — plus the Aurea-exclusive Les Dunes Restaurant. Buffets include Il Mercato and the new 24/7 La Brasserie (a World Class innovation). Specialty dining includes Butcher's Cut steakhouse, Kaito Teppanyaki & Sushi Bar, HOLA! Tacos & Cantina by Ramón Freixa, Chef's Garden Kitchen by Niklas Ekstedt (both Michelin-starred chefs), La Pescaderia — Fish Market, and the Jean-Philippe Maury Chocolaterie & Café. Yacht Club suite guests have the private MSC Yacht Club Restaurant and Grill & Bar.

Which ships are in the World Class?

MSC World Europa (2022) is the lead and first ship of MSC Cruises' World Class. Her sisters are MSC World America (delivered 2025, Miami-based, Caribbean) and MSC World Asia (scheduled for November 2026 delivery, currently at the shipyard). MSC World Atlantic is planned for February 2027. All World Class ships are built by Chantiers de l'Atlantique at Saint-Nazaire and feature the class-defining Y-shaped aft with floor-to-ceiling glass walls, LNG dual-fuel propulsion, the 11-deck Venom Drop @ The Spiral dry slide, and the seven-deck outdoor World Promenade.

Does MSC World Europa have Cirque du Soleil?

No. The MSC × Cirque du Soleil at Sea partnership was limited to earlier Meraviglia-generation ships and has since been phased out entirely from the MSC fleet. MSC World Europa was never part of the Cirque partnership — her main production shows take place in the World Theatre (~1,153 seats) with additional entertainment in the Luna Park Arena (family/VR), the aft Panorama Lounge with its interactive LED floor, and the LED-dome World Galleria indoor promenade. Her christening in Doha in November 2022 featured tenor Matteo Bocelli, a drone show, and hull video mapping — no Cirque.

What is the Venom Drop @ The Spiral?

The Venom Drop @ The Spiral is MSC World Europa's signature attraction — an 11-deck stainless-steel dry slide approximately 76 metres (250 feet) long, the longest dry slide at sea. The entrance is on Deck 20 aft at the top of the ship, and the exit is on Deck 8 at the World Promenade level. Riders descend inside an enclosed stainless-steel spiral tube. The Venom Drop is a class-wide feature built into every World Class ship.

Who christened MSC World Europa?

MSC World Europa was christened on 13 November 2022 at the Grand Cruise Terminal in Doha, Qatar by Sheikha Hind bint Hamad Al Thani, Vice-Chairperson and CEO of the Qatar Foundation. This broke MSC Cruises' long-standing tradition of having Sophia Loren christen every new MSC ship. The ceremony featured tenor Matteo Bocelli (his first solo performance in Doha), a drone show, hull video mapping, and fireworks. The ship had been delivered 20 days earlier, on 24 October 2022, by Chantiers de l'Atlantique at Saint-Nazaire, and then sailed directly to Doha to serve as a floating hotel during the 2022 FIFA World Cup. Her first commercial cruise departed Qatar on 20 December 2022.
