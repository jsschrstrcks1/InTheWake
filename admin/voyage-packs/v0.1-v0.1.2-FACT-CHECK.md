## Royal Caribbean — appended 2026-05-06 (for v0.1 Symphony Emergency Contacts section)

Target operating company: **Royal Caribbean International** (the cruise-line brand, not the Royal Caribbean Group parent that also owns Celebrity / Silversea). All numbers below are taken from royalcaribbean.com properties (the operator's primary site).

| Claim | Source check | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| Ship-to-shore family contact (US) | https://www.royalcaribbean.com/faq/questions/onboard-phone-call-services | ✅ verified — **(888) 724-7447** ("Your friends and family can contact the ship by calling (888) 724-7447 … The cost is $7.95 USD a minute and can be charged to their MasterCard® or Visa®.") |
| Ship-to-shore family contact (international) | https://www.royalcaribbean.com/faq/questions/onboard-phone-call-services | ✅ verified — **(321) 953-9003** ("from outside the U.S. they can call (321) 953-9003"). Same $7.95/min rate; "additional long distance charges will also apply." |
| Air2Sea flight assistance hotline | https://www.royalcaribbean.com/resources/contact-us (also referenced at /faq/questions/what-happens-when-my-air2sea-flight-is-delayed-or-changed) | ✅ verified — **844-278-9745**, listed under "Air2Sea arrangements," available **24/7** |
| Customer service main line — Individual Reservations | https://www.royalcaribbean.com/resources/contact-us | ✅ verified — **866-562-7625**, "Individual Reservations, Mon–Sun 7am–2am (EST)" |
| Customer service main line — Post-Cruise / Day-of-Sailing (24/7) | https://www.royalcaribbean.com/resources/contact-us | ✅ verified — **800-256-6649** (US) / **305-539-4107** (international); the contact page lists 800-256-6649 for both "Post Cruise Assistance" (Mon–Thu 24h, Fri 12am–9pm, Sat 9am–6pm, Sun 9am–12am ET) **and** "Day of Sailing help" (24/7). 305-539-4107 is the international Day-of-Sailing line. |

Notes:

- **Two distinct "main" lines.** Royal Caribbean's Contact Us page does not publish a single number labelled "Customer Service." It splits the function: **866-562-7625** for booking/reservations and **800-256-6649** for post-cruise + day-of-sailing. For the v0.1 Symphony Emergency Contacts section, the most operationally useful "general line" is **800-256-6649** because it has 24/7 day-of-sailing coverage (matching the spirit of "call this if something is going wrong"). Royal Caribbean Blog's editorial guide also identifies 800-256-6649 as "the general Royal Caribbean customer service phone number." Recommend listing both with their roles labelled rather than picking one.
- **Ship-to-shore is per-minute paid by the caller** ($7.95 USD/min). It is *not* an emergency dispatch line — it connects the caller to the ship's onboard phone system. For genuine family emergencies (e.g., death in family ashore, urgent message), the operationally correct path Royal Caribbean has historically pointed callers to is the 24/7 Day-of-Sailing line **800-256-6649** (US) / **305-539-4107** (Int'l), which can relay urgent messages to the ship via the bridge. The pack should explain this distinction so a family in crisis doesn't get charged $7.95/min while trying to deliver a death notification.
- **Hours are EST (Eastern Time), not ET-with-DST-noted** — the Royal Caribbean page literally says "(EST)" year-round, which is technically imprecise (they do follow DST in practice), but copy the published string as-is.
- **No separate "emergency travel team" number was found on royalcaribbean.com primary pages.** Cruise Critic forum threads cite 866-562-7625 and 866-829-1280 as a "land-based emergency travel team," but these are user-board posts, not a primary-source page on royalcaribbean.com — so they are **not** carried into the pack. (866-562-7625 is the published Individual Reservations line; 866-829-1280 could not be confirmed against a Royal Caribbean primary URL and is **⏳ unable to confirm**, deliberately omitted.)
- Independent cross-checks consulted: Royal Caribbean Blog ("5 easy ways to contact Royal Caribbean customer service" — 2023-04-20) and royalcaribbeanblog.com/royal-caribbean-contact. Both agree on 800-256-6649 as the general customer-service number, 866-562-7625 as reservations, and 844-278-9745 as Air2Sea. None of the cross-check sources contradict the primary-source phone strings above.
