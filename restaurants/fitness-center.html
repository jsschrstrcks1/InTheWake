Fitness Center

Royal Caribbean — Fitness & Wellness

Quick Answer: The Fitness Center is free on all Royal Caribbean ships. Open 6am-10pm with cardio machines, free weights, and stunning ocean views. Located adjacent to Vitality Spa.

Best For: Fitness-minded cruisers wanting to maintain workout routines, early risers, and anyone seeking equipment with ocean views.

Key Facts

  • Price: Equipment complimentary; Classes $12-18
  • Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Location: Adjacent to Vitality Spa
  • Equipment: Cardio, weights, machines

Classes & Pricing

Equipment: Free · Classes: $12-18 · Personal Training: $85-150

Complimentary

  • All Equipment: Cardio, weights, machines
  • Morning Stretch: Free on pool deck
  • Ab Blast: Free core workout

Premium Classes (Fee)

  • Spinning: $12-18
  • Yoga: $12-18
  • Pilates: $12-18
  • Personal Training: $85-150/session

Tips & Best Times

Pro Tip: Best times are 6-7am (nearly empty), port days (guests ashore), and after 8pm. Avoid mid-morning on sea days (peak crowds).

What to Bring

  • Workout clothes and proper athletic shoes
  • Water bottle (fill at coolers)
  • Headphones for cardio machines

Hours & Location

Open 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM. Located on upper forward decks near Vitality Spa. Available on all Royal Caribbean ships fleet-wide. Larger ships (Icon/Oasis Class) have the most extensive facilities.

The Logbook — Real Guest Soundings

4.0 ★ out of 5 Regular cruiser • Symphony of the Seas • 2024

The gym on Symphony was surprisingly well-equipped. Plenty of treadmills with ocean views — makes the time fly by. Go early (I was there at 6:15am) and you'll have your pick of equipment. By 9am on sea days it gets crowded. The free weights section is smaller than land gyms but adequate. Spinning classes are worth the fee if you're into that.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the gym free?

Yes, all equipment is complimentary. Some group classes have a $12-18 fee.

What equipment is available?

Treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowing machines, free weights, and weight machines. Most cardio has personal screens.

Is there a running track?

Most ships have an outdoor jogging track (0.25-0.33 miles per lap) plus sports courts.
