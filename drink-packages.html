Cruise Drink Packages

Every cruise line approaches beverages differently — from “classic” packages to tiered premium options, from soda-only plans to all-inclusive beverage programs. Prices shift often, and what’s included varies widely between lines.

In the Wake is developing clear, side-by-side explanations of every major cruise line’s drink package systems, plus interactive calculators to help you decide whether the math works out for your sailing style.

Coming Soon

  • Per-line package breakdowns (Royal Caribbean, Carnival, NCL, MSC, and more)
  • Cost-per-drink comparisons and onboard price data
  • Interactive “Worth It?” calculators for each cruise line
  • Accessibility notes for guests with dietary or medical beverage restrictions

These tools are in progress and will roll out line by line. Our goal is transparency: no fluff, no pressure — just the truth about what’s in your glass and what it really costs.

Why This Matters

The average cruiser spends more on beverages than on excursions — yet drink packages remain one of the most confusing parts of booking. We’re building resources that put clarity ahead of marketing, helping you understand value before you swipe your card.

Because calm seas — and calm decisions — are what we’re all about.
