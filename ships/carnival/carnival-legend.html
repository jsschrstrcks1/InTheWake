Ship Overview

Carnival Legend made history in 2002 as the first Carnival Fun Ship with an onboard wedding chapel. Christened by acclaimed British actress Dame Judi Dench in Harwich, England, she offers intimate Spirit-class cruising with classic Fun Ship amenities.

Legend operates seasonally from Baltimore and Tampa, offering Caribbean, Bahamas, and Bermuda itineraries. With 80% of staterooms featuring ocean views, she's ideal for scenic cruising.

FAQ

Does Legend have a wedding chapel?

Yes! Legend was the first Carnival Fun Ship with an onboard wedding chapel, making her popular for destination weddings at sea.

Who is the godmother?

Dame Judi Dench, the acclaimed British actress, christened Legend on August 21, 2002 in Harwich, England.

Where does Legend sail from?

Baltimore and Tampa seasonally, offering Caribbean, Bahamas, and Bermuda cruises.
