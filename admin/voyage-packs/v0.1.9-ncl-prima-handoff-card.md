## What the at-home contact does if 36+ hours pass without word

1. **Don't panic** — cruise Wi-Fi is unreliable; silence usually means signal, not emergency. Hurricane-season reroutes can also affect cellular at unexpected ports.
2. **Call the NCL Care Team** above; they'll confirm you boarded and relay messages.
3. **Reach Tina** at tina@maulsbytravel.com or 910-528-5077 — booking agent and on-ship host.
4. **If NCL confirms an incident:** US Embassy Kingston (Jamaica/Cayman); US Consulate Mérida (Mexico); ship medical (health). Track ship at /tools/ship-tracker.html. Weather updates: NCL daily program + nhc.noaa.gov.
