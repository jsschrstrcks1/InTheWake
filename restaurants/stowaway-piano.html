Stowaway Piano

Royal Caribbean — Piano Bar

Quick Answer: Stowaway Piano is a lively piano bar on select Royal Caribbean ships featuring talented musicians playing request-driven sets. The atmosphere encourages sing-alongs and audience participation, making it one of the most fun evening venues onboard.

Best For: Music lovers. Groups looking for fun evening entertainment. Anyone who enjoys sing-alongs. Those who love piano bar atmosphere.

Key Facts

  • Entry: Complimentary
  • Drinks: À la carte or drink package
  • Entertainment: Live piano music
  • Atmosphere: Interactive, sing-along
  • Hours: Evening entertainment

Where the music comes alive.

Stowaway Piano captures the classic piano bar experience at sea. Talented musicians take requests and lead the room in everything from classic rock to show tunes to modern hits. The energy builds as the night goes on—by midnight, the whole room is usually singing along. It's consistently one of the most fun, low-key entertainment options on the ship. Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cover charge?

No, entry to Stowaway Piano is free. Just buy drinks if you want them.

Can I request songs?

Yes! Requests are encouraged. Tips for the pianist are appreciated for requests.

What time does it start?

Typically evening entertainment starting around 8-9 PM and running until late.

Is it appropriate for families?

Yes, earlier sessions are family-friendly. Late-night can get rowdier.

Do I need reservations?

No reservations needed—just show up. Arrive early for the best seats.

Are drinks covered by the package?

Yes, most drinks are covered by the Deluxe Beverage Package.
