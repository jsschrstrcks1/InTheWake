Royal Escape Room

Royal Caribbean — Puzzle Experience

Quick Answer: Royal Escape Room is an immersive puzzle-solving experience on select Royal Caribbean ships. Teams of 2-8 players work together to solve clues, find hidden objects, and escape before time runs out. Themed rooms vary by ship, and the experience costs approximately $20-25 per person.

Best For: Puzzle enthusiasts. Groups and families traveling together. Team-building seekers. Anyone who enjoys mental challenges and mystery-solving.

Key Facts

  • Cost: ~$20-25 per person
  • Team Size: 2-8 players
  • Duration: Approximately 60 minutes
  • Reservations: Recommended
  • Age: Varies (typically 8+)

Can your team escape in time?

Royal Caribbean's escape rooms bring the popular puzzle experience to sea. Lock yourselves in a themed room and work together to solve a series of clues, puzzles, and challenges before the clock runs out. Themes vary by ship—some feature spy adventures, others mysterious scenarios. It's a great rainy day activity or something different for groups who want a shared challenge. Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Approximately $20-25 per person, though prices may vary by ship and theme.

Can kids participate?

Yes, typically ages 8 and up can participate. Some themes may have different age requirements.

What if we don't escape in time?

You'll still get the full experience and learn the solution. It's about the journey, not just winning!

Do I need a full team?

No, smaller groups can book and may be paired with other guests to fill the room.

How do I book?

Book through the Cruise Planner before sailing or at the activities desk onboard.

Is it scary?

Most themes are not scary—they're puzzle-focused rather than horror-themed.
