Mystery Dinner Theater

Royal Caribbean — Interactive Dining Experience

Quick Answer: Mystery Dinner Theater combines dining with interactive murder mystery entertainment. Enjoy a multi-course meal while actors perform a mystery that you help solve. Available on select Royal Caribbean ships as a fee-based experience requiring reservations.

Best For: Mystery and theater enthusiasts. Couples seeking unique date nights. Groups wanting interactive entertainment. Anyone who enjoys dinner theater.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Fee-based (varies by ship)
  • Includes: Multi-course dinner + show
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Reservations: Required
  • Style: Interactive murder mystery

Dine, sleuth, solve.

Mystery Dinner Theater brings the classic murder mystery dinner experience to sea. As you enjoy a multi-course meal, actors perform a mystery that unfolds around you. Gather clues, question suspects, and work with your tablemates to solve the crime before dessert. It's dinner and a show in one unique package. Return to the Restaurants hub →

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Pricing varies by ship. Check your Cruise Planner for current rates.

Is food included?

Yes, a multi-course dinner is part of the experience.

Do I have to participate?

Participation is encouraged but you can observe if preferred.

Is it suitable for kids?

Generally better for adults, but check the specific show's rating.

How long is it?

Typically 2-3 hours including dinner.

How do I book?

Through Cruise Planner pre-cruise or onboard if available.
