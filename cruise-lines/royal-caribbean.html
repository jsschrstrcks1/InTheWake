Royal Caribbean

Neighborhood-style ships, headline entertainment, and a specialty-dining bench deep enough to turn a sea day into a progressive tasting. Start with your class—Icon, Oasis, Freedom, Voyager, Radiance, Vision—then pick the ship whose vibe (and venues) match your crew.

Packing for slides, show nights, and breezy pool decks? See Packing Lists.

Icon Class

Royal’s “world’s best family vacation” era: waterpark neighborhood, seven pools, and a show slate that spans air, ice, water, and stage—max choice with calmer pockets for date-night escapes.

Premium venues (varies by ship): Empire Supper Club · Izumi / Teppanyaki · Hooked Seafood · Giovanni’s · Chops Grille · Chef’s Table

Oasis Class

The original “neighborhood” revolution: Central Park, Boardwalk, AquaTheater, and big-evening shows. Resort-level pools meet casual-to-premium dining.

Premium venues (varies by ship): 150 Central Park · Wonderland · Chops · Giovanni’s · Izumi / Teppanyaki · Hooked · The Mason Jar · Portside BBQ · Chef’s Table

Freedom Class

Sweet-spot scale—big-ship staples without mega-ship sprawl. FlowRider, sports decks, comedy/live music, and versatile dining for mixed-age groups.

Premium venues (varies by ship): Chops · Giovanni’s · Izumi · Chef’s Table · Johnny Rockets

Voyager Class

The class that taught cruising to skate—ice rinks, Royal Promenade parades, and compact layouts that make “do it all” days surprisingly easy.

Premium venues (varies by ship): Chops · Giovanni’s · Izumi · Chef’s Table · Johnny Rockets

Radiance Class

Glass-and-views ships built for scenery: Alaska, fjords, and coastals. Floor-to-ceiling windows, calmer vibe, and classic specialty pairings.

Premium venues (varies by ship): Chops · Izumi · Chef’s Table

Vision Class

Cozy classics with atrium life and big-itinerary charm—Bermuda, New England, or Caribbean hops with personable crews.

Premium venues (varies by ship): Chops · Chef’s Table
