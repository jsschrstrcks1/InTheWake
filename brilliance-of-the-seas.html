Brilliance of the Seas

Radiance Class blends big‑ship polish with small‑ship sightlines. Designed with extensive glass and wide ocean views, these ships sail beautifully in scenic regions — think fjords, Alaska, and Mediterranean coastlines where ‘outside’ is the headliner.

Onboard you’ll find a relaxed rhythm: sun‑splashed Solariums, a bright atrium for evening music, and dining that feels curated rather than crowded. It’s an itinerary‑first style of sailing — with just enough sparkle to feel special after sunset.

Brilliance (2002) refines the formula with standout glasswork and a warm, contemporary palette. Her Centrum comes alive at night with live music and aerial moments, while specialty dining — from Giovanni’s Table to Izumi — adds ‘date‑night’ variety without overwhelming the ship’s scale.

Travelers praise her flow: easy to learn in an afternoon, but with enough tucked‑away nooks to feel new on day five. She’s a strong pick for Mediterranean and Caribbean seasons where port days lead the story and evenings wind down elegantly.

Choose Brilliance if you want a cruiser’s cruiser — thoughtfully laid out, quietly upscale, and always looking outward.
