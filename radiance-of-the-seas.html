Radiance of the Seas

Radiance-class ships are famed for glass and light—nine-deck atriums, floor-to-ceiling views, and solariums that make Alaska and fjords feel close enough to touch.

Radiance hits that sweet spot: large enough for dining variety and entertainment, small enough to keep the ocean front and center.

Expect relaxed days—live music wafting through the Centrum, trivia in the lounges, and long afternoons watching the scenery roll by.

Dining ranges from the classic MDR and Windjammer to crowd-pleasers like Giovanni’s and Izumi (ship dependent).

If your ideal cruise is more panorama than theme park, Radiance is your lens on the sea.
