Grandeur of the Seas

Grandeur of the Seas is classic Royal Caribbean: intimate, friendly, and refreshingly easy to get around.

For couples and families who prefer connection over crowds, Grandeur’s scale is a feature, not a compromise.

Food highlights lean into comfort—classics in the Main Dining Room and specialty favorites like Chops.

Cabins are smartly laid out with that Royal Caribbean practicality; book an oceanview or balcony.

If you want a cruise that feels like a ship—not a theme park—Grandeur is quietly perfect.
