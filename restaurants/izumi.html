Izumi Sushi — Refined Japanese Craft at Sea

Royal Caribbean’s Izumi Sushi offers an authentic Japanese dining experience with fresh sushi, sashimi, and small plates in a tranquil, contemporary setting. Ideal for romantic dinners or wellness-minded cruisers craving light, precise flavors.

Izumi Hibachi — The Teppanyaki Spectacle

For those who like their dinner served with a show, Izumi Hibachi delivers interactive flair as chefs flip, flame, and entertain around the grill. Perfect for families and groups, the meal includes multiple courses and a side of laughter.
