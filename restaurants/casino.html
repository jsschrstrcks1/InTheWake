Casino Royale

Royal Caribbean — Gaming & Entertainment

Quick Answer: Casino Royale is Royal Caribbean's onboard casino on all ships. Features slots, blackjack, roulette, craps, poker and more. Opens in international waters only (18+). Join Club Royale immediately — even minimal play earns rewards toward free cruises.

Best For: Adults seeking gaming entertainment, Club Royale loyalty members, poker enthusiasts, and anyone wanting complimentary drinks while playing.

Key Facts

  • Age: 18+ required
  • Hours: International waters only (sea days: 10am-3am)
  • Table Minimums: $5-25 (varies by game/time)
  • Slots: $0.01-$5 denominations
  • Rewards: Club Royale (free enrollment)

Games & Minimums

Table Games: $5-25 min · Slots: $0.01-$5 · Poker: $35-120 buy-in · Minimums vary by time/ship

Table Games

  • Blackjack: $10-25 min (6:5 and 3:2 tables)
  • Roulette: $5-10 min (American double-zero)
  • Craps: $10-25 min
  • Caribbean Stud: $5-10 min (progressive jackpot)
  • Three Card Poker: $5-10 min
  • Let It Ride: $5-10 min

Slots & Poker

  • Penny/Nickel Slots: $0.01-0.05
  • Quarter/Dollar Slots: $0.25-1.00
  • Video Poker: $0.25-5.00 (best odds)
  • Hold'em Tournaments: $35-120 buy-in
  • Cash Games: $1/2 NL (larger ships)

Club Royale Rewards

Pro Tip: Sign up for Club Royale (free) immediately. Even minimal play accumulates points toward free cruises, drinks, and priority boarding. Points never expire if you sail every 24 months.

Tier Benefits

  • Classic (0 pts): Birthday free play, promotional offers
  • Select (2,000 pts): Priority check-in, 10% shop discount
  • Elite (7,500 pts): Specialty dining discount, priority boarding
  • Signature (25,000 pts): VIP events, complimentary internet
  • Masters (75,000 pts): Dedicated host, balcony upgrades
  • Prime/Seven Stars: Suite upgrades, hosted cruises

Earn 1 point per $5 (slots/video poker) or $10 (table games) wagered.

Hours & Operations

  • Embarkation Day: Opens ~1 hour after departure
  • Sea Days: 10:00 AM - 3:00 AM
  • Port Days: Opens upon departure from port
  • Final Night: Closes approaching US waters

Casino operates in international waters only per maritime law. Available on all Royal Caribbean ships fleet-wide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum gambling age?

18+ on all Royal Caribbean ships, regardless of departure port. Valid photo ID required.

Is smoking allowed?

Policies vary by ship. Many have smoke-free or partially smoke-free casinos. Non-smoking slot sections available on all ships.

Are drinks free while playing?

Yes, complimentary drinks for active players. Cocktail servers circulate the floor. Tip your servers for better service.

Can I use credit cards?

Yes, but cash advance fees apply (typically 3% + ATM fees). Cash or SeaPass is more economical.
