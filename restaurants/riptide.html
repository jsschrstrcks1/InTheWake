Riptide

Royal Caribbean — Mat Racer Waterslide

Quick Answer: Riptide is a headfirst mat racer waterslide on select Royal Caribbean ships. Grab a mat, race side-by-side with friends or family down parallel high-speed slides. The competitive element makes it extra fun—and it's completely free.

Best For: Competitive groups and families. Thrill seekers who want bragging rights. Waterslide enthusiasts. Anyone who loves racing.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Style: Headfirst mat racer
  • Lanes: Side-by-side racing
  • Height Requirement: Varies by ship
  • Ships: Select Royal Caribbean ships

Race your way to the splash zone.

Riptide brings competitive fun to the water. Lie headfirst on a mat and race side-by-side with friends through parallel slide lanes. It's fast, it's thrilling, and the racing element adds extra excitement. Who will cross the finish splash first? There's only one way to find out—and you can ride again and again since it's free. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Riptide free?

Yes, completely complimentary.

How do I ride?

Lie headfirst on a provided mat and race down the slide.

Is there an age/height limit?

Yes, height requirements apply and vary by ship. Check with crew.

Can I race by myself?

Yes, but racing with others is more fun!

Is it scary?

It's fast but not extreme—most people who meet requirements enjoy it.

How long are waits?

Typically shorter than tube slides since multiple people race at once.
