Special Accommodations

Special Accommodations

  • Gluten‑free and vegetarian options available on request.
  • Allergy protocols follow Royal Caribbean SAFE Food Policy; notify staff of any allergens before ordering.
  • Children’s selections are available on request.

For severe allergies, contact Royal Caribbean’s accessibility team at least 30 days prior to sailing and confirm with the maître d’ on embarkation day.
