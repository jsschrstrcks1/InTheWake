CATS

Royal Caribbean — Broadway Musical

Quick Answer: Andrew Lloyd Webber's legendary musical comes to sea in a bespoke production created specifically for Royal Caribbean. The Jellicle Cats make their annual choice at the Jellicle Ball, performed with full costumes, makeup, and the iconic Memory sequence.

Best For: Broadway fans, theater lovers, families, date nights at sea, anyone who enjoys live musicals.

Key Facts

  • Type: Broadway Musical
  • Venue: Royal Theater
  • Cost: Complimentary (reservation recommended)
  • Duration: Approximately 75 minutes
  • Ships: Oasis of the Seas
  • Premiered: 2019

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CATS free?

Yes, CATS is complimentary and included in your cruise fare. No additional charge.

Do I need reservations for CATS?

Reservations are strongly recommended for Broadway musicals — book through the Royal Caribbean app or Cruise Planner before sailing.

Which ships have CATS?

CATS is currently available on: Oasis of the Seas. Show lineups can change, so verify with Royal Caribbean before sailing.

How long is CATS?

CATS runs approximately 75 minutes. There is no intermission in the at-sea version.

Is CATS appropriate for children?

CATS is family-friendly, though younger children (under 5) may have difficulty sitting through the full performance.

Where should I sit for the best view?

Center seats in the middle rows offer the best sightlines. The balcony level provides a good overview of choreography and staging. Arrive 15-20 minutes early.
