Accessibility & Inclusion Commitment

In the Wake exists to ensure that every traveler — regardless of ability, age, or circumstance — can explore the wonder of cruising without barriers. We believe accessibility is not simply a regulation to meet, but an act of love and respect.

Ensuring Access for Every Voyager

From visual clarity to cognitive simplicity, every choice we make aims to serve travelers of every ability.

Provider Spotlight: Autism on the Seas

We’re proud to recommend Autism on the Seas, which partners with major cruise lines to provide dedicated services for travelers with autism and other cognitive or developmental disabilities.
