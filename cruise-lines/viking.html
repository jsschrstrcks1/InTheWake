Viking

Quiet luxury, destination immersion, and Nordic calm. No kids. No casinos.

Why Viking

Viking keeps the spotlight on the destination: resident historians, chamber music, and unhurried days carried by clean Scandinavian design. Ships feel like boutique hotels at sea — serene spaces that frame the view rather than compete with it.

Beer & wine with meals, Wi-Fi, self-service laundry, and select excursions are included. No casinos, no kids, and no constant upsell — just thoughtful service and time to linger ashore. Cabins are limited and sell early.

Ship Classes

Ocean Ships launched 2015

All-veranda ocean ships designed around light and views. Hallmarks include the Wintergarden, Explorers’ Lounge, a full thermal suite, and destination-focused enrichment with lectures and classical music.

  • Viking Sky

Longships (River) launched 2012

Efficient river ships with airy lounges and the Aquavit Terrace, built for scenery and culture. Think included tours, regional menus, and unhurried days that maximize time in port.

  • Viking Egil
