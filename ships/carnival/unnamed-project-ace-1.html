Deck Plans

Project Ace represents Carnival's next-generation large ship platform, expected to enter service around 2026-2027. Following the Excel Class success, the Project Ace ships are expected to deliver expanded dining concepts, refined waterpark infrastructure, enhanced entertainment technology, and improved cabin design efficiency. Carnival Cruise Line has positioned these ships as the evolution of its most successful large-ship formula, building on the operational learnings from Mardi Gras, Carnival Celebration, and Carnival Jubilee to deliver a refined and expanded experience for families and all traveler types. The Project Ace class is expected to maintain Carnival's value-oriented pricing model while delivering meaningful improvements across all guest touchpoints from embarkation to disembarkation.

Deck plans for Project Ace 1 will be published closer to the ship's delivery date.
