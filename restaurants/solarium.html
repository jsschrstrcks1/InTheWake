Solarium

A tranquil escape for guests 16 and older

Age 16+
Setting Indoor/Outdoor
Features Pool + Hot Tubs
Price Complimentary

Images: Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA)

Quick Answer: The Solarium is Royal Caribbean's complimentary adults-only (16+) retreat featuring a pool, hot tubs, and loungers in a serene glass-enclosed setting. Located forward on most ships with beautiful ocean views. Best for: relaxation away from family pool crowds, morning coffee, afternoon reading, and peaceful hot tub soaks. Includes Solarium Bistro for lighter fare on many ships.

Overview

The Solarium represents Royal Caribbean's commitment to providing space for every type of cruiser. This adults-only retreat offers a peaceful alternative to the main pool deck, where the atmosphere tends to be more lively and family-oriented.

Most Solariums feature a stunning glass-roofed design that provides natural light and ocean views while offering protection from weather and wind. On pleasant days, the roof may open for an al fresco experience. The combination of enclosed comfort and outdoor beauty makes the Solarium special.

What You'll Find

  • Swimming Pool: Temperature-controlled pool, typically smaller than main pool
  • Hot Tubs: Usually 2-4 whirlpools with jets
  • Loungers: Cushioned lounge chairs for sunbathing and relaxation
  • Solarium Bar: Poolside bar service for drinks
  • Solarium Bistro: Complimentary light-fare dining (many ships)

Ship Availability

The Solarium is available on most Royal Caribbean ships:

  • Icon Class: Two-deck Solarium with infinity edge
  • Oasis Class: Forward Solarium with cantilevered hot tubs
  • Quantum Class: Multi-level Solarium with bistro
  • Freedom Class: Classic Solarium design
  • Voyager Class: Glass-enclosed retreat

Features & Amenities

The Solarium offers a complete relaxation experience:

Pool & Hot Tubs

  • Solarium Pool Complimentary

    Temperature-controlled pool under the glass canopy. Perfect for leisurely swimming without splashing children.

  • Whirlpools Complimentary

    Multiple hot tubs with powerful jets. Some ships feature cantilevered tubs extending over the ocean.

Solarium Bistro (Where Available)

  • Breakfast Complimentary

    Fresh fruit, yogurt parfaits, omelets made-to-order, lighter options than Windjammer.

  • Lunch Complimentary

    Salads, grilled fish, healthy sandwiches, fresh soups. Tranquil alternative to buffet crowds.

Bar Service

  • Solarium Bar À la carte / Pkg

    Full bar service with cocktails, frozen drinks, beer, and wine. Drink package accepted.

* Pool and bistro are complimentary. Drinks from the bar are additional unless on drink package.

Tips for Enjoying the Solarium

Best Times to Visit

  • Early morning: Nearly empty before 9am—perfect for sunrise coffee
  • Port days: Most guests are ashore; you'll have loungers to spare
  • Late afternoon: Crowds thin as people prepare for dinner
  • After dinner: Quiet evening soaks under the stars

Seat Saving

  • Seat saving is officially discouraged but happens on sea days
  • Arrive early on sea days if you want prime spots
  • Staff may remove items from chairs left unattended too long
  • The shaded areas under the glass roof fill first

What to Bring

  • Book or e-reader—it's quiet enough to actually read!
  • Sunscreen even if overcast (glass magnifies UV)
  • Cover-up for Bistro if using—no swimwear requirement but some prefer
  • SeaPass card for bar service

Age Enforcement

Staff actively enforce the 16+ policy. They may ask for SeaPass or ID, especially if someone appears young. This ensures the tranquil atmosphere is maintained for all adult guests.

Hours & Access

Operating Hours

  • Pool & Hot Tubs: 6:00 AM - 12:00 AM (varies by ship)
  • Solarium Bistro: Breakfast 7:30-11:00 AM, Lunch 12:00-2:30 PM
  • Bar Service: 10:00 AM - closing

Access

No reservation or fee required. Simply show up and find a lounger. All guests 16+ welcome.

Policies

  • Age requirement: 16 years and older only
  • Attire: Swimwear in pool area; cover-ups appreciated in Bistro
  • Glass: No glass containers in pool area
  • Music: Personal devices with headphones only—no speakers
  • Towels: Pick up poolside towels at the towel station

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is required for the Solarium?

The Solarium is for adults ages 16 and older only. Staff actively enforce this policy to maintain a peaceful atmosphere and may ask to see identification or SeaPass card.

Is the Solarium free on Royal Caribbean?

Yes, the Solarium pool, hot tubs, loungers, and Solarium Bistro are all complimentary for guests 16+. Only drinks from the bar are additional (or included with drink package).

Is the Solarium indoor or outdoor?

Most Solariums feature a glass-enclosed or retractable roof design. This provides climate control and protection from wind while maintaining ocean views. On nice days, sections may open for fresh air.

What is Solarium Bistro?

Solarium Bistro is a complimentary light-fare restaurant located in or near the Solarium on many ships. It offers healthier breakfast and lunch options in a tranquil, adults-only setting—a peaceful alternative to the crowded Windjammer buffet.

Is the Solarium heated?

The pool and hot tubs are temperature-controlled. The enclosed design also helps maintain comfortable air temperature regardless of outside weather conditions.

Can I eat in the Solarium in my swimsuit?

Yes, at Solarium Bistro swimwear is generally acceptable since it's right next to the pool. However, you may wish to use a cover-up out of personal preference. Shoes or sandals are recommended.
