Ship Classes

Icon Class debut 2024

Royal’s “world’s best family vacation” era: waterpark neighborhood, seven pools, and a show slate that spans air, ice, water, and stage. Max choice with smarter crowd flow and calm pockets for date night.

Premium venues (varies): Empire Supper Club, 150 Central Park, Wonderland, Chops, Izumi/Teppanyaki, Hooked, Giovanni’s, Chef’s Table.

Oasis Class neighborhood megaships

The original neighborhood revolution: Central Park, Boardwalk, and AquaTheater. Broadway-scale shows meet resort pool decks and a deep specialty bench.

Premium venues (varies): 150 Central Park, Wonderland, Chops, Giovanni’s, Izumi/Teppanyaki, Hooked, The Mason Jar, Portside BBQ, Chef’s Table.

Freedom Class

Sweet-spot scale—FlowRider surf, sports decks, comedy/live music, and a flexible dining mix for varied groups.

Premium venues (often): Chops, Giovanni’s, Izumi, Chef’s Table, Johnny Rockets.

Voyager Class

Promenade parades and ice rinks made “do-it-all” days easy. Compact layouts with big-ship staples for short or port-heavy runs.

Premium venues (often): Chops, Giovanni’s, Izumi, Chef’s Table, Johnny Rockets.

Radiance Class

Glass-and-views ships made for scenery: Alaska, fjords, and coastals. Floor-to-ceiling windows and calmer pace.

Premium venues (typical): Chops, Izumi, Chef’s Table.

Vision Class

Cozy classics with atrium life and itinerary-first charm. Smaller-ship warmth, personable crews, and simplified logistics.

Premium venues (select): Chops, Chef’s Table.
