Virgin Voyages

Quick Answer: Rebel against ordinary vacations. Picture yourself sipping a perfectly crafted cocktail at The Dock — Virgin's floating social hub — as a DJ spins sunset vibes and the ocean breeze carries the scent of Korean BBQ from Gunbae. Virgin Voyages isn't just adults-only; it's a full-throttle celebration of culinary discovery, electric nightlife, and the freedom to experience every meal, every moment, exactly as you choose.

Is Virgin Voyages Right for You?

Virgin Voyages invites travelers who believe vacation should feel like the best night out of your life, every single day. Lady Class ships feature 20+ eateries — all included, no upcharges — from the theatrical flames of Gunbae Korean BBQ to the intimate Italian perfection of Extra Virgin, from the Mexican street food vibes of Pink Agave to steakhouse elegance at The Wake. No main dining room. No assigned seating. Just freedom to graze, discover, and feast.

Shows run late because the best parties always do. The Manor hosts immersive performances until 2 AM. Scarlet Night transforms the ship into a crimson-hued celebration you'll remember forever. The Athletic Club lets you sweat out last night's adventures. And The Perch — a treehouse-style escape — offers unexpected quiet when you need it.

Perfect for couples seeking romantic adventure, friend groups wanting unforgettable experiences, and solo travelers drawn to social energy. Virgin's adults-only policy (18+) ensures the vibe stays vibrant and sophisticated. This is cruising redefined for people who don't do ordinary.

Key Facts at a Glance

  • Brand Identity: "Rebel against ordinary" — modern, social, adults-only celebration
  • Guest Policy: Adults-only (18+), no kids, no casinos — just pure vibe
  • Dining: All 20+ restaurants included — no main dining room, no upcharges, chef-forward venues
  • Best For: Foodies, nightlife lovers, design enthusiasts, social travelers, couples, friend groups
  • Signature Experiences: Scarlet Night party, The Manor late-night shows, Gunbae Korean BBQ, The Athletic Club

The Virgin Experience

From the moment you board, Virgin wraps you in an atmosphere of joyful rebellion. The design is bold — think Richard Branson meets Studio 54 meets boutique hotel — with public spaces that flow from energetic to intimate depending on where you wander. The Dock serves as social headquarters; Sip offers champagne in a hammock; and The Perch provides unexpected treehouse-style escape.

Dining is the revolution. Pink Agave serves elevated Mexican with a mezcal bar that rivals any land-based restaurant. Gunbae turns Korean BBQ into theatrical performance. Extra Virgin delivers authentic Italian from imported ingredients. Razzle Dazzle offers plant-forward brunch with drag queen hosts. And it's all included — no bills, no tipping, no surprises.

What cruisers say: "Scarlet Night was absolutely wild — dancing until 2 AM with amazing people we'd just met, and everything was included!"

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Virgin Voyages unique?

Virgin Voyages is adults-only (18+) with a restaurant-first philosophy—all dining is included with no traditional main dining room. Expect contemporary design, chef-forward eateries, festival-style entertainment, and late-night programming that makes sea days feel like city nights.

What dining is included on Virgin Voyages?

All restaurants are included on Virgin Voyages—no upcharges, no main dining room. Choose from multiple chef-driven venues ranging from Korean BBQ to Italian, Mexican, steakhouse, and more. The focus is on grazing and discovery, not assigned seating.

Are Virgin Voyages cruises adults-only?

Yes. Virgin Voyages is exclusively for adults 18 and older. The atmosphere is social, upbeat, and designed for travelers who want nightlife, chef-forward dining, and modern energy without kids' clubs or family programming.

What is the entertainment like on Virgin Voyages?

Virgin Voyages features festival-style entertainment with late-night shows, live music, immersive performances, and upbeat programming. The energy extends well past sunset, creating a vibrant social scene that feels more urban than traditional cruise ship.
