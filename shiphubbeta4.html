Will it feel crowded?

Gross tonnage is the headline number, but it's the wrong one for how a ship actually feels. The number that predicts crowding is space ratio — gross tonnage divided by passengers at double occupancy. Higher means more shared room per person. Bigger does not mean roomier: Icon of the Seas dwarfs Radiance of the Seas by tonnage, yet they sit within a few percent of each other per passenger.
