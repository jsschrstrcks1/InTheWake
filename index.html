Welcome Aboard

In the Wake is a practical cruise companion built from real sailings. Use it to compare ships, scan restaurant menus, and map ports—fast. Each page follows the same, clean layout so you can jump from a ship to its dining and back again without losing your bearings. We favor family‑friendly sources, surface accessibility notes wherever possible, and mark anything uncertain as To Verify so you’re never surprised onboard. Prices change and itineraries shift, so treat numbers as snapshots in time and lean on cross‑links to double‑check the latest details. Whether you’re picking a cabin, planning a sea day, or deciding if a specialty venue is worth it, the goal is simple: empower you to make confident choices—and feel like it was your idea all along. Fair winds and following seas.
