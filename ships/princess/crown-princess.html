Crown Princess — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

Crown Princess is a Grand Class cruise ship operated by Princess Cruises. She entered service in 2006, measures 113,561 gross tons, and carries approximately 3,082 guests at double occupancy.

Key Facts

  • Class: Grand Class
  • Year: 2006
  • Tonnage: 113,561 GT
  • Guests: 3,082
  • IMO: 9293399

Quick Answer: Crown Princess (113,561 GT, 3,082 guests, 2006) is a Grand Class ship — part of Princess's reliable mid-fleet with MedallionClass technology, Movies Under the Stars, and the Piazza atrium. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Best For: Value cruisers who want Princess's classic experience on a proven ship — MedallionClass tech at heritage-ship pricing with diverse itineraries.

Crown Princess is part of the Grand Class quartet that forms Princess's mid-fleet backbone. She shares her platform with Emerald Princess and Ruby Princess. MedallionClass technology provides touchless boarding and on-demand services. Movies Under the Stars remains a signature Princess amenity. If you want a newer Princess ship, the Royal Class has Sky Suites and more dining; for the newest, Sun Princess and Star Princess are the Sphere Class flagships.
