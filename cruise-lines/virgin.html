Adults Only. All Dining Included. Go Virgin.

Virgin Voyages is unapologetically adult—no kids, no buffets—just chef-forward eateries, late-night energy, and shows that keep the ship humming long after sunset. Dining is included, and the vibe is as social as it is stylish.

Sailors love the freedom to graze, linger, and discover. If you’re after modern design, strong coffee, and nightlife with a wink, this fleet delivers. Cabins can go quickly on popular sailings.

Ship Classes

Lady Class launched 2020+

Virgin’s Lady Class ships lean into contemporary lines, social spaces, and a restaurant-first philosophy—more eateries, no main dining room, and a culinary roster that rewards curiosity.

Expect upbeat public rooms, festival-style entertainment, and late-night programming that makes sea days feel like city nights.
