Poolside Movies

Royal Caribbean — Outdoor Cinema

Quick Answer: Poolside Movies brings the outdoor cinema experience to sea. Watch films on giant LED screens by the pool deck, lounging under the stars with popcorn and drinks. Screenings include recent releases, family favorites, and classics. Completely complimentary.

Best For: Families with kids. Couples wanting a casual evening. Movie lovers. Anyone who enjoys outdoor cinema experiences.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Screen: Giant LED poolside screen
  • When: Evening screenings (after sunset)
  • Movies: Mix of recent and classic films
  • Ships: Most Royal Caribbean ships

Movies under the stars at sea.

Few things are more relaxing than watching a movie outdoors by the pool with the ocean breeze. Poolside Movies offers exactly that—a giant LED screen shows films after dark while you lounge in a deck chair. Popcorn is often available, and you can grab drinks from nearby bars. Frequently Asked Questions

Are poolside movies free?

Yes, screenings and often popcorn are complimentary.

What movies do they show?

A mix of recent releases, family films, comedies, and classics. Check your Cruise Compass.

What time are movies?

Usually after sunset, typically 8-9 PM or later.

Can I swim during the movie?

Yes! The pool is usually open during screenings.

What if it rains?

Outdoor screenings may be canceled in inclement weather.

Is sound good from the pool?

Speakers are positioned around the deck area—sound is generally good throughout.
