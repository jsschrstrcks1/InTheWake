Enchantment of the Seas

Enchantment’s dramatic mid‑body stretch added airy deck space and glass‑lined lounges, making her the peppiest of the Vision quartet.

She excels at short, sun‑forward getaways where the ship is your bright, easy home base between ports.

Guests praise her friendly crew and just‑right size: big enough for choice, small enough to find your people by day two.

Evenings feel classic — music in the Centrum, al fresco strolls on teak, a sunset drink that doesn’t require a reservation.

Choose Enchantment when you want a lively, compact Royal Caribbean experience with plenty of ocean in view.
