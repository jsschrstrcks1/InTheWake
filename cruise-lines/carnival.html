Carnival Cruise Line

Quick Answer: Carnival's "Fun Ships" deliver casual, high-energy cruising with WaterWorks water parks, Guy's Burgers, BlueIguana Cantina, and value pricing that makes cruising accessible to families. Known for party vibes, big laughs, and endless entertainment from sea to shore.

Is Carnival Right for You?

Carnival invites cruisers who want fun-first experiences without formal restrictions. These ships bring the party with WaterWorks water parks, casual dining like Guy's Burgers, and high-energy entertainment including comedy clubs, deck parties, and live music.

Perfect for families seeking value, groups looking for nonstop fun, and first-time cruisers who want an accessible entry point to cruising. Carnival's relaxed dress code and inclusive pricing make it easy to say yes to vacation. Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Carnival unique among cruise lines?

Carnival is known as "The Fun Ships" with a casual, high-energy atmosphere. Signature features include WaterWorks water parks, Guy's Burgers, BlueIguana Cantina, and value-focused pricing that makes cruising accessible to families.

What dining options does Carnival offer?

Carnival ships feature complimentary dining including the main dining room, buffet, Guy's Burgers, BlueIguana Cantina, and pizza. Specialty dining options vary by ship.

Is Carnival good for families?

Yes! Carnival offers extensive family-friendly features including WaterWorks water parks, Camp Ocean kids' clubs, family staterooms, and casual dining that appeals to all ages.

What is Carnival's dress code?

Carnival maintains a relaxed, casual dress code. Most sailings include one or two "Cruise Elegant" evenings where guests can dress up if they choose, but casual attire is always accepted in most venues.
