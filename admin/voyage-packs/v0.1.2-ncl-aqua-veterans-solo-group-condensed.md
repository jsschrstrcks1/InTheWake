## Getting to the ship — and getting home

### Arrival: Saturday recommended, Sunday required

**Saturday Dec 11 — fly in.** December weather is generally good in Central Florida, but Sunday-morning flights to a 3:30 PM all-aboard leave no margin for delays. Pre-cruise hotel near Orlando MCO (cheaper) or in Cocoa Beach (closer to the port) gives breathing room.

**Sunday Dec 12 — embarkation.**

- **Terminal:** **Port Canaveral, Cruise Terminal 10** (NCL's regular berth — verify).
- **MCO → Port Canaveral:** Uber/Lyft $60–80, 45–60 min. Shared cruise shuttle ~$30/person, slower.
- **If driving:** Port Canaveral garage ~$17–22/day (cheaper than PortMiami); reservable in advance.
- **Embark window:** 12:00 – 3:30 PM. **All-aboard 3:00 PM.** Ship departs 4:00 PM.
- **NCL app online check-in** — complete before arrival.

**Cocoa Beach overnight option.** If you'd rather be 15 minutes from the port than 45, a Cocoa Beach hotel Saturday night puts you walking distance to the beach Sunday morning before boarding.

### Day 8 debark and the trip home

- **The night before:** bags in the hall by ~11 PM with color-coded tag.
- **5:30 – 7:00 AM:** Breakfast at Indulge Food Hall.
- **6:30 AM:** Customs clearance complete. Self-disembarkation begins.
- **7:00 – 9:30 AM:** Walk-off in waves. All passengers off by 10:00 AM.

**Carry-on with you:** change of clothes, all medications, passport, phone charger, valuables.

**Getting from Port Canaveral to MCO.** Uber/Lyft $60–80, 45–60 min. Cocoa Beach Shuttle (~$30/person, advance booking) is the formal alternative.

**Killing 4–6 hours before an evening flight:**

- **Cocoa Beach pier** — 15-min Uber. Beach walk + casual lunch.
- **Kennedy Space Center** — 20-min Uber, $75 entry. Half-day minimum.
- **Cocoa Village** — historic small-town main street; lunch + shops.

**Airport-arrival timing for MCO (45+ minutes away):**

| Flight | Leave Port Canaveral by |
|---|---|
| Domestic, after 4 PM | 12:30 PM |
| International, after 6 PM | 2:00 PM |

## Areas of interest during the cruise

### On the ship — Aqua spots worth knowing

- **The Penrose Atrium** — three-deck central gathering space; orienting landmark.
- **Ocean Boulevard** — outdoor wraparound walkway Deck 8. Sunrise coffee, sailaway viewing.
- **Indulge Food Hall** — included quick-casual with multiple stations.
- **Aqua Slidecoaster** — the signature attraction. First hour after opening has no line.
- **Studio Lounge** (Deck 11) — solo cabins perk. Hosted coffee hour Day 2 morning typically.
- **Mandara Spa thermal-suite day pass** — sea-day intro rates.
- **The Haven** (private suite enclave) — if you booked Haven, the private restaurant, lounge, and pool deck are the point.
- **Cagney's Steakhouse** — the included-line steakhouse standard.

### Sea-day priorities (Day 2 + Day 4)

1. **Sunrise on Ocean Boulevard** — best 30 minutes of the week.
2. **Studio Lounge meet-and-greet** Day 2 — NCL hosts this; check the daily program.
3. **Aqua Slidecoaster** first hour after opening.
4. **Thermal suite at Mandara Spa** — sea-day intro rate.
5. **Specialty dining reservation** for Day 4 evening — Cagney's, Le Bistro, Palomar, Onda by Scarpetta all fill.

### Specialty dining picks

- **Cagney's Steakhouse** — the steakhouse celebration meal.
- **Le Bistro** — French; the most romantic specialty room.
- **Onda by Scarpetta** — modern Italian, Scott Conant partnership.
- **Palomar** — Mediterranean-Latin fusion.
- **Sukhothai** + **Hasuki** — Asian; teppanyaki is the hibachi room.

### Port-day picks (one each)

- **Great Stirrup Cay** — NCL's private island under redevelopment. Tender required. Beach + buffet day.
- **Jamaica** (Ocho Rios / Falmouth / Montego Bay) — Dunn's River Falls climb (~$30 indep, $60 ship) is the iconic. Or stay at the pier complex (Island Village) for the easy day. Mystic Mountain bobsled + zipline (~$120) is the splurge.
- **Grand Cayman** — Stingray City sandbar (~$60 indep) is the highest-value half-day. Or 7-Mile Beach (~$5 #1 bus) for the easy day.
- **Cozumel** — Palancar Reef snorkel (~$50 indep / ~$95 ship). The trip that converts non-snorkelers. *The* Western Caribbean dive.

### Where Tina sets the rhythm

- **Day 1 sailaway** — Penrose Bar typically; verify Tina's communications.
- **Group dinners** — likely Hudson's or one of the included MDRs; show up when you want.
- **Studio Lounge** — solo cruisers cluster regardless of who's hosting.

### Photo spots worth knowing

- **Sailaway from Ocean Boulevard Deck 8** — Cape Canaveral coastline at golden hour.
- **The Penrose Atrium** at evening — three-deck chandelier moment.
- **Dunn's River Falls climb** — bring an underwater camera; the climb gets wet.
- **Palancar Reef** from the snorkel boat — underwater shots worth saving.
