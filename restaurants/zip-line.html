Zip Line

Soar nine decks above the Boardwalk

Length 82 feet
Height 9 decks up
Weight 75-275 lbs
Price Complimentary

Images: Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA)

Quick Answer: The zip line is a free thrill ride on Oasis and Icon Class ships, launching from Deck 16 and soaring 82 feet across the Boardwalk neighborhood. No reservation needed—just walk up during operating hours, meet the weight requirements (75-275 lbs), and fly. Best times: early morning or during dinner for shorter waits.

Overview

Spanning the open-air Boardwalk neighborhood, Royal Caribbean's zip line delivers an exhilarating rush as you soar nine decks above sea level. The 82-foot journey takes you from a launch platform on Deck 16 to a landing zone on Deck 15, passing directly over the vintage carousel, Johnny Rockets, and other Boardwalk attractions below.

What makes this zip line unique is the setting—you're not zipping through a forest or over a canyon, but across a bustling ship neighborhood with the ocean stretching endlessly beyond. Spectators below often cheer as riders zoom overhead, adding to the excitement.

The Experience

After climbing to the launch platform and getting harnessed in, you'll step up to the edge and wait for your signal. Then you launch—gravity takes over as you accelerate across the Boardwalk. The ride lasts only about 10-15 seconds, but the combination of height, speed, and ocean views makes every moment count.

Two Parallel Lines

Oasis Class ships feature two parallel zip lines, allowing friends or family to race side by side. This dual setup also helps reduce wait times as two riders can go simultaneously.

The Ride Experience

Here's what to expect when you zip:

Your Zip Line Journey

  • Check-In & Waiver ~5 min

    Sign the waiver (once per cruise) and step on the scale to verify you meet weight requirements.

  • Climb to Launch Platform ~2 min

    Take the stairs up to Deck 16 launch platform. Anticipation builds as you see the Boardwalk far below.

  • Gear Up ~3 min

    Staff fit you with a harness and attach you to the zip line cable. All gear is checked twice for safety.

  • The Zip! ~15 sec

    Step off the platform and soar 82 feet across the Boardwalk. Take in the views—it goes fast!

  • Landing & Exit ~2 min

    Staff help you dismount on Deck 15. Exit through the Boardwalk—grab a Johnny Rockets milkshake to celebrate!

* Total experience typically takes 15-30 minutes including any wait time. Ride multiple times at no extra cost!

Tips for the Best Experience

Best Times to Go

  • Opening time: First riders have the shortest waits
  • During dinner: Most guests are eating; walk right up
  • Port days: Majority of passengers ashore
  • Night zipping: If operating after dark, the lit-up Boardwalk below is spectacular

What to Wear

  • Shoes: Closed-toe shoes required—no sandals or flip-flops
  • Clothing: Comfortable clothes that won't ride up
  • Secure everything: No loose items (hats, sunglasses, phones)—lockers available
  • Hair: Tie back long hair

Photography Tips

  • Ship photographers are often stationed to capture your flight
  • Have travel companions wait at the Boardwalk to photograph from below
  • GoPros and secure camera mounts may be permitted—ask staff
  • Photos available for purchase at the photo gallery

Race Your Friends

With two parallel lines, you can zip side-by-side with a friend or family member. The lines are identical in length, so it's a fair race—though results mostly depend on body weight and air resistance!

Availability & Requirements

Operating Hours

  • Sea Days: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM typical (weather permitting)
  • Port Days: May have extended hours; check Cruise Compass
  • Evening: Sometimes operates after dark on select nights

No Reservation Needed

Walk up during operating hours. Sign the waiver (once per cruise), verify weight, get harnessed, and zip. Multiple rides welcome throughout your cruise at no additional cost.

Requirements

  • Minimum weight: 75 lbs (34 kg)
  • Maximum weight: 275 lbs (125 kg)
  • Age: No minimum age if weight requirements met
  • Footwear: Closed-toe shoes required
  • Waiver: Required for all participants
  • Health: Not recommended for pregnant guests or those with heart conditions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the zip line free on Royal Caribbean?

Yes, the zip line is completely complimentary and included with your cruise fare. You can ride as many times as you want throughout your cruise at no extra charge.

How long is the Royal Caribbean zip line?

The zip line spans approximately 82 feet (25 meters) across the Boardwalk neighborhood, launching from Deck 16 and landing on Deck 15.

What is the age and weight limit for the zip line?

Riders must weigh between 75-275 lbs (34-125 kg). There's no minimum age as long as the child meets the weight requirement and can be properly fitted in the harness.

Which Royal Caribbean ships have a zip line?

The zip line is available on all Oasis Class ships (Oasis, Allure, Harmony, Symphony, and Wonder of the Seas) and Icon Class ships (Icon of the Seas).

Can I go on the zip line with someone else?

Yes! Oasis Class ships have two parallel zip lines, so you can ride side-by-side with a friend or family member. It's a popular way to share the experience.

Is it scary?

That depends on your comfort with heights! The launch platform is high above the Boardwalk and stepping off requires a leap of faith. Once you're zipping, most people find the fear transforms into exhilaration. Staff are supportive of nervous riders.

What happens if it's windy or raining?

The zip line may close during high winds, rain, or other inclement weather for safety. Check the daily Cruise Compass for operating hours and any weather-related closures.
