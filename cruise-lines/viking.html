Viking

Quick Answer: Viking delivers quiet luxury and destination immersion for adults 18+. Ocean and river ships offer Nordic calm, thoughtful design, and resident historians—plus included beer and wine at meals, Wi-Fi, laundry, and select shore excursions. No casinos, no kids, no upsell.

Is Viking Right for You?

Viking invites thoughtful travelers who seek cultural enrichment over high-energy entertainment. Ocean ships sail worldwide with all-veranda staterooms and ~930 guests; river Longships navigate Europe's historic waterways with ~190 guests and floor-to-ceiling views. Both prioritize destination over spectacle.

Perfect for couples and solo travelers drawn to lectures, classical music, regional cuisine, and unhurried discovery. Viking's adults-only policy (18+) ensures calm public spaces, and generous inclusions—beer & wine at meals, Wi-Fi, laundry—eliminate nickel-and-dime surprises. Staterooms sell early; book ahead for best availability.

Key Facts at a Glance

  • Brand Identity: Quiet luxury, destination immersion, Nordic design

Ship Classes

Ocean Ships launched 2015+

Viking’s 930-guest Ocean sisters embody quiet luxury at sea: all-veranda accommodations, warm woods, daylight-rich lounges, and design that defers to the view. The pace is unhurried; enrichment and place take center stage.

No casinos and no forced spectacle—just thoughtful venues like the Explorers’ Lounge, serene Wintergarden, and destination-driven dining. Life on board orients you to life ashore.

  • Viking Star
  • Viking Sea
  • Viking Sky

Longships (River) launched 2012+

Viking Longships bring ~190 guests into the historic hearts of Europe’s rivers. Floor-to-ceiling lounges, the Aquavit Terrace, and walk-off access keep you close to the water—and to the cultures that grew up upon it.

Inside you’ll find Scandinavian restraint: light, air, and textures that serve the story outside your window. Lectures, regional cuisine, and effortless steps ashore complete the picture.

  • Viking Bragi
  • Viking Egil

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Viking unique among cruise lines?

Viking offers adults-only (18+) ocean and river cruising focused on destination immersion and cultural enrichment. No casinos, no kids—just quiet luxury with resident historians, classical music, beer and wine at meals, Wi-Fi, laundry, and select shore excursions all included.

What is included on Viking cruises?

Viking includes beer and wine at lunch and dinner, Wi-Fi throughout the ship, laundry services, and one complimentary shore excursion in each port. Most sailings also feature resident historians and cultural programming.

Are Viking cruises adults-only?

Yes. Viking Ocean and River cruises are designed for adults 18 and older. The atmosphere emphasizes quiet sophistication, cultural enrichment, and destination-focused travel without the energy of family-oriented cruise lines.

What is the difference between Viking Ocean and River ships?

Viking Ocean ships carry ~930 guests on worldwide itineraries with all-veranda staterooms and enrichment programming. Viking Longships navigate European rivers with ~190 guests, floor-to-ceiling windows, and intimate access to historic port towns.
