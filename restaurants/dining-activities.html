Dining Activities & Classes

Quick Answer: Royal Caribbean offers rotating culinary workshops and specialty dining experiences across the fleet — Candy Sushi Making ($35), On a Roll Sushi Making ($40), Sprinkle Time Cupcake Decorating ($35), Taste of Royal lunch ($45), and Sushi & Sake lunch ($45). Pre-cruise discounts vary by sailing; classes often sell out, so book early.

How to Use This Guide

This guide details Royal Caribbean's hands-on dining activities and exclusive tasting experiences. Whether you're planning family fun with Candy Sushi or exploring chef-curated lunches, you'll find typical pricing, pre-cruise savings examples, and guest soundings to help you decide which activities to book.

Review each activity's description and pricing, note that offerings vary by ship and itinerary, then check your Cruise Planner for sailing-specific availability and discounts. Guest soundings provide real feedback on energy levels, portions, and what to expect.

Key Facts at a Glance

  • Activities Covered: 5 experiences (3 hands-on classes, 2 specialty lunches)
  • Typical Price Range: $35–$45 per person
  • Pre-Cruise Savings: Often $27.99–$30.99 (varies by sailing)
  • Availability: Not all activities offered on all ships or sailings
  • Booking Tip: Pre-book via Cruise Planner — classes sell out
  • Best For: Families, foodies, hands-on experiences, specialty tastings

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need reservations for Dining Activities & Classes?

Reservations are recommended for specialty dining. You can book through the cruise planner before sailing or onboard via the app or guest services.

What is the dress code for Dining Activities & Classes?

Dress codes vary by venue. Check the dress code section on this page for specific requirements. Most specialty restaurants request smart casual attire.

Is Dining Activities & Classes included in my cruise fare?

Complimentary vs. specialty pricing varies by venue. Check the pricing information on this page. Specialty restaurants typically have a cover charge or a la carte pricing.

Which ships have Dining Activities & Classes?

Availability varies by ship and class. Check the ships section on this page or the Royal Caribbean website for current fleet availability.
