Personal Website Facebook Instagram Photography Portfolio Photography Instagram Cruising in the Wake (Facebook) About In the Wake

Ken Baker founded In the Wake to create the planning resources he wished existed—tools that respect your time, clarify confusing policies, and help you make confident decisions. A pastor and photographer by trade, Ken brings both pastoral care and visual storytelling to cruise planning. With 50+ sailings under his belt (specializing in Royal Caribbean), he writes from experience about accessibility, solo travel, and the practical details that make or break a cruise.

His motto: "The calmest seas are found in another's wake." Ken sails ahead to smooth the waters for you.
