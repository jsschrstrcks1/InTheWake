Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Carnival Elation sail from?

Carnival Elation sails from Jacksonville, Florida, offering 4 and 5-day cruises to the Bahamas including Nassau and Half Moon Cay (Carnival's private island).

What makes Carnival Elation unique?

Carnival Elation made cruise history in 1998 as the first cruise ship equipped with AZIPOD propulsion (electric azimuth thrusters). This gives her superior maneuverability compared to her Fantasy Class sisters that use traditional shaft propellers.

How big is Carnival Elation?

Carnival Elation is 71,909 gross tons with a length of 855 feet (262m). She accommodates 2,190 guests and 900 crew members across 11 passenger decks.

What onboard features does Carnival Elation have?

Features include WaterWorks water park with twisting slides, Serenity adult-only retreat, Cloud 9 Spa, casino, duty-free shops, multiple pools, Guy's Burger Joint, and BlueIguana Cantina.

When was Carnival Elation last refurbished?

Carnival Elation underwent major refurbishments in October 2017 (adding an extra deck) and March 2020 (adding 30 new cabins, Guy's Burger Joint, BlueIguana Cantina, and other modern amenities).
