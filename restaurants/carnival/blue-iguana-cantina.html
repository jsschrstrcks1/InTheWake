Blue Iguana Cantina Review: Carnival's Poolside Taco Stand

Introduction. Blue Iguana Cantina is one of Carnival's most popular complimentary quick-service spots — a build-your-own taco and burrito bar near the pool deck. It punches well above what you'd expect from a free poolside counter, and the line at noon on a sea day tells you everything you need to know about its reputation.

Food & Drinks

The freshly made tortillas are the foundation — warm, soft flour or corn, griddled to order. The carne asada tacos are the crowd favorite: seasoned beef with a decent char, topped with pico de gallo, shredded cheese, and a squeeze of lime. The chicken burrito is generous and well-stuffed. The salsa bar has real range, from mild to a habanero option that actually has heat. Where it falls short: the fish taco can be hit or miss depending on timing — sometimes crispy and fresh, sometimes soggy from sitting. The rice and beans side is functional but forgettable. No drinks are served here beyond what you bring from the nearby bar — pair it with a frozen margarita from the BlueIguana Tequila Bar next door.

Service

Counter service, assembly-line style. The crew behind the line are efficient and genuinely cheerful — they'll customize your order and pile on extra toppings if you ask. The bottleneck isn't service, it's demand: on sea days between 11:30 a.m. and 1 p.m., the line wraps around the pool deck. Going at 11 a.m. or after 1:30 p.m. cuts your wait dramatically.

Atmosphere

Outdoor poolside seating — plastic chairs, ocean breeze, no pretense. This is bathing-suit dining with a paper plate and a cold drink. The vibe is casual in the best possible way. It's loud (pool music, kids, the occasional belly-flop contest nearby), and there's zero ambiance in the traditional sense. That's the point.

Conclusion

Rating: 3.9/5. Blue Iguana Cantina is one of the best complimentary quick-service venues in cruising. The fresh tortillas and carne asada tacos are legitimately good — not "good for a cruise ship," just good. It loses a tick for the fish taco inconsistency and the brutal sea-day lines. But for a free taco bar by the pool? This is hard to beat. Tip: go at 11 a.m. for zero line and the freshest batch of tortillas.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Blue Iguana Cantina on Carnival?

Complimentary poolside Mexican cantina serving tacos (chicken, pork, fish) and build-your-own burritos at lunch. Breakfast features arepas and huevos rancheros.

How much does Blue Iguana Cantina cost?

Blue Iguana Cantina is complimentary — included in your Carnival cruise fare at no extra charge.

What is the dress code for Blue Iguana Cantina?

Come as you are. Blue Iguana Cantina is a casual venue — pool attire and casual clothes are fine. Carnival has Cruise Casual and Cruise Elegant nights.

Do I need reservations for Blue Iguana Cantina?

No reservations needed. Blue Iguana Cantina is walk-up service — just show up and enjoy.

What are the menu highlights at Blue Iguana Cantina?

Popular items include Chipotle Rubbed Chicken Taco, Ancho Roast Pork Taco, Seasoned Battered Fish Taco, Step 1 Tortilla: Wheat or Jalapeño, and more. The menu may vary by ship and sailing.
