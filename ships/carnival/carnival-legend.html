Carnival Legend

The Wedding Chapel Pioneer — Dame Judi Dench's Godchild

Active Spirit Class 85,942 GT 2,124 Guests Baltimore/Tampa

Carnival Legend is a Spirit Class cruise ship operated by Carnival Cruise Line. She entered service in 2002, measures 86,000 gross tons, and carries approximately 2,124 guests at double occupancy.

Quick Answer: Carnival Legend (86,000 GT | 2,124 guests | 2002) is a Spirit Class ship with a mythological theme — one of Carnival's more intimate vessels offering seasonal Alaska and Europe itineraries. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Best For: Cruisers who want Carnival's Fun Ship personality on more exotic itineraries — Alaska glaciers and European ports on an intimate Spirit-class ship.

Carnival Legend combines Spirit-class intimacy with worldwide deployment. Her mythological interior theme and smaller guest count offer a different Carnival experience from the mega-ships.

Ship Overview

Carnival Legend made history in 2002 as the first Carnival Fun Ship with an onboard wedding chapel. Christened by acclaimed British actress Dame Judi Dench in Harwich, England, she offers intimate Spirit-class cruising with classic Fun Ship amenities.

Legend operates seasonally from Baltimore and Tampa, offering Caribbean, Bahamas, and Bermuda itineraries. With 80% of staterooms featuring ocean views, she's ideal for scenic cruising.

Frequently Asked Questions About Carnival Legend

What are the dining options on Carnival Legend?

Carnival Legend features Truffles Restaurant main dining room with Your Time Dining flexibility. Complimentary casual options include Guy's Burger Joint, BlueIguana Cantina for fresh tacos and burritos, and Pizzeria del Capitano for 24-hour pizza. Specialty restaurants include Fahrenheit 555 steakhouse, Bonsai Sushi, JiJi Asian Kitchen, and Cucina del Capitano Italian dining.

Where does Carnival Legend sail from?

Carnival Legend operates seasonally from Baltimore, Maryland and Tampa, Florida. From Baltimore, she offers Caribbean, Bahamas, and Bermuda cruises. Tampa sailings focus on Western Caribbean destinations. The seasonal rotation allows East Coast cruisers convenient departure options.

Does Carnival Legend have a wedding chapel?

Yes! Carnival Legend made history as the first Carnival Fun Ship with an onboard wedding chapel when she debuted in 2002. This feature makes her particularly appealing for destination weddings and vow renewals at sea. Dame Judi Dench served as godmother at the christening in Harwich, England.
