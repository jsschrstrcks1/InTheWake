Chill Island

Royal Caribbean — Ship Neighborhood

Quick Answer: Chill Island is Icon Class's relaxation-focused neighborhood featuring an infinity pool that appears to blend with the ocean, a swim-up bar, private cabanas for rent, and a peaceful atmosphere. Designed for guests seeking a quieter pool experience away from the main activity areas and kids' zones.

Best For: Adults seeking relaxation, couples on romantic getaways, anyone wanting peaceful pool time, and guests who prefer a laid-back atmosphere.

Key Facts

  • Location: Icon Class ships only
  • Pool: Infinity pool with ocean views
  • Bar: Swim-up bar included
  • Cabanas: Available for rent
  • Cost: Pool free; cabanas extra

Your peaceful retreat at sea.

While Icon of the Seas is known for its thrilling activities, Chill Island offers the opposite—a serene escape where the main attractions are sunshine, a stunning infinity pool, and cocktails from the swim-up bar. Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: Chill Island pool area is wheelchair accessible. Pool entry may require assistance—lifts available on request. Cabanas can accommodate wheelchairs. Staff can assist with accessibility needs.

Where You'll Find It

Chill Island occupies its own section of Icon Class ships, positioned away from the busier Surfside and Thrill Island areas. The infinity pool faces the ocean for optimal views.

Best Times to Visit

  • Morning: Quietest time, perfect for peaceful swims
  • Afternoon: More activity but still calmer than main pools
  • Sunset: Spectacular views from infinity pool edge
  • Sea Days: More guests but still relaxed atmosphere

Chill Island Review: Our Daily Escape

Introduction. My husband and I booked Icon specifically for the thrill activities, but we found ourselves at Chill Island every single morning. It became our peaceful ritual before the chaos of the waterslides.

The Infinity Pool

The infinity edge is stunning. Swimming up to the edge where the water seems to merge with the Caribbean horizon is genuinely beautiful. The pool is heated perfectly, and the in-water loungers let you soak while watching the ocean.

The Swim-Up Bar

Having a bartender hand you a cocktail without leaving the water is cruise luxury at its finest. Our drink package covered everything. We'd spend hours floating with drinks in hand.

The Atmosphere

Noticeably quieter than the main pool area. Not silent—you're still on a cruise ship—but kids naturally gravitate elsewhere, leaving Chill Island to adults seeking relaxation. Exactly what we wanted.

Conclusion

If you're sailing Icon Class and want a balance of thrills and tranquility, Chill Island delivers on the relaxation side. We didn't rent a cabana, but next time we definitely will. Rating: 4.5/5.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chill Island free to use?

Yes, the pool and general area are complimentary. Only cabana rentals and drinks (unless on a drink package) cost extra.

Is Chill Island adults-only?

Not officially, but the atmosphere naturally attracts adults seeking quiet. Families with young children typically prefer Surfside.

How much are cabanas?

Cabana prices vary from $200-500+ per day depending on the sailing date, cabana location, and demand. Check the Cruise Planner for current pricing.

Is the pool heated?

Yes, the infinity pool is heated for comfortable swimming regardless of weather conditions.

Can I use my drink package at the swim-up bar?

Yes, the swim-up bar accepts drink packages. Drinks are served to you right in the pool.
