NFL Royalty's Blessing

Carnival Inspiration carried a champion's blessing. Mary Anne Shula, wife of legendary Miami Dolphins coach Don Shula — the winningest coach in NFL history — christened her in 1996. That connection to sports excellence defined the sixth Fantasy Class ship.

The Shula name meant perfection. Don Shula led the only undefeated team in NFL history. His wife blessed a ship that would pursue its own form of excellence: 24 years of reliable service, delivering affordable cruises to California families.

Inspiration primarily served Long Beach, offering Mexican Riviera itineraries alongside her sister Imagination. Together they gave California two Fantasy Class options, doubling the West Coast's access to Fun Ship cruising.
