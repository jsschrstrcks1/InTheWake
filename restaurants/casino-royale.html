Casino Royale

Royal Caribbean — Entertainment Venue

Quick Answer: Casino Royale is Royal Caribbean's onboard casino available on all ships. Features slot machines, table games (blackjack, roulette, craps, poker), and sports betting. Open during sea days and evening hours in port. Must be 21+ to gamble. Club Royale loyalty program offers comps and perks.

Best For: Casino enthusiasts, poker players, slot fans, sports bettors, and guests seeking evening entertainment and nightlife atmosphere.

Key Facts

  • Age Requirement: 21+ to gamble
  • Hours: Sea days open; closed when docked during day
  • Games: Slots, blackjack, roulette, craps, poker, baccarat
  • Loyalty: Club Royale rewards program
  • Smoking: Designated smoking areas in casino

Try your luck at sea in Royal Caribbean's full-service casino.

Casino Royale offers Las Vegas-style gaming at sea with hundreds of slot machines, popular table games, poker tournaments, and sports betting kiosks. The casino operates in international waters, meaning favorable rules and no required taxes on winnings. Club Royale members earn comps toward drinks, merchandise, and cruise credit. Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: Casino Royale is wheelchair accessible with pathways between gaming areas. Some slot machines and table games can accommodate wheelchairs. Staff can assist with accessibility needs. Note that the casino has designated smoking sections which may affect guests with respiratory sensitivities.

Where You'll Find It

Casino Royale is typically located on Deck 4 or 5, often near the Royal Promenade or main entertainment areas. The casino is a central hub for nightlife activity.

Operating Hours

  • Sea Days: Open most of the day (varies by ship)
  • Port Days: Closed during docking, reopens evening before departure
  • Late Night: Often open until 2-4 AM
  • Tournaments: Scheduled events posted in Cruise Compass

Casino Royale Review: Vegas at Sea

Introduction. We enjoy gambling casually, and Casino Royale delivered a decent experience. It's no Vegas, but for a cruise ship, it has everything you'd expect—slots, tables, and a lively atmosphere once the sun goes down.

The Games

Good variety of slots from pennies to high-limit. Table minimums were $10-15 for blackjack on our sailing—reasonable for a cruise. The dealers were friendly and knew their games. We played craps a few times and the energy was fun when the table got hot.

The Atmosphere

The casino gets crowded on sea days, especially evenings. Smoke can be an issue if you're sensitive—there are non-smoking sections but the air still circulates. Drinks flow freely while you're playing, which is a nice perk.

Club Royale

Sign up for Club Royale before you start playing. We earned enough points for drink vouchers and a discount on our next cruise. The perks accumulate across sailings if you cruise often.

Conclusion

Casino Royale is what it is—a ship casino with reasonable games and a fun atmosphere. Don't expect to win big, but it's entertaining for a few hours. The smoking was our biggest complaint. Rating: 3.5/5.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Club Royale?

Club Royale is Royal Caribbean's casino loyalty program. Earn points while playing to receive comps like free drinks, merchandise, and cruise credit. Tiers include Classic, Prime, Signature, and Masters.

Can I use cash in the casino?

Yes, you can use cash or your SeaPass card. For tables, exchange cash for chips. Slots accept bills, coins, or TITO (ticket-in/ticket-out) vouchers.

Is there a dress code for Casino Royale?

No strict dress code, but casual resort wear is expected. Avoid swimsuits or overly casual attire. Evening hours tend to see slightly dressier guests.

Are drinks free in the casino?

Complimentary drinks are served to guests actively gaming. Cocktail servers circulate the floor. Tip is appreciated but not required.

Can I smoke in Casino Royale?

Smoking is permitted in designated sections of the casino. Non-smoking areas are available but share air circulation with the rest of the casino.
