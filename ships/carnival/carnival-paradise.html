Frequently Asked Questions

What are the dining options on Carnival Paradise?

Carnival Paradise offers multiple dining venues including the Paradise Restaurant and Destiny Restaurant main dining rooms serving multi-course dinners each evening. Casual options include Guy's Burger Joint with hand-crafted burgers, BlueIguana Cantina for freshly made tacos and burritos, and the Lido Restaurant buffet with live cooking stations.

Where does Carnival Paradise sail from?

Carnival Paradise is homeported in Tampa, Florida, offering 4- and 5-night Western Caribbean cruises year-round. Typical itineraries visit Cozumel, Costa Maya, Progreso, and Grand Cayman. Tampa's cruise terminal is conveniently located near the downtown area with ample parking facilities.

What makes Carnival Paradise historically significant?

Carnival Paradise made history as the first completely non-smoking cruise ship when she launched in 1998. Country music legend Reba McEntire served as godmother at the christening ceremony. As the final Fantasy Class ship built, she represents the pinnacle of that design series. Today, only Paradise and sister ship Elation remain in the Carnival fleet from this classic class.
