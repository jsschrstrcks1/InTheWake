Surfside Bites

Royal Caribbean — Complimentary Quick Service

Quick Answer: Surfside Bites is a complimentary quick-service snack spot in the Surfside family neighborhood on Icon Class ships. Offers convenient grab-and-go items including sandwiches, snacks, treats, and beverages—perfect for hungry kids between activities.

Best For: Families with children seeking quick snacks. Parents who need grab-and-go options while the kids are at Surfside activities. Anyone looking for casual bites between meals.

Key Facts

  • Location: Surfside neighborhood, Icon Class
  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Service Style: Quick service, grab-and-go
  • Hours: Open during daytime hours
  • Ships: Icon of the Seas, Star of the Seas

Quick snacks steps from the splash pads.

Surfside Bites serves up convenient grab-and-go items in the heart of Icon Class's family neighborhood. No need to leave the kids' play area when hunger strikes—sandwiches, snacks, and treats are just steps away. Everything is complimentary, making it easy to keep little ones fueled throughout the day. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Surfside Bites free?

Yes, all food and non-alcoholic drinks are complimentary.

What are the hours?

Surfside Bites operates during daytime hours, typically from morning through late afternoon.

Do they have healthy options?

Yes, you'll find fresh fruit, sandwiches, and other healthier choices alongside treats.

Is seating available?

Yes, casual seating is available nearby in the Surfside area.

Can adults eat here?

Absolutely! While designed with families in mind, anyone can grab food from Surfside Bites.

Which ships have Surfside Bites?

Surfside Bites is available on Icon Class ships: Icon of the Seas and Star of the Seas.
