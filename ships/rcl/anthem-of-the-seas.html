    • Welcome Aboard Anthem of the Seas

    Step into the future of cruising with Anthem of the Seas, where Royal Caribbean's Quantum-class innovation transforms every voyage into an extraordinary adventure. Launched in 2015, this 168,666-ton marvel sails with 4,905 guests across 18 decks, delivering experiences that redefine what's possible at sea.

    Thrill-Seekers

    Defy gravity with RipCord by iFLY skydiving and bumper cars at SeaPlex. The world's first indoor skydiving at sea awaits.

    Scenic Views

    Soar 300+ feet above deck in the North Star observation capsule for breathtaking 360° panoramas of Alaskan fjords and Pacific sunsets.

    Relaxation

    Unwind in the Solarium spa or poolside with ocean breezes. The Vitality Spa offers treatments inspired by the sea.

    Ready to chart your course? Explore current sailings or discover onboard adventures.

    Vibe & Style

    Sleek, modern, and adventurous. Anthem blends high-tech thrills with sophisticated spaces—from neon-lit skydiving to elegant wine tastings. Perfect for families, couples, and first-time cruisers seeking innovation.

    History & Milestones

    2012Ordered as part of Royal Caribbean's "Project Sunshine" with Meyer Werft, Germany.
    April 2015Delivered and christened in Southampton by Katy B. Maiden voyage to Mediterranean.
    2016Homeported in New York for Bermuda, Caribbean, and Canada/New England sailings.
    2020-2021Pioneered UK "cruises to nowhere" from Southampton post-COVID restart.
    2024Repositioned to Australia for South Pacific season, including Sydney departures.
    202510th anniversary celebrated with Alaskan summers from Seattle and transpacific voyages.

    Throughout her career, Anthem has weathered challenges like Hurricane Hermine (2016) while maintaining her reputation as a resilient, innovative flagship.

    Signature Features

    North Star®

    The engineering marvel rises 300+ feet above sea level, offering 360° views through floor-to-ceiling glass. Capacity: 9 guests per 15-minute ride. Featured in movies and countless proposals.

    See it in action

    RipCord® by iFLY®

    World's first indoor skydiving at sea. Neon-lit chamber simulates freefall at 120 mph. Sessions for all ages; instructors guide first-timers. Helmet cams available.

    Watch the experience

    SeaPlex®

    Largest indoor activity space at sea (82,000 sq ft). Transforms from basketball to bumper cars to roller disco. Features suspended flying trapeze and circus school.

    Explore the space

    Two70°

    Futuristic lounge with 270° ocean views enhanced by Vistarama® 18K-resolution screens. Hosts roboshows, aerial drone performances, and Chaz & AJ's Comedy Hour.

    View the shows

    Dining Experiences

    Main Dining Room

    Three-story venue serving multi-course dinners with rotating menus. My Time Dining (flexible) or Traditional Dining (assigned times). Features sommelier service and live music.

    Complimentary

    Chops Grille

    Signature steakhouse serving USDA prime cuts, Maine lobster, and seafood towers. Filet mignon, New York strip, and rack of lamb highlights. Reservations recommended.

    $39-59

    Izumi

    Traditional Japanese with sushi rolls, sashimi, and interactive teppanyaki. Hand-roll bar offers custom creations. Sake flights and green tea crème brûlée for dessert.

    $20-50

    Windjammer Marketplace

    Casual buffet with 10+ live cooking stations. Breakfast favorites include made-to-order omelets and crepe station. Dinner features international themes nightly.
