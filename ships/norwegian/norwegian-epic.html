Norwegian Epic — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

Norwegian Epic is an Epic Class cruise ship operated by Norwegian Cruise Line. She entered service in 2010, measures 155,873 gross tons, and carries approximately 4,070 guests at double occupancy with a crew of 1,724.

Key Facts

  • Class: Epic Class
  • Year: 2010
  • Tonnage: 155,873 GT
  • Guests: 4,070
  • IMO: 9410569

Quick Answer: Norwegian Epic (155,873 GT, 4,070 guests, 2010) is the only one of her kind ship — the only Epic Class vessel, featuring innovative Studio cabins for solo travelers, Cirque du Soleil-style entertainment, and an Aqua Park with the largest (as of 2026) bowl slide at sea. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Best For: Solo travelers (Studio cabins with private lounge), entertainment lovers, and cruisers who want this unique mega-ship that doesn't look like anything else in the fleet.

Norwegian Epic is NCL's one-off design — NCL's bold experiment that introduced solo Studio cabins with their own lounge. She carries 4,070 guests and features Cirque-style shows, a comedy club, and the Aqua Park. Her unconventional layout divides opinion but rewards exploration. If you prefer a more conventional layout, the Breakaway Plus ships are newer; if you want solo cabins on a newer ship, Norwegian Prima also offers Studios.

Frequently Asked Questions About Norwegian Epic

What dining options are available on Norwegian Epic?

Norwegian Epic offers over 20 dining venues including complimentary options like The Manhattan Room, Taste, and O'Sheehan's Neighborhood Bar & Grill. Specialty restaurants include Le Bistro French cuisine, Cagney's Steakhouse, Moderno Churrascaria, Teppanyaki, and the unique Cirque Dreams Epicurean dinner show combining dining with acrobatics.

What are the Studio cabins on Norwegian Epic?

Norwegian Epic pioneered solo cruising with 128 Studio staterooms designed specifically for single travelers. These compact, stylish cabins include access to the exclusive Studio Lounge, a private social space for solo guests to meet and mingle, making Epic ideal for cruisers traveling alone.

Where does Norwegian Epic sail?

Norwegian Epic primarily sails Mediterranean cruises from Barcelona and Civitavecchia (Rome) during summer, and Caribbean itineraries from Port Canaveral, Florida during winter. The ship offers 7-day voyages visiting popular ports in both regions.

What entertainment is unique to Norwegian Epic?

Norwegian Epic features the legendary Blue Man Group performing exclusively at sea, Cirque Dreams & Dinner in the Spiegel Tent, Howl at the Moon dueling pianos, and the first Ice Bar (debuted 2010) at sea where guests enjoy cocktails in sub-freezing temperatures surrounded by ice sculptures.
