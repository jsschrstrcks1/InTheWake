Sailed aboard Icon of the Seas.

Royal Caribbean Sorrento’s Pizza Review 2025: The Ultimate Free Slice Haven on the High Seas

Introduction. Sorrento’s is a slice of pure, uncomplicated joy — a complimentary pizzeria delivering hot, crave-worthy New York-style pies with zero fuss. Tucked in the heart of the ship, it turns hunger into happiness without reservations or cover charges. I grabbed slices for lunch and late-night over three days on Icon of the Seas; the value is unbeatable for quick, tasty fuel.

Food

The classic pepperoni slice hit all the right notes: cupped edges with a little crisp, bubbly mozzarella, and tangy sauce over a thin, chewy crust that held without sagging. Rotations kept things lively — “Caribbean Dream” balanced sweet pineapple and smoky bacon with a subtle BBQ note — while the veggie slice stayed bright and crisp with peppers, onions, mushrooms, and olives. If a slice leaned greasy, a quick blot fixed it; flavors stayed crowd-pleasing and consistent.

Service

Counter service was fast and friendly, even at peak. Staff guided us to fresh pies coming out, offered kid-size cuts, and handled simple tweaks (extra basil on Margherita) without slowing the line. The pace felt energetic in a good way — more buzz than bottleneck.

Atmosphere

Set along the Royal Promenade, Sorrento’s blends cozy pizzeria charm with the ship’s lively hum — perfect for people-watching or a quick refuel between activities. Late nights bring a fun, communal vibe that feels like a little street-corner slice shop at sea.

Conclusion

Flavorful slices, zippy service, and a lively Promenade perch make Sorrento’s an easy go-to. Minor grease or rush is part of the authentic, shareable fun. Rating: 4.5/5. Ideal for families, night owls, and pizza purists chasing convenient indulgence.
