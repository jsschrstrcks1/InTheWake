Ship Overview

Carnival Miracle is the 4th and final Spirit-class ship, christened by Jessica Lynch — the retired U.S. Army Private renowned for her dramatic rescue during the Iraq War — in Jacksonville, Florida in 2004.

As the culmination of the Spirit-class, Miracle combines intimate ambiance with classic Fun Ship amenities. Her 2015 Fun Ship 2.0 upgrade added Guy's Burger Joint, BlueIguana Cantina, Red Frog Rum Bar, and Alchemy Bar. She now sails Alaska from Seattle and Caribbean from Tampa and Galveston.

FAQ

Does Miracle sail to Alaska?

Yes! Miracle sails Alaska's Inside Passage from Seattle during summer months, offering 7-day itineraries to Juneau, Ketchikan, and Glacier Bay.

Who is the godmother?

Jessica Lynch, the retired U.S. Army Private famous for her rescue during the Iraq War, christened Miracle in Jacksonville in 2004.

What is Fun Ship 2.0?

Miracle's 2015 refurbishment added Guy's Burger Joint, BlueIguana Cantina, Red Frog Rum Bar, and Alchemy Bar.
