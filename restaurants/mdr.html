Main Dining Room

Main Dining Room

Royal Caribbean — Complimentary Dining

Rotating menus, classic cruise favorites, and white-tablecloth service — included in your cruise fare for breakfast, lunch (on select days), and dinner nightly.

The Main Dining Room (MDR) anchors your onboard dining with a mix of staples and nightly features. It’s the most reliable way to sample a little of everything — with vegetarian options, kids’ choices, and allergen support always available. Return to the Restaurants hub →

v2.257.001

Menu & Price

Price: Complimentary (included). Service: Breakfast daily, Lunch on sea days (and select port days), Dinner nightly. Menu rotates by night and itinerary.

Starters & Soups

  • French Onion Soup · Escargots à la Bourguignonne
  • Caesar or Mixed Green Salad
  • Shrimp Cocktail [select nights]
  • Tomato Bisque · Seasonal Soup

Mains

  • Grilled Salmon · Herb Roasted Chicken
  • Beef Tenderloin / Prime Rib [feature nights]
  • Vegetarian Pasta or Curry of the Day
  • Chef’s Regional Feature (varies by itinerary)

Sides & Desserts

  • Mashed Potatoes · Seasonal Vegetables · Rice Pilaf
  • New York Cheesecake · Warm Chocolate Cake
  • Crème Brûlée · Ice Cream & Sorbets (GF)
Ship-Specific Notes
  • [To Verify] “Classic” items available nightly (e.g., grilled chicken, sirloin, pasta) on most ships.
  • [To Verify] Formal night often features prime rib or lobster on select itineraries.

Kids’ Menu: Chicken tenders, pasta with marinara or butter, mini burgers, grilled chicken, veggies, and fruit are commonly available. Ask your server — they’ll accommodate picky eaters.

Special Accommodations

Allergen & Dietary Notes: Royal Caribbean follows SAFE Food Policy. Please disclose allergens to your server before ordering. Gluten-free, dairy-free, vegetarian, and many vegan adjustments are available on request. For severe allergies, contact Royal Caribbean’s accessibility team at least 30 days prior to sailing and confirm with the venue on embarkation day.
