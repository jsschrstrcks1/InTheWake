Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Carnival Encounter sail from?

Carnival Encounter sails year-round from Brisbane, Australia. She offers cruises along the Australian coastline (including Cairns and the Great Barrier Reef) and to South Pacific islands like Noumea and Lifou.

What is Carnival Encounter's history?

Built in 2002 as Star Princess for Princess Cruises, she was the third Grand Class ship. In 2021, she transferred to P&O Australia as Pacific Encounter. When Carnival discontinued P&O Australia in 2025, she was rebranded as Carnival Encounter.

What is the Byron Beach Club?

The Byron Beach Club is an exclusive retreat for suite and Byron Beach Club stateroom guests. It features a private pool, spa, sundeck, and priority services including dedicated dining and embarkation.

What family activities are available?

Carnival Encounter features Edge Adventure Park with water slides and zip-lining, Camp Ocean for kids, Seuss at Sea activities, Turtles for toddlers under 2, and the adults-only Serenity retreat.

Who is Luke Mangan?

Luke Mangan is an acclaimed Australian celebrity chef. His signature restaurant, Luke's Bar & Grill, aboard Carnival Encounter features premium steaks and seafood with his distinctive Australian culinary style.
