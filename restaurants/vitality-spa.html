Vitality Spa

Royal Caribbean — Spa & Wellness

Quick Answer: Full-service spa on all Royal Caribbean ships, operated by Steiner. Massages from $139, facials from $79, thermal suite day passes $40-60. Best tip: Book on port days for 10-20% discounts and better availability.

Best For: Cruisers seeking relaxation, couples wanting spa treatments, solo travelers enjoying thermal suite tranquility, and anyone wanting salon services.

Key Facts

  • Massages: $139-229
  • Facials: $79-249
  • Thermal Suite: $40-60/day
  • Gratuity: 18% auto-added
  • Location: Forward, upper decks

Services & Pricing

Prices vary by sailing · 18% gratuity auto-added · Pre-book for best selection

Massages

  • Swedish (50 min): $139-159
  • Deep Tissue (50 min): $169-189
  • Hot Stone (75 min): $199-229
  • Couples (50 min): $299-349

Thermal Suite & More

  • Day Pass: $40-60
  • Cruise Pass: $149-249
  • Express Facial: $79-99
  • Manicure: $45-65

Tips

Pro Tips: Book on port days for 10-20% discounts. Pre-book via Cruise Planner for best selection. Arrive 15-20 min early to relax. Decline product upsells gracefully if not interested. Thermal suite cruise pass is worth it on 7+ night cruises.

Hours & Booking

Spa: 8am - 8pm typical. Thermal Suite: 8am - 8pm. Fitness Center: 6am - 10pm (complimentary). Booking: Pre-cruise via Cruise Planner, or visit spa reception onboard. 24-hour cancellation policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do spa services cost?

Massages $139+, facials $79+, thermal suite day passes $40-60.

Is the fitness center free?

Yes, the fitness center is complimentary. Some classes have a small fee.

Should I book in advance?

Yes, especially for sea days. Pre-book via Cruise Planner for best availability.
