## Rule
Content must not contain road-map paragraphs that announce what the page will cover. "In this guide, you'll discover..." and "Let's explore the top things to do..." are machine tells — a human writer starts with the content itself, not a meta-description of the content.

The voice-audit transition-tells list includes: Moreover, Furthermore, Additionally, It's worth noting, In conclusion, In today's world, At its core, In essence, Let's dive in. These are flagged as overused AI transitions.

## Fix guidance
Delete the road-map paragraph. Start with the content. If the reader needs orientation, the page structure (headings, section titles, answer-line) provides it — not a prose announcement of what's coming.
