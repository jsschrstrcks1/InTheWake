Boardwalk Carousel

Royal Caribbean — Family Attraction

Quick Answer: A complimentary hand-carved merry-go-round in the Boardwalk neighborhood on Oasis and Icon Class ships. All ages welcome, no reservation needed. Especially magical at night when illuminated.

Best For: Families with children, nostalgic adults, couples seeking romantic evening atmosphere, and anyone wanting great photo opportunities.

Key Facts

  • Price: Complimentary
  • Ride Time: ~3 minutes
  • Ages: All ages welcome
  • Location: Boardwalk neighborhood
  • Ships: Oasis & Icon Class

The Carousel Experience

All rides complimentary · Unlimited rides throughout your cruise

Ride Details

  • Duration: ~3 minutes per cycle
  • Animals: Hand-carved wooden horses and figures
  • Jumpers: Some horses rise and fall
  • Chariots: Bench seating for young children or those preferring not to mount

Best Times

  • Morning: Shortest lines, great for photos without crowds
  • After Dinner: Magical with lights on
  • Port Days: Most guests ashore, minimal wait

Tips

Pro Tips: Best photos at night when illuminated. Staff help young children mount and dismount. Chariot benches work for those nervous about mounting horses. Parents can stand beside mounted children during the ride.

Hours & Availability

Sea Days: 10am - 11pm typical. Port Days: Often extended morning hours. May close during rain or high winds (open-air location). Available on: Oasis, Allure, Harmony, Symphony, Wonder of the Seas, and Icon of the Seas.

The Logbook — Real Guest Soundings

4.6 ★ out of 5 Family of 3 • Symphony of the Seas • 2024

My daughter rode the carousel at least 20 times during our cruise. The crew knew her by name by day 3! It's free, it's fun, and the hand-carved horses are genuinely beautiful. The evening rides with all the lights on were magical for the whole family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the carousel free?

Yes, completely complimentary with unlimited rides throughout your cruise.

Which ships have the carousel?

Oasis Class (Oasis, Allure, Harmony, Symphony, Wonder) and Icon Class (Icon of the Seas).

Can adults ride?

Absolutely! Many adults ride, especially in the evening for the romantic atmosphere.
