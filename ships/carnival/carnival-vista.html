Overview

Carnival Vista revolutionized the Carnival fleet when she debuted in 2016 as the lead ship of the innovative Vista-class. At 133,500 GT, she introduced groundbreaking features that have become staples of modern cruise ships. She currently sails from Galveston, Texas to the Western Caribbean year-round.

Vista pioneered the SkyRide suspended bike course, debuted the first IMAX theater at sea, and introduced the popular Havana Cabanas with exclusive outdoor retreat. Her success spawned sister ships Carnival Horizon and Carnival Panorama, plus Italian-heritage variants Costa Venezia and Costa Firenze.

Ship History & Timeline

April 2016

Delivered from Fincantieri shipyard in Monfalcone, Italy. First ship of the revolutionary Vista-class platform.

May 1, 2016

Maiden voyage from Trieste, Italy on a 13-day European cruise to Barcelona. Introduces SkyRide and IMAX to the cruise industry.

November 4, 2016

Christening ceremony in New York City. Deshauna Barber (Miss USA 2016, U.S. Army Reserve Captain) serves as godmother in a ceremony honoring military service members.

Year-round deployment from Galveston, Texas to the Western Caribbean, becoming a Texas Gulf Coast favorite.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Carnival Vista special?

Carnival Vista is the lead ship of the Vista-class and introduced many industry firsts when she debuted in 2016: the SkyRide suspended bike course, the first IMAX theater at sea, Havana Cabanas with exclusive outdoor area, and an enhanced RedFrog Tiki Bar. She set the template for modern Carnival ships.

Where does Carnival Vista sail from?

Carnival Vista is homeported in Galveston, Texas, offering year-round cruises to the Western Caribbean including Cozumel, Costa Maya, Roatan, and Belize. She's a popular choice for Texas cruisers seeking the Vista-class experience.

Who is the godmother of Carnival Vista?

Deshauna Barber, Miss USA 2016 and U.S. Army Reserve Captain, serves as godmother. The christening ceremony on November 4, 2016 in New York City honored military service members, marking a historic moment as Barber was the first woman serving in the U.S. military to win the Miss USA title.

What is the Vista-class?

The Vista-class is Carnival's 133,500 GT ship platform introduced in 2016. The class includes three Carnival ships (Vista, Horizon, Panorama) and two Costa ships now sailing for Carnival (Venezia, Firenze). Vista-class ships feature innovative amenities like SkyRide, IMAX, and Havana Cabanas.

What dining options are on Carnival Vista?

Carnival Vista features Guy's Burger Joint (by Guy Fieri), BlueIguana Cantina (Mexican), Bonsai Sushi, Fahrenheit 555 Steakhouse, Cucina del Capitano (Italian), Ji Ji Asian Kitchen, Seafood Shack, and two main dining rooms. The RedFrog Tiki Bar offers Caribbean-inspired drinks poolside.
