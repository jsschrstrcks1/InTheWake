Menu & Price

Price: As of 2025, Basecamp food is complimentary (typical window ~11:00 AM–6:00 PM; hours extend during select events). The on-site bar is à la carte.

Tip: Lines peak right after big slide sessions — pop by mid-hour for shortest waits. Grab sauces first for quicker exits.

Royal Caribbean Basecamp Review 2025: Fast Fuel in Thrill Island

Introduction

Basecamp turns post-slide hunger into a quick win — hot, crispy comfort within shouting distance of the action.

Food

Waffle Chicken Nuggets with maple or honey-hot are the headliners; the smash burger and cheese curds round out a snacky but satisfying lineup. Portions skew shareable.

Service

Grill crew moves fast and adjusts sauces on request; peak lines pop right after big ride cycles. Mid-hour visits are breeziest.

Atmosphere

Open-air energy with skyline views and splash-side soundtracks — pure Thrill Island vibes, built for swimsuit-friendly bites.

Conclusion

Not fancy, but perfectly placed and pleasantly free. Rating: 4.1/5.
