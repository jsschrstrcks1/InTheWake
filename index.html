Welcome aboard

Trim your sails and settle in. Out at sea, the smoothest passage often comes when you ease in behind a larger ship and let her wake iron the chop. This site is the same — a traveler’s logbook to help you find calmer water: planning charts, ports and menus, solo guides, and reflections tested in real wakes, not borrowed winds.
