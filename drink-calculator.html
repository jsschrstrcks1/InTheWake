FAQ (quick)

Why does everyone in a cabin need the same package?
A: Royal Caribbean requires all adults in a stateroom to purchase the same beverage package to prevent sharing.

Can I buy onboard?
A: Yes, but pre-cruise prices in the Cruise Planner are almost always cheaper. This calculator uses pre-cruise estimates.
