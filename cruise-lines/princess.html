Princess Cruises

Quick Answer: Where the destination takes center stage. Princess pioneered Alaska cruising and continues to craft itineraries that put you closer to the world's wonders. MedallionClass technology makes the ship feel like it knows you — doors open as you approach, drinks find you poolside, crew greet you by name. Add Movies Under the Stars, the serene Sanctuary retreat, and gracious service that never feels stuffy, and you have a cruise line that balances exploration with relaxation.

Is Princess Right for You?

Picture yourself on a private balcony as glaciers calve into sapphire waters, or watching the sunset paint the Mediterranean while a movie plays on the giant poolside screen. Princess excels at destination-focused cruising — their itineraries linger longer in port, their shore excursions dig deeper, and their onboard programming prepares you to truly experience each place you visit.

The MedallionClass experience transforms your cruise from the moment you arrive. The Ocean Medallion — a small wearable device — becomes your key, your wallet, and your connection to personalized service. Want a drink by the pool? Order from your phone and it finds you. Approaching your stateroom? The door unlocks automatically. It's technology that enhances without intruding.

Perfect for couples seeking romantic escapes, multigenerational families who value quality time together, and destination-focused travelers who want the journey to match the ports. Princess strikes a balance between casual comfort and polished elegance — you'll never need a tuxedo, but you might want to dress up for a special dinner at Crown Grill.

Key Facts at a Glance

  • Brand Identity: "Come Back New" — Destination-driven cruising with gracious, unobtrusive service
  • Signature Features: MedallionClass technology, Movies Under the Stars, The Sanctuary (adults-only retreat), Chef's Table dining experience
  • Best For: Couples, multigenerational families, destination-focused travelers, first-time cruisers
  • Dress Code: Smart casual with optional formal nights — relaxed elegance
  • Fleet Highlight: Sphere Class ships (Sun Princess, Star Princess) with innovative design
  • Signature Destinations: Alaska, Mediterranean, Caribbean, Japan, Australia

The Princess Experience

Princess built its reputation on Alaska — their decades of experience show in every glacier viewing, every wildlife encounter, every moment of awe. But their destination expertise extends worldwide: from the temples of Japan to the fjords of Norway, Princess itineraries are designed by people who understand that the best cruise isn't just about the ship.

That said, the ship matters too. The newest Sphere Class vessels represent Princess's vision for modern cruising: expansive pool decks, The Dome (a transforming entertainment venue), and thoughtful spaces that flow naturally from activity to relaxation. MedallionClass technology weaves throughout, creating experiences that feel both high-tech and deeply personal.

What cruisers say: "We've done Alaska three times with Princess — no one else comes close. The naturalists, the viewing platforms, the excursions... they understand what makes Alaska special."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MedallionClass on Princess?

MedallionClass is Princess's innovative technology platform centered around a wearable Medallion device. It enables keyless stateroom entry, contactless payment, on-demand food and beverage delivery anywhere on ship, and personalized service as crew members can greet you by name.

What is Movies Under the Stars?

Movies Under the Stars is Princess's signature poolside cinema experience. A giant LED screen shows first-run films, concerts, and sporting events under the open sky, complete with complimentary popcorn and comfortable loungers with blankets.

What is the Sanctuary on Princess ships?

The Sanctuary is Princess's adults-only retreat — a serene outdoor spa area with premium loungers, dedicated attendants, healthy cuisine, and spa treatments. It's a quiet escape from the busier pool areas, available for an additional fee.

What dining options does Princess offer?

Princess offers multiple complimentary dining venues including the main dining room with anytime dining, buffet, pizzeria, and International Café. Specialty options include Crown Grill steakhouse, Sabatini's Italian, and the exclusive Chef's Table experience.
