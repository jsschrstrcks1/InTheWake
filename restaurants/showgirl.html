Showgirl!

Royal Caribbean — Production Show

Quick Answer: Showgirl! is a dazzling Vegas-style production show celebrating the art of the showgirl. Features stunning costumes, high-energy dance numbers, live singing, and theatrical spectacle. A visual feast that pays tribute to showgirls throughout entertainment history.

Best For: Fans of Vegas-style shows. Broadway enthusiasts. Anyone who appreciates costumes, dance, and spectacle. Date nights at sea.

Key Facts

  • Venue: Main Theater
  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Duration: Approximately 45-50 minutes
  • Style: Vegas-style revue
  • Shows: Multiple performances per cruise

Vegas glamour comes to sea.

Showgirl! brings the glitz and glamour of a Vegas showroom to Royal Caribbean's main theater. Elaborate costumes, dazzling choreography, and talented performers create a high-energy celebration of showgirl culture from the Ziegfeld Follies to modern spectacle. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Showgirl! free?

Yes, all main theater production shows are complimentary.

Do I need reservations?

No, seating is open and first-come, first-served.

Is it appropriate for kids?

Yes, Showgirl! is family-friendly entertainment, though younger children may lose interest.

How long is the show?

Approximately 45-50 minutes.

Which ships have Showgirl!?

Showgirl! rotates among various Royal Caribbean ships. Check your specific ship's entertainment schedule.

Can I take photos?

Photos without flash are typically allowed, but flash photography and video recording are prohibited during performances.
