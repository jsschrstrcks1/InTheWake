Welcome Aboard

In the Wake is a practical cruise companion shaped by real sailings and careful curation. Use it to compare ships, scan menus, scout cabins, and sanity-check tips without losing your bearings. We prioritize family-friendly creators, surface accessibility notes wherever possible, and treat numbers as snapshots in time. Cross-links connect ships, restaurants, and ports so you can verify details from more than one angle. The goal isn’t to sell you on anything—it’s to keep you oriented, remove friction, and help you make confident choices that feel like your idea all along. Fair winds and following seas.
