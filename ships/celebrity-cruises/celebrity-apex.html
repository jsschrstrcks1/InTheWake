First Look: Celebrity Apex

Celebrity Apex is the second ship in Celebrity Cruises' revolutionary Edge Class, debuting in 2020. Designed by world-renowned architects and interior designers, Apex embodies modern luxury cruising with its outward-facing design philosophy that connects guests to the sea like never before. The ship features the innovative Magic Carpet, a cantilevered platform that moves between decks to serve as a restaurant, bar, and tender embarkation station.

At 130,818 gross tons and accommodating 2,910 guests, Celebrity Apex offers a spacious and refined experience. The ship features the signature Infinite Veranda staterooms where floor-to-ceiling windows transform into open-air balconies at the touch of a button. Eden, the three-story venue at the aft of the ship, combines dining, entertainment, and relaxation in a stunning botanical setting with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the wake.

The Retreat, Celebrity's exclusive suite experience, offers a private sundeck, restaurant, and lounge exclusively for suite guests. Celebrity Apex also features 29 different restaurant and bar venues, including the immersive Le Petit Chef dining experience where animated characters prepare your meal right on your plate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options are available on Celebrity Apex?

Celebrity Apex offers exceptional dining variety as an Edge Class ship. Complimentary options include the elegant main dining rooms, Oceanview Cafe buffet, and Eden Cafe. Specialty restaurants requiring reservations include Fine Cut Steakhouse, Le Grand Bistro French cuisine, Raw on 5 seafood, Rooftop Garden Grill, and the innovative Eden Restaurant.

How do I find the deck plans for Celebrity Apex?

Deck plans for Celebrity Apex are available through the links on this page. You can also find official deck plans on the Celebrity Cruises website or in the Celebrity app after booking.

Where does Celebrity Apex sail?

Celebrity Apex offers diverse itineraries including Mediterranean voyages from Rome and Barcelona, Caribbean sailings, and transatlantic crossings. Check the Celebrity Cruises website for current schedules.

What makes Celebrity Apex special?

Celebrity Apex features the Magic Carpet moving platform, outward-facing infinite verandas, and Eden - a multi-story entertainment venue with floor-to-ceiling windows. The ship showcases Celebrity's Revolution design concept with stunning architecture and exceptional attention to detail throughout all public spaces.
