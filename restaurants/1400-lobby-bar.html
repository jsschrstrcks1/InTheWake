1400 Lobby Bar

Royal Caribbean — Icon Class Cocktail Lounge

Quick Answer: 1400 Lobby Bar is an upscale cocktail lounge on Icon Class ships, located in the main lobby atrium. Features craft cocktails, premium spirits, and an elegant atmosphere perfect for pre-dinner drinks or evening nightcaps. Most drinks included with Deluxe Beverage Package. Open afternoon through late night.

Best For: Couples seeking sophisticated atmosphere, cocktail enthusiasts, pre-dinner drinks, nightcaps, and guests wanting refined bar service in a central location.

Key Facts

  • Price: $12-18 per cocktail (most included with Deluxe Beverage Package)
  • Hours: ~4:00 PM - 1:00/2:00 AM daily
  • Dress Code: Smart casual (no swimwear)
  • Reservations: Not required — walk-up service
  • Ships: Icon of the Seas, Star of the Seas (Icon Class)

Sailed aboard Icon of the Seas.

1400 Lobby Bar Review: Sophisticated Cocktails in the Heart of Icon

Introduction. 1400 Lobby Bar quickly became our go-to spot for pre-dinner drinks and late-night nightcaps during our Icon of the Seas sailing. Located in the central atrium, it's perfectly positioned for people-watching while enjoying expertly crafted cocktails. The atmosphere is upscale but welcoming—think sophisticated lounge rather than stuffy bar.

Drinks

The bartenders here know their craft. I started with a classic Old Fashioned, which was made exactly to my preference after a brief conversation about bourbon choices. My partner ordered an Espresso Martini that she declared the best she'd had at sea. On subsequent visits, we explored the signature cocktails—each one thoughtfully constructed with quality ingredients. The whiskey selection is impressive, and the bartenders are happy to offer recommendations based on your preferences.

Service

Service was attentive without being intrusive. The bartenders remembered our preferences after the first visit, which added a personal touch. Even during busy pre-dinner hours, we never waited long for drinks. The staff handled custom requests with enthusiasm—one bartender created an off-menu variation that became my cruise favorite.

Atmosphere

The design is contemporary and elegant with comfortable seating options including bar stools and lounge chairs. The central location means there's always interesting activity to observe, but the bar maintains its own intimate feel. Lighting is warm and flattering. On some evenings, there's live music nearby that adds to the ambiance without overwhelming conversation.

Conclusion

1400 Lobby Bar delivers exactly what you'd hope for in a sophisticated ship bar—quality drinks, skilled bartenders, elegant atmosphere, and convenient location. It's become essential to our Icon Class cruise experience. Rating: 4.5/5. Highly recommended for cocktail enthusiasts, couples, and anyone seeking refined bar service. Most drinks covered by the Deluxe Beverage Package make it excellent value.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is 1400 Lobby Bar on Royal Caribbean?

1400 Lobby Bar is a sophisticated cocktail lounge on Royal Caribbean Icon Class ships (Icon of the Seas, Star of the Seas), located in the main lobby atrium. It features craft cocktails, premium spirits, fine wines, and an elegant atmosphere perfect for pre-dinner drinks, nightcaps, or evening socializing.

Is 1400 Lobby Bar included in the drink package?

Most cocktails and spirits at 1400 Lobby Bar are included in Royal Caribbean's Deluxe Beverage Package. Drinks priced at $15 or under are typically fully covered. Ultra-premium selections and champagne may have an upcharge.

What are the hours for 1400 Lobby Bar?

1400 Lobby Bar typically opens around 4:00 PM and stays open until 1:00-2:00 AM. Peak hours are 5:00 PM to midnight when guests gather for pre-dinner and post-show drinks.

Do I need a reservation for 1400 Lobby Bar?

No reservation is needed. 1400 Lobby Bar operates on a walk-up basis. Seating is available on a first-come, first-served basis, including bar stools and lounge seating.

Is there a dress code at 1400 Lobby Bar?

Smart casual attire is expected. Swimwear and pool attire are not permitted in the bar area. This is a more refined venue, so most guests dress nicely, especially in the evening.
