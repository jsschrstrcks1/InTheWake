Rock Climbing Wall

Royal Caribbean — Adventure Activity

Quick Answer: A complimentary rock climbing wall available on every Royal Caribbean ship. 30-40 feet tall with multiple routes from beginner to expert. No reservation needed—walk up during operating hours. Closed-toe shoes required.

Best For: Active cruisers, families with children ages 6+, those seeking adventure with ocean views, and climbers of all skill levels.

Key Facts

  • Price: Complimentary
  • Height: 30-40 feet
  • Routes: Multiple (beginner to expert)
  • Ships: Fleet-wide
  • Shoes: Closed-toe required

Routes & Difficulty

All climbs complimentary · Multiple routes available · Ring the bell at the top!

Beginner Routes

  • Green: Large, frequent holds — Most climbers reach top
  • Blue: Slightly smaller holds with lateral movement

Advanced Routes

  • Yellow/Orange: Requires technique and planning
  • Red/Black: Expert — Few complete; includes overhang

Tips

Pro Tips: Best times are early morning or during dinner (shortest waits). Use your legs, not arms. Keep three points of contact. Plan your route before climbing. Trust the auto-belay when descending. Loaner shoes available if needed.

Hours & Requirements

Sea Days: 9am - 6pm (weather permitting). Port Days: Often extended hours. Requirements: No minimum age (must fit harness, typically 6+), max ~300 lbs, closed-toe shoes, one-time waiver. Available on all Royal Caribbean ships fleet-wide.

The Logbook — Real Guest Soundings

4.5 ★ out of 5 Family • Symphony of the Seas • 2024

I've climbed at gyms for years, but nothing compares to reaching the top and looking out at the Caribbean stretching to the horizon. Pro tip: go during dinner time on sea days — we walked right up with zero wait.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the climbing wall free?

Yes, completely complimentary with unlimited climbs.

Do I need experience?

No. Routes range from beginner to expert. Staff provide safety briefing and equipment.

What shoes should I wear?

Closed-toe athletic shoes required. Loaner shoes available (limited sizes).
