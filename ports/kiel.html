From the Pier

  • Harbour Promenade
    Waterfront walk, maritime museums, restaurants — right at the terminal
    🚶 5 min
  • Holstenstrasse
    Pedestrian shopping street — one of Germany's oldest
    🚶 10 min
  • Laboe Naval Memorial & U-995
    WWII submarine you can walk through — memorial tower with bay views
    🚌 20 min
  • Kiel Canal Observation
    World's busiest artificial waterway — 30,000+ ships per year
    🚌 15 min
  • Hamburg
    Speicherstadt, Elbphilharmonie, Miniatur Wunderland — 1 hour by train
    🚆 1 hr

Ships dock at Ostseekai in the city centre — walkable to most attractions. Bus 100/102 to Laboe (20 min). Kiel is primarily an embarkation port for Baltic cruises. If you have a day, Laboe's submarine and Hamburg by train are the best options.

Quick Answer: Kiel is the sailing capital of Germany — Olympic harbor, massive locks, and the world's busiest canal right at your gangway.
