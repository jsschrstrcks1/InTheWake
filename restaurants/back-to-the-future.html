Back to the Future: The Musical

Royal Caribbean — Broadway Production

Quick Answer: Back to the Future: The Musical brings the beloved 1985 film to life on Icon Class ships. This full Broadway production features Marty McFly's time-traveling adventure, the iconic DeLorean, Doc Brown's eccentric genius, and all the memorable moments from the film—plus new original songs.

Best For: Fans of the original film. Broadway enthusiasts. Families. Anyone who loves 80s nostalgia. Musical theater lovers.

Key Facts

  • Venue: Royal Theater
  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Duration: Approximately 1 hour 45 minutes
  • Ships: Icon of the Seas, Star of the Seas
  • Style: Broadway musical

"Great Scott!" — Broadway at sea.

Royal Caribbean has partnered with the producers of the hit West End and Broadway musical to bring Back to the Future to sea. The show recreates the magic of Marty McFly's accidental trip to 1955 in Doc Brown's DeLorean time machine. Expect spectacular special effects, including a DeLorean that appears to fly, plus hit songs from the movie and new original numbers. What We Loved: The production value is genuinely stunning. When the DeLorean "takes off" at the end of Act One, the entire audience gasps. The cast is Broadway-caliber and the special effects rival anything on land.

Pro Tips:

  • Arrive at least 20-30 minutes early—this show fills up fast
  • Center orchestra seats offer the best view of the DeLorean effects
  • Know the film? You'll catch all the references. Don't know it? You'll still love it
  • Consider seeing it twice—there's a lot to take in

This isn't a cruise ship show pretending to be Broadway—it IS Broadway quality. An absolute must-see on Icon Class.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Back to the Future: The Musical free?

Yes, this full Broadway production is complimentary and included in your cruise fare.

Do I need reservations?

Reservations are typically not required, but arrive early as this is one of the most popular shows on the ship.

How long is the show?

Approximately 1 hour and 45 minutes including intermission.

Is it appropriate for kids?

Yes! It's family-friendly and great for all ages, though very young children may find the length challenging.

Do I need to have seen the movie?

No, the story is told completely in the show. Fans of the film will appreciate extra details.

Which ships have this show?

Back to the Future: The Musical is exclusive to Icon Class: Icon of the Seas and Star of the Seas.
