Room Service

Royal Caribbean — In-Room Dining (24/7 on most sailings)

Complimentary continental breakfast and all-day in-cabin bites delivered to your door. Add hot favorites for a small per-order delivery fee.

Think of it as a cozy extension of the ship’s restaurants — perfect for sunrise coffee, balcony lunches, or late-night snacks. Order via the Royal App or in-room phone (dial 32). Return to the Restaurants hub →

v2.257.001

Menu & Price

Price: Continental & cold items are typically $0. Hot items usually incur a flat $7.95 delivery fee + 18% gratuity per order (≈ $9.50). Fees & hours vary by ship; confirm in the Royal App on board.

Tip: Each hot order shares one delivery fee — bundle items together. Suite perks on select tiers may waive the fee.

Fees & Suite Notes
  • One delivery fee per hot order (bundling saves).
  • Suite tiers may receive complimentary hot delivery — confirm on board.
  • Beverages like specialty coffee / soda follow standard pricing unless covered by package.

App ordering isn’t available on every ship; if missing, use the door-hanger or in-room phone.

Special Accommodations

Allergen & Dietary Notes: Specify allergies in the App or when calling. Staff flag orders (e.g., no-nut parfait, gluten-free bun). Vegetarian & lighter options always available.

Sailed aboard Symphony of the Seas.

Royal Caribbean Room Service Review 2025: Effortless In-Cabin Indulgence Around the Clock

Introduction

Room Service turns your stateroom into a private dining nook — reliable, crave-worthy bites with minimal fuss and maximum relaxation. From a free continental spread at dawn to a hot cheeseburger at midnight, it nails the balance of quality and ease. On my 7-night Bahamas cruise, I ordered continental breakfast most mornings, plus all-day items for lunch and a late-night snack. The value is strong: cold items = $0; hot items ≈ $7.95 + gratuity.

Food

The complimentary yogurt parfait was a daily win — creamy yogurt, crunchy granola, and bright berries in a spill-safe cup, paired with a flaky croissant and juice. Midday, the club sandwich (cold) arrived deli-fresh with pickle spears — simple, balanced, and free. For a hot upgrade, the personal cheese pizza came bubbling and crisp, perfect balcony fare. Desserts like chocolate-dipped strawberries capped things sweetly. It’s not gourmet, but it’s comfort that travels well.

Service

Ordering via the app or phone took under a minute; deliveries averaged 15–20 minutes. Operators clearly explained free vs fee items and flagged my nut allergy across later orders. On busier sea days, waits grew slightly but included courteous follow-up calls. The crew’s calm efficiency makes Room Service a quiet highlight.

Atmosphere

Because the “dining room” is your room, the mood is whatever you choose — sunrise trays on the balcony, lights-down movie snacks, or PJs after port days. Privacy is the luxury, and it feels indulgent without costing time or effort.

Conclusion

Dependable food, courteous timing, and made-for-coziness convenience make Room Service an easy yes. The fee structure simply teaches smarter ordering — bundle hots, lean on free continentals. Rating: 4.5 / 5. Ideal for families, couples, or suite guests; use the app to time orders and bundle for value.
