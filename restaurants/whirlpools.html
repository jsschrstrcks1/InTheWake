Whirlpools

Royal Caribbean — Hot Tubs

Quick Answer: Whirlpools (hot tubs) are complimentary on all Royal Caribbean ships. Found on the main pool deck and in the adults-only Solarium. Open from early morning until late night. No reservation needed—first-come, first-served seating.

Best For: Relaxation seekers, couples, anyone wanting to unwind, and guests who enjoy socializing in a casual setting.

Key Facts

  • Price: Complimentary
  • Hours: ~6 AM - 11 PM (varies)
  • Locations: Pool deck, Solarium, specialty areas
  • Adults-Only: Solarium whirlpools (16+/18+)
  • Capacity: 6-8 people per whirlpool typically

Relax and unwind in heated whirlpools with ocean views throughout your cruise.

Larger ships (Oasis, Icon Class) have 6-10+ whirlpools. Smaller ships have 4-6. All are heated and maintained at comfortable temperatures.

Special Accommodations

Safety Notes: Children must be supervised at all times. Solarium whirlpools are adults-only (age varies by sailing). Pregnant guests and those with heart conditions should consult physicians before use. Maximum time recommended is 15-20 minutes. Shower before entering.

Tips for Best Experience

  • Early Morning: Quietest time, often empty
  • Port Days: Much less crowded
  • Evening: Popular but romantic atmosphere
  • Late Night: Some stay open until 11 PM+
  • Solarium: Best for quiet relaxation (adults only)

Etiquette

  • Shower before entering
  • Limit time to 15-20 minutes during busy periods
  • Keep conversations at reasonable volume
  • Don't save seats with towels

Whirlpools Review: Essential Cruise Relaxation

The whirlpools became our daily ritual—morning coffee in the Solarium hot tub, afternoon soak after excursions, evening relaxation before dinner. The Solarium's adults-only policy made it our favorite; quieter and more serene than the main pool deck.

Main pool deck whirlpools are fun for socializing but can get crowded on sea days. Pro tip: port days are the best time to enjoy them with plenty of space. Early morning is also excellent—sometimes we had a whirlpool entirely to ourselves.

Rating: 4.0/5. Nothing fancy, but a cruise essential. The Solarium whirlpools elevate the experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are whirlpools free?

Yes, all whirlpools on Royal Caribbean ships are complimentary.

Are there adults-only whirlpools?

Yes, the Solarium area has adults-only whirlpools (age 16+ or 18+ depending on sailing).

What time do whirlpools open?

Most whirlpools open around 6-7 AM and remain open until 11 PM-midnight.

Can I bring drinks to the whirlpool?

Yes, beverages from the bars can be brought to whirlpools. Glass containers are typically not allowed on the pool deck.

How many whirlpools are there?

Varies by ship—larger ships have 6-10+, smaller ships have 4-6. Oasis and Icon Class ships have the most.
