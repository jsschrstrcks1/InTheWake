RipCord by iFLY

Royal Caribbean — Indoor Skydiving

Quick Answer: A complimentary indoor skydiving simulator on Quantum Class ships. Vertical wind tunnel generates 120 mph air for freefall sensation. Includes training, flight suit, and two flights. Book via app immediately on embarkation day.

Best For: Thrill-seekers, adventure lovers, those curious about skydiving, and families with children ages 3+.

Key Facts

  • Price: Complimentary
  • Duration: ~1 hour total
  • Flight Time: Two ~30 sec flights
  • Min Age: 3 years
  • Max Weight: 300 lbs

The Experience

Complimentary session: Training + 2 flights · Premium add-ons: $29-49+

Session Timeline

  • Training: ~30 min — Learn body position, hand signals, safety
  • Gear Up: ~10 min — Flight suit, helmet, goggles, earplugs
  • First Flight: ~30 sec — Initial hover experience
  • Second Flight: ~30 sec — Apply skills, more confidence

Optional Add-Ons

  • High-Flying Experience: $49+ — Extended time with spins and flips
  • Photo/Video Package: $29+ — Professional documentation

Tips

Pro Tips: Book within 15 minutes of boarding—slots fill fast. Wear athletic clothes and lace-up sneakers (required). Relax during flight—tension makes it harder. Keep chin up and eyes forward. Trust your instructor.

Hours & Requirements

Sea Days: 10am - 6pm (weather permitting). Requirements: Ages 3+, max 300 lbs, lace-up sneakers, signed waiver, parental consent for under 18. Available on: Quantum, Anthem, Ovation, and Spectrum of the Seas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RipCord free?

Yes, basic sessions are complimentary. Premium High-Flying packages available for additional cost.

Do I need experience?

No experience needed. Comprehensive training and instructor guidance included.

What should I wear?

Athletic clothes and lace-up sneakers (required). No sandals, flip-flops, or slip-ons.
