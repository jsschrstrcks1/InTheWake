First Look: Celebrity Ascent

Celebrity Ascent is the fourth ship in Celebrity Cruises' acclaimed Edge Class, debuting in December 2023. As the newest member of this revolutionary fleet, Ascent combines the best elements of Edge, Apex, and Beyond while introducing refined touches and enhanced guest experiences that represent the pinnacle of Edge Class evolution.

At 140,600 gross tons and accommodating 3,260 guests, Celebrity Ascent shares the stretched hull design introduced on Beyond, providing exceptional space for guests. The ship features Le Voyage, the world-class restaurant by Michelin-starred chef Daniel Boulud, along with all the signature Edge Class venues including Eden, the Rooftop Garden, and the Magic Carpet cantilever platform.

The Retreat suite-class experience on Ascent offers the expanded Sundeck with private cabanas, a dedicated plunge pool, and the exclusive Luminae restaurant. Infinite Veranda staterooms provide the innovative transformation from enclosed cabin to open-air balcony at the touch of a button, while the ship's outward-facing design philosophy ensures guests feel connected to the sea throughout their voyage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options are available on Celebrity Ascent?

Celebrity Ascent offers exceptional dining variety as an Edge Class ship. Complimentary options include four elegant main dining rooms (Normandie, Tuscan, Cyprus, and Cosmopolitan), the Oceanview Cafe buffet, Eden Cafe, and the Spa Cafe. Specialty restaurants requiring reservations include Fine Cut Steakhouse, Le Grand Bistro French cuisine, Raw on 5 seafood bar, Le Voyage by Chef Daniel Boulud, and the innovative Eden Restaurant with its unique dinner-and-a-show experience.

How do I find the deck plans for Celebrity Ascent?

Deck plans are available through the links on this page. You can also find official deck plans on the Celebrity Cruises website or in the Celebrity app cruise planner. Celebrity Ascent spans 16 passenger decks with The Retreat suite area on upper decks, the Resort Deck pool area on Deck 15, and the Grand Plaza spanning Decks 3-5.

Where does Celebrity Ascent sail?

Celebrity Ascent deployments vary by season. The ship typically sails Caribbean itineraries from Fort Lauderdale during winter months and repositions for European voyages including Mediterranean sailings during summer. Check the Celebrity Cruises website for current itineraries and departure ports.

What makes Celebrity Ascent unique among Edge Class ships?

As the fourth and newest Edge Class ship, Celebrity Ascent incorporates refined design elements learned from her sister ships Edge, Apex, and Beyond. She features Le Voyage restaurant by Michelin-starred chef Daniel Boulud, the signature Magic Carpet cantilevered platform, and expanded Retreat suite amenities including a dedicated sundeck with private cabanas and plunge pool.
