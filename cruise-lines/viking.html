QUIET LUXURY. NO KIDS. NO CASINOS. VIKING.

Destination-focused journeys with resident historians, classical music, and a serene Nordic aesthetic. Every ship is a study in calm: light woods, airy lounges, and terraces designed to frame the scenery.

Beer & wine with lunch and dinner, Wi-Fi, laundry, and select excursions are included — without the constant upsell. Cabins are limited and sell early; secure yours while they exist.
