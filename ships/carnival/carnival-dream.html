Ship Overview

Carnival Dream is the pioneering lead ship of the Dream Class that transformed Carnival Cruise Line in 2009. At 128,250 GT, she introduced WaterWorks aqua park, Serenity adult retreat, and Ocean Plaza — innovations that became fleet standards.

Sailing from Galveston, Texas, she offers Western Caribbean itineraries with stops including Cozumel, Costa Maya, Roatan, and Belize.

FAQ

Where does Carnival Dream sail from?

Galveston, Texas — Western Caribbean itineraries including Cozumel, Costa Maya, Roatan, and Belize.

What class is Carnival Dream?

Lead ship of the Dream Class (2009), with sisters Carnival Magic (2011) and Carnival Breeze (2012).

What innovations did Dream introduce?

WaterWorks aqua park, Serenity adult retreat, Ocean Plaza, and the Lanai open-air dining concept.
