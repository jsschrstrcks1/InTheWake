## What the at-home contact does if 36+ hours pass without word

1. **Don't panic** — cruise Wi-Fi is unreliable; silence usually means signal, not emergency. Thanksgiving holiday volume can also slow messages, and Great Stirrup Cay has no terrestrial cellular.
2. **Call the NCL Care Team** above; they'll confirm boarding and relay messages.
3. **Reach Pam** via the Thanksgiving Group Cruise Host Pam Facebook chat: facebook.com/share/1DuHTTtgdc — she's the on-ship host and can confirm whether the cruiser is with the group.
4. **If NCL confirms an incident:** US Embassy Santo Domingo for DR (+1 809-567-7775); US Coast Guard for US waters; US Consulate Bridgetown for BVI (+1 246-227-4000); 911 for US ports; ship medical for health. Track ship at /tools/ship-tracker.html.
