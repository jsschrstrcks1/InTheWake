Venues, Experiences & Shows

Main Dining Room

Rotating themed menus. Status: Included.

Windjammer Café

Casual buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Status: Included.

Chops Grille

Classic steakhouse, à la carte or package.

Giovanni’s Table

Family-style Italian dining experience.

Izumi

Sushi, sashimi, and hot-rock plates. À la carte pricing.

Johnny Rockets

Retro 1950s burger and shake joint. Status: Specialty.

Café Promenade

Sandwiches, pizza, and pastries 24/7. Status: Included.

Schooner Bar

Nautical-themed piano bar with live music, trivia, and cocktails. Every ship.

Boleros

Latin-inspired lounge with mojitos, salsa dancing, and live bands. Most ships.

English Pub

Classic taproom with beers, gins, and live music. Nearly every ship.

Viking Crown Lounge

Observation lounge with panoramic views and DJs. Voyager class and above.

All Access Ship Tour

Behind-the-scenes guided tour of the bridge, galley, and engine areas. Premium.

Taste of Royal Lunch

Signature multi-course tasting experience with curated pairings. Premium.

The Quest

Adults-only scavenger hunt game show with audience participation. Legendary.

Lineups vary by sailing. Check your Cruise Compass or app for up-to-date times and venues.
