Radiance of the Seas

Radiance Class blends big‑ship polish with small‑ship sightlines. Designed with extensive glass and wide ocean views, these ships sail beautifully in scenic regions — think fjords, Alaska, and Mediterranean coastlines where ‘outside’ is the headliner.

Onboard you’ll find a relaxed rhythm: sun‑splashed Solariums, a bright atrium for evening music, and dining that feels curated rather than crowded. It’s an itinerary‑first style of sailing — with just enough sparkle to feel special after sunset.

Launched in 2001 as the class flagship, Radiance introduced a sleeker, diesel‑electric/gas‑turbine hybrid power system that keeps things remarkably smooth and quiet. Her public rooms are wrapped in glass, from the poolside Solarium to the panoramic lounges — perfect for glacier mornings and sunset departures.

Guests love Radiance’s balance: a real theater for classic productions, intimate bars for conversation, and outdoor spaces that never feel far from the ocean. She’s a perennial Alaska favorite thanks to ample rail space and wind‑sheltered vantage points.

Sail Radiance if you want ‘scenic first, ship second’ — without giving up a polished evening or a great steak under the stars.
