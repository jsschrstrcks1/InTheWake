## Getting to the ship — and getting home

### Arrival: Friday optional, Saturday required

**Friday, July 10 — optional "Get Acquainted" dinner.** Kristie hosts an 8:30 PM happy-hour gathering at **Currant Bistro** inside the **Sound Hotel Seattle Belltown** (2120 4th Ave). Casual; appetizers and drinks à la carte. Confirm attendance with Kristie if you want a seat saved.

**Where to stay Friday night.** Sound Hotel Belltown is convenient (you're already there for dinner). Anywhere in Belltown, Downtown, or near Pike Place puts you within walking distance of Pier 66.

**Saturday, July 11 — embarkation.**

- **Terminal:** **Bell Street Cruise Terminal at Pier 66**, 2225 Alaskan Way, Seattle. *Different terminal from Pier 91 used by RCL/Princess/HAL.*
- **Sea-Tac (SEA) → Pier 66:** Uber/Lyft 30–40 min, $40–60.
- **If driving:** Bell Street Pier garage directly across from the terminal, ~$33/day, max vehicle height 6'6", reservable in advance.
- **Early luggage drop-off:** **8:00 AM** at the terminal.
- **Embark window:** 11:30 AM – 3:00 PM. **All-aboard 2:00 PM.** Ship departs ~4:00 PM.
- **Saturday morning options after bag drop:** Pike Place Market (quietest before 9 AM); Biscuit Bitch Belltown breakfast; waterfront walk; back to terminal by 2 PM.

### Day 8 debark and the trip home

- **6:30 AM:** Bags must be in the hall the night before — NCL color-codes by tag.
- **7:00 – 9:30 AM:** Walk-off in waves. **Self-disembarkation** (carry all bags off) starts ~7:00 AM, fastest if you have a flight to catch.
- **10:00 AM (latest):** All passengers off.

**Port Valet — use it.** The Port of Seattle's free service transfers your checked bag from the ship directly to your final-destination airline check-in. Walk off Bliss with only your carry-on; bag meets you in baggage claim at home. Available for most major airlines on most departure days; sign-up sheet distributed onboard mid-cruise (typically Day 4 or 5 — watch the *Freestyle Daily*). **The single most useful piece of post-cruise logistics in Seattle.**

**Bag storage** (if Port Valet didn't fit your routing): Pier 66 Crew Center, **$6/bag/day, pickup by 3 PM**.

**Getting to SEA without an Uber — Pier 66 is the pack where this actually works.** From Pier 66, walk 8 minutes to **Westlake Station** and catch the **Link light rail** directly to SEA: **$3.50, ~40 minutes, no transfer**. The cheapest, most reliable airport route from any Seattle cruise pier. Trains run every 8 min.

**Uber/Lyft from Pier 66 to SEA:** $40–60, 25–35 minutes.

**Killing 4–6 hours before an evening flight.** Pier 66 is downtown — most of central Seattle is walkable from the terminal:

- **Pike Place Market** — 10-min walk, uphill on the return.
- **Olympic Sculpture Park** — 5-min walk north along the waterfront. Free.
- **Seattle Aquarium** — 5-min walk south, $40, ~90 min.
- **Space Needle + Chihuly Garden** — 15-min walk or 5-min Uber to Seattle Center; ~90 min combined visit.

**Airport-arrival timing.**

| Flight | Leave Pier 66 by |
|--------|-------------------|
| Domestic, after 5 PM | 2:00 PM (Link) or 2:30 PM (Uber) |
| International, after 7 PM | 3:00 PM |

SEA TSA lines run 30–60 min during Saturday afternoon cruise debark.

### What an at-home contact actually does

If they haven't heard from you for 36+ hours and they're worried:

1. **Don't panic.** Cruise Wi-Fi is unreliable; silence usually means signal, not emergency.
2. **Call the NCL Care Team** at the number above. NCL will confirm you boarded and can relay messages to the ship.
3. **Reach Kristie** if you shared her contact info — she's your in-person on the ship.
4. **If NCL confirms an incident:** US Coast Guard for Alaska waters; US Consulate Vancouver for Victoria; NCL's on-board medical team for medical issues.

### What to pack for emergencies

- Printed list of medications, allergies, and emergency contacts (in your cabin nightstand).
- Photocopy of passport, separate from the passport itself.
- Travel insurance policy number, printed.
- This handoff card, in your phone's notes app and shared with one trusted person ashore.
