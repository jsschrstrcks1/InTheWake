Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hairspray free?

Yes, Hairspray is complimentary and included in your cruise fare. No additional charge.

Do I need reservations for Hairspray?

Reservations are strongly recommended for Broadway musicals — book through the Royal Caribbean app or Cruise Planner before sailing.

Which ships have Hairspray?

Hairspray is currently available on: Symphony of the Seas. Show lineups can change, so verify with Royal Caribbean before sailing.

How long is Hairspray?

Hairspray runs approximately 75 minutes. There is no intermission in the at-sea version.

Is Hairspray appropriate for children?

Hairspray is family-friendly, though younger children (under 5) may have difficulty sitting through the full performance.

Where should I sit for the best view?

Center seats in the middle rows offer the best sightlines. The balcony level provides a good overview of choreography and staging. Arrive 15-20 minutes early.
