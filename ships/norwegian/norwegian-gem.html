First Look: Norwegian Gem

Norwegian Gem is a Jewel Class vessel that launched in October 2007, bringing Norwegian Cruise Line's freestyle cruising concept to new heights. At 93,530 gross tons and carrying 2,394 guests, she offers an intimate yet feature-rich cruise experience with year-round sailings from the Northeast United States.

The ship underwent an extensive refurbishment that introduced The Haven, NCL's luxury ship-within-a-ship concept featuring exclusive suites with private courtyard, restaurant, and dedicated concierge service. Entertainment options include the Stardust Theater, multiple lounges, and a variety of bars throughout the ship.

Dining aboard Norwegian Gem showcases NCL's commitment to variety with numerous specialty restaurants including Le Bistro French cuisine, Cagney's Steakhouse, and Moderno Churrascaria. The ship's freestyle dining philosophy lets guests eat where and when they choose, with no fixed dining times or assigned seating.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options are available on Norwegian Gem?

Norwegian Gem offers excellent dining variety as a Jewel Class ship. Complimentary options include the main dining rooms, Garden Cafe buffet, and O'Sheehan's pub. Specialty restaurants requiring reservations include Cagney's Steakhouse, Le Bistro French cuisine, La Cucina Italian, Teppanyaki Japanese, and Moderno Brazilian steakhouse. The ship also features Orchid Garden Asian cuisine.

How do I find the deck plans for Norwegian Gem?

Deck plans for Norwegian Gem are available through the links on this page. You can also find official deck plans on the Norwegian Cruise Line website or in the NCL app after booking your cruise.

Where does Norwegian Gem sail?

Norwegian Gem primarily sails Caribbean and Bahamas itineraries from New York and other East Coast ports. Check the Norwegian Cruise Line website for current itineraries and departure ports.
