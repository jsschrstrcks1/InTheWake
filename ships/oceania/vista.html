Vista — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

Vista is a Allura Class cruise ship operated by Oceania Cruises. She entered service in 2023, measures 67,000 gross tons, and carries approximately 1,200 guests at double occupancy.

Key Facts

  • Class: Allura Class
  • Year: 2023
  • Tonnage: 67,000 GT
  • Guests: 1,200
  • IMO: TBD

Quick Answer: Vista is Oceania's first new-generation ship — 67,000 GT, 1,200 guests, launched in 2023. She introduces the Allura Class with refreshed public spaces, new dining concepts, and the line's most spacious suites. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Best For: Oceania fans who want the newest ship with the latest design. Vista suits food-focused travelers who value new-ship condition and want Oceania's culinary pedigree with updated public spaces and a refreshed dining lineup.

Vista represents Oceania's first new-build in over a decade, evolving the Oceania Class blueprint with fresh design and slightly fewer guests (1,200 vs. 1,250). She retains the culinary DNA — specialty restaurants included in the fare, no formal nights, destination-rich itineraries — while adding new venue concepts. If you want the proven Oceania Class experience, Marina and Riviera remain excellent. If you want Oceania's most intimate ships, the R Class (684 guests) offers a different feel entirely. Allura (2025) will be Vista's identical sister.
