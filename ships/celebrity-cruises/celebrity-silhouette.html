First Look: Celebrity Silhouette

Celebrity Silhouette is the fourth ship in Celebrity Cruises' acclaimed Solstice Class, debuting in 2011. At 122,210 gross tons and carrying 2,886 guests, Silhouette continues the Solstice Class tradition of modern luxury cruising while offering unique venues and experiences that distinguish it from its sister ships.

The ship features the signature Lawn Club with half an acre of real grass on the top deck, ideal for lawn games, outdoor movies, and al fresco dining. Specialty dining options include the elegant Murano French restaurant, the interactive Qsine creative dining experience, Tuscan Grille Italian steakhouse, and Sushi on Five Japanese cuisine. The AquaSpa offers rejuvenating treatments and the signature Persian Garden relaxation room.

Following extensive renovations in 2020 as part of Celebrity's Revolution program, Celebrity Silhouette features modernized staterooms with contemporary design elements, refreshed public spaces, and enhanced dining venues. The ship offers AquaClass spa-inspired accommodations with exclusive access to Blu restaurant, while The Retreat provides suite guests with a private sanctuary and dedicated concierge service.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options are available on Celebrity Silhouette?

Celebrity Silhouette offers complimentary dining including the main dining room and buffet. Specialty restaurants vary by ship class. Check the dining section above for specific venues.

How do I find the deck plans for Celebrity Silhouette?

Deck plans are available through the links on this page. You can also find official deck plans on the Celebrity Cruises website or in the cruise planner app.

Where does Celebrity Silhouette sail?

Celebrity Silhouette offers Caribbean cruises from Fort Lauderdale and Mediterranean voyages during summer months. The ship features excellent views from the Lawn Club and Rooftop Terrace. Check the Celebrity Cruises website for current itineraries and departure ports.

What makes Celebrity Silhouette special?

Celebrity Silhouette is a Solstice-class ship featuring the Lawn Club with real grass on the top deck, the Hot Glass Show presenting live glassblowing demonstrations, and the innovative Rooftop Terrace venue. The ship carries 2,886 guests and includes AquaClass spa staterooms with exclusive Canyon Ranch dining.
