Frequently Asked Questions

What is Giovanni's Italian on Royal Caribbean?

Royal Caribbean offers two Giovanni's Italian concepts: Giovanni's Italian Kitchen (modern trattoria with hand-tossed pizzas, antipasti, pastas, and shareable plates in a casual atmosphere) and Giovanni's Table (rustic family-style dining with classic Italian pastas, osso buco, and hearty entr√©es in a cozy, romantic setting). They have different menus, pricing, and vibes.

How much does Giovanni's cost?

Italian Kitchen: $14.99 lunch, $34.99 dinner (includes gratuity). Giovanni's Table: $18.99 lunch, $34.99‚Äì$39.99 dinner (varies by ship class). Both offer value through Royal Caribbean's unlimited dining packages.

Do you need reservations for Giovanni's?

Yes, reservations are recommended for both concepts. Book through the Royal Caribbean Cruise Planner before sailing for the best availability, or via the Royal Caribbean app once onboard. Walk-ins may be accommodated based on capacity, but peak times often fill up.

What are the menu highlights at Giovanni's?

Italian Kitchen: Hand-tossed pizzas (Margherita, Pepperoni, Giovanni's with salami & honey), lobster ravioli with sage cream, lasagna Bolognese, chicken parmesan, calamari fritti, gigantic meatball, tiramisu. Giovanni's Table: Osso buco with risotto, lobster ravioli, lasagna Bolognese, shrimp scampi bruschetta, fettuccine Alfredo, classic antipasti, tiramisu and cannoli.
