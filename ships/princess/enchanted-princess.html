Enchanted Princess

Enchanted Princess is a Royal Class cruise ship operated by Princess Cruises. She entered service in 2020, measures 145,281 gross tons, and carries approximately 3,660 guests at double occupancy.

Key Facts

  • Class: Royal Class
  • Year: 2020
  • Tonnage: 145,281 GT
  • Guests: 3,660
  • IMO: TBD

Quick Answer: Enchanted Princess (145,281 GT, 3,660 guests, 2020) is the fifth Royal Class ship — featuring Sky Suites with the largest balconies at sea, a glass-floored SeaWalk, and Princess's full MedallionClass technology suite. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Best For: Mediterranean and Caribbean cruisers who want Princess's modern Royal Class with all the latest refinements — Sky Suites, MedallionClass, and diverse specialty dining.

Enchanted Princess is the fifth Royal Class ship, building on the formula established by Royal Princess. She features Sky Suites, the glass-floored SeaWalk extending over the ocean, and Movies Under the Stars. MedallionClass provides touchless boarding and on-demand services. If you want the most refined Royal Class, Discovery Princess is the last and most polished; for Princess's newest design, the Sphere Class (Sun Princess, Star Princess) is the current flagship.

Key Facts

  • Ship Class: Royal Class — 145,281 GT, 3,660 guests
  • Signature Features: Sky Suites, SeaWalk, MedallionClass, Movies Under the Stars
  • Itineraries: Mediterranean, Caribbean, transatlantic
  • Entered Service: 2020
