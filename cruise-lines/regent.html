Regent Seven Seas Cruises

Quick Answer: The most inclusive luxury at sea. Regent Seven Seas Cruises wraps every voyage in uncompromising elegance — all-suite ships with no more than 750 guests, where shore excursions, fine dining, unlimited drinks, Wi-Fi, and gratuities are all included. With 7 intimate vessels and the legendary Regent Suite, this is cruising without compromise.

Is Regent Seven Seas Right for You?

If the phrase "all-inclusive" speaks to your soul — and you want it to truly mean everything — Regent Seven Seas was built for you. This is the cruise line where shore excursions in every port, specialty dining at every restaurant, premium beverages, unlimited Wi-Fi, and pre-paid gratuities all come standard. No nickel-and-diming, no surprise charges, just pure relaxation from the moment you step aboard.

With ships carrying no more than 750 guests, the experience is intimate and personal. Every accommodation is a suite with a private balcony, and the service ratio ensures staff know your name and your preferences. The Compass Rose restaurant serves exquisite cuisine, while the Regent Suite — the most luxurious suite at sea — sets the standard for opulence afloat.

Ideal for experienced cruisers who appreciate luxury without pretension, couples celebrating special occasions, and discerning travelers who want everything included in one seamless fare. Part of Norwegian Cruise Line Holdings, Regent consistently delivers the gold standard in all-inclusive ocean cruising.

Key Facts at a Glance

  • Brand Identity: Ultra-luxury, all-inclusive cruising — the most inclusive in the world
  • Parent Company: Norwegian Cruise Line Holdings
  • Signature Features: All-suite ships, included shore excursions, Regent Suite, Compass Rose restaurant, unlimited beverages & Wi-Fi, pre-paid gratuities
  • Best For: Luxury travelers wanting all-inclusive, experienced cruisers, special occasions
  • Fleet Size: 7 all-suite ships (max 750 guests)
  • Primary Itineraries: Mediterranean, Northern Europe, Alaska, Caribbean, World Cruises

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included with Regent Seven Seas Cruises?

Regent Seven Seas is the most inclusive luxury cruise line in the world. Every fare includes all-suite accommodations, unlimited shore excursions, specialty dining, unlimited beverages including fine wines and premium spirits, pre-paid gratuities, unlimited Wi-Fi, and roundtrip business-class airfare on select bookings.

What is the Regent Suite?

The Regent Suite is widely regarded as the most luxurious suite at sea. Found aboard Seven Seas Explorer and Seven Seas Splendor, it spans over 4,000 square feet and features a private spa, grand living room, king-sized bed, and a sprawling private balcony with personal hot tub.

Where does Regent Seven Seas cruise?

Regent Seven Seas offers itineraries across the Mediterranean, Northern Europe, Alaska, the Caribbean, Asia, South America, Australia, and Africa. They also operate annual World Cruises that circumnavigate the globe over several months.
