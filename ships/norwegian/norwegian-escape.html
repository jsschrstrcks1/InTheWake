First Look: Norwegian Escape

Norwegian Escape is the lead ship of Norwegian Cruise Line's Breakaway Plus Class, launching in October 2015 as the line's first vessel purpose-built for year-round Caribbean cruising from Miami. At 164,600 gross tons and carrying 4,266 guests, she introduced many innovations that defined NCL's next generation of cruise experiences.

The ship features The Haven, NCL's exclusive ship-within-a-ship concept offering luxury suites with private pool, sundeck, and restaurant. Entertainment highlights include Tony Award-winning Broadway shows like "After Midnight," the thrilling Aqua Park with five multi-story waterslides, and the three-story Ropes Course adventure attraction suspended above the deck.

Dining aboard Norwegian Escape offers unparalleled variety with over 25 dining options. From specialty restaurants like Le Bistro French cuisine and Cagney's Steakhouse to unique concepts like Food Republic's fusion small plates, guests enjoy freestyle dining that lets them eat where and when they want. The Observation Lounge offers panoramic views while enjoying cocktails and light fare.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options are available on Norwegian Escape?

Norwegian Escape offers exceptional dining variety as a Breakaway Plus Class ship. Complimentary options include the main dining rooms, Garden Cafe buffet, and O'Sheehan's pub. Specialty restaurants requiring reservations include Cagney's Steakhouse, Le Bistro French cuisine, Los Lobos Mexican, La Cucina Italian, Ocean Blue seafood, and Teppanyaki. The ship also features Food Republic and Jimmy Buffett's Margaritaville.

How do I find the deck plans for Norwegian Escape?

Deck plans for Norwegian Escape are available through the links on this page. You can also find official deck plans on the Norwegian Cruise Line website or in the NCL app after booking your cruise.

Where does Norwegian Escape sail?

Norwegian Escape offers diverse seasonal itineraries including Caribbean voyages from Miami and Port Canaveral. Check the Norwegian Cruise Line website for current itineraries and departure ports.
