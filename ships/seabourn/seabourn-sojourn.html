Seabourn Sojourn — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

Seabourn Sojourn is an Odyssey Class cruise ship operated by Seabourn Cruise Line. She entered service in 2010, measures 32,346 gross tons, and carries approximately 458 guests at double occupancy with a crew of 330.

Key Facts

  • Class: Odyssey Class
  • Year: 2010
  • Tonnage: 32,346 GT
  • Guests: 458
  • Crew: 330
  • IMO: 9417098

Quick Answer: Seabourn Sojourn is the second Odyssey Class sister — 32,346 GT, 458 guests, launched in 2010. All-suite, all-veranda, with open-seating dining and a popular world-voyage assignment. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Who She's For: Intimate luxury cruisers, particularly those interested in extended voyages and world cruises. Sojourn has a loyal following among Seabourn's world-voyage passengers who appreciate her 458-guest scale on long itineraries.

Seabourn Sojourn is an identical sister to Odyssey and Quest — same 458-guest capacity, same all-suite layout, same dining venues. She has earned a reputation as a world-voyage favorite, and her loyal repeat passengers choose her for extended itineraries. The choice between the three Odyssey Class sisters is driven by itinerary and dates. If you want a larger Seabourn ship, the Encore Class (604 guests) offers more public space. For expedition cruising, Venture and Pursuit (264 guests) are the fleet's adventure ships.

Frequently Asked Questions About Seabourn Sojourn

What dining options are on Seabourn Sojourn?

Seabourn Sojourn has five dining venues — all complimentary. The Restaurant is the open-seating main dining room. Specialty venues include The Grill by Thomas Keller, The Colonnade, The Patio, and Sushi. No surcharges. See the Dining section above.

How big is Seabourn Sojourn?

32,346 GT with 225 suites for 458 guests, served by 330 crew. Launched 2010 as the second Odyssey Class sister.

Which ships are Sojourn's sisters?

Odyssey (2009) and Quest (2011). Same hull, same 458-guest capacity, same dining. Choice is about itinerary.

How does the Odyssey Class compare to the Encore Class?

Odyssey Class (458 guests, 32,346 GT) is smaller and more intimate than Encore Class — Encore and Ovation carry 604 guests at 40,350 GT.

Where does Seabourn Sojourn sail?

Seasonal luxury itineraries including the Mediterranean, Caribbean, Northern Europe, and repositioning voyages. Check the live tracker above for current position.
