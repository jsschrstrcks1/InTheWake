Room Service

Room Service

Royal Caribbean — In-Room Dining (24/7 on most sailings)

Complimentary continental breakfast and all-day in-cabin bites delivered to your door. Add hot favorites for a small per-order delivery fee.

Think of it as a cozy extension of the ship’s restaurants — perfect for sunrise coffee, balcony lunches, or late-night snacks. Order in the Royal App or by phone (stateroom quick dial). Return to the Restaurants hub →

Menu & Price

Price: Continental & cold items are typically $0. Hot items usually incur a flat $7.95 delivery fee + 18% gratuity per order (≈ $9.50 total). Fees and hours can vary by ship/itinerary; check the Royal App once on board.

Tip: Each hot order shares one delivery fee — bundle items together. Suite perks on select tiers may waive hot delivery.

Fees & Suite Notes
  • One delivery fee per hot order (bundle items); continental/cold items are generally free.
  • Suite guests on some tiers may receive complimentary hot delivery — verify in the Royal App or with your stateroom attendant.
  • Beverages like specialty coffee/sodas may carry standard drink pricing unless you have a package.

Special Accommodations

Allergen & Dietary Notes: Note allergies/preferences in the Royal App or when calling — staff can flag orders (e.g., no-nut parfait, no dairy). Vegetarian and lighter options are available; gluten-free adjustments (e.g., no bun/lettuce wrap) on request. Confirm onboard for the latest procedures.

Royal Caribbean Room Service Review 2025: Effortless In-Cabin Indulgence Around the Clock

Introduction

Room Service turns your stateroom into a private dining nook — reliable, crave-worthy bites with minimum fuss and maximum relaxation. From a free continental spread at dawn to a hot cheeseburger at midnight, it nails the balance of quality and ease. On my 7-night Bahamas cruise, I ordered continental breakfast most mornings, plus all-day items for lunch and a late-night snack. The value is strong: cold items at $0; hot items generally carry a flat per-order fee with gratuity.

Food

The complimentary yogurt parfait was a daily win — creamy yogurt, crunchy granola, and berry brightness in a spill-safe cup, paired with a flaky croissant and juice. Midday, the club sandwich (cold) landed as a tidy, deli-style stack with pickle spears — simple, fresh, and free to deliver. For a hot upgrade, the personal cheese pizza arrived bubbling and crisp enough to hold its slice, perfect balcony food. Desserts like chocolate-dipped strawberries delivered a chilled, photogenic finish. It’s not a gourmet showcase, but the focused menu highlights reliable hits that travel well.

Service

Ordering via the app or phone was quick, with delivery typically in 15–20 minutes. Operators clearly called out what’s free vs. fee and noted a nut allergy once so it was flagged thereafter. On busier sea days, waits ticked up slightly, but follow-up calls checked that everything arrived as expected. The team’s friendly, low-pressure approach makes Room Service a trusted cabin ally.

Atmosphere

Because the “dining room” is your room, the vibe is whatever you want — sunrise trays on the balcony, dim lights for a movie night, or PJ lunches after a long excursion. That privacy is the point, and it feels indulgent without costing time.

Conclusion

Dependable food, courteous timing, and made-for-coziness convenience make Room Service an easy yes. The delivery fee and lean menu simply encourage smarter choices (bundle hot items; lean on free continental). Rating: 4.5/5. Great for families, couples, and suite guests; use the app to time orders and bundle for value.
