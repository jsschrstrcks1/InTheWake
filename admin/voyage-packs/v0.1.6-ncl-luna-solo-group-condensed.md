## Getting to the ship — and getting home

### Arrival: Friday recommended, Saturday required

**Friday May 15 — fly in.** Same-day flights to a 3:00 PM all-aboard are risky. Pre-cruise hotel in Brickell, Downtown Miami, or Miami Beach gives you margin.

**Saturday May 16 — embarkation.**

- **Terminal:** **Terminal B, PortMiami** (NCL's regular Miami berth).
- **MIA → Terminal B:** Uber/Lyft $30–45, 20–30 min. Taxi flat-rate $32.
- **FLL → Terminal B:** Uber/Lyft $60–90, 45–60 min.
- **If driving:** Terminal AA garage **$35/day** (~$245/week, firsthand May 2026 — verify); other terminals similar. Offsite Miami long-term lots $7–14/day with shuttle.
- **Embark window:** 11:30 AM – 3:00 PM. **All-aboard 3:00 PM.** Ship departs ~4:00 PM.
- **NCL online check-in** — complete in the NCL app before arrival.

### Day 8 debark and the trip home

- **The night before:** bags in the hall with color-coded tag by ~11 PM.
- **5:30 – 7:00 AM:** Breakfast at Indulge Food Hall or Surfside Café.
- **6:30 AM:** Customs clearance complete. Self-disembarkation begins.
- **7:00 – 9:30 AM:** Walk-off in waves. All passengers off by 10:00 AM.

**Carry-on with you:** change of clothes, all medications, passport, phone charger, valuables.

**Getting from PortMiami to MIA.** Uber/Lyft on Saturday morning, **$30–45, 25–30 minutes**. Surge pricing is real; pre-book if possible.

**Killing 4–6 hours before an evening flight:**

- **Bayfront Park / Bayside Marketplace** — 10-min walk from the terminal.
- **Pérez Art Museum (PAMM)** — 15-min walk. $20 entry. Air-conditioned.
- **Brickell** — short Uber. Cuban coffee, lunch, Uber to MIA when ready.

**Airport-arrival timing.**

| Flight | Leave PortMiami by |
|---|---|
| Domestic, after 4 PM | 1:30 PM |
| International, after 6 PM | 2:30 PM |

MIA TSA lines run 30–60 min during Saturday cruise-return rush.

## Areas of interest during the cruise

### On the ship — Luna spots worth knowing

- **The Penrose Atrium** — Luna's three-deck central gathering space; the orienting landmark and group-gathering venue.
- **Indulge Food Hall** — included quick-casual; multiple stations. The right call when you don't want to commit to a sit-down meal.
- **Ocean Boulevard** — outdoor wraparound walkway on Deck 8. Sailaway viewing and sunrise coffee.
- **Aqua Slidecoaster** — the signature attraction; first hour after opening has zero line.
- **Mandara Spa thermal-suite day pass** — sea-day intro rates appear.
- **Studio Lounge** (Deck 11) — solo-cabin perk. Hosted coffee hour Day 2 morning typically.
- **Penrose Bar** — Tina's gathering spot some evenings; check group communications.

### Sea-day priorities (Day 2 + Day 6)

1. **Sunrise on Ocean Boulevard** — best 30 minutes of the week.
2. **Solo Studio meet-and-greet** Day 2 — NCL hosts this; check the daily program for time.
3. **Aqua Slidecoaster** in the first hour it opens.
4. **Thermal suite at Mandara Spa** — sea-day morning intro rate.
5. **Specialty dining reservation** for Day 6 night (the last sea day) — Cagney's, Le Bistro, Palomar, Onda by Scarpetta all fill.

### Specialty dining picks

- **Cagney's Steakhouse** — the included-line steakhouse standard. Good for a celebration night.
- **Le Bistro** — French. The most romantic specialty room on NCL ships.
- **Onda by Scarpetta** — modern Italian, partnership with Scott Conant.
- **Palomar** — Mediterranean-Latin fusion.
- **Sukhothai** + **Hasuki** — Asian; teppanyaki room is the showy hibachi seating.

### Port-day picks (one each)

- **Puerto Plata** — Damajagua 27 Waterfalls (~$95 ship excursion; $60 indep) for the adventurer, or stay at Amber Cove's pool day for the easier choice.
- **St. Thomas** — Magens Bay beach taxi (~$12 r/t per person) is the highest-value low-stress day. Skykart from Mountain Top is the splurge.
- **Tortola** — Cane Garden Bay taxi (~$15 r/t); back by 1:30 PM for the 2:00 PM all-aboard. The Baths on Virgin Gorda is the postcard but only via a tightly-scheduled ship excursion.
- **Great Stirrup Cay** — NCL's private island under redevelopment. Tender required. Beach + buffet day; manage expectations on amenity completeness.

### Where Tina sets the rhythm

- **Day 1 sailaway** — Penrose Bar typically; verify Tina's communications.
- **Hudson's 5:30 PM nightly** — the recurring group dinner; show up when you want.
- **Studio Lounge** — solo cruisers cluster here regardless of who's hosting.

### Photo spots worth knowing

- **Sailaway from Ocean Boulevard Deck 8** — Star Island superyachts, Miami skyline at golden hour.
- **The Penrose Atrium** at evening — three-deck chandelier moment.
- **Cane Garden Bay** — curve of white sand with the BVI hills behind.
- **Magens Bay** — top-ranked beach panorama.
