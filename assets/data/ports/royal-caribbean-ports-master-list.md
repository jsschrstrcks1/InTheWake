## Notes

- Royal Caribbean's **highest concentration**: Caribbean/Bahamas (Oasis, Freedom, Voyager classes year-round)
- **Seasonal deployments**:
  - Alaska: May-September (Quantum, Ovation, Radiance, Serenade)
  - Europe: April-October (Oasis, Anthem, Odyssey, Brilliance, Rhapsody)
  - Caribbean: Year-round (majority of fleet)
  - Asia: October-April (Spectrum, Ovation seasonally)
- **Private islands**: CocoCay appears on 90%+ of Bahamas itineraries
- **Newest ships**: Icon of the Seas, Star of the Seas focus on 7-night Eastern/Western Caribbean
- **Longest itineraries**: World cruises, transpacific repositionings add 50-100+ rare ports
