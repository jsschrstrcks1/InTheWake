Costa Cruises Fleet

Welcome aboard Costa Cruises! The fleet of 9 ships brings la dolce vita to every voyage — from the LNG-powered Excellence Class flagships to intimate Spirit Class vessels. Italian heritage, authentic cuisine, and Mediterranean itineraries define the Costa experience.

Costa Cruises Guide Ship Quiz Packing Lists

Excellence Class

Costa's LNG-powered flagships at 185K GT. Colosseo atrium, 13 restaurants, Solemio Spa. Italian piazza concept at its grandest.

Signature venues: Colosseo Atrium, Solemio Spa, Teppanyaki, Piazza Colosseo

  • Costa Smeralda
  • Costa Toscana

Dream Class

Italian-themed ships on a Vista-class platform at 135K GT. Piazza Colosseo, Mediterranean flair, vibrant entertainment.

Signature venues: Piazza Colosseo, Samsara Spa, Pizzeria Pummid'Oro

  • Costa Firenze
  • Costa Venezia

Concordia Class

Mid-size 114K GT workhorses with Samsara Spa, grand piazzas, and classic Costa style. Reliable Mediterranean favorites.

Signature venues: Samsara Spa, Grand Bar, Pizzeria Pummid'Oro, Lido Restaurant

  • Costa Diadema
  • Costa Fascinosa
  • Costa Favolosa

Musica Class

114K GT with music-themed decor and Mediterranean character.

Signature venues: Samsara Spa, Grand Bar, Lido Restaurant

  • Costa Pacifica

Spirit Class

Intimate 92K GT vessel. Refined Italian atmosphere ideal for longer voyages.

Signature venues: Samsara Spa, Aperitivo Bar

  • Costa Deliziosa
