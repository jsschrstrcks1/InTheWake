Quick Answer: The Fitness Center is free on all Royal Caribbean ships. Open early morning to late evening with full cardio section, free weights, and weight machines. Located adjacent to Vitality Spa with stunning ocean views. Group classes range from free stretching to paid spinning and yoga. Best times: early morning or port days for empty equipment.

Overview

Royal Caribbean's Fitness Center allows you to maintain your workout routine while cruising, featuring modern equipment and panoramic ocean views that make exercise feel less like a chore. Located adjacent to Vitality Spa on the forward upper decks, the gym is complimentary for all guests.

Equipment Available

  • Cardio: Treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, rowing machines, stair climbers
  • Strength: Free weights, dumbbells, weight machines, cable systems
  • Flexibility: Stretching mats, foam rollers, yoga mats
  • Technology: Most cardio equipment has personal screens with TV/entertainment

Ship Availability

Fitness Center available on all Royal Caribbean ships. Largest facilities on:

  • Icon/Oasis Class: Extensive equipment with dedicated group fitness studio
  • Quantum Class: Multi-level fitness area with outdoor running track
  • Freedom Class: Full-service gym with ocean views

Classes & Services

Complimentary Classes

  • Morning Stretch Free

    Wake up with guided stretching session. Usually on pool deck.

  • Ab Blast Free

    Quick core workout. Check Cruise Compass for schedule.

Premium Classes (Fee)

  • Spinning/Cycling $12-18

    High-energy indoor cycling class with instructor.

  • Yoga $12-18

    Various styles from gentle to power yoga.

  • Pilates $12-18

    Core-focused mat Pilates classes.

  • Personal Training $85-150

    One-on-one training session with certified trainer.

FAQ

Is the gym free on Royal Caribbean?

Yes, equipment use is complimentary. Some group classes have a small fee.

What equipment is available?

Treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowing machines, free weights, and weight machines. Most cardio has personal screens.

Is there a running track?

Most ships have an outdoor jogging track on the sports deck. Laps are typically 0.25-0.33 miles depending on ship.
