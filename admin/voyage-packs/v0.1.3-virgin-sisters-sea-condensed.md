## Getting to the ship — and getting home

### Arrival: Saturday recommended, Sunday required

**Saturday Feb 13 — fly in.** Same-day Sunday flights to a 4:00 PM all-aboard are risky, especially Valentine's weekend. Pre-cruise hotel in Brickell, Downtown Miami, or Miami Beach gives margin.

**Sunday Feb 14 — embarkation.**

- **Terminal:** **PortMiami** (Virgin's regular Miami berth — confirm in your booking).
- **MIA → PortMiami:** Uber/Lyft $30–45, 20–30 min. Taxi flat-rate $32.
- **FLL → PortMiami:** Uber/Lyft $60–90, 45–60 min.
- **If driving:** Terminal AA garage **$35/day** (~$245/week, firsthand May 2026 — verify); offsite long-term lots $7–14/day with shuttle.
- **Embark window:** 1:00 – 4:00 PM (later than other lines). **All-aboard 4:00 PM.** Ship departs 6:00 PM.
- **Sailor App** online check-in — complete before arrival.

### Day 8 debark and the trip home

- **The night before:** bags in the hall by ~10 PM (Virgin's timing is slightly earlier than other lines).
- **6:30 – 8:00 AM:** Breakfast at The Galley or Sun Club Café.
- **7:30 AM:** Customs clearance complete. Self-disembarkation begins.
- **7:30 – 10:00 AM:** Walk-off in waves. All passengers off by 10:00 AM.

**Carry-on with you:** change of clothes, all medications, passport, phone charger, valuables.

**Getting from PortMiami to MIA.** Uber/Lyft on Sunday afternoon, **$30–45, 25–30 min**. Don't overthink it.

**Killing 4–6 hours before an evening flight:**

- **Bayfront Park / Bayside Marketplace** — 10-min walk.
- **Pérez Art Museum Miami (PAMM)** — 15-min walk. $20 entry. Air-conditioned.
- **South Beach / Lincoln Road** — 20-min Uber. Brunch + Art Deco walk.

**Airport-arrival timing.**

| Flight | Leave PortMiami by |
|---|---|
| Domestic, after 4 PM | 1:30 PM |
| International, after 6 PM | 2:30 PM |

## Areas of interest during the cruise

### On the ship — Resilient Lady spots worth knowing

- **The Manor** — two-story nightclub designed with House of Yes. Scarlet Night venue.
- **The Red Room** — transformable theatre running Virgin's in-house immersive shows (*Persephone*, *Booked!*, *Duel Reality*, *Scarlet Night*).
- **Sun Club Café** — poolside Mediterranean lunch; the underrated meal of the week.
- **Squid Ink** — the first tattoo studio at sea, if you want a permanent souvenir.
- **Grounds Club** — specialty coffee; the morning gathering spot.
- **Sip Lounge** — wine bar, hosted tastings on sea-day afternoons.
- **The Athletic Club** — outdoor pool deck + boxing ring. Daytime workouts; nighttime parties.
- **Sea Terrace cabin balconies** — if you booked one, the hammock-style chair changes the texture of the week.

### Sea-day priorities (Day 2, 4, 6 — three of them)

1. **Grounds Club coffee morning** — sea-day mornings are when the place is quietest.
2. **The Test Kitchen lunch** — Virgin's chef-driven tasting venue; shorter and quieter than dinner.
3. **Manor dance class** — salsa, swing, hip-hop; check the daily program.
4. **The Galley** — food-hall-style with multiple stations; not a buffet. Lunch here is the variety play.
5. **Spa pass** — sea-day intro rates.

### The 20+ included restaurants — first-time picks

- **The Wake** — steakhouse + raw bar with tableside martini service. The most refined meal.
- **Pink Agave** — contemporary Mexican; full menu is gluten-free; widest tequila selection at sea.
- **Extra Virgin** — Italian trattoria.
- **Gunbae** — Korean BBQ at round social tables with soju. Best for groups.
- **Razzle Dazzle** — vegetable-forward.
- **The Test Kitchen** — chef's experimental tasting menu.
- **The Pizza Place** — Neapolitan-style, made to order.
- **Lick Me Till Ice Cream** — yes, that's the real name. Free.

**All included with fare.** No upcharges for any of them. Book ahead in the Sailor App.

### Port-day picks (one each)

- **Freeport** — Gold Rock Beach in Lucayan National Park ($40–50 r/t taxi) is one of the prettiest beaches in the Bahamas. Or Port Lucaya Marketplace (~$10 r/t) for the easy half-day.
- **Progreso** — Mérida day-trip via $60-ish indep tour for Yucatán colonial city. Or Uxmal ruins ($90 indep) for the Mayan archaeology day. Or a beach-club day at one of the Progreso resort properties.
- **The Beach Club at Bimini** — Virgin's private beach. Lunch buffet, lounge chairs, multiple pools, all included. Cabana upgrade $200–500 if you want premium.

### Scarlet Night (Thursday Day 6)

**Dress red.** Drag Brunch in the afternoon may roll into the evening's Manor party. The fleet-wide signature event — every Virgin sailing does it once. Don't miss.

### Where Tina sets the rhythm

- **Day 1 sailaway** — likely Athletic Club pool deck or Sun Club Café terrace; verify Tina's communications.
- **Group dinners** — Tina often does a Pink Agave or Extra Virgin night; she'll book ahead with party size.
- **Sip Lounge or Grounds Club** — informal gathering spots.

### Photo spots worth knowing

- **Sailaway from the Athletic Club pool deck** — Miami skyline + Government Cut at golden hour.
- **The Manor staircase** — Scarlet Night, red lighting, the whole house dressed.
- **Sea Terrace balcony hammock chair** — sunrise coffee, your own pace.
- **Bimini Beach Club lighthouse spit** — the iconic Bahamas private-island shot.
