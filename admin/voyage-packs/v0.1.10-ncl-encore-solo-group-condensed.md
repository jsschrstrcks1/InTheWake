## Getting to the ship — and getting home

### Arrival: Sunday recommended, Monday required

**Sunday Oct 11 — fly into Vancouver (YVR).** Same-day Monday flights to a 4:00 PM all-aboard are risky given border-crossing or YVR weather delays. Pre-cruise hotel in downtown / Coal Harbour / Gastown / Yaletown puts you walking distance to Canada Place.

**Monday Oct 12 — embarkation.**

- **Terminal:** **Canada Place** — the white sail-roofed downtown terminal.
- **YVR → Canada Place:** Taxi flat-rate ~CA$35–45, 30–40 min. **Canada Line SkyTrain** ~CA$10, 30 min (transfer at Waterfront Station, 5-min walk to terminal).
- **From Seattle:** Quick Coach / Quick Shuttle ~$60–75 USD, 4 hours. Border can run 30–90 min on cruise day.
- **Embark window:** 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM. **All-aboard 4:30 PM.** Ship stays overnight in Vancouver.
- **NCL online check-in via the NCL app** before arrival.

### Day 7 debark and the trip home

- **The night before:** bags in the hall by ~11 PM with color-coded tag.
- **6:00 – 8:00 AM:** Breakfast at Garden Café or The Local.
- **6:30 AM:** Customs clearance complete. Self-disembarkation begins.
- **7:00 – 10:00 AM:** Walk-off in waves. All passengers off by 10:30 AM.

**Carry-on with you:** change of clothes, all medications, passport, phone charger, valuables.

**Getting from San Pedro / World Cruise Center to LAX:** Uber/Lyft **$40–55, 30–45 minutes** (traffic-dependent). Karmel Shuttle (port-to-LAX) ~$25/person with advance booking. **Long Beach Airport (LGB)** is $20–30 Uber, 15 min — smaller but quieter.

**Killing 4–6 hours before an evening flight:**

- **Battleship USS Iowa Museum** — 10-min walk from the terminal. $25 entry, ~90 minutes.
- **San Pedro Fish Market** — 15-min Uber. Massive seafood market + restaurant.
- **Manhattan Beach / Hermosa Beach** — 30-min Uber. Classic SoCal beach towns.

**Airport-arrival timing for LAX:**

| Flight | Leave San Pedro by |
|---|---|
| Domestic, after 3 PM | 11:30 AM |
| International, after 6 PM | 1:30 PM |

LAX TSA on Sunday afternoons runs 30–90 min. Build margin.

## Areas of interest during the cruise

### On the ship — Encore spots worth knowing

- **Observation Lounge** (Deck 15 forward) — heated, glass-roofed, panoramic. The trip's best 30 minutes are sunrise here (Pacific sunrise off port; ocean stretching to horizon off starboard).
- **Manhattan Room** (Deck 7 aft) — main dining room with live music most evenings. Most atmospheric of the included rooms.
- **The two-level go-kart track** (Encore Speedway) at the aft — the longest two-level track at sea when she launched in 2019 (NCL's Joy brought go-karts to sea first, in 2017). Paid (~$15/race). First hour after opening empty.
- **Aqua Park** with Aqua Racer (twin slides, race a friend) and Ocean Loops (the loop slide). Both free.
- **Vibe Beach Club** — adults-only with private pool. $99/day or $399/week pre-cruise.
- **Galaxy Pavilion** — VR + immersive games arcade. Paid per experience.
- **Studio Lounge** (solo-cabin perk) — hosted coffee hour Day 2 morning typically.
- **The Cavern Club** — Beatles tribute most nights (verify it's still running).

### Sea-day priorities (Day 4 — Thursday)

1. **Sunrise on the Observation Lounge** — Pacific to the horizon. Best 30 minutes of the week.
2. **Bonine the morning of** if you're prone to motion — real Pacific swells possible.
3. **The race track + Galaxy Pavilion first hour** they open — empty.
4. **Mandara Spa thermal suite** — sea-day intro rate.
5. **Port talk for San Francisco** — knows the Golden Gate approach timing.

### Specialty dining picks

- **Cagney's Steakhouse** — celebration meal; reliably good.
- **Le Bistro** — French; the most romantic specialty.
- **Food Republic** — pan-Asian small plates; the most distinctive room on Encore.
- **Teppanyaki** — hibachi; group-friendly round tables.
- **Q Texas Smokehouse** — Texas BBQ.

### Port-day picks (one each)

- **Vancouver (overnight)** — go ashore for dinner. Gastown or Yaletown (10–15 min walk). Or do a 7 AM walk through Stanley Park before the ship gets moving.
- **Victoria, BC** — Inner Harbour walk + Parliament tour (free). Or commit to Butchart Gardens (~$60 + ~$60–75 transport, 3–4 hours total).
- **San Francisco (overnight)** — Golden Gate at dawn or sunset if timing aligns. Friday night dinner in the city. Saturday morning Ferry Building farmers market 8 AM – 2 PM.

### The two photogenic bridge passages

- **Lions Gate Bridge** (Vancouver) — Tuesday morning/afternoon as the ship departs. Suspension bridge, ~60 ft clearance for Encore. Stanley Park to port.
- **Golden Gate Bridge** (San Francisco) — Friday morning approach and/or Saturday evening departure. Best photograph at sunset.

### Where Allison sets the rhythm

- **Day 1 settle-in** — likely Observation Lounge or one of the bars. Watch her communications.
- **Group dinners** — Allison typically books one or two specialty group nights (Cagney's, Le Bistro, or Food Republic). RSVP early.
- **Studio Lounge** — solo cruisers cluster here regardless of who's hosting.

### Photo spots worth knowing

- **Sailaway from Observation Lounge** — Vancouver skyline + Coast Mountains at dawn (Mon overnight or Tue departure).
- **Lions Gate Bridge passage** — Stanley Park on port; Vancouver skyline behind.
- **Golden Gate approach at dawn** — fog optional but signature.
- **Victoria Parliament Buildings illuminated** — late afternoon as ship departs.
- **Golden Gate from departing San Francisco** at sunset — the trip's most spectacular photograph if timing aligns.
