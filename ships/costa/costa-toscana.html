Costa Toscana — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

Costa Toscana is an Excellence Class cruise ship operated by Costa Cruises. She entered service in 2022, measures 185,010 gross tons, and carries approximately 5,324 guests at double occupancy with a crew of 1,678.

Key Facts

  • Class: Excellence Class
  • Year: 2022
  • Tonnage: 185,010 GT
  • Guests: 5,324
  • Crew: 1,678
  • IMO: 9781891

Quick Answer: Costa Toscana is the second Excellence Class ship — 185,010 GT, 5,324 guests, launched in 2022. LNG-powered sister to Costa Smeralda with Tuscan-themed design and a distinctly European cruise atmosphere. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Who She's For: Travelers who want Costa's newest mega-ship experience on Mediterranean or repositioning itineraries. Toscana suits those who enjoyed Smeralda's Italian-forward style and want the same ship class on different routes.

Costa Toscana is Smeralda's near-identical sister — same 185,010 GT hull, same LNG propulsion. Where Smeralda's MDRs honour Italian commedia dell'arte characters, Toscana's are themed after Tuscan landscape and culture (La Maremma, L'Oliveto, Il Vigneto Osteria, Le Anfore). The onboard experience is Italian-forward with multilingual service and cuisine that emphasizes regional Italian dishes. She and Smeralda are the only Excellence Class ships, so the choice between them is about itinerary. For a smaller Costa experience, the Vista Class (Firenze, Venezia) and Concordia Class ships offer a different scale.

Frequently Asked Questions About Costa Toscana

What dining options are on Costa Toscana?

Tuscan-themed bistro-style main dining rooms (L'Oliveto, La Maremma, Il Vigneto Osteria, Le Anfore) plus the loyalty-only L'Argentario and the La Sagra dei Sapori buffet are complimentary. Specialty venues with cover charges include Archipelago (Michelin-inspired Mediterranean), Teppanyaki, Sushino, and the family/kids Squok concept. See the Dining section above.

How big is Costa Toscana?

185,010 GT, 5,324 guests at double occupancy, 1,678 crew. Excellence Class, entered service 2022. Sister to Costa Smeralda.

Where does Costa Toscana sail?

Western Mediterranean year-round itineraries from Italian ports. Check the live tracker above for current position.
