### Pass Criteria:
- Ideal match appears in top 3 results: **EXCELLENT**
- Acceptable match appears in top 3 results: **GOOD**

### Fail Criteria:
- Red flag ship appears as #1 recommendation: **CRITICAL FAILURE**
- All top 3 results are inappropriate: **FAILURE**
