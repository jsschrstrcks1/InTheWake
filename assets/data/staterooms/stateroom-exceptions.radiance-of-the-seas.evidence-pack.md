Human-Readable Summary
This dataset compiles persistent stateroom exceptions for Radiance of the Seas based on recurring reports from CruiseCritic, Reddit, CruiseMummy, CruiseDeckPlans, and YouTube. Focused on structural issues like obstructed views, noise from public areas, and connecting door sound bleed. 18 exceptions identified, conservative to avoid subjective complaints. Trust scores average 84, with high consensus on view obstructions. Data spans 2010-2024, weighted post-refit (2016, 2021, 2024). Minimum 5 reports per exception for inclusion.
