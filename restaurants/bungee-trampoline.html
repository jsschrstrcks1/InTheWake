Bungee Trampoline

Royal Caribbean — Activities & Sports

Quick Answer: Bungee Trampoline is a complimentary high-flying activity on select Royal Caribbean ships. Guests are harnessed to bungee cords and bounce on trampolines, launching up to 20 feet in the air. Great for all ages meeting weight requirements. Staff operate the equipment and ensure safety.

Best For: Kids and teens, families, thrill seekers, and anyone wanting to experience weightlessness and aerial acrobatics safely.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Weight Range: ~25-200 lbs (varies by ship)
  • Duration: ~5 minutes per session
  • Equipment: Harness provided
  • Location: Sports deck area

Bounce to new heights with bungee-assisted jumps.

The bungee trampoline combines the fun of trampolining with the support of bungee cords, letting you bounce much higher than possible on a regular trampoline. It's safe, exhilarating, and a favorite among kids who can go again and again during the cruise. Return to the Restaurants hub →

The Experience

Cost: Free · Duration: ~5 minutes · Setup: Quick harness fitting

What to Expect

  • Harness fitting by staff
  • Bungee cord attachment
  • Individual trampoline station
  • Bounce up to 20 feet high
  • Try flips and tricks

Skills to Try

  • High bounces
  • Front flips
  • Back flips
  • Twist jumps
  • Seat drops

Safety Features

  • Full body harness
  • Multiple bungee attachment points
  • Trained operators
  • Cushioned landing surface
  • Weight-appropriate cord tension

The bungee cords are adjusted for each person's weight, ensuring safe and controlled bouncing regardless of size.

Ships with Bungee Trampoline
  • Select Freedom Class ships
  • Select Voyager Class ships
  • Select smaller vessels with sports facilities
  • Note: Not all ships have this activity—check your ship's amenities

Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: Bungee trampoline requires the ability to stand and jump. Weight limits apply for safety (typically 25-200 lbs). Guests with back, neck, or joint problems should consult a physician before participating. Staff can discuss individual situations.

Where You'll Find It

The bungee trampoline is typically located on the sports deck near other activities like the rock climbing wall and basketball court. It's usually an open-air installation.

Best Times to Go

  • Morning: Shortest wait times
  • Port Days: Less crowded with guests ashore
  • After Lunch: Before afternoon rush
  • Avoid: Sea day afternoons (busiest times)

Checking Availability

Not all Royal Caribbean ships have bungee trampolines. Check your ship's deck plans or amenities list before sailing. Operating hours are listed in the daily Cruise Compass.

4 ★ out of 5 Freedom of the Seas • Family Cruise

Bungee Trampoline Review: Kids' Favorite Activity

Introduction. My 8-year-old daughter went on the bungee trampoline six times during our cruise. Each time, she came back grinning and asking to go again. It became her favorite thing on the ship.

The Setup

The staff were efficient at getting kids harnessed and ready. The harness fits around the waist and thighs, attached to bungee cords on either side. Within a minute of stepping onto the trampoline, she was bouncing high into the air.

The Experience

She started with simple high bounces, then got brave enough to try front flips. The bungee support made it safe—even when her flip wasn't perfect, the cords kept her from falling. By the end of the cruise, she was doing backflips confidently.

Parent Perspective

Lines were manageable—usually 10-15 minutes during busy times. Early morning had almost no wait. It's free, which meant we didn't feel guilty letting her go multiple times. Great activity to burn off energy before dinner.

Conclusion

If your ship has the bungee trampoline and you're traveling with kids in the right weight range, they'll love it. Simple, safe, and endlessly entertaining for the younger crowd. Rating: 4/5.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the bungee trampoline safe?

Yes, the bungee trampoline is designed for safety. The harness and bungee cords prevent falls, and trained staff supervise all sessions. Follow instructions for a safe experience.

Can adults use the bungee trampoline?

Adults can use it if they meet the weight requirements (usually up to 200 lbs). It's designed primarily for kids but adults within the weight range can participate.

How high can you bounce?

With the bungee assistance, you can bounce 15-20 feet in the air—much higher than a regular trampoline allows.

Do I need to make a reservation?

Usually no reservation is needed—it's first-come, first-served. Just show up during operating hours listed in the Cruise Compass.

Does every ship have a bungee trampoline?

No, not all Royal Caribbean ships have this activity. Check your specific ship's amenities before sailing or ask at Guest Services once onboard.
