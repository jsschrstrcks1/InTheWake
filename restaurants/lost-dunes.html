Lost Dunes

Royal Caribbean — Mini Golf

Quick Answer: Lost Dunes is the themed mini golf course on Icon Class ships. The immersive desert-dunes theme creates a unique atmosphere as you navigate challenging holes with ocean views. A complimentary outdoor activity perfect for families, friendly competition, or a relaxing afternoon on deck.

Best For: Families with kids. Groups seeking friendly competition. Casual outdoor activity. Anyone wanting fresh air and fun.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Theme: Desert dunes
  • Location: Outdoor deck
  • Ships: Icon Class
  • Ages: All ages welcome

Putt your way through paradise.

Lost Dunes brings an adventurous desert theme to mini golf at sea. The course winds through themed obstacles and challenging holes while you enjoy ocean breezes and stunning views. Clubs and balls are provided—just show up and play. It's a great family activity that doesn't take too long and provides a nice break from the pools and loungers. Return to the Restaurants hub →

Frequently Asked Questions

Is mini golf free?

Yes, completely complimentary including equipment.

Do I need reservations?

No, just show up and play when there's an opening.

How long does a round take?

About 20-30 minutes for the 9-hole course.

Is it appropriate for young kids?

Yes, all ages can enjoy. Staff can assist young ones.

What if it rains?

The course closes during inclement weather for safety.

Can we keep score?

Yes, scorecards and pencils are provided.
