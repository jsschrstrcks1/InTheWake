Frequently Asked Questions

What is On The Rocks on Virgin Voyages?

The largest bar onboard, set in the Roundabout atrium on Deck 6. Aged whiskies, craft cocktails, and live entertainment most nights.

How much does On The Rocks cost?

On The Rocks is an a la carte venue — you pay per item ordered. Prices vary by selection.

What is the dress code for On The Rocks?

Come as you are. On The Rocks is a casual venue — pool attire and casual clothes are fine. Virgin Voyages has no formal nights.

Is On The Rocks included in drink packages?

Most drinks at On The Rocks are covered by the Virgin Voyages Bar Tab package. Premium and specialty selections may have an upcharge. Non-alcoholic options are available.

What are the signature drinks at On The Rocks?

On The Rocks features Aged Whiskies, Craft Cocktails, Premium Spirits, Wine and Beer, and more. Ask the bartender for the house specialty.
