Vision of the Seas

Vision is the namesake of a class built for scenery: walls of glass, a tranquil adults‑only Solarium, and evenings that glow in the Viking Crown Lounge.

Her itineraries favor destinations over sea‑day spectacles; the ship becomes your floating picture window between ports.

People love the balance: unhurried mornings, port‑first afternoons, and music‑rich nights in cozy venues.

Dining leans familiar and well‑executed, with the Main Dining Room anchoring each evening.

Choose Vision when you want the world outside to be the headliner.
