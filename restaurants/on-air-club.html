On Air Club

Royal Caribbean — Recording Studio Karaoke

Quick Answer: On Air Club is Royal Caribbean's innovative recording studio-themed karaoke venue exclusive to Quantum Class ships. Beyond traditional karaoke, it features private recording booths where you and your friends can record your own music video with professional lighting, sound, and green screen effects — then take home the recording. All complimentary.

Best For: Groups of friends, families creating memories, anyone who's dreamed of recording a music video, karaoke enthusiasts looking for something different.

Key Facts

  • Price: Complimentary (drinks at bar extra)
  • Location: SeaPlex area, Deck 15
  • Recording Booths: Private rooms for music video creation
  • Take Home: Download or email your music video
  • Hours: Varies; check Cruise Compass

Because regular karaoke wasn't innovative enough for Royal Caribbean.

On Air Club takes the cruise karaoke experience to another level. Sure, you can do traditional karaoke in the main lounge area. But the real draw is the private recording booths where your group becomes the stars of your own music video. The technology handles everything — lighting, camera work, special effects — so you just focus on the performance. It's a unique souvenir that captures the fun of your cruise. The Experience

All Activities: Complimentary · Recordings: Downloadable · No reservations required

Recording Booth Experience

  1. Sign up: Check in at the venue; booths are first-come or reservable via app
  2. Choose your song: Browse thousands of tracks across all genres
  3. Enter the booth: Professional studio setup with lighting and cameras
  4. Perform: Give it your all — the cameras are rolling!
  5. Review & save: Watch your video and get instructions to download

What Makes It Special

  • Green Screen Effects: Choose from various virtual backgrounds
  • Professional Lighting: Studio-quality setup makes everyone look good
  • Multiple Cameras: Automatic camera switching for music video feel
  • Special Effects: Video effects add polish to your performance
  • Privacy: Perform without the pressure of a public audience

Traditional Karaoke

The main lounge area of On Air Club also offers traditional karaoke with a stage, professional sound, and an audience of fellow cruisers. It's the best of both worlds — record privately in the booths or perform publicly on stage.

Recording booth sessions last about 15-20 minutes. Popular times fill up quickly — the app reservation system (when available) helps secure your slot. The venue also functions as a daytime hangout space.

Accessibility & Environment

Accessibility Notes: On Air Club's main area and some recording booths are wheelchair accessible. For specific booth accessibility, speak with venue staff. For other needs, contact Royal Caribbean's Access Department.

Venue Details

  • Atmosphere: Modern, tech-forward, recording studio aesthetic
  • Seating: Lounge seating in main area; standing in booths
  • Bar: Full service available
  • Group Size: Booths accommodate 2-4 performers typically

On Air Club Review: We Made a Music Video

Introduction. Our group of four had one goal: make an unforgettable music video of "Bohemian Rhapsody." On Air Club delivered beyond expectations.

The Process

We signed up on the app and got a 9 PM booth slot. The venue has a cool recording studio vibe — think vinyl record décor, neon lights, and professional equipment. When our time came, staff walked us through the setup: choose your song, pick a background effect, and get positioned.

The Recording

The booth was surprisingly professional. Multiple cameras automatically switched angles during our performance. The green screen let us choose a "rock concert" background. The sound was great through headphones, lyrics displayed clearly, and we could see ourselves on screen. We committed fully — head banging during the guitar solo, dramatic poses during "Mama, just killed a man."

The Result

The finished video looked legitimately good. The automatic editing added transitions and effects that made it feel like an actual music video. We downloaded it immediately and have watched it approximately 47 times since. It's the perfect cruise souvenir — way better than another magnet.

Conclusion

Rating: 4.4/5. On Air Club is a genuinely innovative addition to cruise entertainment. The recording booth experience is unique and creates lasting memories. Highly recommend for groups of friends or families wanting something different. Book early — slots fill up.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is On Air Club free?

Yes, On Air Club is completely complimentary. Recording booth sessions, music video creation, and traditional karaoke are all included in your cruise fare. Drinks at the bar are extra or covered by drink packages.

Can I get a copy of my recording?

Yes! Your music video can be downloaded or emailed to you after your session. Staff will provide instructions. It's a great souvenir to share with friends and family back home.

How many people can fit in a recording booth?

Recording booths typically accommodate 2-4 performers comfortably. Perfect for couples, friends, or families. Larger groups may need multiple sessions or can use the traditional karaoke stage.

Do I need reservations?

Recording booth sessions can be reserved through the Royal Caribbean app on some ships, or are first-come, first-served at the venue. Popular evening times fill up quickly, so booking ahead helps.

What's the difference between On Air Club and Spotlight Karaoke?

On Air Club (Quantum Class) features private recording booths for creating music videos you can take home. Spotlight Karaoke (other ships) is traditional stage karaoke. Both offer great karaoke experiences, but On Air adds the recording studio twist.
