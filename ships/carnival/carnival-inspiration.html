Carnival Inspiration carried a champion's blessing from Mary Anne Shula, wife of legendary NFL coach Don Shula. She served Long Beach for 24 years, offering Mexican Riviera itineraries to California families.

Dining Venues

Carnival Inspiration featured classic Fantasy Class dining venues including the main dining room and buffet. She received Fun Ship 2.0 upgrades in 2016 including Guy's Burger Joint and BlueIguana Cantina. The ship offered traditional dining times and flexible casual options.

NFL Royalty's Blessing

Carnival Inspiration carried a champion's blessing. Mary Anne Shula, wife of legendary Miami Dolphins coach Don Shula — the winningest coach in NFL history — christened her in 1996. That connection to sports excellence defined the sixth Fantasy Class ship.

The Shula name meant perfection. Don Shula led the only undefeated team in NFL history. His wife blessed a ship that would pursue its own form of excellence: 24 years of reliable service, delivering affordable cruises to California families.

Inspiration primarily served Long Beach, offering Mexican Riviera itineraries alongside her sister Imagination. Together they gave California two Fantasy Class options, doubling the West Coast's access to Fun Ship cruising.

Frequently Asked Questions

When was Carnival Inspiration retired?

Carnival Inspiration was retired in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic after 24 years of service. She was scrapped at Aliaga, Turkey in 2022.

Who was Carnival Inspiration's godmother?

Mary Anne Shula, wife of legendary Miami Dolphins coach Don Shula — the winningest coach in NFL history — christened Carnival Inspiration in 1996.

Where did Carnival Inspiration sail from?

Inspiration primarily sailed from Long Beach, California, offering Mexican Riviera itineraries alongside her sister ship Imagination.

How many ships were in the Fantasy Class?

Eight ships comprised the Fantasy Class: Fantasy, Ecstasy, Sensation, Fascination, Imagination, Inspiration, Elation, and Paradise. Only Elation and Paradise remain in service today.

What made Carnival Inspiration special?

Beyond her NFL royalty connection through the Shula family, Inspiration served California families for 24 years, making cruising accessible to millions on the West Coast.
