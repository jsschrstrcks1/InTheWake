RipCord by iFLY

Royal Caribbean — Activities & Sports

Quick Answer: RipCord by iFLY is a complimentary indoor skydiving simulator available on Quantum Class ships. Experience the thrill of freefall with professional iFLY instructors—no experience needed. Flight suit, helmet, and goggles provided. Book early through the Royal Caribbean app as slots fill quickly.

Best For: Thrill seekers, bucket list adventurers, families with older kids, and anyone wanting a unique once-in-a-lifetime cruise experience.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary (reservation required)
  • Age: 3+ years old
  • Duration: ~1-2 minutes of flight time per session
  • Equipment: Flight suit, helmet, goggles provided
  • Weight Limit: Up to 230 lbs (varies)

Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: RipCord requires physical ability to maintain body position in high winds. Weight and health restrictions apply for safety. Guests with certain medical conditions (back/neck injuries, heart conditions, pregnancy) cannot participate. Staff can discuss individual situations during the pre-flight briefing.

Where You'll Find It

RipCord is located on the aft upper deck of Quantum Class ships, near the FlowRider and North Star. The distinctive vertical wind tunnel is visible from the pool deck.

Booking Tips

  • Book Early: Reserve through Royal Caribbean app as soon as booking opens
  • Sea Days: Most popular—book port day slots for easier availability
  • Arrive Early: Check in 15-20 minutes before your slot

RipCord Review: Flying at Sea

Introduction. I've always wanted to try skydiving but was too scared to actually jump out of a plane. RipCord gave me the freefall experience in a safe, controlled environment—and it was absolutely incredible.

The Training

A quick 15-minute session covered body positioning, hand signals, and safety procedures. The instructors were professional and reassuring. They made it clear they'd be in the tunnel with us the entire time, guiding our movements.

The Flight

Those 90 seconds of flight time felt both eternal and over in a flash. The wind is intense—around 120 mph—and you genuinely feel weightless. The instructor had me doing basic turns and elevation changes by the end. When they lifted me higher in the tunnel, I couldn't stop grinning.

Tips

Book this immediately when reservations open. We booked a port day slot and had no issues, while sea day times were completely full within hours. Remove all jewelry and wear comfortable clothes (they provide a flight suit over your clothes).

Conclusion

This was the highlight of our cruise—something we'll never forget. The fact that it's included in the cruise fare makes it an incredible value. If your ship has RipCord, don't miss it. Rating: 5/5.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the RipCord flight?

Each flight session lasts approximately 1-2 minutes of actual flight time, plus training beforehand. While it sounds short, it's incredibly intense and memorable.

Is there a weight limit?

Yes, typically around 230 pounds maximum, though this can vary. The limit exists because the wind speed required to lift heavier guests becomes unsafe.

Can kids do RipCord?

Yes, children as young as 3 can participate if they meet the minimum weight requirement (usually around 40 lbs) and can follow instructions.

What if I'm scared of heights?

Many guests with height fears love RipCord because you're in an enclosed tunnel just a few feet off the ground. It's the sensation of flight without actual height exposure.

Can I buy photos or video?

Yes, professional photo and video packages are available for purchase. Staff photograph and record your flight, making for great souvenirs.
