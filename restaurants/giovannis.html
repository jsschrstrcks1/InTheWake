Giovanni’s Italian Kitchen — Details

Positioning: casual trattoria with hand-tossed pizzas, antipasti, hearty pastas, and Nonna’s classics. Launched fleetwide 2019→ with ongoing rollouts.

  • Price: Lunch $14.99; Dinner $34.99 (incl. gratuity). Packages reduce cost.
  • Hours: Sea-day lunch; nightly dinner (check app for your sailing).
  • Highlights: Calamari Fritti; Gigantic Meatball; Lobster Ravioli; Lasagna Bolognese; Chicken Parm; pizza “Giovanni’s” (salami + honey).
  • Who will love it: Families, groups, pizza-people, shareable-plate fans.
Sample Menu & Pricing
  • Antipasti: Calamari Fritti; Stromboli; Meatball (shareable)
  • Insalate: Caprese; Caesar
  • Pizza: Margherita; Pepperoni; Giovanni’s (salami, honey)
  • Pastas: Lobster Ravioli (sage cream); Fettuccine Alfredo (+chicken); Lasagna Bolognese
  • Entrees: Chicken Parmigiana; Osso Buco (Nonna’s); Grilled Mahi Puttanesca
  • Dolci: Tiramisu; Cannoli; Gelato

Lunch focuses on pizza + lighter plates; dinner adds fuller entrées. Actual menus rotate by sailing.

Giovanni’s Table — Details

Positioning: rustic, family-style Italian with generous pastas and classics in a cozy, romantic setting. Long-time Royal staple.

  • Price: Lunch $18.99; Dinner $34.99–$39.99 (incl. gratuity). Packages available.
  • Hours: Sea-day lunch; nightly dinner (check app for your sailing).
  • Highlights: Lasagna Bolognese; Osso Buco with risotto; Lobster Ravioli; Shrimp Scampi Bruschetta; classic antipasti.
  • Who will love it: Date-nighters, carb-comfort seekers, celebration dinners.
Sample Menu & Pricing
  • Antipasti: Calamari; Stromboli; Meatball (shareable)
  • Soups/Salads: Minestrone; Caprese; Italian Sausage Soup
  • Pastas: Lasagna Bolognese; Fettuccine Alfredo; Lobster Ravioli
  • Entrees: Osso Buco; Chicken Parmigiana; Filet (select ships)
  • Dolci: Tiramisu; Cannoli; Cheesecake

Lunch is abbreviated; dinner menu is broader with heavier mains. Rotations vary by ship.

Which One Should You Book?

  • Want pizza + shareables? Pick Italian Kitchen (lunch is a value play).
  • Want cozy, classic, and hearty? Pick Giovanni’s Table (great for occasions).
  • Using a dining package? Either is solid; Kitchen tends to feel “broader” for groups.
  • With kids? Kitchen’s pizzas keep everyone happy; both have kid options.

Content updated for 2025 sailings. Menus, prices, and hours are subject to change by ship and itinerary.
