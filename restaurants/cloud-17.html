Cloud 17 Review: Guest Experience Summary

Introduction. Cloud 17 is an adults-only pool bar on Icon-class ships, offering a serene escape with premium cocktails and relaxed vibes away from the family pools.

Food & Drinks

Craft cocktails, frozen drinks, premium spirits, beer, and wine. The menu leans tropical and refreshing — perfect for poolside sipping. Some light snacks available.

Service

Attentive poolside service. Order from your lounger or at the bar. The adults-only setting keeps things calm and service efficient.

Atmosphere / Ambiance

Tranquil adults-only pool area with comfortable loungers and a sophisticated vibe. The elevated location provides great views. A peaceful retreat from the larger pools.

Conclusion

Rating: 4/5. Cloud 17 is the ideal escape for adults seeking quiet poolside drinks without the family crowd. Premium vibes and solid cocktails.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need reservations for Cloud 17?

No reservations needed — just walk in and grab a seat or stand at the bar.

What is the dress code for Cloud 17?

Resort casual. Swimwear is fine with a cover-up; no formal attire required.

Is Cloud 17 included in my cruise fare?

Drinks are priced individually or included with a drink package. Check your package details for coverage.
