About This Ship

The seventh Oasis-class ship represents Royal Caribbean's continued investment in the world's largest cruise ship class. This future vessel is currently on order with an expected delivery in 2028.

As a member of the Oasis Class, this ship will join her sister ships in offering revolutionary neighborhood designs, thrilling entertainment, and world-class dining options.

More details about the ship's name, features, and itineraries will be announced by Royal Caribbean closer to delivery.

Dining Venues on Unnamed Oasis VII (2028)

As an Oasis-class ship, this vessel is expected to feature signature dining venues including the main dining room, Windjammer Marketplace, Central Park restaurants, and Boardwalk eateries. Full venue details will be confirmed closer to the ship's 2028 debut.
