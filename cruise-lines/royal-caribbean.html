Royal Caribbean

Neighborhood-style ships and Broadway-bold nights; surf, slides, and quiet corners for catching breath. We chart the classes from Icon down to Vision, then point you toward the ship—and supper—that suits your crew. We do this plainly, gratefully, and with care. Soli Deo Gloria.

Dress Code Aboard

Casual: Jeans, polos, sundresses, blouses. Swimwear stays poolside; shorts welcome at breakfast and lunch.

Smart Casual: Collared shirts, dresses, skirts, blouses, pantsuits; jackets welcome.

Formal (Dress Your Best): Suits and ties, tuxedos, or evening gowns. 5-night: one · 6–10: two · 11–14: three · 15+: up to four (captain’s discretion). No one is turned away for wearing smart casual on Dress Your Best nights.

Chef’s Table observes formal; most venues are smart casual.

Shore calls: modest attire for churches and sacred sites; customs vary—see Ports.
