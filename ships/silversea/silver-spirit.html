Silver Spirit — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

Silver Spirit is a Spirit Class cruise ship operated by Silversea Cruises. She entered service in 2009, measures 36,009 gross tons, and carries approximately 540 guests at double occupancy.

Key Facts

  • Class: Spirit Class
  • Year: 2009
  • Tonnage: 36,009 GT
  • Guests: 540
  • IMO: 9437866

Quick Answer: Silver Spirit (36,009 GT, 540 guests, 2009) is the sole Spirit Class ship — lengthened in 2018 by 15 meters to add more suites and public space, making her Silversea's mid-size sweet spot. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Best For: Luxury travelers who want more space than the intimate Shadow Class but a cozier feel than the larger Muse Class — a Goldilocks option in the Silversea fleet.

Silver Spirit occupies a unique middle ground in the Silversea fleet. After her 2018 lengthening, she gained additional suites and expanded public areas while keeping the intimate atmosphere that defines the brand. She carries 540 guests — larger than Silver Shadow (382) but smaller than Silver Muse (596). If you want the newest ships, the Nova Class or Muse Class are better choices; if you want the most intimate ocean experience, Silver Shadow or Silver Whisper are smaller still.
