Royal Caribbean Dining Venues by Ship Class

Every Royal Caribbean ship offers a mix of complimentary dining, specialty restaurants, and bars—but the lineup varies by ship class. Use this guide to compare what's available across the fleet and plan your dining experiences before you sail.

Tip: Most specialty restaurants offer better value when booked as part of a dining package. Check current package pricing on RoyalCaribbean.com.
