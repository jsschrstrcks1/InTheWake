Scarlet Night Review: The Signature Virgin Voyages Experience

Introduction. Scarlet Night is the flagship event of every Virgin Voyages sailing — an immersive, ship-wide celebration drenched in red that features pop-up performances, live music, a massive pool-deck dance party, and a sense of communal euphoria that defines the Virgin brand. Every guest is asked to wear something red (or scarlet), and the ship transforms from bow to stern with scarlet lighting, themed decorations, and roaming performers. It happens once per voyage and is widely regarded as the single most memorable night on any Virgin sailing. No cover; drinks are Bar Tab or per-drink.

Drinks & Service

All bars across the ship participate in Scarlet Night, and many feature special red-themed cocktails. The "Scarlet Night" signature cocktail (vodka, cranberry, blood orange, sparkling wine float) is the event drink and it is festive, photogenic, and surprisingly drinkable. Red wine flows freely from every bar. Champagne is popped on the pool deck during the climactic moments. Where it falls short: every bar on the ship is busy simultaneously, which means wait times are the longest of the voyage at every location. The themed cocktails are more about color than craft. Pre-game in your cabin or at a quieter bar before the main event kicks off around 10 PM.

Service

The entertainment team delivers their best performance of the voyage on Scarlet Night. Pop-up performances appear throughout the ship — aerial artists on the pool deck, musicians in unexpected locations, dancers emerging from crowds. The DJ set builds masterfully toward the pool-deck crescendo. The Captain and officers participate visibly, which adds to the communal feeling. The crew is fully in costume and fully committed. The overall event choreography — the way the energy builds from early evening through the midnight climax — is expertly managed. This is where Virgin's entertainment investment pays its greatest dividend.

Atmosphere

Scarlet Night is genuinely special. The sight of an entire ship — 2,700+ adults — dressed in red, dancing under scarlet-lit skies on the open ocean, creates something that transcends typical cruise entertainment. The pool-deck party is the centerpiece: the pool itself is lit red, performers appear from above and below, and the DJ drives the crowd to a collective peak. Pop-up moments in quieter venues (surprise performances in restaurants, musicians in corridors) reward guests who explore rather than staying in one spot. Where it falls short: the event's scale can feel overwhelming. The pool deck reaches dangerously crowded levels at peak. Guests who dislike loud music, crowds, or themed events may find it exhausting. The quality has become more formulaic across sailings — longtime Virgin guests report that earlier Scarlet Nights had more spontaneity.

Conclusion

Rating: 4.4/5. Scarlet Night is the defining Virgin Voyages experience and arguably the best single-evening event on any cruise line. The energy, spectacle, and communal spirit create memories that guests talk about long after disembarkation. It falls short on bar service (overwhelmed fleet-wide), crowd density at the pool deck, and a slight drift toward formulaic execution on recent sailings. This is the reason many guests sail Virgin. Tip: pack a dedicated scarlet or red outfit, explore the ship throughout the evening for pop-up surprises, and claim your pool-deck position by 10:30 PM — the main event starts around 11 and space disappears fast.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scarlet Night on Virgin Voyages?

Immersive celebration with pop-up performances, live music, and a drenched-in-red pool dance party. Signature fleet-wide event on every voyage.

How much does Scarlet Night cost?

Pricing varies — check the Virgin Voyages app or Sailor Services for current Scarlet Night pricing. Most dining is included in the fare.

What is the dress code for Scarlet Night?

Smart Casual is recommended. Virgin Voyages has no formal nights — dress to express, not to impress. No specific dress code is enforced, but smart casual elevates the experience.

Which ships have Scarlet Night?

Scarlet Night is available on select Virgin Voyages ships. Check the Virgin Voyages app or website for availability.

What are the hours for Scarlet Night?

Hours vary by ship and itinerary. Check the Virgin Voyages app for current hours and show times.
