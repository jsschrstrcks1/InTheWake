Boardwalk Carousel

A hand-carved merry-go-round at sea

Location Boardwalk
Ages All ages
Ride Time ~3 minutes
Price Complimentary

Images: Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA)

Quick Answer: The Boardwalk carousel is a free, hand-carved merry-go-round in the heart of the Boardwalk neighborhood on Oasis and Icon Class ships. Open to all ages with no reservation needed. Especially magical at night when the lights illuminate—a perfect photo opportunity and nostalgic experience for the whole family.

Overview

Rising from the center of the Boardwalk neighborhood, the carousel is one of Royal Caribbean's most photographed attractions. This isn't a mass-produced carnival ride—it's a genuine hand-carved carousel featuring beautifully detailed wooden horses and animals, each one unique and crafted by skilled artisans.

The carousel evokes the charm of early 20th-century seaside boardwalks, complete with ornate decorations, mirrors, and thousands of twinkling lights. During the day, it's a delightful diversion for families. At night, when the lights come alive against the open sky, it transforms into something truly magical.

Design & Craftsmanship

Each carousel animal is hand-carved from wood and hand-painted with intricate details. The horses feature traditional "jumper" and "stander" positions—some rising and falling as the carousel turns, others standing still for those who prefer a gentler ride. Beyond horses, you'll find other whimsical animals depending on the ship.

The Boardwalk Setting

The carousel sits in the open-air Boardwalk, a neighborhood unique to Oasis and Icon Class ships. Surrounded by Johnny Rockets, the candy shop, and various entertainment venues, it creates an authentic boardwalk atmosphere. The AquaTheater sits at the aft end, and the zip line soars overhead—it's the heart of family entertainment at sea.

The Carousel Experience

What to expect when you ride:

Ride Details

  • Ride Duration ~3 min

    Each carousel cycle lasts approximately 3 minutes with classic carnival music playing throughout.

  • Animal Selection Your choice

    Choose from various hand-carved horses and animals. Jumpers move up and down; standers remain stationary.

  • Chariot Seats Available

    Bench-style chariots available for those who prefer not to mount an animal or for parents with small children.

Best Times to Ride

  • Morning Shortest lines

    Early morning rides often have no wait. Great for getting photos without crowds.

  • After Dinner Most magical

    The carousel lights up beautifully at night. Worth the slightly longer wait for the atmosphere.

  • Port Days Quieter

    Most families are ashore; those staying aboard enjoy minimal wait times.

* All carousel rides are complimentary with unlimited rides throughout your cruise.

Tips & Photography

Best Photo Opportunities

  • Golden hour: Late afternoon light creates warm, beautiful photos
  • Night lighting: The carousel's thousands of lights create a magical backdrop
  • From above: Upper deck walkways offer aerial views of the carousel in action
  • Action shots: Capture riders from the Boardwalk as the carousel spins

For Families with Young Children

  • Chariot benches are ideal for very young children or those nervous about mounting animals
  • Parents can stand beside mounted children during the ride
  • Stander horses don't move up and down—better for first-timers
  • Staff assist with mounting and dismounting

Adult Riders Welcome

Don't be shy—adults ride the carousel all the time! It's a nostalgic experience that appeals to all ages. Evening rides are particularly popular with couples enjoying the romantic ambiance of the lit-up Boardwalk.

Accessibility

  • Chariot benches accommodate those who cannot mount horses
  • Staff available to assist guests with mobility needs
  • Wheelchair users may need to transfer to chariot seating
  • Notify staff of any assistance needs before boarding

Availability & Access

Operating Hours

  • Sea Days: 10:00 AM - 11:00 PM typical
  • Port Days: Often extended morning hours
  • Weather: May close during rain or high winds (open-air location)

No Reservation Needed

Simply walk up to the carousel during operating hours. Wait times are typically short—usually just one or two cycles. Ride as many times as you like throughout your cruise.

Guidelines

  • All ages welcome
  • Young children should be accompanied by an adult
  • Secure loose items before riding
  • Remain seated throughout the ride
  • Follow crew member instructions for mounting/dismounting

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the carousel free on Royal Caribbean?

Yes, the Boardwalk carousel is completely complimentary. You can ride as many times as you like throughout your cruise at no additional cost.

Which Royal Caribbean ships have a carousel?

The carousel is available on all Oasis Class ships (Oasis, Allure, Harmony, Symphony, and Wonder of the Seas) and Icon Class ships (Icon of the Seas). These are the ships featuring the Boardwalk neighborhood.

Is the carousel only for kids?

Absolutely not! The carousel welcomes guests of all ages. Many adults enjoy riding, especially in the evening when the atmosphere is particularly romantic and nostalgic.

Is the carousel hand-carved?

Yes, the carousel features genuine hand-carved wooden horses and animals created by skilled artisans. The attention to detail and craftsmanship gives it an authentic vintage feel.

Can I take photos on the carousel?

Yes, photography is welcome. The carousel makes a beautiful backdrop, especially at night when illuminated. Ship photographers sometimes capture riders as well.

Is there a height or age requirement?

There's no height or age requirement. Very young children can ride in the chariot benches with parents, or parents can stand beside children who mount the horses.
