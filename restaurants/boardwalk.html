Boardwalk

Royal Caribbean — Ship Neighborhood

Quick Answer: Boardwalk is an open-air neighborhood at the aft of Oasis Class ships, recreating a classic seaside boardwalk atmosphere. Features a hand-carved carousel, arcade, Johnny Rockets, candy shop, and the AquaTheater amphitheater. Walking around is free; individual attractions have varying costs. Balcony staterooms overlook the neighborhood.

Best For: Families with children, carnival atmosphere lovers, nostalgia seekers, those wanting outdoor entertainment, and guests who enjoy the energy of a seaside pier.

Key Facts

  • Access: Complimentary to walk around
  • Carousel: Free rides for all guests
  • Dining: Johnny Rockets ($), Boardwalk Dog House ($)
  • Entertainment: Arcade, carnival games, AquaTheater
  • Location: Aft of ship, Deck 6

Experience the charm of a classic American boardwalk in the middle of the ocean.

Boardwalk brings carnival fun to sea with its hand-carved carousel, arcade games, and seaside dining. The open-air design with Boardwalk View balcony staterooms overlooking from above creates a unique neighborhood atmosphere. Evening brings the AquaTheater's spectacular diving shows to life. Attractions & Venues

Boardwalk Access: Free · Carousel: Free · Arcade: Pay-per-play · Restaurants: Cover charge or à la carte

Free Attractions

  • Hand-carved carousel
  • Strolling and people-watching
  • AquaTheater shows
  • Live street entertainment
  • Photo opportunities

Dining Venues

  • Johnny Rockets (~$13)
  • Boardwalk Dog House ($)
  • Candy Beach (sweets shop)
  • Ice cream cart

Paid Entertainment

  • Arcade games (varies)
  • Carnival games (prizes)
  • Photo packages

The carousel is complimentary for all guests—one of the most popular free attractions on the ship, especially for families with young children.

Ship-Specific Features
  • Wonder of the Seas: The Mason Jar Southern restaurant
  • Symphony/Harmony: Playmakers Sports Bar & Arcade
  • All Oasis Class: Hand-carved carousel, Johnny Rockets, AquaTheater

Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: Boardwalk is wheelchair accessible via elevators and ramps. The carousel has accessible seating options. AquaTheater has designated wheelchair viewing areas. The area is open-air so weather conditions apply. Restaurants can accommodate dietary needs with advance notice.

Where You'll Find It

Boardwalk runs through the center-aft of Oasis Class ships, spanning Deck 6 with balcony staterooms overlooking from Decks 8-14. Access from multiple stairwells and elevators.

Best Times to Visit

  • Morning: Quiet carousel rides, breakfast at Johnny Rockets
  • Afternoon: Arcade, carnival games, family activity
  • Evening: AquaTheater shows, dinner, vibrant atmosphere
  • Late Night: Quieter strolls, romantic carousel rides

Boardwalk Review: Carnival Magic at Sea

Introduction. Boardwalk captured our kids' hearts from the moment we stepped onto it. The carousel, the arcade lights, the smell of burgers from Johnny Rockets—it genuinely feels like stepping onto a seaside pier, except you're in the middle of the Caribbean.

The Atmosphere

The design is charming, with the hand-carved carousel as the centerpiece. Balconies rise up on both sides, giving the space a village square feel. During the day it's active with families; evenings bring a more romantic atmosphere with the carousel lights twinkling and AquaTheater shows entertaining crowds.

Family Favorites

Our kids (ages 5 and 8) loved the carousel—free rides meant we went multiple times daily. The arcade was a hit, though it does add up quickly if you're not careful. We had dinner at Johnny Rockets twice—classic burgers and shakes at a reasonable cover charge. The AquaTheater shows from Boardwalk seating were spectacular.

Conclusion

Boardwalk offers something for everyone in the family. The free carousel alone makes it special, and the carnival atmosphere is executed beautifully. It's one of the features that makes Oasis Class ships feel like floating resorts rather than just big cruise ships. Rating: 4.5/5.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the carousel on Boardwalk free?

Yes, carousel rides are complimentary for all guests. There's no charge or ticket required.

Is Boardwalk outdoors?

Yes, Boardwalk is open to the sky. Balcony staterooms overlook it from above. In inclement weather, activities may be limited.

What does Johnny Rockets cost on Boardwalk?

Johnny Rockets has a cover charge of approximately $12.95 per person, which includes a burger or sandwich, fries, and a shake or drink.

Are there Boardwalk staterooms?

Yes, Boardwalk View balcony staterooms overlook the Boardwalk from above. They offer a unique perspective but can be noisier than ocean-view rooms, especially during AquaTheater shows.

Which ships have Boardwalk?

Boardwalk is exclusive to Oasis Class ships: Oasis, Allure, Harmony, Symphony, and Wonder of the Seas.
