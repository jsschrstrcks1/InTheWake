Frequently Asked Questions

What is SkyRide on Carnival Vista?

SkyRide is an aerial cycling attraction where guests pedal suspended bikes around an 800-foot track above the ship. Vista was the first ship to feature this in 2016.

What is the Havana section?

The Havana section is a Cuban-themed premium area with exclusive pool, bar, and cabins. Access is limited to Havana cabin guests during daytime.

Where does Carnival Vista sail from?

Carnival Vista is homeported year-round in Galveston, Texas, offering Western Caribbean cruises.
