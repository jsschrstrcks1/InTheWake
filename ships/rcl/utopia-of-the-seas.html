Utopia of the Seas — Deck Plans, Live Tracker, Dining & Videos

Utopia of the Seas is an Oasis Class cruise ship operated by Royal Caribbean. She entered service in 2024, measures 236,857 gross tons, and carries approximately 5,668 guests at double occupancy with a crew of 2,200.

Key Facts

  • Class: Oasis Class
  • Year: 2024
  • Tonnage: 236,857 GT
  • Guests: 5,668
  • IMO: 9711146

Quick Answer: Utopia of the Seas (236,857 GT | 5,668 guests | 2024) is Royal Caribbean's sixth Oasis Class ship, purpose-built for short getaways with the Oasis-class seven-neighborhood design. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Best For: Weekend warriors and short-cruise fans who want Oasis-class scale on 3- and 4-night itineraries — all the neighborhoods, less time commitment.

Utopia of the Seas tends to suit travelers who appreciate grand-scale cruising with seven distinct neighborhoods including Central Park, the Boardwalk, and the AquaTheater. It's ideal if you want one of the world's largest and newest cruise ships (launched 2024) with space for up to 5,668 guests at double occupancy, extensive dining and entertainment options, and cutting-edge amenities. If you're seeking a more intimate ship or prefer mid-sized vessels, you may want to explore Radiance-class or Voyager-class ships instead.

Frequently Asked Questions About Utopia of the Seas

What makes Utopia of the Seas unique compared to other Royal Caribbean ships?

Utopia of the Seas is the newest Oasis-class ship (launched 2024), featuring Royal Caribbean's signature seven-neighborhood layout including Central Park, the Boardwalk with a hand-carved carousel, and the AquaTheater. At 236,857 GT with space for up to 5,668 guests at double occupancy, it offers the latest innovations and amenities across its multiple decks.

Which ships are in the same class as Utopia of the Seas?

Utopia of the Seas is part of the Oasis Class, which includes Oasis of the Seas, Allure of the Seas, Harmony of the Seas, Symphony of the Seas, and Wonder of the Seas. All share the notable seven-neighborhood design and massive scale.

What dining options are available on Utopia of the Seas?

Utopia offers the main dining room, Windjammer Café (buffet), specialty dining including Chops Grille, Giovanni's Table, Izumi, 150 Central Park, and numerous bars and lounges throughout the ship's seven neighborhoods. See the Dining Venues section above for current pricing and full details.

Where does Utopia of the Seas typically sail?

Utopia typically sails short Caribbean itineraries. Deployments vary by season — check the live tracker on this page or Royal Caribbean's official site for current and upcoming voyages.

How can I see Utopia of the Seas' current location?

Use the live ship tracker on this page (in the "Where Is Utopia Right Now?" section) to see the ship's current position, speed, and next port in real-time.
