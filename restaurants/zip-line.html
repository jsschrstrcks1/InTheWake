Zip Line

Royal Caribbean — Thrill Experience

Quick Answer: A complimentary zip line spanning 82 feet across the Boardwalk, nine decks above sea level on Oasis and Icon Class ships. No reservation needed. Weight limits: 75-275 lbs. Two parallel lines let you race friends!

Best For: Thrill-seekers, couples wanting a shared adventure, and anyone seeking a unique at-sea experience with ocean views.

Key Facts

  • Price: Complimentary
  • Length: 82 feet
  • Height: 9 decks up
  • Weight: 75-275 lbs
  • Ships: Oasis & Icon Class

The Zip Line Experience

Complimentary · Unlimited rides · Two parallel lines for racing

Your Zip Journey

  • Check-In: ~5 min — Sign waiver, verify weight
  • Climb: ~2 min — Stairs to Deck 16 launch platform
  • Gear Up: ~3 min — Harness fitting and safety check
  • The Zip: ~15 sec — Soar 82 feet across the Boardwalk!

Best Times

  • Opening: Shortest waits
  • During Dinner: Walk right up
  • Port Days: Most guests ashore
  • Night: Spectacular with lit Boardwalk

Tips

Pro Tips: Wear closed-toe shoes (required). Secure loose items — no hats, sunglasses, phones on the ride. Look DOWN at the carousel as you go over. Two parallel lines let you race side-by-side with friends. Check Cruise Compass for evening hours.

Hours & Requirements

Sea Days: 10am - 6pm typical (weather permitting). Evening: Sometimes operates after dark. Requirements: Weight 75-275 lbs, closed-toe shoes, signed waiver. Available on: Oasis, Allure, Harmony, Symphony, Wonder, and Icon of the Seas.

The Logbook — Real Guest Soundings

4.7 ★ out of 5 Couple • Symphony of the Seas • 2024

My husband and I raced side by side — I won! The whole thing is over quickly but the rush is incredible. We went back three more times during the cruise. Pro tip: look DOWN at the carousel as you go over — that view is amazing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the zip line free?

Yes, completely complimentary with unlimited rides.

Can I race someone?

Yes! Two parallel lines let you zip side-by-side with friends or family.

Is it scary?

The launch platform is high, but once you step off, fear turns to exhilaration. Staff are supportive of nervous riders.
