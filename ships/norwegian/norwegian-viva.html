Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options are available on Norwegian Viva?

Norwegian Viva offers complimentary dining including the main dining room and buffet. Specialty restaurants vary by ship and may require reservations or carry an additional charge. Check the dining section above for specific venues available on this ship.

How do I find the deck plans for Norwegian Viva?

Deck plans are available through the links on this page. You can also find official deck plans on the Norwegian Cruise Line website or in the cruise planner app.

Where does Norwegian Viva sail?

Norwegian Viva offers Mediterranean cruises from Rome and Barcelona, along with Caribbean voyages. As the second Prima-class ship, she debuted in 2023 with enhanced features. Check the Norwegian Cruise Line website for current itineraries.

What makes Norwegian Viva unique?

Norwegian Viva features the Prima Speedway three-level go-kart track, The Drop dry slide, and Bull's Eye dart bar. The ship has Ocean Boulevard wraparound promenade, Metropolitan restaurant, Indulge Food Hall with 11 venues, and the Penrose Atrium with LED infinity ceiling.

How can I track Norwegian Viva's current location?

Use the live ship tracker section on this page to see Norwegian Viva's current position, speed, heading, and next port in real-time via MarineTraffic AIS integration. The tracker updates automatically as the ship moves.

Is this information official?

This page provides planning resources and community insights. Always confirm details with Norwegian Cruise Line or your travel advisor before booking.
