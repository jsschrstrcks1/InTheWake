Menu & Price

Price: Smoothies and snacks typically $4–10 each. Complimentary with Refreshment or Deluxe Beverage Packages. Average smoothie $6–8. Cheapest: fruit cup (~$3–4); most expensive: custom protein blend (~$10–12).

Protein Shakes (Energizing · Post-Workout)
  • Protein-Licious — banana, chocolate, peanut butter, vanilla whey ($7–9)
  • Wake Me Up — espresso, chocolate, peanut butter, banana, whey ($8–10)
  • Custom Protein Blend — build-your-own with whey or plant protein, fruits, & add-ins ($6–12)
Smoothies & Juices (Vitality-Boosting)
  • Immune Booster — orange, ginger, turmeric, lemon ($5–7)
  • Green Vitality — kale, spinach, apple, cucumber ($5–7)
  • Berry Blast — mixed berries, banana, almond milk ($6–8)
  • Make-Your-Own — choose 3–5 fruits/veggies (+chia seeds, supplements) ($6–10)
Snacks & Light Bites (Grab-and-Go)
  • Housemade Granola with Greek Yogurt ($4–6)
  • Fresh Fruit Cup (seasonal berries/melons) ($3–5)
  • Veggie Sticks with Hummus ($4–6)
  • Acai Bowl with Granola & Toppings ($7–9)

Special Accommodations

Allergen & Dietary Notes: Customizable for dairy-free, vegan, and low-sugar needs. Staff can adjust protein sources, milk bases, and toppings on request. Cross-contact minimized but not eliminated.

Royal Caribbean Vitality Café Review 2025: Spa-Side Sips & Light Bites That Actually Satisfy

Introduction

Vitality Café is the ship’s wellness pit stop — smoothies, juices, and light bites served right by the gym and spa. It’s an easy win for mindful mornings or a post-workout refuel, with most items covered under the Refreshment or Deluxe beverage packages and fair à la carte pricing for everyone else.

Food & Drinks

Standouts included the Immune Booster (orange, ginger, turmeric, lemon) for a bright, citrusy kick, and the Protein-Licious shake (banana, PB, chocolate, whey) — thick without the chalk. Housemade granola over Greek yogurt added crunch without being overly sweet. Portions skew light by design; think fuel, not a full meal.

Service

Baristas were quick and clued-in on tweaks (plant milk, low-sugar, extra espresso). Lines formed 7–9 AM but moved fast; most orders landed in under five minutes. A few popular add-ins sold out late day on sea days.

Atmosphere

Spa-adjacent calm with natural light and a steady hum from the fitness center — a relaxed spot for a quiet reset compared with busier venues.

Conclusion

For wellness-leaning cruisers, Vitality Café is a reliable, feel-good stop: customizable drinks, fair value (or included), and a soothing setting. Rating: 4.2/5. Best used as your morning ritual or post-gym refuel; pair it with a Refreshment Package for easy daily sips.
