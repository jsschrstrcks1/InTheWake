Rhapsody of the Seas

Rhapsody of the Seas is a Vision‑class stalwart that leans into classic shipboard pleasures: music‑filled lounges, a sun‑splashed pool deck, and destination‑driven itineraries. Compared with newer megaships, she feels intimate and social—easy to navigate and sized for port‑intensive weeks.

A major refit in 2012 refreshed staterooms and added family‑friendly touches like an outdoor movie screen and updated dining spaces. Subsequent work has kept public rooms in good shape without changing the ship’s traditional character.

Expect Main Dining Room service, Windjammer buffet staples, and a handful of specialty options depending on deployment. Evenings bring production shows and live bands rather than cutting‑edge tech theater, which suits the ship’s personality.

Choose Rhapsody if you want relaxed sea days, strong itineraries, and a human‑scale ship where it’s easy to find your people.
