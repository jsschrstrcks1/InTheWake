## Getting to the ship — and getting home

### Arrival: Saturday recommended, Sunday required

**Saturday Sep 26 — fly in.** Same-day Sunday flights to a 3:30 PM all-aboard leave no margin for September-afternoon Florida thunderstorms. Hotel options:

- **Cocoa Beach** (15 min from port) — beach-town energy, walking distance to pier and beach. Simpler hotels but the right location if you want to be near the port.
- **Orlando MCO area** (45 min from port) — more hotel options, more variety, longer Sunday-morning transfer.

**Sunday Sep 27 — embarkation.**

- **Terminal:** **Port Canaveral, Cruise Terminal 10** (NCL's regular berth — verify your assignment in the NCL app).
- **MCO → Port Canaveral:** Uber/Lyft $60–80, 45–60 min. Shared cruise shuttle ~$30/person.
- **If driving:** Port Canaveral garage ~$17–22/day; reservable in advance.
- **Embark window:** 12:00 – 3:30 PM. **All-aboard 3:00 PM.** Ship departs 4:00 PM.
- **NCL app online check-in** before arrival.

### Day 8 debark and the trip home

- **The night before:** bags in the hall by ~11 PM with color-coded tag.
- **5:30 – 7:00 AM:** Breakfast at Indulge Food Hall or Surfside Café.
- **6:30 AM:** Customs clearance complete. Self-disembarkation begins.
- **7:00 – 9:30 AM:** Walk-off in waves. All passengers off by 10:00 AM.

**Carry-on with you:** change of clothes, all medications, passport, phone charger, valuables.

**Getting from Port Canaveral to MCO.** Uber/Lyft on Sunday morning, **$60–80, 45–60 min**. Surge pricing possible.

**Killing 4–6 hours before an evening flight:**

- **Cocoa Beach pier** — 15-min Uber. Beach walk + casual lunch.
- **Kennedy Space Center** — 20-min Uber, $75 entry. Half-day minimum if done properly.
- **Cocoa Village** — historic small-town main street; lunch + shops.

## Areas of interest during the cruise

### On the ship — Prima spots worth knowing

- **The Penrose Atrium** — three-deck central social space; orienting landmark and Tina's likely sailaway venue.
- **Ocean Boulevard** (Deck 8) — outdoor wraparound walkway at sea level. Sunrise coffee and sailaway viewing.
- **Indulge Food Hall** — 11 quick-casual concepts under one roof; included. The right call when you don't want to commit to a sit-down meal.
- **Studio Lounge** (solo-cabin perk) — hosted coffee hour Day 2 morning typically; the daily social anchor.
- **The Rush** — three-deck twin-tube waterslide. First hour after opening has zero line.
- **The Drop** — three-deck dry slide alongside The Rush. Adult-rated.
- **Vibe Beach Club** — adults-only enclave; $99 day / $399 week. Quietest pool on Prima.
- **Mandara Spa thermal-suite day pass** — sea-day intro rates appear.

### Sea-day priorities (Day 2 + Day 4)

1. **Sunrise on Ocean Boulevard** — best 30 minutes of the week.
2. **Studio meet-and-greet** Day 1 + Studio Lounge coffee hour Day 2 — NCL hosts both.
3. **The Rush** waterslide first hour after opening.
4. **Thermal suite at Mandara Spa** — sea-day morning intro rate.
5. **Specialty dining reservation** for Day 4 evening — Onda by Scarpetta, Le Bistro, Cagney's fill fast.

### Specialty dining picks

- **Onda by Scarpetta** — modern Italian, Scott Conant partnership; the most refined meal.
- **Le Bistro** — French; the most romantic specialty room.
- **Cagney's Steakhouse** — NCL's reliable steakhouse standard.
- **Hasuki** — Japanese teppanyaki (the showy hibachi room).
- **Los Lobos** — modern Mexican.

### Port-day picks (one each)

- **Great Stirrup Cay** — beach + buffet day; rent snorkel gear if visibility looks good. Cabana upgrade $250–400.
- **Falmouth** — Greenwood Great House (Caribbean colonial history, ~$60–80) is closer and more interesting than Dunn's River Falls (long drive). Or a beach club for a low-stress day.
- **Grand Cayman** — Stingray City sandbar (~$60 indep / $90 ship) is the iconic. Or 7-Mile Beach via $5 #1 bus for the easy day.
- **Cozumel** — Palancar Reef snorkel tour (~$50 indep / ~$95 ship). Two hours, calm water, the conversion experience.

### Where Tina sets the rhythm

- **Day 1 sailaway** — likely Penrose Atrium around 3:30 PM. Watch her communications.
- **Hudson's 5:30 PM nightly** — recurring group dinner.
- **Studio Lounge** — solo cruisers cluster here regardless of who's hosting.
- **Tina may book** a specialty group dinner mid-week (Onda, Le Bistro, or Cagney's).

### Photo spots worth knowing

- **Sailaway from Ocean Boulevard Deck 8** — Cape Canaveral coastline at golden hour.
- **The Penrose Atrium** at evening — three-deck atmospheric light.
- **Great Stirrup Cay lighthouse** at the south end.
- **Stingray City sandbar** — bring an underwater camera for the in-water shots.
- **Palancar Reef** from the snorkel boat — underwater shots worth saving.
