Cruise Lines

Quick Answer: Browse 10 major cruise lines with ship-by-ship guides, dining venues, and planning tools to help you compare Royal Caribbean, Carnival, Celebrity, Princess, Disney, Norwegian, MSC, Holland America, Viking, and Virgin Voyages.

How to Use This Directory

This cruise line directory serves as your starting point for exploring the fleet, dining, and character of each major cruise line. Each cruise line page provides ship lists, venue details, and practical planning notes to help you understand what to expect onboard.

Whether you're comparing cruise lines for the first time or looking for specific ship information, these guides connect you to deck plans, live ship trackers, restaurant menus, and accessibility notes. Start with a cruise line that interests you, then explore individual ship pages for detailed planning resources.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which cruise lines are covered on In the Wake?

In the Wake provides guides for 10 major cruise lines: Royal Caribbean, Carnival, Celebrity, Princess, Disney Cruise Line, Norwegian, MSC Cruises, Holland America, Viking, and Virgin Voyages.

What information is included for each cruise line?

Each cruise line page includes ship-by-ship guides, dining venue details, accessibility notes, and links to planning tools like deck plans, live trackers, and restaurant menus.

How do I find a specific cruise ship?

Click into any cruise line page to see all ships for that line, or visit the Ships hub page for a complete searchable directory across all cruise lines.

Are these guides helpful for first-time cruisers?

Yes! Each cruise line guide helps you understand the line's character, ship offerings, and what to expect. Use these guides to compare cruise lines and find the one that matches your travel style.
