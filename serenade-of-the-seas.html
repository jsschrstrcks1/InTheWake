Serenade of the Seas

Serenade feels like a conservatory at sea: light, bright, and designed for unbroken views.

She’s a go-to for scenic cruising—Norway, Alaska, and shoulder-season sun—that rewards decks with glass windbreaks and quiet corners.

Entertainment leans classic; you’ll find polished production shows and a social, music-forward evening vibe.

Dining options cover the essentials with a few upscale additions depending on deployment.

If you want the panorama without the crowds, Serenade is the mood.
