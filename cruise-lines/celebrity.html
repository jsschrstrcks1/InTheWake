Celebrity Cruises

Quick Answer: Step into a world where every sunset is a masterpiece, every meal a Michelin-inspired delight, and every destination a curated discovery. Celebrity Cruises redefines modern luxury with Edge-class ships featuring the revolutionary Magic Carpet, premium dining experiences, and destination-focused itineraries that transform vacations into journeys of self-discovery and indulgence.

Is Celebrity Right for You?

Imagine sipping a handcrafted cocktail at the Magic Carpet — a cantilevered platform floating above turquoise waters — as you sail toward Santorini's whitewashed cliffs. Celebrity Cruises crafts experiences for discerning travelers seeking modern luxury without stuffiness: affluent couples discovering romance, wellness enthusiasts craving serenity, and explorers hungry for cultural immersion.

The revolutionary Edge Class ships (Edge, Apex, Beyond, Ascent) feature infinity verandas that blur the line between stateroom and sea, rooftop gardens under open sky, and Eden — a multisensory space spanning three decks where farm-to-table dishes pair with panoramic views. At the SEA Thermal Suite, rejuvenation becomes ritual. At The Retreat, suite guests discover a ship-within-a-ship experience with exclusive Luminae dining and dedicated concierge service.

Celebrity appeals to those who appreciate the art of travel: clean lines, sophisticated atmosphere, and cuisine that rivals land-based fine dining. Whether you're celebrating an anniversary in the Greek Isles or exploring Alaska's wilderness with expert naturalists, Celebrity ensures every detail is crafted for you.

Key Facts at a Glance

  • Brand Identity: Modern luxury — sophisticated, design-forward, adult-oriented atmosphere
  • Signature Features: Magic Carpet (Edge Class), Eden restaurant, The Retreat suite experience, Luminae exclusive dining, SEA Thermal Suite
  • Best For: Couples, solo travelers (with dedicated solo staterooms), foodies, wellness seekers, destination-focused cruisers
  • Dress Code: Smart casual throughout, with optional elegant evenings
  • Coming Soon: Celebrity Xcel (2025) featuring The Bazaar, a vibrant hub of global flavors
  • Homeports: Fort Lauderdale, Cape Liberty, Seattle, and more

The Celebrity Experience

Celebrity cruises feel different from the moment you board. The design aesthetic whispers luxury without shouting it — clean lines, curated art collections, and spaces that flow with intuitive elegance. Edge Class ships pushed boundaries that others are still catching up to: outward-facing design that brings the sea into every space, infinite verandas with disappearing walls, and the Magic Carpet that transforms from specialty restaurant to tender platform as it glides along the ship's exterior.

Dining ascends to art form here. From the Main Restaurant's rotating fine-dining menus to Le Voyage by Daniel Boulud, every meal reflects Celebrity's commitment to culinary excellence. The Retreat offers suite guests an even more elevated experience with exclusive lounges, private sundeck, and priority access throughout the ship.

What cruisers say: "From the infinity veranda to the rooftop garden, every moment felt like a dream tailored just for us."

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Celebrity Cruises unique?

Celebrity Cruises offers modern luxury with innovative Edge Class ships featuring the Magic Carpet cantilevered platform, premium dining experiences, contemporary design, and destination-focused itineraries with expert-led excursions.

What is the Magic Carpet on Celebrity Edge Class ships?

The Magic Carpet is Celebrity's revolutionary cantilevered platform that moves up and down the side of Edge Class ships, transforming into different venues including a specialty restaurant, bar, and tender embarkation platform.

What type of traveler does Celebrity appeal to?

Celebrity appeals to discerning travelers seeking modern luxury without stuffiness, couples and solo travelers (especially on Edge Class ships), foodies who appreciate premium dining, and destination-focused cruisers who value cultural immersion.

How does Celebrity compare to Royal Caribbean?

Celebrity (owned by Royal Caribbean Group) offers a more refined, adult-oriented experience compared to Royal Caribbean's family-friendly mega-ships. Celebrity focuses on premium dining, contemporary design, and destination experiences, while Royal Caribbean emphasizes active entertainment and water parks.
