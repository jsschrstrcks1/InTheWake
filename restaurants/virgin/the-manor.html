The Manor Review: Guest Experience Summary

Introduction. The Manor consistently receives positive feedback from Virgin Voyages sailors. This composite review reflects common themes from multiple sailings and ships across the fleet.

Drinks & Service

The drink menu at The Manor features both classic and creative options. Bartenders are skilled and attentive, crafting well-balanced drinks with quality ingredients.

Service

Service at The Manor is described as attentive and professional. Staff are friendly, knowledgeable, and happy to accommodate requests. Pacing feels comfortable and unhurried.

Atmosphere

The Manor offers an inviting setting with thoughtful design that complements the drinking experience. Guests appreciate the ambiance and find it a pleasant place to spend time.

Conclusion

Rating: 4/5. The Manor delivers a quality experience that meets guest expectations. Worth visiting during your Virgin Voyages cruise.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Manor on Virgin Voyages?

Two-story signature nightclub inspired by Richard Branson's first recording studio. Hosts headline shows that transition into late-night dancing. Three bars across two levels.

How much does The Manor cost?

The Manor is an a la carte venue — you pay per item ordered. Prices vary by selection.

What is the dress code for The Manor?

Smart Casual is recommended. Virgin Voyages has no formal nights — dress to express, not to impress. No specific dress code is enforced, but smart casual elevates the experience.

Which ships have The Manor?

The Manor is available on select Virgin Voyages ships. Check the Virgin Voyages app or website for availability.

What are the hours for The Manor?

Hours vary by ship and itinerary. Check the Virgin Voyages app for current hours and show times.
