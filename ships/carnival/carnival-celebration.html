Frequently Asked Questions

What is 820 Biscayne on Carnival Celebration?

820 Biscayne is a Miami-themed zone exclusive to Carnival Celebration, celebrating Carnival's founding in Miami in 1972. It features Cuban coffee, art deco design, and Miami-inspired entertainment.

Why is Carnival Celebration the 50th anniversary ship?

Carnival Celebration debuted in November 2022, marking Carnival Cruise Line's 50th anniversary since the original TSS Mardi Gras launched the brand in 1972.

Who is the godmother of Carnival Celebration?

Cassidy Gifford is the godmother. Her mother, Kathie Lee Gifford, was godmother to the original Carnival Celebration in 1987 — a generational connection.

Where does Carnival Celebration sail from?

Miami, Florida year-round, offering Caribbean cruises from PortMiami to Eastern and Western Caribbean destinations.
