# Virgin Voyages - Master Port List

**Generated:** 2025-11-22
**Source:** Virgin Voyages official announcements, CruiseMapper data, 2022-2028 itineraries
**Fleet:** 4 ships (Scarlet Lady, Valiant Lady, Resilient Lady, Brilliant Lady debuting September 2025)
**Total Unique Ports:** ~120 ports (curated, experiential focus)
**Market Position:** Adults-only (18+), premium experiential cruising

**Note:** Virgin Voyages emphasizes quality over quantity with late-night stays, overnights, and "Shore Things" excursions. This list includes all ports visited 2022-2025 and scheduled through 2027-2028. No full world cruises, but repositionings serve as extended voyages (14-16 nights transatlantic/transpacific).

**Recent Changes:**
- 2024-2025 Australia/NZ canceled (Red Sea issues), replaced by Caribbean
- 2026 expansion: Panama Canal full transits, Greenland/Iceland, Alaska, West Coast USA
- 27 new itineraries/19 new ports announced through April 2025

## Notes

- Virgin Voyages **focuses on quality over quantity**: ~120 ports vs 300+ for mass-market lines
- **No children**: Adults-only (18+) creates different port experiences
- **Late-night stays** are signature: ships stay in port until 10pm-midnight for nightlife
- **Overnight stays**: Ibiza and Mykonos are regular overnights
- **Smallest fleet**: 4 ships vs 27-29 for Royal/Carnival creates more curated itineraries
- **Recent cancellations**: 2024-2025 Australia/NZ canceled due to Red Sea/Suez Canal closure
- **New deployments**: Panama Canal (2026), Alaska (2026), Greenland (2026) expand reach
