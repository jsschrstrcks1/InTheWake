Spotlight Lounge

Royal Caribbean — Entertainment Venue

Quick Answer: Spotlight Lounge is an entertainment venue on Royal Caribbean ships featuring karaoke nights, live music performances, and interactive shows. It's where guests can take the stage for karaoke or enjoy watching others perform. Complimentary entertainment with drinks available for purchase.

Best For: Karaoke enthusiasts. Live music fans. Groups looking for interactive fun. Night owls seeking late-night entertainment.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary (drinks extra)
  • Entertainment: Karaoke, live music
  • Atmosphere: Lively, interactive
  • Hours: Evening and late-night
  • Ships: Multiple classes

Your moment in the spotlight.

Spotlight Lounge gives everyone a chance to be a star. Whether you're belting out your favorite karaoke tune or cheering on fellow cruisers, the energy is contagious. Live musicians often perform, and themed nights add variety throughout the cruise. It's casual, fun entertainment that brings strangers together as an impromptu audience. Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?

Yes, entertainment is complimentary. Drinks are extra.

Do I need to sign up for karaoke?

Yes, sign up with the DJ or host when you arrive.

Is it family-friendly?

Varies—earlier sessions may be more family-oriented, while late-night is typically adults.

What kind of music?

Karaoke has a wide song selection. Live music varies by performer.

When is karaoke?

Usually evenings. Check your Cruise Compass for times.

Can I just watch?

Absolutely! Many guests come just to enjoy the show.
