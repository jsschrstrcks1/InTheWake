Solarium

Royal Caribbean — Adults-Only Retreat

Quick Answer: The Solarium is Royal Caribbean's complimentary adults-only (16+) retreat featuring a pool, hot tubs, and loungers in a serene glass-enclosed setting. Includes Solarium Bistro for lighter fare on many ships.

Best For: Adults seeking relaxation away from family pool crowds, morning coffee, afternoon reading, and peaceful hot tub soaks.

Key Facts

  • Age: Adults 16+ only
  • Price: Complimentary
  • Features: Pool, hot tubs, loungers
  • Dining: Solarium Bistro (many ships)
  • Location: Forward, upper decks

Features & Amenities

All features complimentary · Pool · Hot Tubs · Loungers · Solarium Bistro (where available)

Pool & Hot Tubs

  • Solarium Pool: Temperature-controlled, glass-enclosed
  • Whirlpools: 2-4 hot tubs with jets
  • Cantilevered Tubs: Some ships have tubs over the ocean

Solarium Bistro

  • Breakfast: Eggs, pastries, fruit
  • Lunch: Salads, sandwiches, grilled items
  • Bar Service: Poolside drinks

Tips

Pro Tip: Best times are early morning (before 9am) and late afternoon (after 4pm). Hot tubs get busy 5-7pm during happy hour. Save loungers with a towel but don't leave for more than 30-40 minutes.

Ship Availability

Available on most Royal Caribbean ships. Largest on Icon/Oasis Class (two-deck design with infinity edge). Glass-roofed design opens on pleasant days.

The Logbook — Real Guest Soundings

4.5 ★ out of 5 Solo cruiser • Symphony of the Seas • 2024

The Solarium is my favorite spot on the ship. Get there early on sea days — by 10am the loungers fill up. The Bistro breakfast is excellent and less crowded than the Windjammer. Hot tubs at sunset with a cocktail is the perfect end to a cruise day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age for Solarium?

Ages 16+ only. Staff enforce this and may ask for ID.

Is it free?

Yes, completely complimentary including Solarium Bistro food.

Best time to visit?

Early morning or late afternoon. Sea days are busiest.
