First Look

Carnival Jubilee arrived in December 2023 as the third Excel-class vessel and Galveston's largest homeport ship. At 183,521 GT, she features LNG propulsion, BOLT roller coaster, and the unique Texas-themed Shores zone.

Christened by Grammy-winning artist Gwen Stefani, Jubilee brings Gulf Coast culture to sea with Texas BBQ, country music, and coastal vibes. Sister to Mardi Gras (2021) and Celebration (2022), she shares Excel-class innovations while celebrating Texas heritage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Carnival Jubilee sail from?

Carnival Jubilee is homeported at Galveston, Texas year-round, offering 7-day Western Caribbean cruises to Cozumel, Costa Maya, Roatan, and Mahogany Bay.

What is The Shores zone?

The Shores is a Texas-themed zone exclusive to Carnival Jubilee. It features Gulf Coast-inspired dining (The Brass Magnolia), bars, and entertainment celebrating Texas culture and coastal vibes.

Who is the godmother of Carnival Jubilee?

Gwen Stefani, Grammy-winning artist and The Voice coach, christened Carnival Jubilee in December 2023 at Galveston, Texas.

Does Carnival Jubilee have BOLT roller coaster?

Yes, Carnival Jubilee features BOLT: Ultimate Sea Coaster, an electric-powered coaster reaching 40 mph on a track above the ship's top deck. Reservations recommended.

What makes Carnival Jubilee different from other Excel-class ships?

While sharing the Excel-class platform with Mardi Gras and Celebration, Jubilee has the unique The Shores zone (Texas-themed) instead of French Quarter or 820 Biscayne. Each ship celebrates its homeport city.

Is Carnival Jubilee good for Texas cruisers?

Absolutely. As Galveston's flagship and largest homeport ship, Jubilee eliminates the need for Texas cruisers to fly to Florida for a mega-ship experience. The Texas-themed zones add local flavor.
