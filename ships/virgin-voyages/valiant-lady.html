Valiant Lady

Valiant Lady — Virgin Voyages | In the Wake is a Virgin Class cruise ship operated by Virgin Voyages. She entered service in 2022, measures 110,000 gross tons, and carries approximately 2,770 guests at double occupancy.

Key Facts

  • Class: Lady Class
  • Year: 2022
  • Tonnage: 110,000 GT
  • Guests: 2,770
  • IMO: 9817329

Quick Answer: Valiant Lady is Virgin Voyages' second ship — 110,000 GT, 2,770 guests, launched in 2022. Adults-only (18+), she primarily sails Mediterranean and Caribbean itineraries with all dining included. This page covers deck plans, live tracking, dining venues, and videos.

Best For: Adults who want Virgin's unconventional cruise style on Mediterranean or transatlantic routes. Valiant Lady suits travelers looking for European ports without the traditional cruise formality — think Barcelona and Ibiza over black-tie dinners.

Valiant Lady is an identical sister to Scarlet Lady, Resilient Lady, and Brilliant Lady — same 110,000 GT hull, same 20+ included restaurants, same adults-only policy. What sets her apart is itinerary: Valiant Lady splits her time between the Mediterranean and Caribbean, making her the go-to choice for Virgin fans who want European ports. If you want Caribbean-only, Scarlet Lady sails year-round from Miami. If you want Greek Islands, Resilient Lady covers that region. The onboard experience is the same across all four ships — the choice is purely about where you want to go.

Key Facts

  • Ship Class: Lady Class — 110,000 GT, 2,770 guests, 1,160 crew
  • Signature Features: Adults-only (18+), 20+ included restaurants, no buffet, Squid Ink tattoo parlor, The Manor nightclub
  • Itineraries: Mediterranean (Barcelona, Ibiza, Marseille), Caribbean, transatlantic
  • Entered Service: 2022
