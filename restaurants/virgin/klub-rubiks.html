Klub Rubiks Review: The 80s Theme Night That Actually Works

Introduction. Klub Rubiks is Virgin Voyages' 80s-themed dance party — a recurring event in The Manor featuring house, groove, funk, pop, and disco with period-appropriate fashion strongly encouraged. The event draws a crowd that fully commits to the theme (neon, leg warmers, big hair, power suits), and the DJ curation leans into authentic 80s sounds rather than modern remixes. It is one of the most popular themed nights on the ship and regularly fills The Manor to capacity. No cover; drinks are Bar Tab or per-drink.

Drinks & Service

The Manor bars serve the full cocktail menu during Klub Rubiks. The cosmopolitan and the vodka cranberry are the popular picks (keeping with the retro vibe), and the Long Island iced tea makes occasional thematic sense. Draft beer and champagne also move fast. Where it falls short: the bars are predictably slammed during peak hours (11 PM-midnight). Cocktail quality is volume-bar standard. Some sailings feature themed drinks (neon-colored, served in novelty cups) that are more about presentation than taste. Stick to simple, strong drinks and order before the rush.

Service

The entertainment crew goes full 80s — costumes, hairstyles, dance moves. The DJ is critical to the success of Klub Rubiks, and when the music curation hits, it is genuinely excellent — building from early-80s new wave through synth-pop to peak-era dance floor anthems. The crew leads group dances and costume parades that get even reluctant dancers moving. Bar service is stretched thin but the entertainment service is top-notch.

Atmosphere

Klub Rubiks works because the crowd buys in. On a good night, 70-80% of attendees are in costume, and the collective commitment to the theme creates an energy that transcends a typical cruise ship dance night. The lighting, music, and decorations are well-executed. The nostalgia factor hits perfectly for guests who lived through the 80s, and the kitsch appeal works for younger guests who appreciate the aesthetic. Where it falls short: the quality varies by sailing — on some voyages, the crowd energy is electric; on others, participation is lower and the event feels like a standard club night with an 80s playlist. The Manor gets extremely warm during peak attendance. The event runs shorter than you want it to when the energy is right.

Conclusion

Rating: 3.9/5. Klub Rubiks is one of the best themed events on Virgin Voyages — an 80s dance party where the crowd, music, and crew all commit to the concept. It falls short on bar service (overwhelmed), consistency (varies by sailing), and heat management. If you enjoy theme nights, this is the one to attend. Tip: pack or buy an 80s costume in advance — the ship shop sells accessories, but the best costumes come from home. The more you commit, the more fun it is.
