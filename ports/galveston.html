Galveston

Galveston: Texas's Gateway to the Western Caribbean

Galveston is the cruise capital of Texas, offering Gulf Coast cruisers easy access to Caribbean adventures without flying to Florida. The historic island town — with its Victorian architecture, Gulf beaches, and legendary Strand District — makes arriving early an absolute must.

Three major cruise lines operate from Galveston's two terminals, sailing to Cozumel, Costa Maya, Roatán, and beyond. Houston's airports are just an hour away, and for Texans, it's the obvious choice for Caribbean cruising.

Texas-Sized Tip: The Strand District is your pre-cruise playground — Victorian buildings, fantastic restaurants, and the best antique shopping in Texas. Budget an extra afternoon.

Getting to the Port

  • From Houston (IAH): 75-90 minutes via I-45 South
  • From Houston Hobby (HOU): 60 minutes via I-45 South
  • From downtown Houston: 50-60 minutes via I-45 South
  • Galveston Island Trolley: Runs along the Seawall and Strand

Parking Options

  • Port parking: On-site covered and uncovered lots — reserve in advance
  • Off-site lots: Several third-party options with shuttle service
  • Cost: $10-20/day depending on covered vs. uncovered
  • Pro tip: Covered parking is worth it for the Texas sun

Nearby Hotels

  • Hotel Galvez & Spa: Historic beachfront luxury, "Queen of the Gulf"
  • Tremont House: Boutique hotel in the Strand District
  • San Luis Resort: Full resort experience with pools and spa
  • Harbor House: Budget-friendly, walking distance to port

Pre-Cruise Activities

  • The Strand District: Historic Victorian shopping and dining
  • Moody Gardens: Aquarium, rainforest, and discovery pyramids
  • Galveston Island State Park: Beaches, birding, and kayaking
  • Bishop's Palace: Stunning Victorian architecture tour
  • Seawall: 10 miles of beaches, restaurants, and Gulf views

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Which cruise lines sail from Galveston?
A: Royal Caribbean, Carnival, and Disney all operate from Galveston.

Q: Is there Uber/Lyft from Houston airports?
A: Yes, readily available. Expect $60-80 from IAH, $50-60 from Hobby.

Q: Should I arrive a day early?
A: Highly recommended — Galveston is worth exploring and Houston traffic is unpredictable.

Quick Answer: Texas's cruise capital with historic Victorian charm — the Strand District, Moody Gardens, and Gulf beaches make this island worth exploring before Caribbean adventures.
