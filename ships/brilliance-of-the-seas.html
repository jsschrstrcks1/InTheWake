Brilliance of the Seas

Brilliance blends glass-and-steel elegance with friendly, human scale. She’s a favorite for port-intensive routes and scenic regions.

Days onboard swing from coffee with a view to sunset at the heli-pad and late-night acoustic sets in the pub.

Cabins use space efficiently; a balcony elevates everything on a Brilliance itinerary.

Dining highlights include a bright MDR, reliable Windjammer, and intimate specialty venues.

For travelers who value atmosphere and itinerary over superstructures, Brilliance delivers beautifully.
