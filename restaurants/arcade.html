Arcade

Royal Caribbean — Games & Entertainment

Quick Answer: The Arcade offers pay-per-play video games, racing simulators, and prize machines. Games cost $1-5 each, charged to your SeaPass card. Located near Adventure Ocean on most ships.

Best For: Families with kids and teens, gamers wanting entertainment between activities, rainy port days, and evening teen hangout time.

Key Facts

  • Price: $1-5 per game (SeaPass)
  • Hours: 9:00 AM - Midnight
  • Location: Near Adventure Ocean
  • Ages: All ages welcome

Games & Pricing

All games: $1-5 each · Charged to SeaPass · Set spending limits at Guest Services

  • Racing Simulators: $2-4 — Motion-seat car/motorcycle racing
  • Shooting Games: $2-3 — Light gun games, zombie shooters
  • Sports Games: $2-3 — Basketball, air hockey, football toss
  • Classic Arcade: $1-2 — Pac-Man, racing, fighting games
  • Prize/Claw Machines: $2-3 — Win stuffed animals and toys

Tips for Parents

Managing Spending: Visit Guest Services to set daily spending limits on kids' SeaPass cards. Monitor charges via the Royal Caribbean app or stateroom TV.

Hours & Location

Typically 9:00 AM - Midnight. Located near Adventure Ocean and teen areas. Available on all Royal Caribbean ships fleet-wide.

The Logbook — Real Guest Soundings

3.5 ★ out of 5 Family of 4 • Oasis of the Seas • 2024

The arcade was a lifesaver on sea days. Games are pricey — expect $20-40 per kid per session. Set a spending limit at Guest Services on day one. Selection varies by ship; newer ships have better games.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the arcade free?

No, games are pay-per-play ($1-5 each), charged to your SeaPass card.

Can I limit spending?

Yes, set limits at Guest Services or monitor via the app.

What games are available?

Racing simulators, shooting games, sports games, classics, and prize machines. Selection varies by ship.
