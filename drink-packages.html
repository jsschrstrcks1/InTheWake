A prepaid punch card for specialty coffee drinks. Perfect for occasional coffee drinkers who don't want a full beverage package.

How It Works

  • 15 punches per card
  • Small drinks (espresso, cappuccino, latte) = 1 punch
  • Large or iced drinks = 2 punches

Important Policies

Stateroom Requirement

ALL adults (21+) in the same stateroom must purchase the same adult beverage package. There are no exceptions ‚Äî not for pregnancy, medical conditions, religious reasons, or designated drivers. This policy prevents package sharing.

Minor Policy

If any adult in the cabin purchases the Deluxe Beverage Package, all minors (under 21) must purchase the Refreshment Package. This is mandatory.

Gratuity

All packages include 18% automatic gratuity. This is added to the per-day price and cannot be removed.

Pre-Cruise vs. Onboard

Always buy packages before your cruise through the Cruise Planner. Pre-cruise pricing is typically 20-30% cheaper than purchasing onboard.
