Founder & Editor

Ken Baker

Ken Baker is a traveler, pastor, and storyteller with more than 25,000 miles sailed on cruise ships — across the Caribbean, Pacific, Alaska, the Sea of Cortez, and beyond. He founded In the Wake to share practical cruise planning tools, honest ship reviews, and faith-scented reflections for smoother sailings.

After years of helping friends and church members plan their first cruises, Ken kept hearing the same questions: Which ship is right for me? How do I navigate a port day? What should I actually pack? In the Wake grew out of those conversations — a logbook of everything he wished someone had told him before his first sailing.

Ken approaches cruising as both analyst and pilgrim. He loves digging into deck plans and comparing beverage-package values, but he is equally drawn to the quiet moments — the sunrise from a balcony, light on water, the ship's chapel before the crowds wake. Travel, for Ken, is about more than logistics; it is about encountering beauty and returning home changed.

Expertise

  • Cruise planning: first-cruise guidance, itinerary comparison, booking strategy.
  • Royal Caribbean: extensive time across Oasis, Quantum, and Freedom classes.
  • Solo cruising: solo cabins, dining alone, finding community at sea.
  • Accessibility: wheelchair-accessible travel, mobility planning, service-animal policies.
  • Photography: ship and port photography, light-on-water compositions.
  • Pastoral care: faith-scented reflection at sea.

Philosophy

The calmest seas are found in another's wake.

Every page here is made with care, offered as worship to God, in gratitude for the beautiful things He made for us to enjoy. In the Wake exists to be the resource Ken wished he had: honest, thorough, and independent of cruise-line marketing. No ads. No sponsored content. Just one cruiser's logbook, freely shared.
