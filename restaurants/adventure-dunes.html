Adventure Dunes

Royal Caribbean — Mini Golf Course

Quick Answer: Adventure Dunes is a complimentary themed mini-golf course on select Royal Caribbean ships. Features a 9-hole course with ocean views and creative obstacles. Open during daylight hours on a first-come, first-served basis. Family-friendly activity suitable for all ages.

Best For: Families with children, groups looking for casual competition, couples wanting outdoor activity, and anyone who enjoys mini golf with stunning sea views.

Key Facts

  • Price: Complimentary for all guests
  • Hours: ~9:00 AM to sunset (weather permitting)
  • Reservations: Not required — first-come, first-served
  • Duration: 20-30 minutes per round
  • Location: Sports Deck (upper decks)

Course design and theming varies by ship. Some ships feature different named courses like Lost Dunes, Navigator Dunes, or Freedom Fairways.

Ship-Specific Variants
  • Icon Class — May feature enhanced theming and design
  • Oasis Class — Lost Dunes theme with tropical elements
  • Freedom Class — Freedom Fairways with unique obstacles
  • Voyager Class — Classic mini-golf course design

Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: The mini-golf course is located on upper decks and may require navigating steps or uneven surfaces. Guests with mobility concerns should assess accessibility on embarkation day. Junior putters available for children. Staff can provide assistance with equipment selection.

Where You'll Find It

Adventure Dunes is typically located on the Sports Deck near other outdoor activities like the basketball court and jogging track.

Hours

  • Opening: Around 9:00 AM daily
  • Closing: Sunset (varies by season and location)
  • Best Times: Early morning or late afternoon for shorter waits
  • Weather: May close during inclement weather

Tips for Best Experience

  • Visit early morning on sea days for minimal crowds
  • Late afternoon offers beautiful sunset lighting
  • Port days typically have shorter waits
  • Bring sunscreen and sunglasses for daytime play

Adventure Dunes Review: Fun Family Activity with Ocean Views

Introduction. Mini golf at sea is one of those quintessential cruise activities that's easy to overlook but surprisingly enjoyable. Adventure Dunes provided our family with a fun competitive outing on sea days, and the ocean backdrop makes it a unique experience you can't get at your local putt-putt course.

The Course

The 9-hole course features creative obstacles including bridges, tunnels, and themed elements. While not as elaborate as a shore-side mini golf attraction, it's well-maintained and offers enough variety to be engaging. The artificial turf plays consistently, and most holes are achievable for players of all skill levels. A few challenging holes keep it interesting for adults while remaining accessible for kids.

Experience

We played twice during our cruise—once on a sea day morning (moderate wait of about 10 minutes) and once late afternoon (no wait). The views are the real highlight: putting while surrounded by ocean on a beautiful day is genuinely special. Our kids (ages 7 and 10) loved it and asked to return multiple times. The staff was friendly and efficient with equipment distribution.

Conclusion

Adventure Dunes is a pleasant complimentary activity that's perfect for filling time between other activities or shows. Don't expect a world-class mini golf experience, but do expect a fun, family-friendly outing with unbeatable views. Rating: 4.0/5. Recommended for families and anyone who enjoys casual outdoor activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Adventure Dunes mini golf free on Royal Caribbean?

Yes, Adventure Dunes mini golf is completely complimentary for all guests. No reservation, sign-up, or additional fee is required. Simply show up, grab equipment, and play.

What are the hours for Adventure Dunes?

Adventure Dunes is typically open from around 9:00 AM until sunset. Hours may vary based on weather conditions, ship schedule, and time of year. Check the daily Cruise Compass for exact hours.

How long does a round take?

A typical round of mini golf takes 20-30 minutes for a group of 4 players. Larger groups or those with young children may take slightly longer.

Is mini golf suitable for young children?

Yes, Adventure Dunes is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. Junior-sized putters are available for children. Young children (under 5) may need adult assistance and supervision.

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

No, all equipment is provided free of charge including putters (adult and junior sizes), golf balls, and score cards with pencils. Simply return equipment after your round.
