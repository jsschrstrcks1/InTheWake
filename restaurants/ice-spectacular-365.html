Ice Spectacular: 365

Royal Caribbean — Ice Show

Quick Answer: A four-seasons ice production on Wonder of the Seas where each act represents a different season. From the bloom of spring to winter's chill, professional skaters bring the changing year to life with seasonal music, costumes, and stunning choreography.

Best For: Families, figure skating fans, anyone who appreciates artistic performance on ice.

Key Facts

  • Type: Ice Show
  • Venue: Studio B
  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Duration: Approximately 35 minutes
  • Ships: Wonder of the Seas
  • Premiered: 2022

Pro Tips:

  • Arrive 15 minutes early for center seats
  • The rink-side rows offer the most immersive experience
  • It gets cold near the ice — bring a layer
  • No flash photography during performances

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ice Spectacular: 365 free?

Yes, Ice Spectacular: 365 is complimentary and included in your cruise fare. No additional charge.

Do I need reservations for Ice Spectacular: 365?

No reservations required. Seating is open and first-come, first-served. Arrive early for the best seats.

Which ships have Ice Spectacular: 365?

Ice Spectacular: 365 is currently available on: Wonder of the Seas. Show lineups can change, so verify with Royal Caribbean before sailing.

How long is Ice Spectacular: 365?

Ice Spectacular: 365 runs approximately 35 minutes.

Is Ice Spectacular: 365 appropriate for children?

Yes, Ice Spectacular: 365 is suitable for all ages. Younger children may enjoy the visual spectacle even if they don't follow the storyline.

Where should I sit for the best view?

Center seats in the middle rows offer the best sightlines. The balcony level provides a good overview of choreography and staging. Arrive 15-20 minutes early.
