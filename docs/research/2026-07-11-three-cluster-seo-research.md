## Cluster 1 — Costa Maya / Mesoamerican Reef diving

### Keyword intent

Cruisers searching "Costa Maya diving," "Mesoamerican Reef cruise port," "Mahahual scuba" want a honest answer to: *Can I do a real reef dive on a port day, and should I book ship or independent?*

### Key findings

- Mesoamerican Barrier Reef sits **<0.5 mile** off Mahahual; pier-to-bubbles can be **<1 hour** from gangway.
- **Local reef** (two-tank morning, 30–60 ft): forgiving for Open Water and discovery dives; **$85–$140** independent; ship excursions **+$30–50** for ship-wait guarantee.
- **Banco Chinchorro** (~22 mi offshore): UNESCO biosphere; **90 min boat each way**, 4–6 hr trip total; **7–9 hr port commitment** — marginal on standard 8–10 hr port days.
- Operators consistently named: **Doctor Dive**, **Mar Adentro**; also **Alux**, **Pepe Dive** — comparison article still outstanding.
- **Mama Viña wreck** is Playa del Carmen (250 km north) — not a Costa Maya site; common conflation in competitor content.
- Mexican law: **reef-safe sunscreen** required at marine parks.

### Perfect Day Mexico / port shelf-life

Royal Caribbean's original Perfect Day Mexico (Mahahual) was **rejected by SEMARNAT on 2026-05-19**. RC has signaled re-engagement on a redesigned project. Sandbox research noted RC plans **Perfect Day Mexico replacing Costa Maya port late 2027** — this affects how long Mahahual/Costa Maya diving content stays current. Monitor itinerary changes and RC press.
