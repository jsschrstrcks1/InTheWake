Roatán: My Hidden Reef Paradise

Sailing into Mahogany Bay with the jungle dropping straight into the sea is stunning – this is the port everyone underestimates and then falls madly in love with. My perfect day: off the ship and straight to West Bay Beach (15-minute $10 pp taxi) – the snorkeling right off the shore is world-class (I bring my own mask and see more fish than any paid tour).

Lunch at Bananarama or Infinity Bay – fresh lionfish tacos and monkey lalas. Afternoon is either the Gumbalimba Park (monkeys climb on you!) or a private boat to the reef. Sunset at the Roatán sign or back on the ship watching the sun drop behind the mountains.

The Moment That Stays With Me: A spotted eagle ray gliding right under me while I floated over the reef – pure National Geographic moment.

Getting Around Roatán

Mahogany Bay (Royal's private) – free chairlift or walk to beach. Coxen Hole town dock – $10–15 pp taxi to West Bay.

  • West Bay Beach: $10 pp taxi, 15 minutes
  • Gumbalimba Park: Near West Bay
  • Mahogany Bay Beach: Free chairlift from ship

Positively Worded Word of Warning

The laid-back island pace is Roatán's gift – let the "mañana" vibe wash over you and enjoy the cheapest perfect beach day in the Caribbean!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Best snorkeling from shore?
A: West Bay – Tabyana or Infinity section.

Q: Safe?
A: Tourist areas are very safe – stick to West End/West Bay.

Q: Currency?
A: USD accepted everywhere.
