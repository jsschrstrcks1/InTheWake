The Attic

Royal Caribbean — Comedy Club

Quick Answer: The Attic is Royal Caribbean's dedicated comedy club lounge, typically located on or near the Royal Promenade. Features professional stand-up comedians with family-friendly early shows and adults-only late-night shows with more mature content.

Best For: Comedy fans. Couples seeking late-night entertainment. Adults wanting humor for grown-ups after the kids go to bed. Anyone who enjoys live stand-up.

Key Facts

  • Location: Royal Promenade area (varies by ship)
  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Shows: Multiple nightly performances
  • Early Shows: Family-friendly content
  • Late Shows: Adults-only, mature humor

Laugh out loud at sea.

The Attic brings professional stand-up comedy to Royal Caribbean ships. Early evening shows keep content clean for families, while late-night performances unleash the comedians for adults-only sets with edgier material. Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Attic free?

Yes, all comedy shows are complimentary and included in your cruise fare.

What's the difference between early and late shows?

Early shows are family-friendly with clean content. Late shows are adults-only with mature, uncensored humor.

Do I need reservations?

No reservations needed—seating is first-come, first-served. Arrive early for the best spots.

Can I bring drinks?

Yes, bar service is available throughout the show.

What age for late shows?

Late shows are typically 18+ only due to mature content.

How long are the shows?

Shows typically run 45 minutes to an hour.
