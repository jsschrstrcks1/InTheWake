## Rule
Every page must have a `<title>` element with non-empty text. The title is the browser-tab label, the default bookmark name, and the search-result headline.

## Why (rationale)
A page without a title shows as "Untitled" in browser tabs and search results. The 2025-11 audit found 5 pages with missing titles. Basic presence is checked by the port validator's basic-HTML structure function.

## Fix guidance
Add `<title>Page Name — In the Wake</title>` to `<head>`. The convention is `[Page Subject] — In the Wake`. Keep under 60 characters for search-result display.
