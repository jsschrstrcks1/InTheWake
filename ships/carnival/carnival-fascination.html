Frequently Asked Questions

When was Carnival Fascination scrapped?

Carnival Fascination was beached at Gadani Ship Breaking Yard in Pakistan on February 18, 2022. Workers stripped the vessel of valuable items in just under a year.

Who christened Carnival Fascination?

Legendary actress Mary Tyler Moore christened Carnival Fascination in 1994, a perfect choice given the ship's Hollywood and cinema theme.

Why was Carnival Fascination scrapped in Pakistan?

Carnival sold Fascination to Chinese company Century Cruises in November 2020. When they later decided to scrap her instead of operate her, they chose Gadani in Pakistan rather than the Turkish yards Carnival typically uses. She's the only Carnival ship ever scrapped there.

What was Carnival Fascination's theme?

Carnival Fascination featured an elaborate Hollywood/cinema theme celebrating the golden age of film. Her interiors were decorated with movie memorabilia and star-worthy design touches.

What names did Carnival Fascination have?

She sailed as "Fascination" (1994-2007), then "Carnival Fascination" (2007-2020). After sale, she briefly became "Century Harmony" (2020-2021), then "Y Harmony" (2021-2022) before scrapping.
