Carnival Valor in Cozumel

Ship Overview

Carnival Valor is the third of five Conquest-class ships, entering service in 2004. Christened by celebrated journalist Katie Couric in Miami, she sails year-round from New Orleans, Louisiana, delivering the classic Fun Ship experience to Gulf Coast travelers.

Built by Fincantieri at Monfalcone, Italy at a cost of $500 million, Carnival Valor will relocate to Mobile, Alabama in May 2027, marking Carnival's return to that port.

FAQ

Where does Carnival Valor sail from?

New Orleans, Louisiana — Western Caribbean and Mexico cruises. Relocating to Mobile, Alabama in May 2027.

Who is the godmother of Carnival Valor?

Katie Couric christened Carnival Valor in Miami on December 17, 2004, while hosting NBC's Today show.

What class is Carnival Valor?

Third of five Conquest-class ships (2004), with sisters Conquest, Glory, Liberty, and Freedom.
