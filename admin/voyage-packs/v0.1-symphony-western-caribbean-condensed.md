## Getting to the ship — and getting home

### Arrival: Friday recommended, Saturday required

**Friday — fly in.** Same-day Saturday flights to a 3:30 PM all-aboard are risky. Pre-cruise hotel in Brickell, Downtown Miami, or Miami Beach gives margin and lets you use Friday afternoon for Wynwood, South Beach, or Little Havana.

**Saturday — embarkation.**

- **Terminal:** **Royal Caribbean Terminal A** at PortMiami (verify your assignment).
- **MIA → PortMiami:** Uber/Lyft $30–45, 20–30 min. Taxi flat-rate $32.
- **FLL → PortMiami:** Uber/Lyft $60–90, 45–60 min.
- **If driving:** Terminal AA garage **$35/day** (~$245/week, firsthand May 2026 — verify); offsite Miami long-term lots $7–14/day with shuttle.
- **Embark window:** 11:00 AM – 3:30 PM. **All-aboard 3:00 PM.** Ship departs 4:00 PM.
- **Royal app online check-in** — complete before arrival.

### Day 8 debark and the trip home

- **The night before:** bags in the hall by ~11 PM with color-coded tag.
- **6:00 – 8:00 AM:** Breakfast at the Windjammer or a sit-down room.
- **7:00 AM:** Customs clearance complete. Self-disembarkation begins.
- **7:30 – 10:00 AM:** Walk-off in waves. All passengers off by 10:00 AM.

**Carry-on with you:** change of clothes, all medications, passport, phone charger, valuables.

**Getting from PortMiami to MIA.** Uber/Lyft on Saturday afternoon, **$30–45, 25–30 min**. Don't overthink it.

**Killing 4–6 hours before an evening flight:**

- **Bayfront Park / Bayside Marketplace** — 10-min walk.
- **Pérez Art Museum Miami (PAMM)** — 15-min walk. $20 entry. Air-conditioned.
- **Brickell** — short Uber. Cuban coffee, lunch, Uber to MIA when ready.

**Airport-arrival timing.**

| Flight | Leave PortMiami by |
|---|---|
| Domestic, after 4 PM | 1:30 PM |
| International, after 6 PM | 2:30 PM |

MIA TSA lines run 30–60 min during Saturday-afternoon cruise rush.

## Areas of interest during the cruise

### On the ship — Symphony spots worth knowing

- **The Boardwalk** — Coney Island-style outdoor neighborhood with the AquaTheater at the back, hand-carved carousel, and Johnny Rockets.
- **Central Park** — open-air park neighborhood with 12,000+ live plants, walking paths, restaurants, classical music in the evenings.
- **Royal Promenade** — the indoor "main street" running the ship's length; parades, shops, pubs.
- **Ultimate Abyss** — 10-deck slide from Deck 16 down to the Boardwalk. The most signature Oasis-class feature.
- **Solarium** — adults-only glass-domed pool deck. Quiet pool day in the Caribbean.
- **Vitality Spa thermal-suite day pass** — sea-day intro rates.
- **AquaTheater** — open-air water-and-acrobatics show; book in the Royal app.
- **Two70°** — wraparound aft glass lounge; morphing video walls; evening shows.

### Sea-day priorities (Day 2 + Day 6)

1. **Sunrise coffee on the Boardwalk or Central Park** — neighborhoods empty in the first hour.
2. **Book the AquaTheater show** in the Royal app before they fill.
3. **Ultimate Abyss** first hour after opening — no line.
4. **Vitality Spa thermal suite** — sea-day morning intro rate.
5. **Specialty dining reservation** for Day 6 evening — Chops, Jamie's, Wonderland fill.

### Specialty dining picks

- **Chops Grille** — Royal's steakhouse standard.
- **Jamie's Italian** — Jamie Oliver pasta and pizza; family-friendly.
- **Wonderland** — Royal's "imaginative cuisine" tasting menu; the splurge experience.
- **Izumi** — Japanese; sushi + hibachi rooms.
- **Hooked Seafood** — New England-style seafood.

### Port-day picks (one each)

- **Roatán** — West Bay Beach via taxi (~$6–8/person each way) with offshore reef snorkeling. The day most cruisers come for. Snorkel tour to the Mesoamerican Reef ($35–55 indep) is the upgrade.
- **Costa Maya** — pool complex inside the gated port is free with your cruise card (easiest day). Chacchoben Mayan ruins (~$50 indep / ~$80 ship) is the cultural choice.
- **Cozumel** — Palancar Reef snorkel tour (~$50 indep / ~$95 ship). Two hours, calm water, the conversion experience. Or Mercado Municipal in San Miguel for real Mexican shopping (skip the pier tourist shops).
- **CocoCay** — Free Chill Island and South Beach are the included choice. Coco Beach Club ($110) is the adults-quiet upgrade. Thrill Waterpark ($60) is for adventure-seekers.

### Photo spots worth knowing

- **Sailaway from Central Park or the Boardwalk** — Star Island superyachts, Miami skyline at golden hour.
- **Ultimate Abyss top platform** — 10-deck drop perspective.
- **AquaTheater show** — high-dive acrobatics + projection.
- **Roatán West Bay Beach** — turquoise water + reef visible offshore.
- **CocoCay overlook** from the Solarium deck — turquoise lagoon below.
