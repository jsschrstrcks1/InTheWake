Point & Feather

Royal Caribbean — British Pub

Quick Answer: Point & Feather is a classic British-style pub found on Voyager Class ships. Offers a selection of ales, lagers, and drafts alongside traditional pub fare. Features dart boards, pub games, and a warm, convivial atmosphere perfect for an evening pint.

Best For: Beer enthusiasts who appreciate a good selection of ales and drafts. Anyone seeking a casual pub atmosphere. Groups looking to play darts or pub games.

Key Facts

  • Location: Royal Promenade, Voyager Class ships
  • Cost: À la carte or Drink Package
  • Style: Traditional British pub
  • Entertainment: Darts, pub games, live music
  • Hours: Open daily, typically noon to late

A proper British pub at sea.

Point & Feather brings the warmth of an English pub to Royal Caribbean's Voyager Class ships. Pull up a stool, order a pint, and enjoy the cozy atmosphere with dark wood, brass fixtures, and the sounds of friendly conversation. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Point & Feather included in the drink package?

Yes, most drinks are covered by the Deluxe Beverage Package. Some premium selections may have an upcharge.

Do they serve food?

Yes, traditional pub fare like fish and chips, shepherd's pie, and meat pies are available.

Can I play darts?

Yes, dart boards are available. Games are first-come, first-served.

Is there live music?

Yes, some evenings feature live entertainment or music in the pub.

What are the hours?

Point & Feather typically opens around noon and stays open late into the evening.

Is this a good spot for groups?

Absolutely! The pub atmosphere and games make it perfect for groups.
