Ovation of the Seas

Quantum‑class ships lean into tech and smart design: expect the North Star observation capsule for soaring views, the RipCord by iFLY skydiving simulator, and the transformative Two70 venue that blends live performance with panoramic projection.

Indoors are prioritized more than on some classes, with enclosed pool spaces that shine in cooler or variable climates; that’s why these ships often rotate between Asia, Alaska, and seasonal regions. The layout helps the ship feel active even when weather keeps everyone inside.

Dining typically mixes classic main dining with flexible or themed venues, and entertainment ranges from high‑energy production shows to innovative multimedia experiences in Two70. Staterooms tend to be thoughtfully designed with good storage and tech touches.

If you value weather‑proof amenities, modern aesthetics, and signature attractions you can’t find elsewhere, Quantum‑class ships deliver a refined, future‑forward take on mainstream cruising.

Public perception at launch centered on how well the ship delivered the class promise—mixing amenities, entertainment, and service—while early reviews often highlighted crew friendliness and the ship’s signature venues. As the vessel matured, refits and programming kept it aligned with guest expectations.
