Suite Sun Deck

Royal Caribbean — Exclusive Suite Area

Quick Answer: Suite Sun Deck is an exclusive outdoor relaxation area reserved for suite guests on many Royal Caribbean ships. Features private loungers, dedicated bar service, and a peaceful escape from the crowded main pool decks. Access is included with suite bookings.

Best For: Suite guests seeking privacy and exclusivity. Those wanting to escape crowded pool areas. Guests who value quiet relaxation. Anyone celebrating special occasions in a suite.

Key Facts

  • Access: Suite guests only
  • Cost: Included with suite booking
  • Features: Private loungers, bar service
  • Location: Varies by ship
  • Atmosphere: Quiet, exclusive

Your private slice of deck space.

Suite Sun Deck offers suite guests a dedicated outdoor space away from the main pool deck crowds. Comfortable loungers, attentive service, and a relaxed atmosphere make it the perfect place to spend a sea day. Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access Suite Sun Deck?

Only guests booked in suite-class staterooms have access.

Is it free?

Yes, access is included with your suite booking.

Does it have a pool?

It depends on the ship. Some have small pools or hot tubs; others are lounge-only areas.

How do I get access?

Your suite keycard typically grants access. Staff may verify at entry.

Is there food service?

Typically bar service only. Food may need to be brought from nearby venues.

How does it differ from Suite Enclave?

Suite Enclave (Icon Class) is larger and more comprehensive. Suite Sun Deck is an outdoor deck space on other ships.
