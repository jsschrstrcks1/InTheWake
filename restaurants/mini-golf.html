Mini Golf

Royal Caribbean — Outdoor Course

Quick Answer: Mini golf is complimentary on most Royal Caribbean ships. Themed 9-hole courses with ocean views, creative obstacles, and artificial turf. Equipment provided free. First-come, first-served. Great for families, groups, and casual fun. Different ships have uniquely themed courses (Lost Dunes, Freedom Fairways, etc.).

Best For: Families with children, groups seeking casual competition, couples wanting outdoor activity, and anyone who enjoys mini golf with ocean views.

Key Facts

  • Price: Complimentary
  • Hours: ~9 AM to sunset (weather permitting)
  • Equipment: Provided free (putters, balls, scorecards)
  • Duration: 20-30 minutes per round
  • Location: Sports Deck / upper decks

Enjoy a round of mini golf with ocean breezes and stunning sea views.

Mini golf at sea combines the classic putt-putt experience with incredible ocean views. Each ship class has uniquely themed courses—from tropical Lost Dunes to the nautical-themed Freedom Fairways. Course Details

Cost: Complimentary · Equipment: Provided free · Rounds: Unlimited

Course Features

  • 9-hole themed courses
  • Creative obstacles
  • Ocean view backdrop
  • Artificial turf greens
  • Ship-specific theming

Equipment Provided

  • Standard putters
  • Junior-sized putters
  • Golf balls
  • Score cards and pencils

Course Rules

  • Maximum 6 players per group
  • Let faster groups play through
  • Return equipment after play
  • No food/drinks on course

Course names vary by ship: Lost Dunes, Freedom Fairways, Adventure Dunes, Navigator Dunes. All are free and offer similar 9-hole experiences with unique theming.

Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: Mini golf courses are on upper decks and may require navigating steps or uneven surfaces. Guests with mobility concerns should assess accessibility. Junior putters available for children. Staff can assist with equipment selection.

Tips for Best Experience

  • Early morning on sea days = minimal crowds
  • Port days often have shortest waits
  • Late afternoon offers beautiful sunset lighting
  • Bring sunscreen and sunglasses for daytime play
  • Course may close during inclement weather

Ships with Mini Golf

Available on most Royal Caribbean ships. Course names and themes vary:

  • Oasis Class: Lost Dunes
  • Freedom Class: Freedom Fairways
  • Voyager Class: Mini golf course
  • Other Classes: Various themed courses

4.0 ★ out of 5 Royal Caribbean Fleet

Mini Golf Review: Fun Family Activity

Mini golf became our family's go-to activity between other cruise events. Our kids (ages 7 and 10) loved the competition, and the ocean backdrop made it feel special. The course was well-maintained with creative obstacles that kept things interesting for adults too.

We played three times during a 7-night cruise. Morning was best—no wait at all. Afternoon sea days had about a 10-minute wait. The free equipment was in good condition, and junior putters meant even our younger one could play comfortably.

Rating: 4.0/5. Won't blow your mind, but it's a pleasant free activity that's perfect for filling time. The views are the real bonus.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is mini golf free?

Yes, mini golf is complimentary on Royal Caribbean ships. Equipment provided at no charge.

Do I need to bring my own putter?

No, all equipment is provided free including putters, balls, and scorecards.

Is mini golf suitable for young kids?

Yes, it's family-friendly. Junior putters are available. Young children may need assistance.

How long is the wait typically?

Morning and port days often have no wait. Sea day afternoons may have 5-15 minute waits.

What are the hours?

Typically 9 AM to sunset, weather permitting. Check the Cruise Compass for exact times.
