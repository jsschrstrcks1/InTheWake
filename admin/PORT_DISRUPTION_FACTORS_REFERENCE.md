## 1. Religious & Dress Code Requirements

### Islamic Ports
| Port | Mosque Dress Code | Ramadan Impact | Friday Closures |
|------|-------------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Abu Dhabi | Women: cover hair, arms, legs (free abayas provided). Men: long trousers, sleeved shirt | Public eating/drinking/smoking prohibited in daylight | Grand Mosque closes non-Muslims ~12-3pm |
| Dubai | Same as Abu Dhabi | Same as Abu Dhabi | Same pattern |
| Muscat, Oman | Shoulders+knees covered. Women: headscarf at Sultan Qaboos Mosque | Same restrictions | Mosque closed during Friday prayers |
| Istanbul | Blue Mosque: women headscarf + cover legs; men long pants. Remove shoes | Public eating discouraged during fasting hours | Closed during 5 daily prayers |
| Kuala Lumpur | Batu Caves: modest dress, remove shoes in prayer areas | Restaurant hours disrupted | Some attractions modified hours |

**Ramadan dates shift ~11 days earlier annually:** 2026: ~Feb-Mar | 2027: ~Jan-Feb | 2028: ~Dec-Jan

### Jewish Observances (Israel)
- **Shabbat (every Friday sundown – Saturday sundown):** Public transport stops. Restaurants close. Markets close. Attractions shut down.
- **Yom Kippur (Sept-Oct):** EVERYTHING closes — no transport, restaurants, shops, attractions.
- **Passover (Mar-Apr):** Modified hours, some restaurants closed.
- **Affects:** Haifa, Tel Aviv, Ashdod

### Hindu/Buddhist Temples
- **Bali:** Sarongs required (usually provided). Nyepi (Balinese New Year, ~March): entire island shuts down — no transport, restaurants closed, airports closed. If your ship is in port, you cannot leave.
- **Sri Lanka/India:** Modest dress, remove shoes. Avoid pointing feet at statues.
- **Bangkok:** Shoulders+knees covered at temples. Women must not touch monks.

### Christian Holidays
- **Mediterranean/Caribbean:** Easter Holy Week — churches close to tourists. Good Friday processions may block streets.
- **Christmas (Dec 24-26):** Many attractions modified hours or closed.

### Extreme Heat Ports (Outdoor Activity Dangerous)
- **Abu Dhabi/Dubai (May-Sept):** 40-48°C. Walking dangerous after 10am.
- **Muscat (May-Sept):** 35-45°C + high humidity.
- **Red Sea ports (Jun-Sept):** 35-40°C + humidity.
- **South Asia (May-Jun, Sept-Oct):** Monsoon heat 32-35°C + extreme humidity.

### Tender Port Weather Sensitivity
All tender ports vulnerable when: wind >15 knots, significant swell, poor visibility (fog/rain).
Wheelchair users face additional barriers: steep tender steps, swaying boats, beach landings.

## 4. Safety & Access Issues

### Ports Where Ship Docks Far From Town
| Port | Distance to Attractions | Transport Needed |
|------|------------------------|-----------------|
| Kuala Lumpur (Port Klang) | 45km to city center | 45-90 min taxi/shuttle |
| Shanghai (Wusongkou) | 30km to The Bund | 30-45 min taxi |
| Buenos Aires | 20km | 30-45 min taxi |
| Hamburg | 5-10km to city | 20-30 min taxi/metro |
| Singapore (Pasir Panjang) | 8km | 15-20 min transit |
| Barcelona (Adossat terminal) | 5km+ to Gothic Quarter | 20-30 min taxi/shuttle |

### Ports with Known Taxi/Scam Issues
- **Naples:** Unlicensed taxis overcharge. Use only white official taxis.
- **Havana:** Classic car "tourist taxis" at inflated rates. Agree price first.
- **Cartagena:** Unofficial guides overcharge. Pickpocketing in old town.
- **Mexico (general):** Timeshare pitches at ports. Negotiate taxi fares upfront.

## 5. Accessibility Barriers

### Cobblestone/Steep Terrain Ports
- **Venice:** Cobblestones + bridges without ramps. Wheelchair near-impossible.
- **Lisbon:** Extreme hills + cobblestones + old staircases.
- **Athens (Acropolis):** Steps only, no elevator. Wheelchair users cannot reach summit.
- **Barcelona (Gothic Quarter):** Narrow, uneven, steep stairs in some areas.
- **Madeira (Funchal):** Steep hillside city, stairs everywhere.
- **Rome:** Colosseum/Forum: cobblestones, steps, uneven surfaces.

### Heat + Mobility = Dangerous Combination
Middle East ports (May-Sept) where extreme heat + required walking distances = dehydration/heat exhaustion risk, especially for elderly/disabled travelers. Air-conditioned attractions essential.

### Model Accessible Ports
- **Singapore:** Exceptionally accessible modern infrastructure.
- **Dubai:** Modern, excellent wheelchair access.
- **Cozumel:** Flat, beach-accessible, good infrastructure.
