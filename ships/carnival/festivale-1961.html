The Innovation Pioneer

TSS Festivale earned her place in cruise history not through size or luxury, but through innovation. In 1981, she became the first cruise ship to feature a waterslideâ€”a simple addition that revolutionized what passengers expected from cruise entertainment. That single slide opened the floodgates for the elaborate water parks we see on today's mega-ships.
