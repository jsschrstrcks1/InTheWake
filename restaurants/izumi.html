Izumi Sushi & Hibachi — Japanese Flavors at Sea

Experience two distinct takes on Japanese dining: the serene precision of Izumi Sushi and the fiery flair of Izumi Hibachi.

Quick Answer: Izumi offers two Japanese dining experiences: Izumi Sushi (à la carte sushi bar, $39.99 prix fixe for lunch/dinner) and Izumi Hibachi (interactive teppanyaki with chef shows, $45.99 Classic/$49.99 Premier combos for dinner only).
Best For: Izumi Sushi suits couples, sushi lovers, and light eaters seeking quiet romance. Izumi Hibachi fits families, groups up to 8, and anyone wanting theatrical dining with flame-throwing chef tricks.

Key Facts

  • Price: Sushi: à la carte $5-$20 or $39.99 prix fixe · Hibachi: $45.99-$49.99 combos
  • Hours: Sushi: lunch & dinner · Hibachi: dinner only (check Royal Caribbean app)
  • Dress Code: Smart casual
  • Reservations: Strongly recommended (book via Cruise Planner or app)
  • Dietary Options: Vegetarian rolls, gluten-free soy sauce available on request

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Izumi on Royal Caribbean?

Izumi is Royal Caribbean's Japanese specialty dining venue offering two distinct experiences: Izumi Sushi (à la carte sushi bar with fresh sashimi, specialty rolls, and small plates in a tranquil contemporary setting) and Izumi Hibachi (interactive teppanyaki with tableside chef shows featuring flames, knife tricks, and lively entertainment). Both serve authentic Japanese cuisine at sea.

How much does Izumi cost?

Izumi Sushi: À la carte items range $5-$20 per dish, or choose the prix fixe option at $39.99 (includes appetizer, multiple rolls, and dessert). Izumi Hibachi: Classic combo $45.99 (chicken teriyaki, NY strip, shrimp) or Premier combo $49.99 (filet mignon, lobster tail, scallops). All combos include soup/salad, fried rice, vegetables, and dessert.

Do you need reservations for Izumi?

Yes, reservations are strongly recommended for both Izumi Sushi and Izumi Hibachi. Book through the Royal Caribbean Cruise Planner before sailing for the best availability, or via the Royal Caribbean app once onboard. Walk-ins may be available based on capacity, but Hibachi tables (seating up to 8 guests) fill quickly, especially on sea days.

What are the menu highlights at Izumi?

Izumi Sushi: Spicy Tuna Roll ($14), Rainbow Roll ($18), Dragon Roll ($16), Lobster Ravioli Roll, Omakase Set ($16), fresh sashimi assortment (12 pcs $22), edamame, miso soup, mochi ice cream. Izumi Hibachi: Filet Mignon & Lobster Tail Premier combo ($49.99), Chicken Teriyaki, NY Strip, Scallops, fried rice and vegetables, plus the chef's theatrical tableside tricks with flames and knife skills.
