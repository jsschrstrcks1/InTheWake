Oceania Cruises Ships

Oceania Cruises proves that the finest things come in elegant packages. With 8 ships ranging from intimate 684-guest R-Class gems to the new 1,200-guest Allura Class, every vessel delivers the "Finest Cuisine at Sea" — including Jacques Pepin's signature dishes, Polo Grill steaks, and Toscana Italian. No mega-ship crowds, no formal nights required — just extraordinary dining and destination-rich itineraries.
