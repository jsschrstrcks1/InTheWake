About Us

Ahoy, voyagers! Welcome to In the Wake, your trusty compass for sailing through cruise planning with joy and ease. We’re here to chart your course with mariner wisdom—ship overviews, port tips, restaurant menus, solo travel hacks—all crafted to keep your adventure sailing through calm seas, no squalls in sight.

I’m your captain, driven by a love for adventure, beauty, and connection. In the Wake is my flagship project — a logbook sharing real-voyage know-how to ensure your trip feels like smooth sailing. Through Flickers of Majesty, I capture the sea’s fleeting wonders — glowing sunsets, vibrant ports, and the majesty of motion. I also serve as a pastor at Lakeview Fellowship Church, where we live by “Taking Strangers, Making Family.”

Our mission is to turn cruise planning into a delight, not a storm. We dive into the details — accessibility, packing lists, drink packages — so you can savor the memories.

Set sail with us! Explore our guides, pack your sea bag, and let’s make waves together.
