First Look: Norwegian Spirit

Norwegian Spirit is a beautifully renovated vessel that underwent a complete transformation in 2020, emerging as one of the most modern ships in the Norwegian fleet. Originally built in 1998 as SuperStar Leo for Star Cruises, she now carries 2,018 guests at 75,338 gross tons with an intimate atmosphere that larger ships cannot match.

The extensive refurbishment added modern amenities throughout including new restaurants, bars, and completely redesigned staterooms. Entertainment options include the Stardust Theater, multiple lounges, and a variety of bars offering everything from craft cocktails to live music and dancing.

Dining aboard Norwegian Spirit showcases NCL's freestyle dining philosophy with multiple specialty restaurants including Cagney's Steakhouse, Le Bistro French cuisine, and La Cucina Italian. The ship's size creates a welcoming atmosphere where guests quickly feel at home, making her ideal for destination-focused itineraries to Europe, Alaska, and the Caribbean.
