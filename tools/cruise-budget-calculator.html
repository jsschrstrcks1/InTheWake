Quick Answer: The cabin fare is typically 40-60% of your total cruise cost. This calculator helps you see the full picture before you book.

The Hidden 40% Most first-time cruisers budget only for the cabin fare. Gratuities, excursions, drinks, and specialty dining can add 40-60% to the advertised price. This calculator shows you the real number.
Gratuities Are Automatic Most major cruise lines add $16-20/person/day in automatic gratuities. On a 7-night cruise for two, that's $224-280. Budget for it — removing tips is frowned upon and hurts crew.
Drink Package Math Not sure if a drink package is worth it? Use our Drink Calculator to find your break-even point before adding it to your budget.
Free Port Days Not every port needs an excursion. Walking around town, visiting a free beach, or exploring on foot costs nothing. Budget big for your must-do ports and plan free days elsewhere.
Pre-Cruise Hotel Always arrive the day before sailing. Flights get delayed, bags get lost. A $150 hotel room is cheap insurance against a missed departure.
