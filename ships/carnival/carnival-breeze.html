Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Carnival Breeze sail from?

Carnival Breeze is homeported at Galveston, Texas, offering Caribbean cruises year-round.

What class is Carnival Breeze?

Carnival Breeze is a Dream-class ship, sister to Carnival Dream (2009) and Carnival Magic (2011).

Does Carnival Breeze have a water park?

Yes, WaterWorks features water slides, splash zones, and the DrainPipe spiral slide.
