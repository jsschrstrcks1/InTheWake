Hideaway Bar

Royal Caribbean — Bar & Lounge

Quick Answer: Hideaway Bar is the bar at Hideaway Beach, Royal Caribbean's first adults-only (18+) sundeck on Icon Class ships. Features a beach club atmosphere with DJ entertainment, premium cocktails, daybeds, and stunning ocean views. Drink packages accepted. The perfect escape from family areas.

Best For: Adults seeking a kid-free zone, couples, groups of friends, beach club atmosphere lovers, and anyone wanting a party vibe at sea.

Key Facts

  • Age: 18+ only
  • Atmosphere: Beach club/party vibe
  • Entertainment: DJ during peak hours
  • Drinks: Premium cocktails, package accepted
  • Location: Hideaway Beach, Icon Class

Your adults-only beach party at sea.

Hideaway Bar brings the Ibiza beach club experience to the Caribbean. With a DJ spinning beats, cocktails flowing, and no kids in sight, it's become one of Icon Class's most popular venues for adults. Special Accommodations

Accessibility Notes: Hideaway Beach is wheelchair accessible. The bar has accessible seating areas. Pool entry may require assistance. Staff can assist with accessibility needs. Note: This is an 18+ venue.

Where You'll Find It

Hideaway Beach is located at the aft of Icon Class ships, with its own dedicated entrance enforcing the 18+ policy. The bar is at the center of the sundeck.

4.5 ★ out of 5 Icon of the Seas • Girls Trip

Hideaway Bar Review: The Party We Needed

Introduction. Four of us on a girls trip found our home base at Hideaway Beach. The bar, the DJ, the adults-only policy—it was exactly what we were looking for. We spent most of our sea days here.

The Atmosphere

Picture a high-end beach club in Ibiza, but floating in the Caribbean. The DJ plays energetic music all afternoon, people are dancing, cocktails are flowing. It's not rowdy or out of control—just fun, lively energy.

The Drinks

Cocktails were excellent—bartenders know their craft. Our drink packages covered almost everything. The frozen drinks were perfect for the heat. Service was attentive without being pushy.

The Details

Daybeds went fast—arrive early if you want one. The pool was great for cooling off between drinks. Having a guaranteed kid-free zone made the atmosphere so much more relaxed for our group.

Conclusion

If you're sailing Icon without kids and want a party atmosphere, Hideaway is your spot. It's what makes this ship work for adults even with all the family-focused areas elsewhere. Rating: 4.5/5.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hideaway Beach really adults only?

Yes, strictly 18+ only. Staff check IDs at the entrance. This is Royal Caribbean's first adults-only sundeck at sea.

Can I bring my drink package here?

Yes, the Deluxe Beverage Package is accepted at Hideaway Bar. Some ultra-premium items may have an upcharge.

Do I need a reservation?

No reservation needed for general access. Cabanas require advance booking through the Royal Caribbean app. Daybeds are first-come, first-served.

What kind of music does the DJ play?

Expect upbeat beach club vibes—dance, house, pop remixes. The energy is festive without being overwhelming.

Is there food at Hideaway Beach?

Light snacks are available. For full meals, you'll need to visit other dining venues on the ship.
