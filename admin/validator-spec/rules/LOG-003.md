## Rule
The ship logbook's 7-section spine per LOGBOOK_ENTRY_STANDARDS_v2.300 includes:
1. `## Full disclosure` (see LOG-002)
2. `## Crew & Staff`
3. `## Embarkation`
4. `## Real Talk`
5. `## Accessibility`
6. `## Female Crewmate's Perspective`
7. `## Closing Thoughts`

The 2026-02 audit found T2-tier logbooks have none of these `##` headings, no disability content, and no Female Crewmate section. Only 21 of 2,126 stories (1%) follow the full standard.

No validator enforces the 7-section spine — orphan. SHIP-006 captures persona coverage at a higher level (the 7 persona categories); LOG-003 captures the specific structural spine.

## Why (rationale)
The Accessibility section ensures every logbook addresses the experience of disabled travelers — the site's stated audience. The Female Crewmate section captures a perspective rarely heard in cruise reviews. Both are pastoral commitments, not optional content categories.

## Fix guidance
Add the `##` headings to logbook entries as they're written or rewritten. Existing T2-quality logbooks (batch-generated with generic personas) will naturally get these headings as they're upgraded to T1 quality. Do not add empty `##` sections to satisfy the count — each section must carry real content.
