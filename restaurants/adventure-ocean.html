Adventure Ocean

Royal Caribbean — Youth Program

Quick Answer: Adventure Ocean is Royal Caribbean's complimentary kids program for ages 6 months to 17 years. Divided by age groups with supervised activities including crafts, games, science experiments, and themed parties. Free 9am-10pm; late-night babysitting $7-10/hr.

Best For: Families wanting supervised, age-appropriate entertainment for children while parents enjoy adult activities on the ship.

Key Facts

  • Ages: 6 months - 17 years
  • Price: Complimentary (9am-10pm)
  • Late Night: $7-10/hr per child
  • Hours: 9am - Midnight (sea days)
  • Registration: Required on embarkation day

Programs & Age Groups

All daytime programming: Complimentary · Late-night (10pm-2am): $7-10/hr per child

Ages 0-5

  • Royal Babies (6-18 mo): Parent-accompanied sensory play
  • Royal Tots (18-36 mo): Parent-accompanied interactive play
  • Aquanauts (3-5): First drop-off group — games, crafts, imagination play

Ages 6-17

  • Explorers (6-8): Science experiments, adventure games, movies
  • Voyagers (9-11): Tech lab, team challenges, pool parties
  • Teens (12-17): Dedicated lounge, late-night events, social activities

Tips for Parents

Pro Tips: Pre-register online before your cruise. Visit on embarkation day with your child to complete registration and get wristbands. Children 3+ must be fully potty-trained (no pull-ups). Arrive early on sea days — busiest times are mid-morning.

Hours & Registration

Sea Days: 9am - Midnight. Port Days: Variable, often afternoon/evening only. Free Hours: 9am-10pm. Late Night (fee): 10pm-2am. Registration required — complete online pre-cruise or at Adventure Ocean on embarkation day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Adventure Ocean free?

Yes, daytime hours (9am-10pm) are complimentary. Late-night care (10pm-2am) is $7-10/hr per child.

Does my 3-year-old need to be potty-trained?

Yes, children ages 3+ must be fully potty-trained (no diapers or pull-ups) for the drop-off Aquanauts program.

Can Adventure Ocean contact me?

Yes, staff will message you via the Royal Caribbean app or ship phone system if your child needs you.
