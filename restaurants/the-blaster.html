The Blaster

Royal Caribbean — Aqua Coaster

Quick Answer: The Blaster is the longest waterslide at sea, found on select Oasis Class ships. This aqua coaster uses powerful water jets to propel two-person rafts uphill through twists, turns, and thrilling drops. It's a unique ride that defies gravity—and it's completely free.

Best For: Thrill seekers. Waterslide enthusiasts. Families with kids meeting height requirements. Anyone wanting a unique water experience.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Height Requirement: 42 inches (107 cm)
  • Riders: 2 per raft
  • Length: Longest waterslide at sea
  • Ships: Select Oasis Class ships

Defy gravity on the water.

The Blaster isn't your typical waterslide—it's an aqua coaster. Water jets propel your raft uphill through the ride, creating a roller-coaster-like experience on water. Expect twists, turns, tunnels, and thrilling drops as you race through the longest waterslide at sea. It's one of the most popular attractions on ships that have it. Return to the Restaurants hub →

Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Blaster free?

Yes, completely complimentary.

How tall do you need to be?

Minimum 42 inches (107 cm) tall.

Can I ride alone?

Rafts are designed for two people, but single riders may be paired or allowed solo depending on operations.

How long are the waits?

Can be 20-45 minutes during peak times. Go early morning or during dinner for shorter lines.

Is it scary?

It's thrilling but not terrifying. Most people who meet the height requirement handle it fine.

Which ships have The Blaster?

Select amplified ships, including Navigator of the Seas. Check your ship's amenities.
