Gothenburg: My Scandinavian Cool

Walking through the old city center, you can still see their fingerprints ‚Äì the canals, the pragmatic grid, the no-nonsense mercantile spirit.

That Atlantic gateway the kings fought for? Still here, still humming. In 1927, Volvo was founded on Hisingen, the very island where Charles IX first planted his flag. The first Volvo automobile rolled off the line that same year, and both Volvo Cars and Volvo Group are still headquartered here.
