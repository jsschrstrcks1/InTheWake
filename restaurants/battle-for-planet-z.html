Battle for Planet Z

Royal Caribbean — Laser Tag Arena

Quick Answer: Battle for Planet Z is a glow-in-the-dark laser tag arena on select Royal Caribbean ships. Teams battle in an immersive sci-fi themed setting with barriers, hiding spots, and glowing targets. It's free, fun for all ages, and perfect for families or groups.

Best For: Families with kids. Competitive groups. Laser tag enthusiasts. Teens. Anyone looking for active entertainment.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Complimentary
  • Ages: All ages welcome
  • Format: Team vs team battles
  • Setting: Glow-in-the-dark sci-fi arena
  • Ships: Select Royal Caribbean ships

Space battles at sea.

Battle for Planet Z transports you to a sci-fi universe where teams compete in glow-in-the-dark laser tag battles. The arena features barriers, obstacles, and hiding spots for tactical gameplay. Games are action-packed, competitive, and suitable for all ages—making it a hit with families traveling with kids or teens. Best of all, it's free. Return to the Restaurants hub →

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Battle for Planet Z free?

Yes, completely complimentary.

What ages can play?

All ages—younger kids can be paired with parents.

How long are games?

Typically 10-15 minutes per session.

Do I need to book ahead?

Usually first-come, first-served. Check your Cruise Compass for session times.

Is it physical?

Moderately—you'll be walking, crouching, and moving around the arena.

What about flashing lights?

The arena uses glow effects and some flashing lights. Those sensitive should consider this.
