Jewel of the Seas

Jewel is beloved for Mediterranean summers and Caribbean winters, where her glassy profile frames the ports as the headliner.

Crowd flow is excellent; nothing is too far, but you still discover tucked-away nooks for a quiet read with a view.

Crew rapport and repeat cruisers give Jewel a warm, clubby feel.

Choose a balcony for the itineraries that beg for sail‑in and sail‑away moments.

For port collectors and scenery chasers, Jewel is a steady favorite.
