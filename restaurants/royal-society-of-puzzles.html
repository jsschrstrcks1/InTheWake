The Royal Society of Puzzles

Royal Caribbean — Interactive Puzzle Experience

Quick Answer: The Royal Society of Puzzles is an interactive puzzle experience on select Royal Caribbean ships. Teams work together to solve clues, crack codes, and complete challenges in themed environments. Similar to escape rooms but with a unique nautical twist.

Best For: Puzzle lovers. Families with older children. Groups looking for team activities. Escape room enthusiasts. Anyone who enjoys brain teasers.

Key Facts

  • Cost: Fee-based (~$20-25 per person)
  • Duration: Approximately 60 minutes
  • Team Size: 2-8 players
  • Reservations: Recommended
  • Age: Typically 8+

Test your wits at sea.

The Royal Society of Puzzles brings immersive puzzle-solving adventures to Royal Caribbean ships. Work as a team to decipher clues, find hidden objects, and complete challenges before time runs out. Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Approximately $20-25 per person, though prices may vary by ship.

Can kids participate?

Yes, typically ages 8 and up with adult supervision.

What if we don't finish in time?

You'll still enjoy the full experience and can learn the solution afterward.

Do we need a full team?

Smaller groups can book and may be paired with others.

How do I book?

Book through Cruise Planner pre-cruise or at activities desk onboard.

Is it scary?

Generally no—it's puzzle-focused, not horror-themed.
