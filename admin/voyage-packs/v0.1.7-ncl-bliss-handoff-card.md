## What the at-home contact does if 36+ hours pass without word

2. **Call the NCL Care Team** above; they'll confirm you boarded and relay messages.
3. **Reach Kristie** if you shared her contact info — she's the in-person on the ship.
4. **If NCL confirms incident:** US Coast Guard (Alaska waters); US Consulate Vancouver (Victoria); ship medical (health). Track ship position at /tools/ship-tracker.html.
