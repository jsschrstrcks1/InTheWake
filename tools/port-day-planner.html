Quick Answer: Fill in your port day details, add activities to the timeline, and hit Print to take your plan ashore. Your data stays in your browser — nothing is sent to a server.

All-Aboard Rule of Thumb Plan to be back at the pier 60 minutes before all-aboard time. Traffic, lines for tenders, and unexpected delays happen. Missing the ship is not a theoretical risk.
Cash vs. Card Many Caribbean ports prefer USD cash for taxis, markets, and small restaurants. In Europe and Asia, cards are widely accepted but ATMs at cruise terminals often have high fees. Bring some local currency.
Port Guide Links Visit our Port Guides for "From the Pier" walking distances, transport costs, and honest Real Talk about each destination.
Budget Tip The timeline budget column tracks per-person costs. For couples, multiply by 2. Don't forget taxi fare back to the pier — the most common forgotten expense.
