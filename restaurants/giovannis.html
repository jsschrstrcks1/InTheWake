Royal Caribbean — Specialty Italian (Two Concepts)

Quick Answer: Royal Caribbean offers two Giovanni's Italian concepts: Giovanni's Italian Kitchen (modern trattoria with pizzas, $14.99 lunch/$34.99 dinner) and Giovanni's Table (rustic family-style, $18.99 lunch/$34.99-39.99 dinner). Both serve authentic Italian with different atmospheres.

Best For: Italian Kitchen suits families, groups, and pizza lovers seeking casual dining. Giovanni's Table fits date nights, celebrations, and classic carb-comfort feasts. Both offer shareable plates and wine pairings.

Key Facts

  • Price: Kitchen: $14.99 lunch/$34.99 dinner · Table: $18.99 lunch/$34.99-39.99 dinner (gratuity included)
  • Hours: Sea-day lunch, nightly dinner (varies by ship; check Royal Caribbean app)
  • Dress Code: Smart casual
  • Reservations: Recommended (book via Cruise Planner or app)
  • Dietary Options: Vegetarian pastas, gluten-free accommodations on request

Giovanni's comes in two flavors: the newer Italian Kitchen (pizza-forward, modern trattoria) and the classic Giovanni's Table (family-style, rustic Italian). This guide compares both on one page so you can pick the right vibe, price point, and menu for your sailing.

Giovanni’s Italian Kitchen — Details

Positioning: casual trattoria with hand-tossed pizzas, antipasti, hearty pastas, and Nonna’s classics. Launched fleetwide 2019→ with ongoing rollouts.

  • Price: Lunch $14.99; Dinner $34.99 (incl. gratuity). Packages reduce cost.
  • Hours: Sea-day lunch; nightly dinner (check app for your sailing).
  • Highlights: Calamari Fritti; Gigantic Meatball; Lobster Ravioli; Lasagna Bolognese; Chicken Parm; pizza “Giovanni’s” (salami + honey).
  • Who will love it: Families, groups, pizza-people, shareable-plate fans.
Sample Menu & Pricing
  • Antipasti: Calamari Fritti; Stromboli; Meatball (shareable)
  • Insalate: Caprese; Caesar
  • Pizza: Margherita; Pepperoni; Giovanni’s (salami, honey)
  • Pastas: Lobster Ravioli (sage cream); Fettuccine Alfredo (+chicken); Lasagna Bolognese
  • Entrees: Chicken Parmigiana; Osso Buco (Nonna’s); Grilled Mahi Puttanesca
  • Dolci: Tiramisu; Cannoli; Gelato

Lunch focuses on pizza + lighter plates; dinner adds fuller entrées. Actual menus rotate by sailing.

Giovanni’s Table — Details

Positioning: rustic, family-style Italian with generous pastas and classics in a cozy, romantic setting. Long-time Royal staple.

  • Price: Lunch $18.99; Dinner $34.99–$39.99 (incl. gratuity). Packages available.
  • Hours: Sea-day lunch; nightly dinner (check app for your sailing).
  • Highlights: Lasagna Bolognese; Osso Buco with risotto; Lobster Ravioli; Shrimp Scampi Bruschetta; classic antipasti.
  • Who will love it: Date-nighters, carb-comfort seekers, celebration dinners.
Sample Menu & Pricing
  • Antipasti: Calamari; Stromboli; Meatball (shareable)
  • Soups/Salads: Minestrone; Caprese; Italian Sausage Soup
  • Pastas: Lasagna Bolognese; Fettuccine Alfredo; Lobster Ravioli
  • Entrees: Osso Buco; Chicken Parmigiana; Filet (select ships)
  • Dolci: Tiramisu; Cannoli; Cheesecake

Lunch is abbreviated; dinner menu is broader with heavier mains. Rotations vary by ship.

Which One Should You Book?

  • Want pizza + shareables? Pick Italian Kitchen (lunch is a value play).
  • Want cozy, classic, and hearty? Pick Giovanni’s Table (great for occasions).
  • Using a dining package? Either is solid; Kitchen tends to feel “broader” for groups.
  • With kids? Kitchen’s pizzas keep everyone happy; both have kid options.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Giovanni's Italian on Royal Caribbean?

Royal Caribbean offers two Giovanni's Italian concepts: Giovanni's Italian Kitchen (modern trattoria with hand-tossed pizzas, antipasti, pastas, and shareable plates in a casual atmosphere) and Giovanni's Table (rustic family-style dining with classic Italian pastas, osso buco, and hearty entrées in a cozy, romantic setting). They have different menus, pricing, and vibes.

How much does Giovanni's cost?

Italian Kitchen: $14.99 lunch, $34.99 dinner (includes gratuity). Giovanni's Table: $18.99 lunch, $34.99–$39.99 dinner (varies by ship class). Both offer value through Royal Caribbean's unlimited dining packages.

Do you need reservations for Giovanni's?

Yes, reservations are recommended for both concepts. Book through the Royal Caribbean Cruise Planner before sailing for the best availability, or via the Royal Caribbean app once onboard. Walk-ins may be accommodated based on capacity, but peak times often fill up.

What are the menu highlights at Giovanni's?

Italian Kitchen: Hand-tossed pizzas (Margherita, Pepperoni, Giovanni's with salami & honey), lobster ravioli with sage cream, lasagna Bolognese, chicken parmesan, calamari fritti, gigantic meatball, tiramisu. Giovanni's Table: Osso buco with risotto, lobster ravioli, lasagna Bolognese, shrimp scampi bruschetta, fettuccine Alfredo, classic antipasti, tiramisu and cannoli.
