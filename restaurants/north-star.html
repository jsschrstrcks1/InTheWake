For the best experience, choose a sunset time slot and book within the first hour of boarding.

Overview

North Star represents one of Royal Caribbean's most innovative attractions—a stunning engineering achievement that gives passengers a bird's-eye view of their ship and the surrounding seascape. The jewel-shaped glass capsule is attached to a robotic arm that gently lifts guests 300 feet above sea level while rotating 360 degrees for unobstructed panoramic views.

The experience lasts approximately 15-20 minutes, during which the capsule performs a graceful choreographed movement, rising, rotating, and extending over the ocean. Whether you're watching a sunset, approaching a port, or simply enjoying endless ocean views, North Star delivers a perspective unlike anything else at sea.

The Technology

North Star utilizes advanced robotic arm technology adapted from shipyard cranes. The capsule is climate-controlled with floor-to-ceiling glass panels and can operate in winds up to 30 knots. Safety features include multiple redundant systems and the capsule remains level throughout the entire experience regardless of ship movement.

Tips for the Best Experience

Timing Your Visit

  • Book immediately: Reservations open on embarkation day—book within the first hour for best selection
  • Sunset slots: Most popular and book within minutes of opening
  • Port approach: Morning slots when arriving at scenic ports offer unique views
  • Sea days: Less competition for slots than port days

Weather Considerations

  • North Star operates in most weather conditions but may close in high winds or storms
  • If your slot is cancelled due to weather, visit Guest Services for rebooking
  • Overcast days can still offer dramatic views and better photography (no harsh shadows)
  • The capsule is climate-controlled—comfortable year-round

Photography Tips

  • Bring your smartphone or camera—no tripods allowed
  • Glass reflections can be minimized by wearing dark clothing
  • Wide-angle lens recommended for capturing the full panorama
  • Golden hour (sunset) provides the best lighting

Accessibility

  • Wheelchair accessible—capsule floor is level with deck
  • Mobility devices can be accommodated with advance notice
  • Service animals welcome
  • Not recommended for those with severe fear of heights

Availability & Booking

Operating Hours

  • Sea Days: 9:00 AM - 10:00 PM (weather permitting)
  • Port Days: Variable based on sailing schedule
  • Session Duration: 15-20 minutes per ride

How to Book

Complimentary rides: Use the Royal Caribbean app starting on embarkation day. Navigate to "Activities" → "North Star" and select your preferred time slot.

Premium packages: Pre-book through the Cruise Planner before your sailing or visit the North Star desk on Deck 15 once onboard.

Pro tip: Have the app open and ready when reservations open—popular times book within minutes.

Requirements & Restrictions

  • No minimum age requirement
  • Children under 18 must be accompanied by an adult
  • Maximum 14 guests per capsule
  • Loose items must be secured or stored
  • No food or beverages except for premium package inclusions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is North Star free on Royal Caribbean?

Yes, basic North Star rides are complimentary and included with your cruise fare. Premium experiences with champagne, sunset packages, or private charters are available for an additional cost starting at $39 per person.

How high does North Star go?

North Star rises approximately 300 feet (91 meters) above sea level, providing unobstructed 360-degree views of the ship, ocean, and any nearby land masses. The exact height varies slightly based on sea conditions.

How do I book North Star?

Complimentary reservations open on embarkation day through the Royal Caribbean app. Go to Activities → North Star to select your time slot. Premium packages can be pre-booked through the Cruise Planner before your sailing.

Which ships have North Star?

North Star is available exclusively on Quantum Class ships: Quantum of the Seas, Anthem of the Seas, Ovation of the Seas, and Spectrum of the Seas.

Is North Star safe?

Absolutely. North Star features multiple redundant safety systems and is certified by maritime safety authorities. The capsule is climate-controlled and remains level regardless of ship movement. It operates in winds up to 30 knots.

Can I ride North Star if I'm afraid of heights?

The smooth, gradual movement helps many people with mild height concerns. However, if you have a severe fear of heights, you may want to consider whether this activity is right for you. The glass floor and walls provide full visibility of the height.

What happens if weather cancels my North Star booking?

If your reservation is cancelled due to weather, visit Guest Services to rebook for another available time slot. The crew is typically accommodating in finding alternatives for affected guests.
