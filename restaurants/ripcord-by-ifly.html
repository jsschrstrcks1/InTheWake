RipCord by iFLY

Indoor skydiving at sea—experience the sensation of freefall flight

Min Age 3 years
Max Weight 300 lbs
Duration ~1 hour total
Price Complimentary

Quick Answer: RipCord by iFLY is a complimentary indoor skydiving simulator exclusive to Quantum Class ships. You'll experience the sensation of freefall flight in a vertical wind tunnel with speeds up to 120 mph. Sessions include training, flight suit, and instructor guidance. Book via the Royal Caribbean app immediately on embarkation day—slots fill within minutes.

Overview

RipCord by iFLY brings the exhilaration of skydiving to the high seas. Developed in partnership with iFLY, the world's leading indoor skydiving company, this vertical wind tunnel generates air speeds up to 120 mph, creating enough lift to suspend you in mid-air—simulating the freefall sensation of jumping from an airplane.

Located on the upper deck of Quantum Class ships, RipCord offers something no land-based facility can match: flying with panoramic ocean views. The transparent flight chamber lets spectators watch while flyers experience the unique thrill of floating above the waves.

How It Works

The vertical wind tunnel uses powerful fans beneath the flight chamber to generate a column of air strong enough to support human flight. Professional instructors guide you through every moment—from your first hover to controlled movements. Most guests are flying confidently within seconds.

What's Included

  • Pre-flight training session (approximately 30 minutes)
  • Flight suit, helmet, goggles, and earplugs
  • Two flights (approximately 1 minute of total flight time)
  • Hands-on instructor guidance throughout
  • Certificate of completion (on request)

Ship Availability

RipCord by iFLY is exclusively available on Quantum Class ships:

  • Quantum of the Seas (2014)
  • Anthem of the Seas (2015)
  • Ovation of the Seas (2016)
  • Spectrum of the Seas (2019)

The Experience

Your RipCord session includes everything you need for an unforgettable flight:

Session Timeline

  • Check-In & Training ~30 min

    Sign waiver, receive equipment, and learn proper body position, hand signals, and safety protocols from certified instructors.

  • Suit Up ~10 min

    Put on your flight suit, helmet, goggles, and earplugs. Instructors verify proper fit and answer final questions.

  • First Flight ~30 sec

    Your instructor guides you into the wind tunnel. Focus on body position as you experience your first moments of flight.

  • Second Flight ~30 sec

    Apply what you learned—more confident flyers may attempt turns and altitude changes with instructor assistance.

Optional Add-Ons

  • High-Flying Experience $49+

    Extended flight time with instructor-assisted high-altitude maneuvers—spins, flips, and soaring flights.

  • Photo & Video Package $29+

    Professional photos and video of your flight experience to share and remember.

* Complimentary sessions include two flights. Premium packages available for extended experiences.

Tips for Success

Booking Strategy

  • Book on embarkation day: Sessions fill within the first hour of boarding
  • Use the app: Navigate to Activities → RipCord by iFLY the moment you board
  • Sea days are busiest: Port day slots may be easier to secure
  • Morning vs evening: Morning slots often have better availability

What to Wear

  • Athletic wear or comfortable clothes that won't ride up
  • Lace-up sneakers (required—no sandals, flip-flops, or slip-ons)
  • Remove jewelry, watches, and loose accessories
  • Tie back long hair securely

During Your Flight

  • Relax—tension makes flying harder
  • Keep your chin up and eyes forward
  • Spread your fingers slightly for stability
  • Trust your instructor's hand signals
  • Breathe normally through your mouth

Health Considerations

RipCord is generally safe for most guests, but review these guidelines:

  • Not recommended with recent shoulder dislocations
  • Consult ship's medical staff if you have neck or back issues
  • Pregnant guests should not participate
  • Heart conditions may require medical clearance

Availability & Requirements

Operating Hours

  • Sea Days: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM (weather permitting)
  • Port Days: Variable based on sailing schedule
  • Session Length: ~1 hour total (including training)

How to Book

Complimentary sessions are booked through the Royal Caribbean app starting on embarkation day. Navigate to Activities → RipCord by iFLY and select an available time slot.

Pro tip: Have your app open and ready before the ship departs. Sessions can book out within 15 minutes of opening.

Requirements

  • Minimum age: 3 years old
  • Maximum weight: 300 lbs (136 kg)
  • Footwear: Lace-up sneakers required
  • Parental consent: Required for guests under 18
  • Waiver: All participants must sign liability waiver

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RipCord by iFLY free on Royal Caribbean?

Yes, basic RipCord sessions are complimentary and included with your cruise fare. Premium experiences like the High-Flying package and photo/video add-ons are available for an additional cost.

What is the age and weight limit for RipCord by iFLY?

Guests must be at least 3 years old and weigh less than 300 lbs (136 kg). There is no upper age limit as long as you're in reasonable physical condition. Children under 18 require parental consent.

Do I need experience to try RipCord by iFLY?

No prior experience is needed. Every session begins with comprehensive training covering body position, hand signals, and safety procedures. Certified instructors guide you throughout your entire flight.

Which Royal Caribbean ships have RipCord by iFLY?

RipCord by iFLY is available exclusively on Quantum Class ships: Quantum of the Seas, Anthem of the Seas, Ovation of the Seas, and Spectrum of the Seas.

How long is the actual flight time?

The complimentary experience includes two flights totaling approximately 1 minute of actual flight time. While it sounds brief, you'll find it's surprisingly substantial and exhilarating. Premium packages offer extended flight time.

What should I wear to RipCord?

Wear comfortable athletic clothing and lace-up sneakers (required—no sandals or slip-ons). Remove jewelry, watches, and loose accessories. Long hair must be tied back. You'll be provided with a flight suit, helmet, goggles, and earplugs.

Can RipCord operate in bad weather?

RipCord may close during high winds or severe weather conditions. If your session is cancelled due to weather, visit Guest Services to rebook for another available time slot.
