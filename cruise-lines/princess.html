Princess Cruises

Quick Answer: Princess Cruises delivers destination-driven itineraries with MedallionClass technology (keyless staterooms, contactless payment), gracious service, and signature experiences like Movies Under the Stars. The newest Sphere Class ships (Sun Princess, Star Princess) feature refreshed poolscapes, airier atriums, and enhanced dining venues.

Is Princess Right for You?

Princess invites cruisers who value gracious service, destination-focused itineraries, and thoughtful conveniences. MedallionClass technology quietly removes friction—unlocking your stateroom door, ordering drinks poolside, and tracking your loved ones onboard all happen seamlessly through the Ocean Medallion wearable.

Perfect for couples seeking relaxed sophistication, multi-generational families who appreciate varied dining options, and travelers who want Alaska, Mediterranean, or Asia-Pacific itineraries done well. Movies Under the Stars, The Sanctuary adults-only retreat, and Chef's Table elevate the experience for those who seek both relaxation and special moments. Formal nights exist but are never mandatory.

Key Facts at a Glance

  • Ship Classes: Sphere Class (newest, 2023), Royal Class, Grand Class, and earlier ships
  • Signature Technology: MedallionClass (keyless entry, contactless payment, personalized experiences)
  • Best For: Destination-focused travelers, couples, multi-generational families
  • Unique Features: Movies Under the Stars, The Sanctuary, Chef's Table, gracious service

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MedallionClass on Princess Cruises?

MedallionClass is Princess's digital technology platform featuring the Ocean Medallion wearable device. It enables keyless stateroom entry, contactless payment, on-demand services, and personalized experiences throughout the ship.

What is Movies Under the Stars on Princess?

Movies Under the Stars is Princess's signature poolside cinema experience, featuring first-run films and classics shown on a giant LED screen under the night sky, with complimentary popcorn and blankets provided.

What ship classes does Princess Cruises operate?

Princess operates the Sphere Class (newest, launched 2023), Royal Class, Grand Class, and earlier classes. The Sphere Class ships (Sun Princess, Star Princess) feature redesigned spaces and enhanced amenities.

What is The Sanctuary on Princess ships?

The Sanctuary is an adults-only retreat featuring premium lounge chairs, attentive service, light meals, and beverages. It's a tranquil escape from the main pool areas with a nominal daily fee.
