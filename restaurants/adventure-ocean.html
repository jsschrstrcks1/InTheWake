Quick Answer: Adventure Ocean is Royal Caribbean's complimentary kids program serving ages 6 months to 17 years. Divided into age-appropriate groups with supervised activities including crafts, games, science experiments, and themed parties. Open 9am-10pm at no charge; late-night care (10pm-2am) is $7-10/hour per child. Register online before your cruise or at the facility on embarkation day.

Overview

Adventure Ocean is Royal Caribbean's award-winning youth program, providing supervised, age-appropriate activities for children and teens throughout the cruise. The program gives parents time to enjoy adult activities while ensuring kids have an exciting, safe experience with trained youth staff.

The facilities span dedicated areas on the ship, typically including playrooms, science labs, gaming zones, and separate teen lounges. Activities are designed by age group and often tie into port destinations, creating educational opportunities alongside the fun.

Age Groups

  • Royal Babies (6-18 months): Parent-accompanied play sessions, sensory activities
  • Royal Tots (18-36 months): Parent-accompanied programming, interactive play
  • Aquanauts (3-5 years): First drop-off group, games, crafts, imagination play
  • Explorers (6-8 years): Science experiments, creative activities, adventure games
  • Voyagers (9-11 years): Team challenges, technology, more independence
  • Teens (12-14): Social events, sports, game tournaments
  • Teens (15-17): Late-night events, lounge access, social activities

Ship Availability

Adventure Ocean is available on all Royal Caribbean ships. Largest facilities on:

  • Icon Class: Surfside neighborhood with dedicated family zone
  • Oasis Class: Extensive kids areas with Ultimate Family Suite
  • Quantum Class: SeaPlex integration for kids activities

Tips for Parents

Registration

  • Pre-register online: Complete forms before your cruise to save time
  • Embarkation day: Bring your child to Adventure Ocean to complete registration and get their wristband
  • Emergency contacts: Update your cell number for the ship's messaging system
  • Medical info: Disclose allergies, medications, and special needs

Making the Most of It

  • Check the schedule: Pick up the daily activity schedule to plan around special events
  • Encourage participation: Kids often warm up after the first day—keep trying!
  • Coordinate with dinner: Drop off kids before your specialty dining reservation
  • Sea day strategy: Adventure Ocean is busiest on sea days—arrive early

Safety & Security

  • Secure check-in/out system requires parent wristband match
  • Children receive matching wristbands at registration
  • Staff can text you if needed through ship's messaging
  • Teens can self-sign (with parental permission) but younger children must be picked up by registered adults

Port Days

  • Adventure Ocean typically opens later on port days
  • Great option if you want adult shore excursion while kids stay aboard
  • Consider family excursions for port days, Adventure Ocean for sea days

Hours & Registration

Operating Hours

  • Sea Days: 9:00 AM - 12:00 AM (midnight)
  • Port Days: Variable, often afternoon/evening only
  • Free Hours: 9:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Late Night (fee): 10:00 PM - 2:00 AM

Registration Process

  1. Pre-cruise: Complete online registration forms
  2. Embarkation day: Visit Adventure Ocean with your child
  3. Receive wristbands: Both parent and child get matching security bands
  4. Tour the space: Staff give orientation and explain check-in/out

Requirements

  • Potty training: Children 3+ must be toilet-trained for drop-off (pull-ups not accepted)
  • Age verification: Passport or birth certificate may be requested
  • Health policy: Children with fever, diarrhea, or contagious illness cannot attend
  • Pick-up policy: Parents must pick up children by stated closing time

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Adventure Ocean free on Royal Caribbean?

Yes, Adventure Ocean programming during normal hours (typically 9am-10pm) is complimentary for all guests. Late-night babysitting services after 10pm have a per-child hourly fee of approximately $7-10.

What ages does Adventure Ocean serve?

Adventure Ocean serves children from 6 months to 17 years, divided into age-appropriate groups: Royal Babies (6-18 months), Royal Tots (18-36 months), Aquanauts (3-5), Explorers (6-8), Voyagers (9-11), and separate teen programs (12-14 and 15-17).

Do I need to register for Adventure Ocean?

Yes, registration is required. You can pre-register online before your cruise, but you must still visit Adventure Ocean on embarkation day with your child to complete registration and receive security wristbands.

Can my child come and go from Adventure Ocean?

Children under 12 must be signed in and out by a registered parent/guardian. Teens (12-17) can sign themselves in and out with parental permission noted during registration. A secure check-in/out system tracks all children.

Does my 3-year-old need to be potty-trained?

Yes, children ages 3 and up must be fully potty-trained (not in diapers or pull-ups) to participate in the drop-off Aquanauts program. This is for hygiene and safety reasons.

Can Adventure Ocean contact me if my child needs me?

Yes, staff will send a message through the ship's messaging system to your phone. Make sure your cell number is registered and you have the Royal Caribbean app or ship phone access.

Is there a teen area separate from younger kids?

Yes, teens have their own dedicated lounge and programming separate from younger children. Teens 12-17 have exclusive spaces and can self-sign in and out with parental permission.
